    welcome to wow.

    bandit masks are drops from defias mobs thru out westfall.

      re: My Stress Beta Rogue

      i want to get my hands on a mask too, but they arent easy.

      there isnt really any head item (aside from the lucky fishing hat, which looks like ass imo) thats wearable below lvl 25. so until you get into your low 20's, try not to think about it. i settled for some badass looking goggles for my rogue. i can post a pic later if you want.

      my rogue is undead, and ive heard undead have one of the best newbie zones. that aside, ive been doing quests solid up to lvl 27 where i am now, and they dont slow down. my roomate is 52, and has never really sat around grinding for exp like we did in eq. you kill mobs as a means to finish your quest, and the quest rewards are where the real exp is.

      you can sit around and kill mobs if you want, but in general the exp is a lot slower. also quests tend to give other rewards, as im sure you've seen, such as money or items.

      afraid i cant give you any pvp tips/tricks, as i dont pvp.

      enjoy, its a lot of fun, and you're gonna go through some withdrawal when the test is over :>
