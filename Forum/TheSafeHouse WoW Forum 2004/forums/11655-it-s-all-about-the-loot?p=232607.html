    It's all about the loot

    06-25-04, 12:56 AM
    Much as I love City of Heroes, I find myself missing the best and worst part of Everquest. Namely, hunting down treasure. I miss the idea of legendary items, the kinds of things you could just mention and the whole group would say, "Oooh, I wish I had one of those..." DAoC didn't have anything like that, and CoH neatly sidesteps all the problems inherent in these systems by no having loot at all. How does WoW handle this? Are there level limits to equipment like in DAoC?

            Level limits yes - like in DAoC no.

            My 13 rogue currently has basic knowledge in: Fishing, Cooking, Herbalism, First Aid, Skinning, Leatherworking, Alchemy, Enchanting

            This means I can:
            - collect herbs and use them for Enchanting and Alchemy
            - fish and gain food (-> healing)
            - skin animals and gain leather
            - make leather armor, improve leather armor I get
            - make bandages for healing after a fight
            - cook flesh I get from animals to get food (-> for healing)
            - enchant my armor permanently and temporarily by Enchanting (with herbs)
            - make healing potions (from herbs gathered)

            All of this is for free from stuff I collect while leveling/questing except for permantly enchanting (costs minor sums) or buying some spices for food or empty vials for potions.

                        How many new skill points do you get for each level earned
                        You don't get skill points based on level, but rather based on exp you earn.

                        For every 300 points of exp you earn through mobs (base exp, not counting bonus exp for being rested or quest exp) you get one skill point.

                        At level 13 I'd guess I have about between 50-75 points.. not sure about my actual total. But I know out of hand that I needed about 20k exp for level 12, 21k for level 13 and now it's about 22-23k for level 14 (but that includes quest and bonus exp).

                        You get skill points faster the higher you level
