If your drawing aggro like that on a pull you need to adjust your tactics. And more than likely your warrior needs to adjust his.

Before tank pulls, shield him. And if theres more than 2 mobs, Renew him as well. Once the shield and/or renew is gone, pop fade FIRST then heal.

The key is to let the tank build up a little hate before you do anything on every pull. The sooner you learn this technique the better. The top end instance pulls are very unforgiving when aggro goes awry on the initial pull. IE, splatted priest.
