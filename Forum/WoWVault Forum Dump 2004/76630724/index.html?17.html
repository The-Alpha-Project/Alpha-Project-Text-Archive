We have a Board for this very purpose.

I'd like to invite people to check out my new site for a casual World of WarCraft guild called "The Honorable Few". It is an Alliance based PvE server guild. As people register I'll be clearing them for the member's sections of the board, as only being registered you will only have access to the open community boards. Do feel free to read the guild charter and tell us something about yourself in the Petition for Membership forum.

As the Charter states, we are a casual guild. We don't want to put any pressure to do anything you don't want to, we only have a few rules currently, and they are more of a "Code of Conduct" than rules even. Our main concern is that people be friendly, honest and mature. Not just to other members, but to other players on the server as well.

We are considering a Horde based faction as well but right now we'll still need to discuss it. If we do make a Horde faction, it will be on a different PvE server and we will add forums just for it.

While the guild is casual in nature, we are also thinking of the "end-game" material. As a regular member you are encouraged to group with you guild-mates and attend evens, but you are not forced to. Their will be a "Senior" guild section as well though. This Senior section will be for members level 45+ who want more from the guild and willing to commit more. It will have it's own Senior Officers, and Senior members. The difference is the greater commitment to working together to do things the guild wouldn't otherwise be able to do. I'd also like to point out that this part of the guild is completely VOLUNTARY. While you must be level 45 or higher to join this part of the guild, you need not join if you don't want to. You could progress to the level cap without ever having to enter this part of the guild. If you do become a senior member though, more will be expected of you.

Please allow 24 hours before being put to full member status(it shouldn't take that long, but I do work so will be away a good part of the day). In becoming a member, you agree to the terms in the guild charter. Please DO ask for membership in the "Petition for Membership" forums so I know who would like to become a member as opposed to people who just wanted to look at the site. The site is still being worked on, so do expect to see more from it soon. There are currently 5 different areas with over 20 boards between the areas.

Thanks in advance.

http://www.thf.r8.org/

Btw, we are planning on using the US East servers. While we will be Alliance based, we are considering starting a Horde based faction on the US West servers.
