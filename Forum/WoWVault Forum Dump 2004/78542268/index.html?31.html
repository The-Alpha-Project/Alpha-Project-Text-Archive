ball mouse needs a surface that the ball can grip, and this is often not possible on a smooth desk

optical mouse needs a surface that is not too shiny as it will interfere with the reflection of the laser

a poor surface can really mess up the performance of the mouse, so a mousepad is usually pretty important unless you have a perfect desk for computers
