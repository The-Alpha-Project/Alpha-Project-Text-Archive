Currently in the beta there is very little you will miss out on. On the PvE servers, you are not automatically flagged for PvP by entering a region UNLESS you enter an enemy city or a small PvP designated area (not regions). For example, if you are Alliance and you go into Orgrimmar, you will be PvP flagged. There is really no reason to go into those cities other than to PvP.

Here is how you can be PvP flagged on the regular servers:
1. Attack an enemy NPC.
2. Aid a PvP flagged ally.
3. Enter an enemy city.
4. Use the /pvp flag.
5. Enter a PvP designated area like the Arena in Stranglethorn.

The rest of the stuff that people are talking about has not been put into the game yet, so it's pretty hard to know exactly what it will be like.
