My main reason for playing WoW is because of the balance of the classes. There is no one UBER class to play that shines above all others. Any class can kill any class. It all comes down to skills unlike other games that are out.

For example: DAoC's Infiltrator class had Dragonfang (9 second stun style used after evading an attack) which was overpowered for YEARS and it was the "I WIN" button in 99.999999999% of the fights that they had.

Everyday I read the boards about ALL of the WoW classes. I find myself changing my mind at least 4-5 times a day because ALL of the classes look good and the balance is very good amongst them. I have a few that I liked and enjoyed during the Stress Test also.

Just wanted to say to Blizzard 'THANKS' for doing an AWESOME job on the classes in the game (even though some tweaking and upgrading is still to come). No such thing as an "I WIN" button in the game. Comes down to skill when doing PvP in this game. Oh, no buffbots to really worry about either and the crafting is superb.

Can't Wait,
