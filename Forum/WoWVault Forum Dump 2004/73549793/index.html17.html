Welcome back....although I recommend finding another MMO to tide you over until around Nov-Dec, when WoW *might* be released.

There are a number of games to choose from.

For MMO's, I recommend AC2 (Asherons Call 2).

Its gotten much better than it was at release, close to 2 years ago. Give the free trial a shot from Fileplanet. No CC# info needed, just download and start playing!

Aside from that, perhaps AO (Anarchy Online) if you like Sci-Fi. Tons of content and loot, but It has a steep learning curve, so beware.

Heck, you can give SWG (Star Wars Galaxies) a try, but you will notice that it has little content. I tried the free trial when it became availiable, and while much has been added since beta, it remains the same game....you kinda create your own content and fun.

Whatever you choose, welcome back to the WoW boards! (As well as the wait for it to be released!)
