I'd say preist. Preist has option of healing/nuking- not as good a nuker as a mage. But not too shabby either:

Mind Blast (Rank 9) (level 58)
350 Mana Instant cast 30 yard range
8 second cooldown
503 to 531 shadow damage, high amount of threat.

Shadow Word: Pain (Rank 8) (level 58)
470 Mana Instant cast 30 yard range
852 damage over 18 seconds.

Holy Smite (Rank 8) (level 54)
315 Mana 2 second cast 30 yard range
Smite an enemy for 397 to 443 holy damage.

Fireball (Rank 11)
450 Mana 4 second cast 40 yard range
641 to 817 fire damage + 80 damage over 8 seconds.

Frostbolt (Rank 10)
300 Mana 3.5 second cast 30 yard range
Efficiency: 1.64 damage per mana
494 to 534 frost damage and slow move speed 10 sec.

Fire Blast (Rank 7)
360 Mana Instant cast 20 yard range
8 second cooldown
473 to 559 fire damage.

Obviously the Mage has plenty of AoE options as well. But the priest can do plenty of damage to a singel target in a hurry if/when they need to. The mage has the advantage of summoning food and water, which reduces downtime. You also might want to look at paladin if you are going alliance- they get the ability to have nice mana regen (wisdom aura) in addition to healing/damage output/buffing.
