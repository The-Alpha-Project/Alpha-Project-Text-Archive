Date Posted: 11/9 9:15pm Subject: RE: People DO get some exp for killing your mobs?
I think they have some code in place that basically keeps people from powerleveling younger players - take your situation for example, think of it this way:

The level 10 guy is a friend of yours. The way the system should work is that if you get the first hit on a mob, no matter who else touches it afterwards - you get the xp. It's a great system that eliminates the problem of kill stealing (whoever gets it first gets it, period, no such thing as kill stealing, though there are circumstances where people would argue otherwise)

That system could easily be abused by powerlevelers - they simply let you get the first hit on a mob, then they finish them off in 1-2 hits, and you get super-fast xp with little risk to yourself.

Noticed this a few nights back when I was trying to help a lower level friend xp - she got about the same xp in a group with me (at 6 levels lower) than she did "starting" the kill then having me help.
