Every game since the Eq/AC1 Era was supposidly the end all be all MMO game(DaoC, AC2, SWG, Horizons, etc.).

Problem is, people have different tastes and it is difficult for 1 game to fulfill so many *different* peoples vision of an end all be all MMO.

The other problem is that almost all of these post AC/EQ games have been released far too early, and have been a bug filled mess.

To simplify what I am *personally* looking for, I thought about what I disliked from previous MMO releases and made a little list.

Here is what I am looking for:

1) A bugless launch (i.e. A Polished game); no crashes.

2) A content filled game giving me options from the moment I log in as to what I wish to do with the time I have.

3) Plenty of quests for all levels (Im sick of camping).

4) NO LAG....server side or client side. (as to the client side, if the Recommended Specs are on the box, then the recommended specs should play the game without incident)

5) No Forced Grouping. I want to be able to *play* during the time I have (...FFXI...)

6) A thriving economy.



If the above can be done from day 1 of release, then I have found a game I can call home for years to come.

I must hand it to Blizzard though. With no NDA, there shouldnt be any surprises as to how stable the game is, or what to expect. All the others...well, you had to buy to find out.
