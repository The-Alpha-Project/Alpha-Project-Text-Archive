Date Posted: 9/21 8:36am Subject: RE: Are the beta testers doing their job?
There are technical beta testers and there are gameplay beta testers. Some focus on mispelled words, graphic errata, sever/client sync., memory leaks etc. and others focus on continuity, function, effectiveness...

Some balance issues you can't see until you throw a large number of players who are trying to maximize their effectiveness at all the classes. When you see a large number of players gravitating to one class or using a combination of abilities as an "I win" feature, the areas which need balance become much more apparent. Beta testers that do nothing but grind out a single class contribute the most to seeing these types of issues. They know that class inside and out, often reducing the DPS and abilities to spreadsheet equations.

The Mage, Warrior, and Rogue nerfs were all telegraphed far in advance by the the numbers of tester/players flocking to the class or all using specific ability stratagies. Anyone who has any concept of balance was not surprised by the reductions. Warriors Charge was a bugs bunny speed boost that not only negates the traditional melee class weakness to ranged attacks but practically became a ranged attack itself putting the warrior into their most favorable combat situation.
