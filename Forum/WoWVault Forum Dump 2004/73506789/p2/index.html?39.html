I think it's cool. It's nice that blizzard is putting so much love into these classes. I think it's awesome that each class seems to be able to do so much, because it keeps the bore factor out of the game. The Hunter here, compared to the hunter in DAoC is black and white. DAoC classes are come down to being a small, select amount of things you can do.

DAoC Wizard: you're either fire, ice, or earth. With any choice, all you do is cast up to maybe 3 spells, and thats it. Such as fire spec, 2 bolts, and a combustion dd if necessary, mob is dead. Quickly becomes boring.

WoW Mage: 3 different damage lines in comparison, being Arcane, Frost, and Fire. Oh but then you can teleport yourself, you have CC with Polymorph, you can conjure your own food and drink, and mana crystals, plus much more. Obviously not a 3 spell care-bear like the Wizard is.

DAoC Warrior: Wears the best armor in the realm, and can use most weapon types. You spec in 2 handed or 1 handed and a shield, with maybe parry mixed in. So you have 2 good choices and attack styles will come with each choice of weapon. Be it 2 hand or 1 hand. Bottom line to this class is use your attack styles till mob is dead. No spells or frills, just muscle.

WoW Warrior: Also wears the best armors, and uses a wide array of weapons. Same idea as above with the appropriate muscle, mixed with some interesting spell types. Shouts causing fear and panic. Battle howls leaving people stunned and unable to attack for a small amount of time. Warriors should be fearful, muscle bound classes that people respect, not cut down because they can't do anything. WoW seems to provide them this.


This is a very basic comparison of course. I love the fact that classes get to do so much, with so many different choices at any given time. Mages should be very skilled and dangerous, as well as feared. So should the mighty Warrior. These kinds of people in stories and fantasy are just that. Feared and respected because of what they can do. I for one am done with the extremely simple care-bear classes that basically take no skill to play beyond a certain point. Just my rants. I say keep the Hunter and all the other WoW classes this good and keep making them better.
