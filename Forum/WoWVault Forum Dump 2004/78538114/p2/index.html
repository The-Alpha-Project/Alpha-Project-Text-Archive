~ There is talking, but like DAoC, it will occur in guilds, and in special chat groups set up for specific things, or for defense, or raids, or whatever. You have to realize that many people, because it is beta, are not concerned about forming guilds, other than the people that already had a guild formed from other games. Most people do not belong to a gaming guild, and will as such be basically on their own.

Once retail comes, and guilds become more important, you will see them spring up, and it is within the guilds, that you will find your communication and talking.

'Im still working on this because I know sometimes the lower levels can be a hassle. But it doesnt seem like leveling in this game is what it should be. Playing Asheron's Call before this allowed me a degree of freedom in my character that I'll never see again. Even Asheron's Call 2 had a tree. It just seems buying skills and negating xp seems wierd to me still.'
~ That's because AC1 has a fairly poor levelling system. It is fundamentally different from WoW, in that skills are the most important thing there, and level is largely irrelevent. You will find the same discomfort with many of the newest games, because they are all more 'level based' rather than 'skill based'. You'll get used to it, you are just uncomfortable with it now because it is brand new and very different from the system you are currently used to.

'3. This one doesnt really count, but I dont see a story in the game yet. I know it's beta and retail will probably have a storyline, but the reason I left Asheron's Call was because there was no story line, and like I mentioned in my title, Im not insulting the game, these are just concerns.'
~ Warcraft 1-3 forms the requisite backstory. I have no knowledge of what post-release plans are, but I'm pretty sure there will be some sort of events, to further the story of the world. You have to realize, too, that if you are lower level, the story will not be huge and grandiose to start. As you level and progress, I'm sure that the inherent story in the warcraft world will come out.

Again, you can always go back to those other games to get the backstory, if you want it. This is something too, that someone like yourself, coming from AC1, will find different. AC1 is practically unique in that they have a new story each month. Almost every other game doesn't have anything like this.
