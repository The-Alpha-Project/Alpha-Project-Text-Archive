A couple things...

First, this problem is really easy to solve. Know the groups you're in. If you're grouping with people you know, then you can argue it another time. If you're in a group with newcomers...make clear your stance early. Like, before you head out. Say, "Just in case it comes up, as a Druid I use gear other than just INT/SPI. If a piece comes up that I really believe I can use, then I'm going to loot on it." If they're okay, then great. If they have a problem, then bid your farewells right then and there.
