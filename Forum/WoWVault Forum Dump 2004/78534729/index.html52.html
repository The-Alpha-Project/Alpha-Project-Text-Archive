Not having Thottbot would be a huge hit against one of Cosmos' best features.

You NEED nothing from a UI mod. What you desire in a UI mod or not, determines whether it's this so called "bloatware". If you use 90% of the features available in it, then there's not much you don't want in it. If you only use 10%, then *shrugs* none of the features really affect performance, especially when disabled.

Nurfed has no Thottbot, nor does it appear to have a secondary bar, just side bar(s?).

Of course, UIs are very much a personal thing, since it's one of the biggest things you have to put up with when playing a game =-p


[Edit] JJDrakken you seem to be MASSIVLY confused on what a UI modification is. They can do NOTHING that Blizzard doesn't allow. This is no FFACT or Decal.

To fix your party window, uninstall Cosmos. The Cosmos channel is used to communicate things like say... sending a user a page, or a map marker. the Z<name> channel is used to communicate with your party, like when you complete a quest, so it can show it to other cosmos users.

Uninstalling Cosmos will remove the interface directory, if that doesn't finish it, clear out your WDB and WTF folders. They will be recreated.
