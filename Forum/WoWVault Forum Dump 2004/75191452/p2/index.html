Date Posted: 9/9/04 6:28am Subject: RE: Europeans cannot play on American servers..
Two possible reasons for this that I can see other than those already covered:

1) Downtime for patching. Not sure how they will handle it here, but in AC there were periods once a month where the server was down. In the U.S., it was mid-morning, but for the Euro people, it was afternoon and into prime time. That caused some hard feelings from the European people who had at least one night a month where they couldn't play.

2) New content. Associated with the patching, but it was actually somewhat the opposite of number 1 above. The Euro people who waited out the down time got first access to new content. Frequently, things like new housing made available with the patch was long gone (taken by European players) before the American people logged on. Also, one time quests were already completed before prime time playing in America.

Given these two types of issues, it makes some sense to seperate servers geographically so that you don't tick off part of you player base.

That being said, I always enjoyed running into players from a different country. Half of the players in our monarchy in AC were from Germany. I hope to do the same in WoW.
