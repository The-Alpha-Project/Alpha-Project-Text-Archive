Date Posted: 11/10 7:07pm Subject: RE: Think this piece of junk can run WoW?
on my analysis... on a computer of that caliber the distinction between good AGP video card, and a PCI card, would be between "little" and "negligible" since you lift the bar on the video... you'll hit the low bars of a cheap motherboard and celeron processor, then the memory... which order it comes in being arguable and all...

but on a higher end computer, where the rest of the system is good, the difference could be signifigant.

but something like this isn't likely to even poke at thinking about using the 8x... I don't think, though its almost certainly enough, if the rest of the system wouldn't get in the way... to make a signifigant differnce between the bandwitdh and speed on an AGP, over a PCI.

those sorta things are all about balancing it... any one part too high above the system's overall bar, it won't be utilized, if its signifigantly below, it'll pull the preformance down.
