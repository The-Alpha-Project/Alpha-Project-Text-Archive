I'm all for faster levelling than in the typical MMORPG (eg Daoc). In Daoc, you really had to either play for a few months or be a semi-powergamer to have a level 50 character. There was no way you would have more than one 50 being casual and no way you could have more than one 50 with decent items/toa/ra's etc.

What I liked about Diablo 2 was that everyone had the chance to play a character to high levels without having to dump an absolute ****load of time into the game. You could also try out multiple builds of each character and half the fun was doing this.

When you buy a game, ideally you want to be able to try out most of the content. I think that WoW having possibly easier levelling than other games can only be an advantage. Only the uber-hardcore gamers will lose out with this one as they'll run out of characters to try out but the general gamer will have a much better time.

This will work well. It will be far far more appealing to a wider range of people.
