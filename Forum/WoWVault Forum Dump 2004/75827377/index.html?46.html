It's a beta.

Nerf is a stupid word - typically used to describe balance changes made to FIX something by those who were used to exploiting or otherwise enjoying an overpowered and unbalanced aspect of game mechanics.

Get over it. All games, and particularly MMORPGs are subject to ongoing balancing due to the fact that too many players are scumbags looking to exploit and gain any advantage they can to make easier games even easier. No matter how great of a dev you are or how much you test someone will find a way to cheat or abuse a system.

It's a beta - and as such balance issues are more experimental than they will almost certainly be in retail - where experimenting (as the crackheads at Mythic seem to do with every patch) is less acceptible. The problem is too many beta "testers" forget they are testing a game in development and lose their minds and whine like little babies.

It's similar in computer security. You can't ever say you're totally secure - you just say you're secure until someone finds a way in - then you adjust accordingly. Game balance is done the same way - you do your best to balance, and when something is proven to be unbalanced you fix it. It's only a 'nerf' to the pinhead whiners who have their exploiting removed.
