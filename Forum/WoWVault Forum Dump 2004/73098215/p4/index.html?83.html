I've searched the entire official EQ2 forums and have seen no comments by anyone named Moorwolf. So I am assuming you meant Moorgard. So I've checked for all of Moorgard's statements regarding soloing and I've found statements like:

http://eqiiforums.station.sony.com/eq2/board/message?board.id=faq&message.id=2&highlight=solo#M2
"Will there be a particular class designed specifically for players who want to solo?

We are not designing any specific classes to be more efficient at soloing than others. We intend to provide lots of solo content for all classes in EQII.
"

or

http://eqiiforums.station.sony.com/eq2/board/message?board.id=general&message.id=110101&highlight=solo#M110101
"You can solo to your heart's content.

Now assuming this is going to be true for EQ2...isn't this what WoW has going for it? I was under the impression that in WoW you can solo an awful lot but there were somethings that required groups. <-Not in beta so I have no personal experience here.
