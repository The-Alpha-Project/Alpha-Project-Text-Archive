You aren't going to see a whole lot of grouping until around level 20 or so. Most of the lower level quests and monsters can be handled just fine solo...but around 20 things get more challenging. Once you start hunting around Redridge, the Stockades, and Duskwood you'll find yourself grouping quite a bit. No, it isn't as necessary as grouping in DAoC or EQ, you can still function fairly well on your own... But the quests you get are almost all going to be tough enough for you to need a group.
