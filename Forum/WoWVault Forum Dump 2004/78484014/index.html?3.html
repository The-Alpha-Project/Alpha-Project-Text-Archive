so far I can solo effectively as well with my Warlock as I did with my original hunter. I got my hunter to level 24 and so far my warlock is 16.

Both the Blue Tank and pet tanks work about the same, both have an agro drawing ability, both do just enough damage that most times I don't get agro and if I do, they draw it back. Both have ranged attacks, CC attacks, and can do melee.

Advantages of Warlock as I have seen:

Warlock at 16 can sacrifice their pet for a massive damage shield that will not allow spell interrupts, great for your pets gonna die but you wanna live.

Don't have to feed your pet and just have to drain a few souls to make sure you can bring him back if he dies.

Debuffs. Enough said

Advantages of Hunter:

Great variety of pets, pet abilities and a better chance to melee when they close in.

There is probably more, but it seems that the Warlock and the Hunter are pretty compairable. I can tank 4+ levels higher than my warlock and both me and my pet survives, though elites chew him up and spit him out. I was also able to do that with the Hunter. On some mobs I can take out 2-3 at a time, Ol' Blue can handle 2 if I fear the 3rd. My Hunter could also solo 2-3 at times.
