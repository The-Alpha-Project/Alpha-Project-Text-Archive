They have canceled or delayed games simply because they weren't fun enough. Warcraft 3 came out 2 years after it's original release date. Why? Because, it wasn't fun enough and it wasn't of a high enough quality to meet Blizzards standards. Did the delay hurt them in any way? Nope, because people knew that Blizzard had good reasons for it and in the end, they released a great game.

Secondly, they wouldn't release it or bring it into Beta if it didn't meet certain Blizzards standards of quality and playability. Third, Blizzard's game's in Beta tend to be more complete and feature rich than most released products (All reviews so far lead us to believe that this will also be the case with WoW). Because of this, Blizzard spends most of the time in Beta actually Beta testing their games. They don't just test for stability, they test for balance, playability and fun. If any of those factors aren't up to par, they fix them.

Never mind the fact that it's an MMORPG. Never mind the fact that it's their first MMORPG. Instead, look at their past performance. Their past performance and dedication to releasing quality products gives Blizzard a lot of credibility....
