I look at it this way, im sick and tired of the come along lately mmorpgs there throwing at us now.

It's real simple, Give me phantasy world thats immersive. Real time combat where my movements and actions dicate the outcome of the battle. And charcaters that can be specced to be perform certain roles and then built upon. No more of these I can do everything characters.
