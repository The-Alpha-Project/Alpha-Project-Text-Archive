There are some facts people having been getting wrong in this thread here. People are saying the quest system in EQ2 is better than WoWs. "You get EQ2s quests from different sources, you get push and pull quests, and you quests from drops. So far in WoW the only source of a quest is an NPC with a huge question mark over their head."

Keep in mind that the same person who said that also said they only had a level 11 character in WoW. Which would mean he's still in the first or second town you start playing in. I have played WoW and you do get quests from dropped items you had no idea to look for and push and pull quests. The question mark is just to make it easier to find quests and complete them in town.

People say that WoW is anti-grouping which contradicts the point of an MMORPG. I was speeding through many quests when I had atleast one of my friends joining me. Some of the monsters in the game are equal to your strength or more likely 80%-70% of your character and it's difficult to fight them, but it's easy if you have atleast one person helping you. Epecially if you acciedently run into two of these creatures and you'll die if you solo, but not if you have someone on the same quest or a friend helping you out. Dungeons too are where you are forced to group because in dungeons where all the best loot is the monsters are a higher level then you and you are forced to have a group of people. Fortunately it's not hard to find people to group for these quests as usually there are a couple other people on the same quest also.

I don't know about EQ2, but the talent system is a major bonus in the WoW game. Depending on whether you plan to be a two-handed(arms talents), duel-wielding(fury talents), or shield using(protection talents) warrior. You need to set your talents and abilities accordingly. Some spells a warrior can use are completely useless for two-handed, but are perfect for a duel-wielder and vice-versa. Making it seem like there are different kinds of warrior classes in the game. If you misuse your talents and not become a specific type of character of your class you will end up having a weak character.
