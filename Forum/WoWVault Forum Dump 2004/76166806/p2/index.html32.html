Well it depends on your play style really. My own opinion is that all three specs are great with lots of pros/cons and depend on the player.

My mage in closed beta is full Frost with 11 talent points spent in Arcane for Evocation. I could solo even level mobs (and others that were 1-3 levels above mine depending on what they were) non stop and just use Evocation once the mana was low and its timer was up (was 6 minute and now is 10 minutes). It was all about mana management and trial and error. In instanced dungeons where the mobs are much tougher I could cast quite a while without having to slow the group down waiting on my mana.

While fire without a doubt will take things down quicker, you will be stopping more often to drink/med. Especially at the intermediary levels of say 25-50. If you really need to take the mob down quick (especially boss mobs) and they aren't immune or have very high resist rates then definitely fire.

My preference for soloing was frost but again that is MY preference. I would encourage you to play around a little or do some more research and see which spec appeals to you. Even with the new cost of respeccing talents (1g, 5g, etc) that really isnt that much money for you to determine which spec suits you.

Good luck!
