If a mob gets stuck trying to get back to its spot, it'll be on continual evade... not just "improved" evasion, but complete immunity from direct attacks. It's really a drag when it happens to a quest monster, too. I used to see things spawn inside trees and such which caused this effect. You CAN skill up your weapon skills attacking it, though

One interesting thing is that they can be affected by indirect targetted aoe's, although they will still be on super-regenerate. So, you have to do a TON of damage to kill them (and they won't be tagged, so you can't loot, but at least they'll respawn). Flamestrike's DOT and rain of fire will affect them, but hellfire will not (since it's not targetted). Dynamite will also affect them.
