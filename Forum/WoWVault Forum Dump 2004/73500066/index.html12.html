The minimum number of characters is 3 and there are no numbers, symbols or "punctuation" allowed (i.e. no spaces, hyphens or apostrophes), as I recall. I think the max number of characters is 10.

"Last names" were being considered for after release, I believe. I don't know if this essentially means you'll be allowed to use spaces, or what.

More info on naming policy here: http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/policy/naming.shtml
