The benefits of a guild, whether large or small are beneficial. I'm in a guild of about 20 or so friends from DAoC and we are all core PvP players. Being guilded has many advantages; such as the crafters of all types, the comraderie amongst each other, we all use Team Speak to communicate, the help is there when/if you need it, grouping, and the fact that you group with pretty much the same people day in and day out which makes you an effective TEAM, rather then a solo person joining random pick up groups each day.

It's also a nice way to provide feedback on how they have their guild commands set up and to give ideas if you have any, for when the game goes live to ease the time and play of others that weren't able to get into beta.
