Didn't see this mentioned anywhere and if it is, just ignore it. This is going to drastically change the way food/drink currently works in game.

Quote:

I think they are too easy to kill! Here are some solutions incoming...

- Elite monsters will be getting "buffed" from about level 37 onwards. We found that players power levels are increasing faster than elite monsters. In particular you should notice our next dungeon (Blackrock Depths) to be much more challenging for level 48-54 level players.

- It should not be possible to efficiently solo elite monsters. Some classes can do this and we will be looking into this on a case by case basis. Note this does not mean elites should be impossible to solo, just experience inefficient.

- Drinking and Eating in combat is being all but eliminated. You will still be able to eat and drink outside combat to reduce downtime. Note that "in combat" means that a monster nearby is engaged with you, so once you have started a fight with a monster, no more eating/drinking until the monster is dead or is disengaged. This will, of course, make dungeons much more challenging than they currently are.

-Eno

End Quote:
