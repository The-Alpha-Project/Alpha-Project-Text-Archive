There are PLUSES and MINUSES to everything you do in this life. Online gaming is a fun, cheap and easy hobby to have. I've played MMOs for over 5 years now and I can honestly say that it hasn't impacted my real life at all. I didn't meet my wife on an online game but she watches me and is interested in playing now that she had a chance to fish in WoW at Booty Bay area. She loved it.

When I was single I went out on the weekends and blew over $100.00 a weekend easy eating, doing shots and drinking. Not to mention the money I spent bar hopping all over the place. It got expensive as heck and the people that were my so-called friends (drinking buddies rather) only wanted to party and stuff. It got old and EXPENSIVE!!!

My wife asked me why I don't have any guy friends that come around to hang out and just go do stuff with and I just replied to her, "Just watch and see and you'll see why I don't have buddies like that."

So after 1 year she came to me and said, "Honey, I finally understand why you don't have any RL friends but many associates. Their problems become yours and you are somewhat obligated to spend time with them, money on them and invest your feelings and emotions amongst a whole bunch of other stuff. Just not worth it because not many people remain your life-long friends anyway."

By the way- I have a 2 yr old at home that sits on my lap when I play and she's good at picking stuff out and counting numbers and stuff. She likes the graphics, the sounds and the environments. Very beneficial to her. I don't mind it.

To each his/her own. I have no regrets playing MMOs at all. VERY cheap hobby and I don't have to go out in the cold just to have some fun. I have my treadmill and other exercise equipment in the house so I can just watch TV or craft stuff while I run with my cordless keyboard.

MMOs will be around for a long time to come. Just think of all the people that want to socialize and have the friends and not have to spend hundreds of dollars to throw a party and have people over to trash your house. Besides, in WoW you can hit the Tavern(s) and get drunk all night long and don't have to worry about drunk drivers (whether it's you that is drunk or another person).

Just a fun and cheap way to really entertain yourself just like the millions of kids that are playing games. Besides, I REFUSE to grow up anyway and I'm NOT a Toys R' Us kid either!!!
