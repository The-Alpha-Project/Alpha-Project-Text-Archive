I feel old just because I can't handle sleep deprevation as well as I used to.

Sometimes the zones do seem a bit big and your movement a bit slow - but I think it's mostly because the world is freakin' huge.

I think waiting for mounts at 40/60 gives you more incentive to level up (as if you needed more).

It does help to be organized about what you do if this bothers you - but it's not always easy. Some days I run forever to do quests, then sometimes I can do a bunch of quests in one area with minimal travel. Variety is good.

Flight paths are your friend - it's worth establishing them to minimize some of your running. I overlook this a lot. I dunno how many times I ran to Barrens from Orgrimmar before I found out there was a flightpath to Crossroads that turns a 5-10 minute run into a 1 minute flight. DOH!

It would be nice if there were runspeed boost potions - say maybe a 50% movement speed bonus that breaks on combat and that can't be used while in combat. I'd do it as a potion to give alchemists something worth selling - it'd be one hell of a hot commodity, let me tell ya! Maybe make it available in increments of 30/40/50% so that there are different levels of it like other potions.
