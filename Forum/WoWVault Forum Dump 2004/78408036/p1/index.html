Date Posted: 11/8 7:26am Subject: RE: harder == more fun?
It's not really that simple I'm afraid. The general theory is that harder content increases the feeling of accomplishment. If it was easy to do, you don't feel any significant sense of accomplishment. If it was near impossible to do you get a large sense of accomplishment. The goal is to find a good balance.

A harsh death penalty is part of that. When death is meaningless, ala AC2, staying alive means very little. When the death penalty is very harsh, ala EQ1, staying alive means much more. Again, the goal is a good balance.

I'm not saying they hit the balance here, just offering an opinion on why you might want a harsh death penalty/harder content.
