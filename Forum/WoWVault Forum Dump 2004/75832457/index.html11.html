I noticed that you read at least 1 talent wrong, and of course you were looking at 1 point talents.

There is a talent which increases crit chance by up to 5%, and then a follow up talent which give you a 100% chance to get an extra combo point when you crit. The "final" talent in feral gives a 100% chance to get an extra combo point when you ambush someone. If you managed to get crits on both your pounce and your claw (agility potions are you friend), that would mean you could land a 5 point Rip as your targte was becoming unstunned.

If you see caster form as your primary, this isn't a great tree. If you see caster form as something you will enter momentarily when needed, this is a fantastic line.
