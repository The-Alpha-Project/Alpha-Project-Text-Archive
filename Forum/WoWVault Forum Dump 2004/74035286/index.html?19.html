Holy Mastery

Improved Holy Smite: Rank 0/3
Increases the critical strike damage of your holy smite ability by 40%

Holy Specialization: Rank 0/5
Increases the critical effect chance of your Holy spells by 1%

Subtlety: Rank 0/5
Reduces the threat generated by your healing spells by 2%

Spiritual Healing: Rank 0/5
Increases your mana regeneration rate by 100% for 2 seconds after getting a
critical effect from one of yoru heal spells
* Requires HM 5
* Requires 5 points in Holy Specialization

Inspiration: Rank 0/5
Increases your target's armor value from items by 5% for 15 sec after getting a critical effect while healing them.
* Requires HM 5

Improved Resurrection: Rank 0/2
Reduces the resurrection sickness penalty of your Resurrection spell by 10%
* Requires HM 10

Improved Renew: Rank 0/2
Increases the amount healed by yoru Renew spell by 10%
* Requires HM 10

Holy Fire: Rank 0/1
New Spell: Consumes the enemy in flames that cause 74 to 96 fire damage and an additional 20 fire damage over 8 seconds
1 min cooldown, 95 mana, 5 sec cast, 30 yd range
* Requires 3 mpoints in improved Holy Smite
* Requires HM 15

Improved Healing: Rank 0/5
Increses the critical effect chance of your Lesser Heal, Heal
and Greater Heal spells by 1%
* Requires HM 15

Combat Resurrection: Rank 0/1
New Spell: Brings a dead player back to life with 50% of their health and mana. The target does not suffer from resurrection sickness.
45 min cooldown, 150 mana, 2 sec cast, 40 yd range
* Requires HM 20
* Requires 2 points in improved resurrection

Improved Flash Heal: Rank 0/2
Gives you a 35% chance to avoid interruption caused by damage while casting flash heal.
* Requires HM 20
* Requires 1 point in Inspiration

Master Healer: Rank 0/5
Increases the effectiveness of your healing spells by 2%
* Requires HM 25
* Requires 5 points in improved healing

Holy Nova: Rank 0/1
New Spell: Causes an explosion of holy light around the caster, causing 51 to 57 holy damage to all targets within 10 yeards. The effect also temporarily reduces your threat level against nearby targets for 5 seconds.
* Requires HM 30

Improved Shadow Proteection: Rank 0/2
Increases the amount of shadow resistance granted by 15%
* Requires HM 30

Improved Prayer of Healing: Rank 0/2
Reduces the mana cost of your prayer of healign spell by 10%.
* Requires HM 30



Shadow Mastery

Spirit Tap: Rank 0/5
Gives you a 20% chance to gain a 100% bonus to your Spirit for 15 seconds after killing a target.

Blackout: Rank 0/5
Gives your shadow damage and psychic scream spells a 1% chance to stun the target for 3 seconds.

Shadow Affinity: Rank 0/5
Require 5 points in shadow mastery.
Reduces the threat generated by your shadow spells by 5%
* Requires SM 5

Improved Shadow Word Pain: Rank 0/2
Increases the duration of your shadow word pain spell by 3 seconds.
* Requires SM 5

Shadow Focus: Rank 0/5
Reduces your target's chance to resist your shadow spells by 2%
* Requires SM 5

Improved Fade: Rank 0/2
Increases the duration of your fade ability by 5 seconds.
* Require 5 points in Shadow Affinity
* Requires SM 10

Improved Mind Blast: Rank 0/5
Reduces the collown of your mind blast spell by 0.5sec
* Requires SM 10

Shadow Weaving: Rank 0/5
Your shadow damage spells have a 20% chance of causing your target to become vulnerable to shadow damage. This vulnerability increases the dealt to your target by 4% and lasts 10 seconds. Stacks up to 5 times.
* Requires SM 15

Mind Flay: Rank 0/1
New Spell - Assault the target's mind with Shadow energy, causing 180 damage over 3 sec and slowing the target to 50% of their movement speed.
60 mana, instant cast
* Requires 5 points in Shadow Focus
* Requires SM 15

Improved Psychic Scream: Rank 0/3
Gives your psychic scream spell a 15% chance to cause the target to cower in fear rather than running.
* Requires SM 15

Darkness: Rank 0/5
Increases your shadow spell damage by 2%.
* Requires 5 points in Shadow Weaving
* Requires 20 points in Shadow Mastery

Vampiric Embrace: Rank 0/1
New Spell - Afflicts your target with shadow energy healing all party members for 20% of any shadow damage you deal for 1 minute.
* Requires 5 points in Darkness
* Requires SM 25

Silence: Rank 0/1
New Spell - Silences the target, preventing them from casting spells for 6 seconds.
45 sec cooldown, 225 mana, instant cast, 20 yard range
* Requires 3 points in improved psychic scream
* Requires SM 25

Shadowform: Rank 0/1
New spell - Assume a shadowform, increasing your shadow damage by 20% and reducing physical damage done to you by 20%. However, you may only cast shadow and dicipline spells while in this form.
1.5 sec cooldown, instant cast, 71 mana
* Requires SM 30



Dicipline Mastery

Unbreakable Will: Rank 0/5
Increases your chance to resist stun, fear and silence effects by 3%

Silent Resolve: Rank 0/5
Reduces the threat generated by your damage spells by 4%

Improved Holy Word Shield: Rank 0/3
Reduces the duration of your Holy Word Shield's weakened soul effect by 5 seconds.
* Requires DM 5

Improved Holy Word Fortitude: Rank 0/2
Increases the effect of your holy word fortitude spell by 15%
* Requires DM 5

Wand Specialization: Rank 0/5
Increases your damage with wands by 5%
* Requires DM 5

Martyrdom: Rank 0/2
Gives you a 50% chance to gain a focussed casting buff after being the victim of a critical strike
* Requires DM 10

Mental Agility: Rank 0/5
Reduces the mana cost of your instant cast spells by 2%
* Requires DM 10

Inner Focus: Rank 0/1
When activated, reduces the mana cost of your next spell by 100% and increases it's critical effect chance by 25% if iti s capable of a critical effect.
* Requires Mental Agility 5
* Requires DM 15

Meditation: Rank 0/5
Increases your mana regeneration rate by 5%
* Requires DM 15

Improved Inner Fire: Rank 0/3
Increases the effect of your inner fire spell by 15%
* Requires DM 15

Focussed Casting: Rank 0/1
New Spell - While active you no longer lose casting time from taking damage, lasts 8 seconds.
1 minute cooldown, instant cast, 30 mana
* Requires Martyrdom 2
* Requires DM 20

Improved Shackle Undead: Rank 0/2
Decreases the chance your target can resist your shackle undead spell by 5%.
* Requires DM 20

Improved Mana Burn: Rank 0/2
Reduces the casting time of your mana burn spell by 0.5 secs
* Requires DM 25

Divine Spirit: Rank 0/1
New Spell - Holy power infuses the target, increasing their spirit by 13 for 30 minutes
no cooldown, 30 yard range, 290 mana, instant cast

Shadow: wow! Okay, I think I will rock now. They really did listen to our suggestions and five things I specifically asked for I see already...
Discipline: <cries> Okay, stick lots of things that belong in the other lines here for lack of room in the other lines and then cram things I dont want on top of them? Bleh. Thats cool with me I suppost. I wish I had the talents formerly granted before level 10 to makes this my secondary tree)
