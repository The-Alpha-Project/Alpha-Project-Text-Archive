The idea for this week comes from Zerkz.

Feel free to PM me with your own ideas for top 10 lists.


Here is how the top ten list will work. Each week, a new topic will be posted on the vault site as well as on the WoW General Board. I will be accepting submissions via PM as well as posts made on this thread. Then I will choose the ten best (read: the most humorous) and they will be posted on the Vault site. There is no limit to the number of lines you can post. So, start posting and sending me your responses and remember to include your name/alias/board name/etc so that I can give you credit where credit is due!

Tip: Delivery is just as important as the idea itself, so even though you think someone said the same thing you did, their delivery might have just been a bit better

The top response (the response that is judged by me to hold the #1 spot) will get a choice of VIP for a week or a permanent title of his or her choice!
