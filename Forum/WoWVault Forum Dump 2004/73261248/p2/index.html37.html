Do Soul Shards vanish when you log?
Nope. But on that note, soulstones and healtstones do, just fyi.

Can you cast a spell once you have a higher level version of the spell? Specifically, can you cast the first rank Drain Soul for 55 mana, even at higher levels?

I imagine you could use the first rank drain soul if you wanted to. It is still in your spell book. The mana cost for getting shards isn't an issue though. Not too mention the higher level drain soul spells do more damage.

Assuming you has a rezzer in the group, why would everyone need Soulstones?

Several circumstances arise:
-Group wipe outs. Even with a rezzer in your group, sometimes it is in everyone's best interest to get back on their feet asap.
-Rezzer is oom and the battle still rages. There are select times when a person can pop their SS if they fell and help influence a bad pull for the better.
-All sorts of miscellaneous crazy junk can happen.
Its just good sound business to make sure everyone has one.

Is it tough in this game to figure out when the mob is going to die in the next 15 seconds?
Most of the time, no.

All in all when it comes down to the shards, you're right. It is a management issue. I'd say what most warlocks don't like the most about it is the backpack space they take up. Its annoying. But then again, I don't expect any love from the tanks either, with their ammo pouches (I understand that takes up an entire backpack spot). Its not a game breaker for the warlock, it just makes it, for the lack of a better word, "less fun". But all in all, its just us whining .

Do Curses seem to generate much aggro? Or can you safely cast these after the first taunt?
Well, sounds like you're talking while paired with a voidwalker or player tank. With a player tank that is doing his/her job, the curses aren't an agro problem. However, with the VW, even after the first couple of "torments", Curse of Weakness, in particular, can really torque a mobs nerves. Curse of Tongues also seems to be pretty high on the agro scale. The other curses aren't nearly as bad. I usually pull with Curse of Agony because of its really low agro (yeah, I know, I should pull with Corruption ). With the other two pets, just looking at the mob will agro it on you, ehehe.

Do DOTs seem to have an upfront big aggro spike? Or is it aggro over time, as the damage is applied? Even with all three dots applied, CoA, Corruption, and Immolate, you can usually get by without agroing the mob. The only downside is, if you wait for those dots to play out, your VW will more than likely be dead. Doing anything else (funnel, changing over to CoW, drain life, pretty much everything else but drain soul), will gain agro quick. Its like you are teetering on the agro line. Also, reapplying Immolate (it runs its course fairly quick) will gain agro sometimes, but it is also a dd.

My warlock is 37 just fyi.

Also Soulstones play a very critical part in instanced dungeons. Let's say you are more than 3/4 of the way done with a dungeon. You get a bad pull. Everyone dies, even the rezzer.

You have two choices.

The first everyone releases and ghosts back to the dungeon. You automatically unghost upon entering an instance so basically you have to start the whole dungeon over.

The second is give everyone a soulstone. Everyone uses there soulstone to rez at the same time. The warlock tries to give the rezzer the first soulstone as soon as they can in case the group gets aggro during rez effects, and you continue to finish the dungeon.

The problem lies that the warlock can only carry one soulstone at a time so it may take 5 mins to get everyone after dying another one. That is a long time to try to hold off aggro in a dungeon with rez effects, and insuring everyone is safe in case they die again.


So possibly maybe the better solution would be stackable Soulstones with a max of 10 at any one time that are like charges, and they don't disappear from logging.

This reminds me of another frustration with soulstones at the moment. Lets say you are in a full group, and the server crashes. All of you come back, but no one has a soul stone any more. So now the warlock has to farm 10 shards if he doesnt have them farmed already just to give everyone there soulstones again.
