I was running a ati 128 meg 9200. I then upgraded to a Ati 9800 pro.

The 9200 was just dandy for lower resolutions, looked great and had smooth frame rates.

But after I upgraded I was able to run at higher resolutions with all the eye candy turned on.

Basically with wow a budget system will get you by. But if you have to have all the eye candy turned on and run at high resolutions then your gonna need faster cards.

I know a guy whos running a Pentium 3 800 with 512 meg ram and a 64 meg nvidia card. Nothing special at all. and he says it runs fine. Ive seen his screenshots and they look just fine.
