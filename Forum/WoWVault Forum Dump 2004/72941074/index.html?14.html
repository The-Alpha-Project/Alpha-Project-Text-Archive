Probably right around half. Once you find a look ya like, it's easier to collect pieces that match it through crafted gear than the odd assorted quest stuff.

While I do love getting nice quest rewards, many of them don't come until I'm a few levels past the requirement. Buying crafted gear through the auction house at my specific level means it will last much longer (unless I happen across a drop, but most everything I've found isn't above what's crafted at my specific level). If I pick up a vest for a level 24 when I'm that level, I end up saving money on not having to replace it for 3 or 4 levels instead of having a quest drop for a level 22 or so that's weaker. I'm also not going to replace it with newly bought items that only increase my armor by 3 or 4 when my total armor is around 500. My current armor will probably last me until the next tier of crafted armor. At least I hope
