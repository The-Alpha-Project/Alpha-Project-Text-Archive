I ran my 2nd account on my old PC that could barely load the game - it took it like 5 minutes to fire up.

I could two-box with the right setup but it doesn't appeal to me. I usually also don't like encountering or playing with multi-boxers - I've only seen a few that could do it well enough to avoid pissing me off (when I'm grouped with them). Switching back and forth between characters to do the basics is amateur. Having your two characters both chat and talk well enough to fool casual observers and have both doing complex actions at once - that's more like it. If you can two-box well enough that I'm not sure if you're doing it - I can handle it. If you suck and can't play EITHER character better than a complete noob due to two-boxing then stay away!

In some games some people feel the *need* to have a bot - like buffbots in DAoC or whatever bots in EQ (where a lot of people bot whatever class their 72-man raid army lacks). I don't think bots will be necessary in WoW due to gameplay differences.

I'm guessing the only people that will really multi-box in WoW are those that really enjoy it for the extra challenge/fun/whatever of playing two characters instead of one.
