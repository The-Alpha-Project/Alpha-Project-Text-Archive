RE: Whats a hero or prestige class?
The Demon Hunter/Mountain King earns new abilities (Proberly loses most of his old ones) in exchange for new cooler skills/spells, the known spells from the previous game named Warcraft 3 Frozen Throne, included these skills.

Mana Burn: A skill that would burn the enemy casters mana so that it would combust and deal damage to the target.

Immolation: A skill that surronds the Demon Hunter in fire that damages his nearby enemies each second activated.

Evasion: Gives the Demon Hunter a chance to avoid a enemy attack. [Passive]

Metamorphosis: Morphs the Demon Hunter into a actually demon gaining ranged attack and splash damage(Means doing small extra damage over the area)

Mountain King
Storm Bolt: Gives the Mountain king a ability to stun and damage a nearby Player.
Thunderclap: Does AoE damage while slowing the enemy's movement a lot.
Bash: Gives the Mountain King a ability to do extra damage and to stun him [Passive]
Mountain King: The Mountain King gains extra health damage, magic immunity for a limited time only.

These skills are from a previous warcraft game as meantioned, however I would count them as extremly reliable.

Each Race/Class combination is unique, and you will have more than one path to choose from.
The Hero classes are pretty much mysterious for now, but I am sure it will be annouched before the release, if not during the beta.
