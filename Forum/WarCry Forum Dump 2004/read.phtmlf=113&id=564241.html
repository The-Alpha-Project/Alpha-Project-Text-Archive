1. you are correct. Queue lines are down though since they added so many servers

2. I believe you can talk to other people in private messages (not quite sure), but in all other cases (general chat, yells, ect..) you cannot.

3. Well, to cite an exmaple for you. My clan has 30 members in it, it's an Alliance guild. About 75% of our guild is made up of Warriors/Hunters/Paladins. I think we have 1-2 priests, 1 Warlock, and maybe 1 Druid? Anyways, most of the caster classes are ones with lower numbers. Mind you that there are still a lot of people who play caster classes, there are just a lot more people who play the melee classes.

4. You have about 6 options for each of these features: Face, Hair, Skin Color, Male or Female (2 options ), Class, and race. There isn't a great deal of customization when you first start. Believe me though, you will not look like every other person with the same race as you. There are 100s upon 100s of different armor sets that will make your character look different from everyone else.

Edit: I've also tried to tell people this. WoW is not a game about customization for one reason. The races in this game are known as looking like their race. For instance, you will never see a fat Night Elf just as you won't see a seven foot gnome. Customization is fun, but I would much rather prefur realness once I step into the game.
