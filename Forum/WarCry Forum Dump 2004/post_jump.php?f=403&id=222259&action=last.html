EQ Cleric vs WoW Priest
Ok to start I had a Cleric in EQ yes a very High one 54, so I know the ins and outs of what a cleric in that game can do.  I know some people think we were just heal monkeys who sat back and just hit a number every once and while, but I say Bunk to that.  I was damn good at my job, When I was the main healer on raids, I've only lost one main tank ever on countless raids to Kael.  I knew the ins and outs of what heals should be used on what types of players and how much aggro I would be dealing with on account of those heals.  It was a stressful job but the payouts were good, I was usually given the most money out of the groups by getting any Gem Drops and any item for clerics without a roll in raids.  I didnt mind playing the healer, I usually always play a good aligned character just because of the sense of doing good I have in myself. 

The main question is should I attempt to play a priest in WoW, although the payouts in EQ were very nice, the times when you couldnt get a group in the plains sucked, because that left me with no options.  Although the wait was never long b4 someone needed a cleric, it was just annoying to always be doing the plain healing thing and buffs.  You cant have diversity, so will the Priest, although they say they are good at soloing, will they inevitably get turned into healing bitches and will I run into the same problem in WoW??? anyone beta testing a Priest tell me if you think this will happen top end of the game.

As a long time player of EverQuest and a lvl 65 cleric in one of the top guilds on my server I can say that clerics were a class that sat back and pressed a button every now and then, and as Wars stated, as a lvl 54 your experience of EQ was quite different from mine as 65, for example, I could solo most of Kael so easily it was a joke, I was killing Yelinak with 2 other people by the time I quit in early Gates.

During most raids clerics CH chained, and 1 cleric DI'd, that's it, gg a 2 spell class, it was so easy to bot clr that we, like most high end guilds, had 50% botted clr on many raids.

In WoW it doesn't appear that it will be like that.  Without CH clerics would have been a lot different in EverQuest, and Priests seem to be that.  GH is on a 4 sec cast timer which makes it a different beast from CH, the only reason you'll see GH chains is to spread healing out and provide a constant stream instead of with CH rotations where it's the only option for healing, and that will be VERY rare if Onyxia and MC are any indication of raids.  Blizzard appears to quite enjoy throwing AE's and adds at raids to prevent it from a fight against 1 mob's DPS.

Priests' dps is a lot lower than it was before OB, which is only fair, they were too powerful, now they are indeed just healers in the group at 50+, which is why you find almost every SP respecing holy, but that's cool, you want to be DPS play a rogue, don't cry when your GHing priest can't do l33t dps and get parties as a dmger.
