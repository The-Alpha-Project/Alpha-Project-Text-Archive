auction bidding by proxy
does anyone know if the auctions in WoW allow you to place a proxy bid? For those not familiar with the term, a proxy bid is where you set the maximum value you want to bid. The auction agent will then automatically increase your bid as necessary up to the maximum amount.

I've seen posts on the beta forums complaining about last minute bidders and just wanted clarification if these folks are upset because they didn't set their bid at the maximum they were truely willing to pay for the item and thus upset they got beat out, or if the game only allows you to place 1 bid on the item and you have to come back yourself to raise it.

Well i think you are leaving out one important thing. the buy-out option. I think proxy bids combined with that would stay pretty balanced. The seller can put a fair price for starters and set a rather high (but reasonable most of the time, expecially if your in a hurry) and if buyers were just able to leave the maximum amount they would pay and go hunt then they either win or lose no worries. But if someone was attempting to "ninja-bid" them at the last minute, as long as they didn't bid over the other person's maximum bid then all it would accomplish is raising the final price. But leaveing a buy-out on it means that those who have no patience or those who just have that much more money to spend can just snatch up the item. Which works but at the same time you feel bad for the level 10 trying to get his hands on some linen cloth for a decent price while a level 40 can buy a stack for 30 silver and not really care, just to get thier skill up. I hope that doesn't happen too much but they can only make life so fair.

Anyways out of that rambling i was intending this, i think a proxy bid would be a great addition, and take care of the ninja-bidding problem, (mostly). as long as people use the buy-out function i think it will stay quite fair for everyone. Now if they can just work on a reverse bidding system for those who want to commision items to be made/ put in an order for materials at a set price (whoever bids giving the materials at the lowest price wins the bid and then gives the materials to the person who made the auction.)

One other way that seems almost impossible but would be really awesome, if you could put up direct selling into the auction house. It's kind of like a buy store. where you select what items you want to buy, how many, and how much money per item (normally used for reagents/materials, and possibly weapons/armor i guess, maybe even potions and the like) so say i wanted to get myself a whole mess of the plant silverleaf for potions, but didn't want to spend a whole day farming it. I could put a "bid" in the auction saying i will buy up to 100 silverleaf for.. say 50 copper each. the AH then takes 50 silver from you to pay those that turn it in, and any silver leaf sold to your bid gets sent in the mail. Giving good money to people who don't need it or are low level and getting you what you want.

but this could potentially mess up the economy, if people are selling thier light leathers/linens for 3 silver each at level 5 they are gonna be rich mofo's while those high levels buying em can afford that kind of thing. ANnnnnyyways, wow i kinda went off for a long while, if you bothered to read all that let me know what you think, i'm waiting for open beta like all the rest of you guys and don't have anything better to do then ramble on about what a great game this can be. Have fun and feel free to talk to me, i'm a friendly person and will always be up for discussing world of wardcraft. Peace
