Did anyone follow application rules?
Well, Been bored out of my mind since ST so I've been browsing forums daily just to keep my mind occupied.  The more I read the more I notice how many people got into beta that by the official signups shouldn't be in... (insert whatever flame if you want to whine about how your justified)  Don't get me wrong I would give almost anything to be in closed beta since its going to be such a great game and I would like to be a part of it, for history sake and to find as many bugs as I could (find, IE be the first to spot) to help blizz out and in turn give me that much better of an experience when I spend my money on release.  Yet I still only signed up for NA closed beta, even knowing that I could have signed up for EU closed beta.  My main thought was... Man I really want to play but I know there's hundreds of others like me that want in just as bad so I sign up fair and square giving the notion that it would be legit.  Now I look around and see plenty of people from many other places in NA closed beta... and on top of that when blizz gave out dup keys those people in turn gave them to friends over where they are(not all but some).  So this all adds up to a lot of people that aren't in NA testing our release... which isn't ideal to say the least.  On the other side of the argument I've seen NA people sign up for EU closed beta and it just irritates the hell out of me that people can't just take their "fair chances" and be greedy.  And yes this is my biased opinion since I am a "NA that didn't get into beta".  I guess when it comes down to it my sense of honesty was higher than my urge to get into beta, some people may have a high sense of honesty but an even higher urge to play wow?  is the only logical answer to these turn of events as I see them, either that or its less people than I think and they are the less scrupulous types that you find in everyday life so why not online...      

Feel free to flame as long as it is some what intelligent and leads to at least one opposing view, the "you suk" or "stfu noob" reply's just make you(who ever you would be if you reply'ed this way) sound like a complete retard.

Congrates to all the testers that signed up fair and square, its nice to know that at least some of you made it in.
