Nightfury Horde and Nightfury Scouts Guild History
Here is the history on the guild and how it is established. This might give you ideas on if you want to join us or not. Enjoy the short story made by yours truly, Xui Nighthoof!

Tauren
After the Bloodhoof Tauren established Thunder Bluff, many Tauren became regular citizens and guards of Thunder Bluff and dispanded from the Bloodhoof exept for the few who desided to continue the clan of Bloodhoof. Soon after, the clan they called was renamed to the Descendants of Bloodhoof Mercs.

Soon after, a small group in the DoBM dispanded and the rest of the descendants began regular lives in Thunder Bluff untill one day, a young descendant named Xui brought back the outcasts and created the clan known as Night's Fury.

Orc
Soon after the banishing of the demons, Thrall went back to The Barrens and on a north penninsula of it, he named the land Durotar and within it, Trolls and Orcs established a grand city known as Orgrimmar. The descendants of Thrall's horde left adventuring to the lands of Mulgore to help with the Tauren.

Thrall's clan went up to the mesas of Thunder Bluff where they soon met up with the outcasts of Bloodhoof known as Night's Fury Tauren and they joined with the Tauren and renamed the clan to Nightfury Horde. The orcs half was named Nightfury Clan along with the Tauren's side named Nightfury Hooves.

Troll
With the sprout of orcs and tauren in the Nightfury Horde, Xui sent the brave orcs back to Durotar to recruit the trolls of Darkspear to the horde. They eventually reached the troll village where they recruited the trolls but when they got them, they received no message from Xui.

Undead
Before Xui left to Kalimdor, Xui lead the Nightfury Scouts to the Undercity and they allied with the Nightfury Horde on the other side. Xui brought the dead to Stormwind and with their powers, they made it to the docks in time before dawn destroying anything in their way.

Xui lead the undead across the sea but knowing they would not welcome them in Teldrassil, Xui led them off at Winterspring and they went south through the woods to the Barrens where the undead joined the horde. Xui lead the rest back to Teldrassil to now work on their life goal.

Night Elf
In the early adventures of Xui, he discovers a magic artifact that would give him multiple bodies he could own and live in. He stumbled into it and he got absorbed into it with his lifeless body. He created a new Night Elf female body and quickly found clothing before the male Night Elves saw.

Xui noticed it was that shard that created this body so he started making another half of his guild for the alliance. He recruited Night Elves and slowly he convinced the elves to unstead of defeat the horde, to make peace between the two factions.

Dwarves & Gnomes
Xui continued adventures in Teldrassil and Mulgore so he sent messengers to Ironforge and the humans to recruit the members. The elves gathered gnomes and dwarves in Ironforge and Xui continued the recruiting to the Nightfury Scouts.

On the way to the humans land, the first district, the Nightfury Nightwalkers, created the group for gnomes and dwarves known as the Nightfury Minifolk who helped spread the word of the Nightfury Scouts. The outcasts of the kingdom of men became the Nightfury Outcasts.

Humans
All the way to Duskwood, the adventuring elves recruited the humans to the cause as well, slowly, they converted some humans to their cause however, the kingdom found out that there was a secret group of scouts that where allied with the horde so they dispanded them from the kingdom.

Now in secret, they secretly recruit more to their cause and hide their idenity from the kingdom and now, they build secret outposts and grow their recruits so they can stop this madness and create everlasting peace with eachother. The district of men... Nightfury Outcasts...

Aftermath
Xui gathered the divisions of Nightfury Minifolk and Nightfury Outcasts and laid an invasion in Stormwind. Xui took them secretly to the docks without getting cought and excaped uncatched in their boats and sailed to Winterspring in the forgotten lands of Kalimdor.

They left some behind for guild recruiting as the rest excaped to the Night Elf forests of Teldrassil and built a secret base for the Nightfury Scouts. Now having contact with all races, they then started growth on the guild so they would stop this madness.

Nightfury Horde Heirarchy

Moon Overseer
Xui is the only one who can become a Moon Overseer. A Moon Overseer is the main authority and if you are in the guild, you must obey him but dont worry, the Moon Overseer tries not to make orders. The Moon Overseer is the founder and creator of the guild but knowing he could not run the guild by himself, he creates the council ranks where unstead of him runing the whole guild, the others run it for him.

Horde Warchief
There are only 4 warchiefs but that is because there are only 4 races. There is a warchief for every group of races. Depending on your race will determine your official rank name for example, a tauren warchief would be called a Hoof Warchief and an undead warchief would be called a Corpse Warchief. All but the overseer must obey everything the warchief sais but if you think he/she is doing something wrong, inform the overseer asap.

Night Patron
Helpers who help run the guild. All warchiefs need helpers runing the guild so 10 members in each mini clan will be Night Patrons. They also must be respected as they worked hard to get where they are now or are good friends with the warchief or overseer. Night Patrons are assigned to many things such as raid planner or guild crafter. They help allot must should be respected for their hard work.
