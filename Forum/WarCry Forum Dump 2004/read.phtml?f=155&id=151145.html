Looking for a fun group of people?
Hello all.  I'm JoeBiscuit from Synergy Sanctum (www.synergysanctum.com). I want to thank any of you who read and/or make comments to this thread. 

Ok, what is Synergy Sanctum all about?  Well, to simply put it we are a gaming community that plays various online games.  Synergy was formed by four mature gamers who are friends in real life.  We want to make this community about the members and not what WE want.  The admins and moderators are there to help facilitate and expand on what members would like to see for the community since it is they who makes Synergy Sanctum what it is.  We are always open for suggestions for improvements to the community because we are there for the members. 

The site/forums right now do not have a customized skin yet.  We are still working on our graphics guy to get it done lol, so please don't flame how it looks right now; I know.  

We are going to go alliance and are looking for some gamers like ourselves to start a guild in WoW.  Feel free to register at our forums and get to know us before joining.  There are no obligations after registering and we are always open to questions, suggestions, and improvements to the community.  We aren't here for ourselves, but rather for the online friendships formed from playing in-game together and chattin' on the forums.

Again, I want to thank everyone who reads and/or makes comments to this thread.  I appreciate it.
