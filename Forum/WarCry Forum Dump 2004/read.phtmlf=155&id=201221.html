The Warsong Clan
The Warsong clan is a Horde only, Warcraft and World of Warcraft clan. We accept any type of player, be they new or experienced. The Warsong clan also trains its new players to create a most elite army of Tauren, Trolls, Orcs, or Undead.

Ranks in the clan are:
- Chieftains (III, must have Warcraft III): Moderators in forums and leaders of clan.
- Blademasters (II, must have Warcraft III): Moderators in forums and lesser leaders of clan.
- Far Seers (II, must have Warcraft III): Moderators in forums.
- Shadow Hunters (II, must have Warcraft III): Recruiters of clan.
- Grunts (unlimited): New recruits of clan, currently in training.
- Peons (unlimited): New recruits of clan, not yet in training.

Our web site is here and our email is WarsongRecruiter@hotmail.com. To apply for the clan, send an email to that adress. To ally with the clan, send an email to the same adress.

All new player applicants must be finished with training before advancing to Chieftain, Blademaster, Far Seer, or Shadow Hunter. All experienced player applicants may decide on what rank they would like to be.

The Warsong clan is currently inactive, but it will be revived as soon as at least 9 Warcraft III players apply to join. When the clan is revived, you may visit the USEast or Azeroth channel Op Warsong-Clan or Clan Warsong for more information.
