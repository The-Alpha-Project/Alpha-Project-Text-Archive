My Interview with a Rogue

My Interview with a Rogue

Interviewed by Shark (A.K.A. Iceblade)

I had the pleasure od interviewing Alecto (A.K.A. uLxInlike) from United Legions (http://unitedlegions.com) today. We discussed the Rogue class and a few general WoW issues.

 


Shark: Ah greetings mind if I ask you a few questions about Rogues?

Alecto: Sure

Shark: How does the lockpicking skill work? Do you need to use talents to get it or skill points?

Alecto: You buy it from your trainer much like all your other styles and abilites

Alecto: You then need to purchase lock picks

Alecto: And when you find "locked chests" you use your skill and goto town

Shark: Are there diffrent level lock picks for higher level locks?

Alecto: Not that I am aware of

Alecto: But my skill so far is not as high as most, I have found it an non-essential skill up till now

Alecto: Most people who take fishing, raise the skill alot faster

Alecto: Because when you fish, you get locked chests all the time

Alecto: Raising your skill alot faster then most casual exp hunters who find chests here and about

Shark: Have you found it useful while playing in dungeons and exploring?

Alecto: Not as of yet, no

Alecto: Lets put it this way, I have not gone out of my way, to spend alot of time to grind and raise it

Alecto: Although, my partner has, and everyone once in awhile he scores a nice item

Alecto: But typically, chests are rare, and you usually find things like Tailoring supplies, potions, food or leather supplies

Shark: Are there locked doors you have come acrooss?

Alecto: None as of yet

Shark: I read somewhere a while back,that WoW has a new system to poison. Are there many diffrences in WoW than from other games? Is it the simple 20 DPS that makes it so they do not regenerate health?

Alecto: Well, I also had a 50 Nightshade on Daoc, and the poisons there were better

Alecto: Currently, poisons are like a crafting skill

Alecto: You make your own from either store bought ingredients or later on gathered herbs

Alecto: Right now, most are either insta damage shots, dots and snares

Alecto: Down the road there are a few feign death poisons and varying damage ones

Alecto: But since you dont get poisons until level 20 and the cap being level 30, there is not much variation yet

Alecto: One bug is that all the poisons share the same icon, so it gets confusing

Alecto: I liked the debuff, dot, snare line of poisons in daoc better

Alecto: I have submitted some bugs on it already

Shark: So overall it could be better?

Alecto: Yes

Shark: Have you invested much in Pickpocketing?

Alecto: Yes

Alecto: That is bugged too atm

Alecto: Whereby you only get skill increases on times you PP and fail

Alecto: But the skill works on humanoid creatures

Shark: Have you found any useful items from pickpocketing?

Alecto: other then coin or sellables, no.

Shark: What trade skills do you have currently on your Rogue?

Alecto: Skinning and Leather Working both very useful

Alecto: and I may pickup Herbalism 1 just so I can get herbs for future poisons

Shark: Now with skinning is there only a chance that you will get the skin?

Alecto: well, I think there is a random modifier based on level of the mob to dictate whether you get leather scraps, light leather, medium leather, heavy leather, light hides or medium hides

Alecto: So lower level mobs typically only give light leathers or leather scraps

Alecto: But, level 28 mobs still have a lower chance of still giving out a light leather, but more often give medium leathers

Alecto: I rarely see that I did not skin something

Alecto: If I were to guess a ratio, it would be 5-8% of the time I dont get am item

Shark: Can you ever skin "special" kinds of leather from mobs?

Alecto: No, i have never seen special items

Shark: Now with Leather Working what kinds of ingrediants are required to make something and, how does the process work

Alecto: Well, for the most part it is like combining

Alecto: Every level you gain 1 skill point when you ding

Alecto: It costs skill points to become either a skinner, leatherworker etc.

Alecto: Now, as you leather work, you gain skill

Alecto: As skill goes up you gain access to new recipes

Alecto: Now each recipe when it is your interface is either orange, yellow, green or grey

Alecto: When crafting orange items, you always get a skill increase, yellow % based, green % but lower and grey never

Alecto: Now, depending on the recipes you either need combinations of leathers or hides as well as vendor purchased thread

Alecto: Either coarse thread, fine etc.

Alecto: Not with hides, you need to cure them first so you buy salt and cure them

Alecto: You can get leatherworking skill points off this as well

Alecto: You can also make armor kits

Alecto: The first one you will is light leather kit, it costs 4 light leathers to make

Alecto: And this is what you will probably start off doing to raise the skill

Alecto: Once you make a kit, you can add it to armor pieces to add +2 armor bonus

Alecto: Later down the road, you can purchase recipes from rare vendors, or they are drops from mobs

Alecto: Or the odd time they are in chests

Shark: So the "kits" are like the "patches" we have been hearing about? how many "kits" can you add to armor and what kinds of armor can you add it to?

Alecto: Aye

Alecto: Light armor, medium armor and heavy armor kits so far. Light armor kits give +2 medium armor kits give +4 and heavy armor kits give +8 and can be added to arms, head, boots, hands, and chest.

Shark: Are there any recipes that can add magical bonuses you have found?

Alecto: No, you need an enchanter to do that

Alecto: But, there are recipes with bonuses naturally inherent to the recipe ie, Heavy Leather Tunic recipes is 2 med hides, 12 med leathers 2 fine thread and gives ac36 BP with +5 sta

Shark: When you add a kit to a piece of armor, does it last forever or is it a temporary bonus?

Alecto: Forever

Shark: Have you found stealth important?

Alecto: Super important

Alecto: Especially for getting to and from dangerous places

Alecto: 2 forms of stealth, regular stealth and an insta-vanish

Alecto: The skill raises when you stealth near aggressive mobs

Alecto: They either see you, or auto face you and do a roll/check

Alecto: If you are successful you gain a point

Alecto: Your main front loaded damage style is called Ambush at level 20

Alecto: It is a rear position strike that you need to be stealthed for

Alecto: So typically, when I duo, 1 person will pull, gouge, then BStab, then I will ambush

Alecto: Or vice versa

Alecto: Vanish is nice get out of jail free card or a de-taunt

Shark: That is the insta stealth?

Alecto: Yes, the insta-stealth

Alecto: Basically, instant stealth with a 5 minute recast timer

Alecto: You need a re-agent of flash powder

Shark: How long does it last?

Alecto: Until de-stealthed

Alecto: Vanish basically drops you into stealth instantly

Alecto: Works great when running from mobs or in group situations if Feint is not working (detaunt style) you can use it to de-aggro yourself

Shark: Have you found any recipes for leather working off of mob drops?

Alecto: Yes, as well as other types of crafting recipes

Alecto: Some of the higher level ones will only drop off mobs

Shark: Were they more powerful than the ones you buy?

Alecto: Yes

Shark: When you find recipes can you add them to a "recipe book" or are they 1 time only?

Alecto: You can add them to a recipe book

Alecto: But once added, you are the only one that gains anything from that scroll or recipe

Alecto: Not like you can copy it and then give it to someone else

Shark: Are you duel wielding?

Alecto: Yes

Shark: Do you recommend it?

Alecto: Aye

Alecto: The damage output is nice. As well as you can poison that second dagger, typically for me with a snare poison. The DPS you gain is better then the block percentage defense you would get with a shield

Shark: How important is money?

Alecto: Well, money is very important

Alecto: But not to the point at this stage, where I have had to say, damn I really need to take 5hrs to farm "x" mob so I can buy some gear

Alecto: its not to an extreme as of yet.

Alecto: But the market is only varied at this point to level 30, so cost inflation is going to change as items get harder to craft and rarer to find base materials or recipes

Shark: Do you think it is easier to make an income with pickpocketing than other classes?

Alecto: Yes, I am sure it could because with PP, you are scoring the loot first verus taking the time to kill and loot

Alecto: So ideally, you can make a run threw a camp of humanoids, PP em all, and leave

Shark: What happens if you get caught

Alecto: The mob aggros you

Shark: Can you pickpocket members of your faction?

Alecto: No

Shark: What talents are most important for a rogue? What ones are useless

Alecto: For me, I have been pumping Agility and Dagger Specialization

Alecto: The more +agility the higher DPS you get and Dagger Spec adds +1 to that as well

Alecto: In the early stages I made the mistake of putting 1 point into stealth which means my normal cap of lets say 150 at level 30 is now 152

Alecto: So I would say avoid that unless it has some advantage in stealth detection for PvP

Alecto: Other areas to dump points into is Stamina and Prowess. It raises your hp's

Alecto: Avoid +str, aucity, intellegince etc.

Alecto: There are shield specializations as well as defense specs, but I have avoided those as well, opting for front loaded damage

Shark: When should you spend points into stealth?

Alecto: I am not sure if you should at all, but depending on how enemy stealth detection will work in End Game, it might be good to pop points into it later if it helps decrease your uncover or detection range, but I would have to advise that it is a waste early on

Shark: so your natural stealth is enough?

Alecto: Yes

Alecto: 2points over cap at this stage is really marginal improvement over the more impressive increases of lets say +30 hits from a point into sta.prowess

Shark: Are there diffrent forms of backstab available or does it level up enough to be useful througout your entire career?

Alecto: 2 forms

Alecto: 1. Backstab

Alecto: 2. Ambush

Alecto: 1. backstab works as an anytime style, rear positional.

Alecto: With each successful strike you earn 1 combo point

Alecto: It scales as well depending on level

Alecto: You goto your trainer and he gives ya updated ones based on current level

Alecto: Now, Ambush, rear positional style from stealth

Alecto: High damage with a refresh timer of 1 min I believe

Alecto: I have hit for 227 with it, nice style

Alecto: Earns 1 combo point as well

Shark: Do other players see you while you are stealthed?

Alecto: Yes they do

Alecto: It is almost like a shade form and it gets harder to see as you raise the skill

Alecto: You gotta look hard for people sometimes, but they are never quite invisible

Alecto: You can use terrain to your advantage this way and you really can only sometimes see people when you are real close are they move

Alecto: I like its current form because in daoc, it was pure invis and detect based on a radius

Shark: What should you build at lower level that will help you with higher level

Alecto: In terms of actual abilites, everyone is fairly cookie cutter because we can purchase all the same skills when they become available

Alecto: The only "spec" variation is on how you spend TP points

Alecto: You can gimp yourself if you spec for example all defense points each time you level

Alecto: So there is character variation that way

Alecto: So you could either go defensive rogue or build up stamina or go pure agility

Alecto: I know I would like the 10pts i spent into beast slaying and 10pts I put into stealth lore back. Later down the road though, ie. beast slaying or undead slaying, those skills may become more helpful, but I cant say whether they will or not

Shark: What are your experiances with the mass groups?

Alecto: I havent really "zerged" it up yet

Alecto: But I have done the 5 member group thing alot

Alecto: The ability to "sap" gives the rogue some more groupability if the group doesnt have a priest and you are fighting baf mobs

Alecto: As well as the high DPS is nice

Alecto: Im a little disappointed in the 5 size, wish it was 8

Alecto: makes it hard to be versatile. You will end up getting cookie cutter groups

Alecto: 1 priest, 1 hybrid buffer, 1 mage, 1 pure tank w/ taunt and 1 hybrid tank

Alecto: But I hear there is a "mass group" function soon but not sure what it will do, probably be like a battlegroup from daoc and eq

Shark: How fast is the day/night cycle? I know there was alot of controversy about it.

Alecto: Im not sure actually

Alecto: Looks kinda broken atm

Alecto: I have never really seen it "night-time"

Shark: So are you overall impressed with the game so far? would you support the people saying the beta is better/more complete than most MMORPG's?

Alecto: Yes, defiantely

Alecto: I would support that statement

Alecto: I have played alot of beta's and this game is so far ahead it is not even funny

Alecto: Yes, alot of work needs to be done, in ways of fine tuning certain quest bugs, or artwork errors, or even a bit of balance on damage or spells etc.

Alecto: But that whats the Beta people will do in time for Blizz

Alecto: But server stability, world stability, lag, etc. all top notch so far

Alecto: And that is the most important

Alecto: Will be interesting to see what happens when I log in with an extra 3000 people on the server though

Shark: Any final thought about the rogue class? Anything important you thin we might have missed?

Alecto: Hmm.

Alecto: Sap is important tool for crod control

Alecto: Feint is great for detaunt

Alecto: Explore alot that helps, and take every quest you can get

Shark: Well that about wraps it up

Shark: Thank you for your time

Alecto: my playsure
