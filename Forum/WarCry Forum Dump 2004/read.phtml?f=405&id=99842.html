Okay I've been waiting to readd to this for quite some time. Just to see how things balanced out. First, starting stats were added to the game quite some time ago. Rogue starting stats are:

Str/Agi/Stn/Int/Spt/HP
Dwarf 23 /20 /24 /19 /19 /80
Gnome 16 /26 /20 /23 /20 /48
Human 21 /23 /21 /20 /20 /56
Night Elf 17 /28 /20 /20 /20 /48
Orc 24 /20 /23 /17 /21 /72
Troll 21 /25 /22 /16 /21 /64
Undead 20 /21 /22 /17 /25 /64

Damage is based 50/50 on str/agi
Agility also effects your chance to dodge/parry/ and crit (about 2x as it does other classes)
Stanima effects your hitpoints (as show in the starting HP field)
Int effects the speed at which you level your melee skills up.
Spirit effects your HP Regeneration ( in and out of combat )

Shadowmeld is not in the game (though it might but i don't see it being a huge boon to rogues). Feign death is a hunter skill, not undead, so that is not a factor. Its true that undead are immune to most forms of crowd control (sheep, sleep, mind control) but as said before palidan > undead most of the time.

Int doesn't have a huge effect on a rogue. Usually we cap our weapon skills quickly anyway so the gnome advantage (other then size) is very limited.

So in compairing alliance side rogues they are pretty balanced. Nightelves are going to be able to evade damage and crit more often, but they are going to hit for less then thier human/dwarf counter parts. And the human/dwarf rogues are going to have more HP then the night elf. The human is going to crit more and do more damage then the dwarf, but again the dwarf has the most HP and is able to take the most punishment.

On the horde side. The orc has the most HP, but the troll is going to hit more, for more, and take less damage. And the undead is going to have the least amount of downtime between enemies. (also, just as a note last i heard undeads sleep, fear, polymorph resistance is just against other PC's not against mobs. Im not sure if it was chanced in this last patch or not)


Again just for reference I'm playing a level 30 Troll Rogue on the PvP server
