Gnomish Paladin Petition!
I have been playing MMORPGs since the first day Everquest went public, and since that time, I have been a fan of the little people.  I don't mean just anyone who is short, I mean the shortest of the short, the gnomes!  In Everquest, there were no gnomish paladins either, but the public has since changed Verant's mind. I was a gnome (or the shortest race if gnomes were not a choice) for all my years of MMORPGing, and I would hate to see the shortest people continue to pull a straw that matches their height.

I have created a petition that you can sign asking Blizzard Entertainment to allow Gnomish characters to play as paladins.  Please give it a read, and sign it if you agree to what it is saying.  If you are like the rest of the people who have signed this petition, and believe that it's time to stop "overlooking" the little people in MMORPGs, make your voices heard!

The link to the petition is:  http://www.petitiononline.com/Xrix/petition.html
