RE: Are people leaving WoW?
If only it were true...the population on my server seems to go up every day, causing more lag problems. Levelling isn't a grind at all and is very fun, especially the quests.

EQ had people in forums whining for *years* on end about how the game was awful, and people were leaving in droves, etc etc. In reality, it wasn't happening and EQ, to this day, has a huge player base that is happy with the game. People who are mad can make alot of noise easily, while people who are happy with a game usually don't go to a forum to defend it--they place their vote by playing the game instead!

Certainly, WoW isn't some gaming utopia. It's a better video game, by far, than EQ, but as a Role playing game it barely rates mention. Battles are so fast that choosing where to fight, paying attention to what creatures are nearby, etc, becomes unnecessary. Groups of people that know where to find Elite mobs in their level range can level extremely quickly, especially if they don't bother doing anything else like quests, exploring, or tradeskills.

From my 19 levels of perspective WoW is amazing, and alot of fun. Noone on my considerable friends list has left, and noone I know has gotten fed up with their class and rerolled (a common occurence in EQ). So while I have no doubt there are some people leaving WoW over gameplay or (more likely) server issues, I'd be very surprised if any servers are "starting to empty".

But if they do, let me know, because Alleria is packed.
