Eternal Night: Night Elf only guild
Eternal Night is a guild for Night Elven only roleplayers. We plan to form the day World of Warcraft is released. The guild itself is split into five houses, out of which the Elders lead their respective houses. The five houses are:-

House Light: Deals with interguild relations, in all aspects, from trade, to treaties, to declarations of war. Inside the guild they act as Arena referrees and settle disputes. This house is for ambassadors, diplomats, and advisor style characters. Classes accepted: All classes, but invite only, apply to another house first, if you get noticed you may recieve an invite.

House Protector: Deals with the defence of the elven lands, this house comprises 90% of the elven army and nearly all of the soldiers, sheriffs, and generals. Classes accepted: Warrior, Hunter, Druid

House Healer: Deal with the welfare and care of all elves. This house is spread throughout the land to care for all elves. A small division of them live with the soldiers of house Protector and supply the healing and spells an army needs. Classes accepted: Priest, Druid

House Keeper: Charged with the care of the woodlands and all its creatures. This house tends the glades guardens and trees, always watchful of the balance of nature, they defend their personal groves with determination but would rather leave the bulk of the fighting to the soldiers. Classes accepted: Druid, Hunter, Priest

House Shadow: This house serves as the scouts and border watchers. This house deals with the dark deeds the elves would rather not speak of. Accepted classes: Rogue, Hunter, Warrior


You can find our forums here.

Also, check out our website!

Happy Roleplaying!
