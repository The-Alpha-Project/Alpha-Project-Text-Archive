Seeking Roleplayers: Paladins, Priests, & Rogues
http://www.mithriltwilight.com

Currently recruiting Paladins, Priests, and Rogues. Full membership requirements includes active participation on the forums and in game as well a mature behaviour. Lack of activity will be met by removal from the roster.

The Order of Mithril Twilight was founded by Duke Theobane Endsong and Lady Kurumi Shepchild on October 6th of 2003. The name of our Guild was taken from the story surrounding it's creation. It is derived from Our Lady Twilight and her Greatsword, Seraph-Mithril, hence the name The Order of Mithril Twilight. You can find further information of Our Lady Twilight in the history thread on our forum.

We are a roleplaying guild dedicated to the eradication of the Undead off the face of this world and more importantly, our old home of Lordaeron. Expect our forces to be amongst those crashing into the horde lines that will lie in shallow graves as we retake various towns and assault and attempt to retake our old capital. We do this in the name of lost love ones and friends, we do this for our lost King and brethrens, we do this to rid the world of this blight, to destroy the corruption of undeath, to free any souls and minds tainted and twisted under the corruption of an eternal undeath. Only through the victory of the Alliance over the Horde and the utter elimination of the undead cancer shall there be a lasting peace for everyone. We will kill and die for our cause, let there be no mistake about that. Lordaeron shall be purified once again.

We believe in carrying ourselves honorably and assist our brethrens and sisters in distress, be they of our own or of our allies. Our undead adversaries however will not find the same merciful kindness at the end of our manifested will.

Our nobler members adhere to a sense of chivalry. Some of us are agents living only in the shadows to assist those of us that are in the light. Generally you are free to roleplay your character as you wish under certain guidelines which are specified in our enlistment requirements. If you wish for assistance in how to roleplay your character, we are here to assist you to become a good roleplayer.

The Order is lead by Holy Brothers and Sisters of the Order, encouraging on those of the light and other faiths towards a crushing victory over the occupation of Lordaeron. Because of the nature of the Order, we are both PvPand PvE oriented Order.

We prefer to play with civil minded players, you do not need to be the smartest person in the world, so long as you know how to respect others, you will be welcome to join us for our discussions and roleplaying forum as well as in game.

Our website is under construction and is slated to be rehauled with new artwork sometime during the fall. It will also be updated with new guild content that was recently developed on this forum by active participants of this guild.

Currently I am painting digital character portraits of members who receives a rank of Captain or higher in our guild. It is a token of thanks for their hard work and their continued future efforts in jointly creating a great guild for everyone to belong to. isplay&num=1090099648" target=_new>http://endsong.proboards24.com/index.cgi?board=inform&actionisplay&num=1090099648

Further artwork on the wish list includes small generic character portraits to be used as avatars and signatures exclusively for the members of the Order.

The basic command structure is at the top is the Duke and Lady - Canoness. Following are the military officers; the Orders two greatest Paladins: Commander and Marshal. Under their command are the Captains. Down the line after the Captains are the Lieutenants, further on our order comprises of many Master Sergeants, Sergeants, Honor-Brethren/Sister, and Brethren/Sister.

We also plan to join a small alliance of like minded guilds to increase our chance in securing our end goal (It is not possible to retake Lordaeron, the game does not allow it, however it gives us an eternal goal to keep us preoccupied when we are playing and make our roleplayed enemies' lives close to hell), generally I dislike larger alliances. Also we intend to roleplay some events with WoW-RAhorde/undead guilds for some fantastic battles or story driven crisis.

We intend to have player journals that will be published onto our website. We encourage active participations in roleplaying related activities including our roleplaying forums, developing character history, telling tales/fan fiction, and other artistic/literary pursuits related to World of Warcraft and the Order.

The Order will be located on a (official or not unofficial) roleplaying server and will be adhering to roleplaying rules. We intend to be roleplaying alongside with the guilds of the WoW-Roleplaying Association

That's all for the introduction, if you wish to find further information about us, please read the other threads and look around on our forum. There's little tidbits of information littered everywhere

Silentium Arcani
This unit is comprised of Rogue and lead by a Captain of the Order. It's role is a RP PvP unit that conducts missions alone and unsupported using only stealth as it's most valuable asset in the battlefield.


The Inquisitors
This unit is comprised of Paladins and Priests and is lead by the Grand Inquisitor. It's role is a RP PvP unit whom are fanatics and spout many references from holy texts as they purge the heretics of undeath from this land.



We hope to see your steel and magic along our side in this great crusade.

 Inquestorial Doctrine and Protocol



Inquisitors have but one purpose in life, one purpose that must be sought at all costs: the eradication of that which threatens their beliefs. Traditionally, Inquisitors are judges, handing down rightious retribution in the form of punishment, allowing others to deal with the "dirty work" of bringing the accused to justice. This organization, however, will not follow the guidlines that tradition has laid out. Nay, this branch shall step from the role of judge and into the role of zealot. The purpose is still the same; destroy, purify, and redeeme those who have become tainted with the corruption that threatens our lives, our homes, and the very land we walk upon. No longer shall the name of "Inquisitor" be thought of as a passive, ruthless figure handing down sentence from behind a bench. We shall bestow upon this name an image of strength, ferocity, and unrelenting determination to our cause. We shall never allow the taint of the d**ned to flow freely within our lands. We shall dam it up at the source, we shall strike within it a fear unknown to those of the Light. We shall carve a name for ourselves in the heat of battle, with the rightious Light as our guide and guardian. We shall never forgive.



Within the Order of the Mithril Twilight, the Inquestorial Branch shall fullfill two positions. One being the ever-diligent eradication of threats from outside sources and the other to deal with percieved threats from within the order itself. While this Inquisition will not follow tradition in its methods, it will take from tradition several guidelines that will aid the Order to remain unified and unshaken. Therefore, the Inqestorial Branch shall serve as a Tribunal. As such, any threats from within shall be relayed to the Tribunal so that they may be dealt with in a fair and timely manner. Given the nature of the Inquestorial Branch, however, a final judgement can only be made and authorized by the leading officers of the Order, the Tribunal can only analyze and give forth a fair and accurate compilation of the evidence, the accused, and the crime.



As an Inquisitor, an individual must devote himself solely to the cause of the Order. That cause being to wipe the lands of those corrupted by the Burning Legion. One of the first things that must be accepted is that not all foes are readily apparent. Even within our own alliance there reside those who bear this taint, and it must be ever present upon the minds of Inquisitors that these souls are never to be trusted. In his devotion, an Inquisitor is expected to allot a great deal of his time in training both alone and with his fellows so that he may understand the nature of combat. Such training will unify the Inquisition and the Order so that when the time for battle comes, we are not found lacking or worse still, a hindrence.



If the Inquestorial Branch is to be successful, it must draw from the entire Order. From each member the need to stand to arms may be called in times of extreme duress. Beyond that, however, comes the relationship with the Silentium-Arcani. The Inquisitors shall draw heavily upon those within this shaded Branch to gain information, and at times, intermingle skills to strike deep within enemy territory and cleanse even the most tainted of lands of the corruption that lies within. It will be the combined efforts of these two forces that will create the most potent force for this purpose, a blind eye cannot and shall not be turned to that fact. Make no mistake, though, the Inquestorial Branch shall be equal to any other in battle. It is this that will define us.



Each member shall be required, if able, to attend Inquisitions. These encursions shall take place only after careful planning and proper preperation so that all may be present and ready when the time comes. There shall be both "practice" and "True" Inquisitions. Practice Inquisitions shall be those targetted at stationary, perpetual threats which require less planning, and shall contribute to the furtherment of the training of all Inquisitors (instances, raid groups on NPC targets). True Inquisitions shall strike deep within contested and enemy territory and shall target foes possessing their own cunning and intellect equal to ours. Such Inquisitions shall occure only after extensive planning and training have occured to ensure that each member is capable of the task. Once an Inquisition is begun, those Inquisitors who are a part of it are effectively severed from the rest of the world, operating as an efficient, self-contained force. None shall falter until the task is complete.



As a member of the Inquisition, you shall combat the darkest forces Azeroth can contain. You shall see the true nature of evil, and destroy it. This is not a place for those weak of spirit or doubtful of mind. No longer shall we sit in the background directing from a distance, cowardly, feeble. No. Now is the time to step foreward, bearing the Light as our Standard, and swing the mighty hammer of rightiousness with our own hand. Sieze your destiny, and weild it like a weapon. Take up your fate, and don it as a shield. Shoulder your purpose, and thrust it into every dark corner of Azeroth. May the corrupt tremble when they hear our name.
