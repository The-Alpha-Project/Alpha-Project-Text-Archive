How to get from Night Elf areas to Dwarven areas
Journey from Shadowglen to Ironforge: (Recommended level 10-12)

This is a quick guide for those who want to run to Teldrassil (the Night Elf starting area) to Ironforge (the Dwarf and Gnome starting area). You can do it at level 1, but you'll almost certainly die a few times. You may want to wait until you do some of the starting quests and gain a few levels. Though I suggest you wait until you're higher level and are either in Dolanaar or (preferably) Auberdine, the guide starts where a level 1 night elf starts for completeness.

Step 1: Shadowglen to Darkshore: http://i7.thottbot.com/Interface/WorldMap/Teldrassil/Teldrassilbig.jpg

You run south out of Shadowglen and follow the road as it curves west, through Dolanaar, until you reach a fork in the road going north or west (north takes you to the Oracle Glade) and you continue west into Darnassus. Continue straight west to the small island at the end with the glowing purple area under a tree, and go into that purple area to teleport to Rut'theran village. There go ahead and left to the gryphon point and select Darkshore as your destination.

Step 2: Darkshore to Wetlands

You land in Auberdine, and don't even leave the city. Walk down the ramp from the Gryphon platform and into the inn, then head left out onto the docks. Wait on the docks for the boat, and board the boat which will take you to Menethil Harbor, in the Wetlands.

Step 3: Wetlands to Loch Modan: http://i7.thottbot.com/Interface/WorldMap/Wetlands/Wetlandsbig.jpg

This is the dangerous part. First, make sure to see the Gryphon Master in Menethil harbor to get that gryphon point. Then head north out of the city, then east as the road turns. You want to stay on the road and avoid any mobs, since they're probably going to eat you alive. Eventually, you'll turn south into the mountains at Dun Algaz. Make sure to stay on the road and aggro as little as possible. Watch wander paths when possible, but crocolisks and raptors might attack you. If they do, run like hell, and if they kill you, just run back as a ghost, res up on the far side of your corpse, and run some more.
Stay on the road as it turns south and enters the mountains, and goes through the tunnels of Dun Algaz and eventually lets out in the northern part of Loch Modan.

Loch Modan to Dun Morogh: http://i7.thottbot.com/Interface/WorldMap/LochModan/LochModanbig.jpg

You want to head straight south into Thelsamar here, grab the gryphon point just left off the road on the hill just past where the road decends into the town, then continue south on the road until its first fork west (to your right) and a little northward, and take that into the tunnel leading into Dun Morogh.

Dun Morogh to Ironforge: http://i7.thottbot.com/Interface/WorldMap/DunMorogh/DunMoroghbig.jpg

I forget where exactly you come out, but essentially you want to take the road west and then northwest to Ironforge. When the road ends just north of Kharanos, turn north and then follow the road along the side of the mountain northeast up into Ironforge. Now you're either with your friends or can safely take the Deeprun Tram to Stormwind. Congratulations!
