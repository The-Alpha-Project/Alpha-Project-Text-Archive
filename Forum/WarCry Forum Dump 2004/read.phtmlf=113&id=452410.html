neat info!
Thanks to our staff member Avina for giving me the heads up on some new information about World of Warcraft:
  1. Realms for WoW will be divided into USWest, USEast, Europe, and Asia. Initially, the server you play on will be determined by where you live geographically.
  2. The European launch is expected before Christmas 2004, and the US release is expected approximately 3 months before that. In other words, very, very soon.
  3. The release date will not be simultaneous worldwide. US and Asia will come first, with Europe a few months afterwards.
  4. The European beta is just around the corner, approximately 2 weeks away.
  5. Some special rewards for PvP include special titles, battlefields, PvP rankings, and special vendors which only the best PvP'ers will be able to access.
  6. It is confirmed that you will not be able to create both horde and alliance characters on a single server.
