I honestly don't think I will ever see the need for any profession other than Alchemy. Alchemy benefits you the most while playing. You can always buy the crafted things, but the Potions keep me alive. Sure you can always buy potions too, but I prefer to make mine on the spot, hand them out before an instance, and have an "endless supply" since they are consumable.

I'd say that if you're going to go with an Equipment Crafting profession, you've gotta stay on top of it or you'll surpass it and it will be useless except for some extra $. I'm in a deal right now with a leather worker to sell him 2 elixers of defense so he can make a pair of Blue Gloves.
