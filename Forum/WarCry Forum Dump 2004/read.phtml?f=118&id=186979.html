I played a tauren shaman til 18th lvl and can tell you this.  They have to be re-worked.  They do have soloing potential, but the group buffs leave much to be desired.  The buffs are given via summoned totems that plant themselves right next to you.  Only one totem per element can be summoned by any one shaman at any one time.  Their armor is light, the HPs gained per level is not on par with a paladin or warrior.  At higher levels, they are not a tank.  ALSO, shamen are NOT the mages of the hoarde.  Mages are the mages of the hoarde.  It is also too early to choose a class and race (for me).  They are all getting make-overs.
