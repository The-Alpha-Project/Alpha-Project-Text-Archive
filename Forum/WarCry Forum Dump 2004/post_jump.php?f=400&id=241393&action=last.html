Now, now. Let's not bicker. First of all, Xalamar, the information that Chaospawn has posted is not only more than accurate, it's almost a direct quote (besides the comparisons) from GFraizer. The planned release cap is 50 -- they may raise or lower it depending on how testing is. That is a common fact. Pets can be 90% of the hunters current level, which means the max for a pet is currently 45.

Pets stay with you until you release them or until they are felled. Should they be felled, *I believe* there is an option to ressurect that specific pet, but I could be wrong. You are able to train your pet skills for each type of pet at specific trainers, so that when you tame a pet of that type, you are able to use it to its full potential -- which gives the hunter more uniqueness, because it can utilize it's pet in more than one way.
