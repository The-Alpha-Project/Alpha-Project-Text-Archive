Black Lion Guard is now recruiting members for our WoW Pvp West Guild! Accepting all Alliance races and classes for a tight knit Pvp oriented clan comprised of mature and active individuals.

Black Lion Guard has roots in Uo, Eq, Daoc, Ao, Swg, Horizons, and Lineage 2 and one of our main focuses is developing a close relationship between all members of our guild be it through questing, dungeon crawls, raids, or simple clan chat to prevent player burn out. Our members should expect to set examples for others to follow by, through proper grouping etiquette and general experiance of the game itself.

The Guild will compose of 3 separate Divisions which will together be known as Black Lion Guard. Each division will be made up of 40-60 Members depending on the preference of the Division Leader.
 
Guild Leader (1): Oversees the 3 Divison Leaders. Has final say in all Guild matters. All important decisions regarding each individual Division must be first approved by the Guild Leader.

Co-Guild Leader (1): Is the second in command of the Guild. Has jurisdiction over the 3 Division Leaders and serves as the temporary Guild Leader if I take a leave of absence.

Sub Leader/Div Leaders (3): Each Division Leader has a two fold job. First is the running of their individual Division which involves everything from recruiting new players to organizing division/guild raids. The second aspect of their job involves relaying important guild information to the other Division Leaders and organizing joint efforts between the 3 Divisions and their members.

Div Sub Leaders (3-6): Each Division Leader is responsible for selecting up to 2 Division Sub Leaders that will help manage their Divisions and serve in the absence of the Division Leader.

Members (??): The bulk of our Guild, Members are the backbone of our main fighting force. Members will start off in our basic division and will move on via promotion by their Division Leader. Everyone has a say in the guild and all concerns by Division Members should be relayed to their commanding officers.

 
Individuals interested in a guild that prides itself on Honor and Integrity need only apply. We can be reached at www.blacklionguard.com and future guild members can drop us a line on our message boards there.
