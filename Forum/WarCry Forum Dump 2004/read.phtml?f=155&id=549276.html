PVP.Horde.Hardcore.East.Bleeding Hollow...you like?
We come from many games, but we come together with common goals for WoW.

We're a horde-based PvP guild on the Bleeding Hollow server. We have members from all time zones, but we all play from roughly 4pm-midnight CST, nearly every night... we will *easily* put in 40-50 hours every week. We're planning on approaching level 60 within the month. We have a 50 man ventrilo server to facilitate guild communication.

As far as rules go, obviously the Golden Rule is in effect. Just be courteous, and treat everyone with respect. Don't do anything that will ruin your name or reflect poorly on the guild, etc. In PvP, we do not support ganking or trash-talking. We're aiming to establish our reputation as a skillful, honorable PvP guild, and ganking and trash-talking tend to give people the opposite impression.

We are mature players. We are skilled players. We know our individual classes, and the game as a whole, extremely well. If you aren't, and if you don't, then we don't want you.

We currently have about 20 members. We are planning on hard-capping our membership at 40-45 members so as we may self-sufficiently compete in all the PvE and PvP raid content that WoW has to offer. Regardless of the stage of development, we refuse to accept any members who cannot dedicate themselves to the game, and to being the best, in the ways that we are willing to. Inactive or immature players will not be tolerated, and will be kicked should they somehow manage to get into the guild.

Basically, we strive to be the best. We want members who have the capabilites and desire to be the best.


Hopefully I covered everything. If you have any further questions, please don't hesitate to ask--just send me a private message.


If you are interested in joining, or wish to simply say hello and meet some of the members, stop by our forums (the lodge) at http://www.direomen.com and leave us a message telling us a little bit about yourself. You may also contact Rahm, Excelsias, or Menthos in-game.



NOTICE:

We are currently only looking to recruit *skilled* players of the Druid, Mage, Hunter, Rogue, and Warlock classes, as well as possibly a Shaman or two. Recruiting for Warriors and Priests is closed.

Thank you for your time.

 

Menthos
