I agree with Jeffowa, that the shaman is the Horde paladin. Of course they are not the "same," or else it'd be W2 all over again. Shamans will have decent melee compared to other magic casters (Thrall in W3 is a shaman, and he's pretty darn powerful).

Now, paladins and shaman are no where the same in abilities, etc. But, if you want to play a warrior but still cast spells, then you pick paladin for Alliance and Shaman for Horde. That's what makes them similar. Both classes are faction-specific too. In that respect, it seems pretty logical to assume that the shaman is the Horde's answer to the paladin.
