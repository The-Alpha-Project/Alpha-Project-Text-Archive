Priests have inner fire (a great self-buff) and an instant-cast damage absorbing shield. They can also raise their (and others) stamina by quite a large number during the higher levels. They have massive heals, mana burn, shadow word: pain, a holy nuke and plenty of combat-controlling tricks up their sleeves. Great soloists, great in groups, first and primary targets in any sort of PvP and heavy PvE (healing = much aggro).

Priest will be one of my first characters, probably after Paladin.
