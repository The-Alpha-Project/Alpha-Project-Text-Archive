RadicalPally wrote:

Warriors are supposed to be the best melee character in the game. 




Rogues are supposed to be the highest Melee Damaging class in the game. Warriors are a mix of meatshield and melee, giving them great 'soloability' and a decent role in grouping. Rogues, on the other hand, dish out the most melee damage with dual weilding small weapons and adding poisons -- making them one of/the highest DPS class in the game. To balance this, rogues can't take nearly as many hits as a warrior.

Now, if warriors were able to dual weild, they'd be completely over-powered because they have tons and tons of HP, defensive/offensive warrior abilities AND can use two weapons. Having a class such as warrior dual weild would completely unbalance the game, in my opinion, and I personally hope they can't.
