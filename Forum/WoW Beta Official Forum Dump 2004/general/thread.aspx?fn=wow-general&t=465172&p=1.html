Night Elves will NOT have Paladins, entirely different belief structure. The Night Elves do not follow the Holy Light, and the Holy Light is the backbone of the Paladin. You can't be a Paladin without using the Holy Light, its like being a Arch-Mage without using Arcane Spells.

Wrong...

The Night Elves follow the powers of Elune, a gift that have been CONNECTED with the Holy Light, but has different overall practices. Blizzard simply for balance reasons decided to not make every class and race combo look and play unique, so the Night Elf Priests cast the same Priest Spells as the Human and Undead ones.

The Undead DO have Priests that use a dark perverted form of the Holy Light, but they also get Holy Spells for BALANCE reasons.

You will not change this, but adding Night Elf Paladins not only is not connected with the Lore, but it also would NOT balance anything in the end.
