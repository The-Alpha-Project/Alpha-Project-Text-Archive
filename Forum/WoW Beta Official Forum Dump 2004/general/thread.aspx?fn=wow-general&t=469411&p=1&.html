 1. Horde Flight Paths -- REWORKED. | 10/23/2004 6:36:58 AM GMTDT
All/Blizzard:

I've decided to create a mockup of what the Kalimdor flight paths should/could be. First, here's a bit of information:

From Thunder Bluff -- Crossroads (Barrens), Sun Rock Retreat (Stonetalon), Desolace (Shadowprey Village), Orgrimmar (Durotar), Freewind Post (Thousand Needles), Feralas.

**Shadowprey Village (Desolace) ONLY accessed through Thunder Bluff.**

**Feralas ONLY accessed through Thunder Bluff (Mulgore)**

From Crossroads (Barrens) -- Thunder Bluff (Mulgore), Sun Rock Retreat (Stonetalon), Blood Venom River (Felwood), Coast of Ashenvale (Ashenvale), Orgrimmar (Durotar), Freewind Post (Thousand Needles).

**Blood Venom River (Felwood) ONLY accessed through Crossroads (Barrens).**

**Coast of Ashenvale ONLY accessed through Crossroads (Barrens).**

From Orgrimmar (Durotar) -- Crossroads (Barrens), Thunder Bluff (Mulgore), Gadgetzan (Tanaris), Everlook (Winterspring), Azshara.

**Tanaris ONLY accessed through Ogrimmar (Durotar).**

**Everlook (Winterspring) ONLY accessed through Ogrimmar (Durotar)."

**Azshara ONLY accessed through Ogrimmar (Durotar)."

From Sun Rock Retreat (Stonetalon) -- Crossroads (Barrens), Thunder Bluff (Mulgore).

---------

Just by looking at these available paths you will see that seven (7) of them require a mandatory connecting flight if you are anywhere other than Crossroads, Orgrimmar, and/or Thunder Bluffs. In Azeroth, the Alliance is capable of flying to any destination [typically] from one of two major hub locations -- Stormwind and Ironforge. Therefore, I am suggesting that we do two things for the horde to make it more agreeable to play:

1: Create two hubs that seperate, yet maintain a cohesive continent. Like Azeroth has Stormwind and Ironforge, we can see Orgrimmar and Crossroads -- as its aptly named -- taking a center stage role. We can also incorporate Thunder Bluff as another "mini" connection hub for each surrounding lands.

2: Intermingle the flight paths that are closest to one another. For example, it makes no sense that you are incapable of flying from Desolace to Sun Rock Retreat (Stonetalon) when there is a path leading from one to the other and the lands are literally right next to each another. If the flight paths are mingled, you'll see less downtime on the horde side.

If you look at the map of Kalimdor below, you'll see flight paths that are centered around two hubs -- Orgrimmar and Crossroads. Each Wind Rider Master will allow several possible routes.

>>>>>>> http://home.earthlink.net/~ag3/img/hordeflightpaths.jpg <<<<<<<

Last, this is a suggestion for flight paths that will help not only the Horde, but also the Alliance -- especially at open beta and retail. We need to see each flight path, whether it is available to us or not. For example, if I wanted to fly from Feralas to the Barrens [according to the link listed in this post] I would see a green dot and solid black line going from Feralas to Thunder Bluff. The Barrens destination would be visible, however the dot would be gray and the line would be faded out to suggest that there is no absolute direct path. This tells players where they have to connect [if they even have to connect] in order to get to their intended, final destination. No more remembering, no more guessing.

Anyway, these are just suggestions that I hope may be implemented to some varying degree over the next patches before open beta/retail. Thank you for reading.

all the best,

- Putressa
