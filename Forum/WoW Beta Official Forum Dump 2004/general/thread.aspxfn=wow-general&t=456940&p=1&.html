Q u o t e:
It's just a hearthing bug.


It's not even entirely a bug. When you run around, your computer is constantly loading information about the area around you. You only have to fetch data for the things that change as you move.

When you use a hearthstone, you suddenly have a big area of unknown around you and your computer has to try reading all of that in at once. That's why you get extra lag.

(There are actual bugs with various cities that have nothing to do with this and do cause extra lag.)
