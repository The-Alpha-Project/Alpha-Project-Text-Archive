 109. Re: shaman more versatile then a druid why? | 10/21/2004 1:25:34 PM GMTDT
Druids have the same problem that jack-of-all-trades classes have always had at first in MMORPG's. They can do many things, but are sub par in all of them. Generally a group is looking for one more of something. A tank, a healer, a dps class...so why pick a Druid for their tanking or healing or DPS when there's better options? A group looking for a tank dosen't care that a druid can cast Starfire or turn into a cat, they want a tank. A group looking for a healer dosen't care that a druid can turn into a bear or spam Moonfire, they want someone who can heal and rez.

That's the issue I see with druids. It's hard to make a true jack-of-all-trades without also giving them something that's noticably useful and unique as well.
