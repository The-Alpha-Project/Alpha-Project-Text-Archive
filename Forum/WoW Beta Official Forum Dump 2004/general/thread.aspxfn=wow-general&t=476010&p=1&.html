Dear Taron and Wavepath, beautiful young Nightelves of Duskwood...

Thank You for entertaining the boys tonight on the rooftop in Darkshire. We came to that small gloomy town in hopes of throwing a big party in an out of the way place. My girlfriend Wyvvy built a bonfire in the town square, but those uptight provincial guards didn't appreciate her 'explosive' methods:

http://www.unknown-insanity.com/uploader/userfiles/Wyverngod/WoWScrnShot_102504_013030.jpg

So we retreated to a nearby rooftop to avoid all the racket, and a lot of angry partygoers got out of hand on dwarven ale and kept trying to shoot at us. We couldn't speak the language too well, so we did the only thing that got their attention and took a hostage for ransom! Poor sweet Taron was the closest victim possible... I pleaded with Wyvvy not to hurt her, so she nudged her out onto the ledge of the roof wrapped in soft lambs-wool (in case she fell) so that everyone could see she was ok. We demanded to be left alone by the angry young alliance who kept throwing bottles at us, unless stronger bouncers could be hired to keep order:

Here's poor Taron 'bleating' for her life (don't worry, Scorch has on his special safety helmet and bungie pole incase he needed to rescue her...)

http://www.unknown-insanity.com/uploader/userfiles/Wyverngod/WoWScrnShot_102504_010146.jpg

Taron was a very brave elf, and suddenly pleaded for mercy and offered to help us with our party plans instead of calling the militia for a very reasonable fee which you will see I negotiated with her. She seemed like a really nice girl, and she said she had a girlfriend who would come party with us too! She called up Wavepath on her Darnassus celphone and quickly set to work providing lapdances (lucky lady Wyvvy got the first one!) while we waited and enjoyed the scenery...

Notice KHB reaching out his hand to her in sudden and unusual friendship and intrest:

http://www.unknown-insanity.com/uploader/userfiles/Wyverngod/WoWScrnShot_102504_012139.jpg


The undead priest "King Hillbilly" (KHB) who was once well known in his previous 'lifetime' as a country music star, seemed to recognize this young nightelf exotic dancer... perhaps some long lost groupie he never got to meet before his undeath? Taron and KHB embraced passionately while the rest of us partied on into the night.

I couldn't help myself, caught up in the love and fun of the evening, I snapped a picture of the two lovebirds curled up together, snuggling on the rooftop:

http://www.unknown-insanity.com/uploader/userfiles/Wyverngod/WoWScrnShot_102504_011955.jpg

Lovely Wavepath arrived soon, and Wyvvy and myself got down with them, boogying the night away as the guys stood around watching and keeping the local law enforcement and volunteer alliance militia at bay.

We made it as clear as possible in our terribly broken common language, that NE Females were welcome to dance on the roof!:

http://www.unknown-insanity.com/uploader/userfiles/Wyverngod/WoWScrnShot_102504_012439.jpg

Finally, the four of us spent some time talking and comparing fashions... we were soon very close friends! I never imagined in all my dead years that I'd have two great Nightelf girlfriends on the other end of the world, how cool!

Here's a picture of Wyvvy and I enjoying our time with our new best friends. We've made plans to go shopping at the Ironforge mall next weekend, but we haven't figured out how to disguise Wyvvy and myself in those tight nightelf clothes just yet...

http://www.unknown-insanity.com/uploader/userfiles/Wyverngod/WoWScrnShot_102504_012526.jpg

The party wound down after that, and a cranky old human warlock showed up on the scene to ruin our dancing with some ill advised hellfire, letting us know in no uncertain terms that our evening with the ladies was over. They sulked (as he called their parents I'm sure) as they put their clothes back on and blew us kisses as we all rode out of town.

It was a GOOD TIME had by all!

<3 <3 Taron and Wavepath! <3 <3

Call me soon hon!
