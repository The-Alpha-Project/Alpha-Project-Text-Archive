Here's a list of applications and websites you can use. If you don't have something that'll protect your computer, then get these for free. It's so much better than nothing.

Zonealarm- It's a firewall. Firewalls block unsolicited internet connections, which almost always are viruses or warez. The free version lets you choose what programs have permission to use the internet. It blocks everything else, and can block outward connections. Many keyloggers, info stealers etc. make outward connections.
http://www.zonelabs.com/

AVG Virus Scanner- Scans your computer for viruses, and gets rid of them. Some of the newer viruses may still need a special download to get rid of them.
http://www.grisoft.com/

Adaware- Scans for Illegal warez, and kills them. Warez can steal personal information, CD keys, and will slow down your computer. Get rid of that junk.
http://www.lavasoftusa.com/

Spybot, Search and Destroy- It's an even better warez scanner. This program is much more hardcore than Adaware. It'll scan your system, and kill warez. A must have for everyone.
http://www.safer-networking.org/

Windows Update- Run this at least once a month. This will seal some of the many leaks in windows, and leave you a little more secure. You have to run this in Internet Explorer.
http://v4.windowsupdate.microsoft.com/en/default.asp

Mozilla, or Firefox+Thunderbird- A great replacement for Internet Explorer. Internet Explorer has tons of holes in it's security. These programs do not. Get them, and you'll be much safer on the internet.
http://mozilla.org

ShieldsUP! Free system probe- This website is devoted to system security. They offer a free system probe, so you can see just how secure your computer is on the internet. Try it out.
http://grc.com/default.htm

A CD burner- Okay, it's not a program, but you should have one. Take all that security stuff you got, and burn it to a disc! I cannot emphasize how dangerous a fresh Windows installation is. With an up to date CD, you can install all the security stuff, and secure your system before you connect to the internet.

There's no reason to leave your computer naked when you surf the internet. So go to these websites, get the programs, and test it at ShieldsUP! After you do that, backup all your security downloads on a CD.

Hardware firewalls are nice, but they can be defeated if you let a program get on to your computer accidently, such as downloading a new type of trojan.
