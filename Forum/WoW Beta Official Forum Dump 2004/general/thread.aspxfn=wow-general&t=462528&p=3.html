I think I tend to agree with your initial post. And like a lot of people are saying in the thread, duo'ing or having a three person group seems to be about the "sweet-spot" for a lot of things, not too hard and not too easy. In fact, that's how I've spent most of my playtime within the game(partially because I have a roomate to group with).

When I first started playing WoW I would have whole-heartedly agreed with you. For the longest time, being in a group was usually more of a hassle and more dangerous than going solo or smaller. Most of the reason are, like you said, players are ill-prepared. But as I play the game more I start so see a ever subtle shift in this, what I think it is is that the Devs are giving the players a long ride training-wheels for the good stuff.

I think this is good and different in a few ways.
1- Group play is not manditory, those that want to solo can.
2- The transition into highly disciplined is a smooth one, albeit long, instead of a jarring one(EQ) or an everlasting soloplay.
3- High level content (raids, PvP, and possibly further expansions) is being 'reserved' for the group-minded people since the 'base' game will always have solo-ability.
I think it would be harder for them to try and go backwards and tweak classes and mobs that were meant for groups to try and go solo(look at EQ now) than having everyone strong enough to solo and then tweaking their group roles and group encouters.

While there are definately solo styles and group styles, what I think that you bring up but didn't emphasise enough is that the content that *IS* suppose to be group oriented (ie: instances) is far too easy when you are in said group. Damage is doled out so quick that unless it's a pull of 5 or more mobs there's no real challenge, and even then it can just be a quick shift to spam-AE tactics.
I'd like to see more intelligent-mob encouters, where instead of 10 generic tank-mobs rushing my group, why not a group of mobs that are made up like player groups(ie:healer, tank, mage, ranged, stealth, etc)? That involves far more tactics, challenge, and thinking than just throwing more mobs at a player or mobs with just more hitpoints.

Dynamic group vs group encouters(sound similar to PvP?) is what would be great for group content.
