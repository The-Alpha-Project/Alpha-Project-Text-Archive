It is curious that the dark irons haven't driven out the orcs in so many years, yet have time to do attacks in Thandol Span and the Loch Modan Dam ... instead of attacking the orcs in the Spire which is their own backyard. It seems that the black dragons wanted this towers eliminated so that the black dragonflight and the orcs could invade Dun Morogh, Iron Forge and Wetlands. While there is a discovery of plans to invade Lakeshire, Duskwood and Stormwind to the south.

The storyline would look interesting if there was a alliance of the dark gods and Deathwing for a same goal, but it indeed adds a totally different flavor of enigma if indeed the Black dragonflight with the orcs/trolls/ogres of BRS want to wipe the Dark Iron Dwarves and Ragnaros in order to have access of their armies through Cauldron to attack Iron Forge.

Everyone knows that Lady Katrana Prestor is highly possibly a black dragon shapeshifted; as prestor is the human name that Deathwing used in Alterac Mountains during the Day of the Dragon storyline.

It looks like the black dragonflight is weakening the alliance forces from within, shapeshifted as humans in the politic and military command positions. What are the goals, well, that is something I am looking forward to find out in retail =]
