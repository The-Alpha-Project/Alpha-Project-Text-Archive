Way back in the first Horde push (or maybe was second...) in Alpha, a friend and I were working some quests. This was back before collision was implemented, and we found a way up a huge hill, and ended up on top of the world. We travelled ALL around the place...via the top - where the sky meets the pixels, and where landscaping is all about squares and lines. lol.

It's really weird to go back now to these zones in a legit way, and see the once empty place, completely populated; still, it was very cool. :)
