     299. Re: Mask classes in PvP,and group levels by c | 10/21/2004 6:30:06 PM GMTDT
    Reasons why its needed:

    1) Having levels and classes viewable to the enemy encourages 'weak-sauce' players who only start fights they are assured of winning (level 40 fights level 30).

    2) Having levels and classes viewable encourages target calling on the weaker armored/hit point characters. You can have all the tools in the world to defend your class but if you are ALWAYS singled out first youll spend most of your game time dead. Sure people will figure out who the weak links are in battle and over time remember but why help bring on a bad thing?

    3) It doesnt take anything of value FROM the game for the potential of adding so much into 'fair' play.

    P.S. This was the ONLY feature I liked in Lineage 2's pvp system.
