 1. Tigole, Stratholm Balance Issues | 10/22/2004 5:11:57 PM GMTDT
We attempted Stratholm last night as a group of 5 level 60's (Warrior, Paladin, Rogue, Mage and Priest).

We were very successful, came close to a complete wipe thrice, but we were able to avoid them and move forward.

The Baroness Anastari was a fun battle, we chose to sheep the possessed, as opposed to killing them, this resulted in enough time for the group to gel as it really posed some comedic entertainment. Unfortunately, should the mage be possessed, we had to kill her (cry). I guess, this would be the positive of keeping us mages in cloth gear. Of course this strategy would only work, if you are able to pull her away from any possibility of adds, we were successful in pulling her down to the gated area and fight her there.

My only suggestion here, is to reduce the length of time one is possessed, so the battle can move on faster. I can see that you might think this would be a faster battle if we killed the possessed, however in a group of 5 losing one is not necessarily a smart move. Specifically if it's your priest, or your DPS class.

After the Baroness we moved through the rest of the area towards the next boss, (can't recall his name.) who holds the key to the gate? Easy battle for five..

We entered the area of the Abominals, med, buffed and healed, we charged forth. We had no major concerns here, their agro range has been increased somewhat this push, as we noticed the battle was non ending. Abom after Abom entered the area and there was little time to med up. We were successful tho, and Ramstein was a challenging fight, but again... we made it through.

After we cleared this area, we rebuffed, medded as needed. Moved toward the Baron...

This is where we ended our fight. I'm pretty sure, that it is quite possible to do this battle with a group of five, as we did manage to bring the Baron down to 50% of his health. However, once you fail to beat him, you must reset the instance. This was a great disappointment for me.

A group of five runs a high risk of wiping at the Baron, but there is no way other than to tack on another 2 hours to your time, to re-strategize. Not many people have this time to run through content we just completed just to have a chance to retry another strategy.

Perhaps, allowing us re-entry into this area so we can implement a different strategy? To be honest, I am not even sure, that a new strategy would be successful, his Mortal Strikes were hitting for 1900+ (often times, greater than my HP).

I just find it odd, that we get one try at The Baron, with no recoup possible, but in Scholomance you produced a shortcut to the Headmasters Quarters. If there is a way to recoup quickly without having to spend all that time running through the initial content to get to him, can you enlighten me with this?

I do recall last push, that the door never reopened. Even as we sat there waiting for release of our spirits last night, that door remained closed. sigh
