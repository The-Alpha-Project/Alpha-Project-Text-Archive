Facts about Water Walking that everyone crying for a nerf needs to know:

1. It is dispellable by priests. If a shaman is buffing others with it you can have a priest dispelling it just as quickly.

2. If someone is water walking and they attack a target in the water, they drop into the water as well.

3. It is nearly impossible to see when a target is near you if they're underwater and you're on top. The red name does not appear until they get close enough to attack, you cannot look into the water at them without submerging yourself.

What should be done to Water Walking?

IMO, the only problem with it is the fact that you can get back up on top of the water after you submerge. Require a trip back to land to do that, and it's perfectly fine.

This thread should not have even been made to call for a nerf on WW, because the problem Kali ran into was faulty guard code combined with the new neutral city pvp rules. In a neutral city, guards should be able to get to people on water. It's as simple as giving them the ability to swim without lowering their movement speed in water. Problem solved.

Anyone else calling for a nerf simply doesn't understand the ability well enough or wants to stack the odds in their favor by crying foul on abilities they don't have. WW is nothing like Invis, it's not overpowered, and there are plenty of strategies to deal with it.
