 55. Re: Ogre-What's up with Orgrimmar low FPS? | 10/13/2004 2:52:56 AM GMTDT
It has nothing to do with rendering actually. Huge particle type objects (and some very tall objects, such as the hanging lamps in Undercity) are affecting the bounds calculations for their parent objects, causing them to become very large. This has a very adverse affect on performance, as the game has to consider far more polygons for collision and intersection testing (in the Ogrimmar case, 20x more than it did last patch).

We are currently working on the fix for the next patch.
