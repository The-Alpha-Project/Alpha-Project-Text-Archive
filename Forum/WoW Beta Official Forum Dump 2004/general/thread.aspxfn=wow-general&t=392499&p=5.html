Usually there is NO character or item wipe going into open beta.

First, you still have to test brand new high level content being patched in trying to get the game ready for live.

Second, you have to test new quests, quests you are fixing (like The Completed Robe, Felhound Warlock pet, hint hint), and new NPCs and NPC dialogs.

Finally, you want to see how the game world reacts with a mix of levels and activities which more closely matches what you will see during the first 6 months to a year of live.

So basically, Blizzard has about one month to run a whole lot of new characters through the early levels of content to make sure its all working right, as well as to fix the huge number of broken quests and such at mid- and high-levels to make the game ready for live.

November 16th is right around the corner. :)
