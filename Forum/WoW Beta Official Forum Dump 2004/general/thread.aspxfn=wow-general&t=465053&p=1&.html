As a database admin myself it seems you could store 2 sets of data, one for PvE or not PvP flagged and one where you were PvP flagged.

Then once you gain/lose the PvP flag it just sets your abilities/spells/stats on gear accordingly.

Take the walk on water issue...

if(pvpFlag == 1)
walkWaterSpellAvailable = false;
else
walkWaterSpellAvailable = true;
end

-or- for some stats on gear

if(pvpFlag == 1)
blueUberSwordAgilityMod = 5
else
blueUberSwordAgilityMod = 20;
end

Sure it might take some more coding/checking but then stuff that is good for PvE but wreaks havoc in PvP could be "insta nerfed" for the moment.
