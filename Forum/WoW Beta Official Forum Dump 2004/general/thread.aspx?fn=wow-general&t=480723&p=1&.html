Asking for yet another limit to the game? BoP bags are just about one of the worst ideas I've heard in a while. There haven't been and will never be an "stable" economy within an mmorpg, no matter how hard they try to restrain it.

There's 2 sides of the economy as it is right now in WoW. Pre-Lvls 35/40, Post-Lvls 40/45+ and they are very limiting as it is. Yes. I agree, you can get money once you reach a certain point in the game and if you know what to sell, how to sell it and farming is a key to this sucess. Questing is out of the question, since most quest rewards blow and you don't get more than 2 gold [ so far, lvl 54 ] in any of the rewards. So really, you have to do one of the most boring things within a game in order to get some cash, farm. And I personally loathe it.

Player wealth should and it's to some extent more natural. Professions such as tailoring are a way to provide that. I never sold much items from my profession, but I sure did some decent cash and kept both myself and my friends stacked with the latest bags I could craft. At some point, yes, there's going to be way too many bags in the market, like there's going to be many other things. "Necessity" becomes "Common" after an ammount of time. And something so needed like a bag during the first 2 weeks of the game, will no longer be "hot" in the market nor popular. It happens with almost every item out there.

There's already way too much BoP items that limit the economy as it is. We don't really need more screw ups in the profession system.
