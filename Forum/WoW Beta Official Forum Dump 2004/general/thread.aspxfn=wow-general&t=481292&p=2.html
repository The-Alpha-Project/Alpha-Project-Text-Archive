The thing about Blizzard is that everyone knows that they release high quality products. Much of this stems from the fact that all previous Blizzard games have only come into the market once the developers were completely satisified with it. Blizzard games are always a great deal more refined than the games other companies release.

The phrase most people attribute to Blizzard is:

"when it's ready".

The result is that Blizzard has one of the best reputations in the gaming industry.

Now compare this with a company like SOE which has a repuation for being 100% profit driven without any personal pride in their games or respect for the consumer. I have never purchased a game from SOE, and I never will. For the same reason I won't purchase used cars from shady dealers.

I'm concerned that Blizzard is moving away from this philosophy. If they release WoW early in order to maximise profits, my fear is that it will set a precedent for future Blizzard games, which may gradually degrade in quality from what we have come to expect from Blizz. This would really be a blow to the industry and my hobby (addiction) of choice. =/

Sure, I'm in the WoW beta. But I wasn't in the beta's for starcraft or diablo 2, and despite the fact that I wanted to play these games very badly, the fact that I knew the games would meet and exceed my expectations made the wait much more bearable.
