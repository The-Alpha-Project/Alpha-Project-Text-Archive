Main problems IMO are:

XP distributions for various levels:
You get only slightly more xps for killing a mob 3 levels higher than you versus a mob 3 levels below you. This is actually true for loot as well, rarely is there really that much of a difference in the loot you will receive in such a level range.

Difficulty attacking higher level critters:
As a warrior I just really cannot fight creatures 3 or more levels higher than me. I can't hit them and thus I cannot get rage leaving me with little to do or contribute to a group.

The result is that it is a no-brainer to always be fighting lower level creatures and it is a no-brainer to do so solo.

Instead, lower level creatures need to give far fewer xps and higher level creatures need to be more realistically accessible to good groups with better xps rewards. This needs to be tweaked until grouping against challenging higher level content is feasible and efficient -- in terms of experience and loot.
