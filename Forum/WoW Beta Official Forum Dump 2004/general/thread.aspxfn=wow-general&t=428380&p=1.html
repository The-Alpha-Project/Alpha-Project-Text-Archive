 1. Request on [14]: Hit/Miss Ratios | 10/14/2004 11:31:37 PM GMTDT
Brief summary:
We brought this issue up with the developers, stating that players believe that the hit/miss ratios were changed. Developers stated that no such change was made and that QA might be able to track down what if any issues there might be.

QA stated that miss ratios were not changed after several tests but that NPC block/parry successes were showing as misses if the NPC is not carrying a shield or weapon.

QA is currently doing further testing and as yet, cannot achieve the "40%-50" miss ratios that players are claiming to be true. They are currently undergoing more tests and eventually we hope to see where these rumors are coming from.

UPDATE:
String of misses: Is it possible that players are receiving EVADE messages when attacking NPCs?

EVADE occurs under 2 conditions:
-An NPC returning to it's spawn point after you've run away.
-A problem with the NPC's position, which used to happen when a mob was stuck in a wall or somthing similar.
