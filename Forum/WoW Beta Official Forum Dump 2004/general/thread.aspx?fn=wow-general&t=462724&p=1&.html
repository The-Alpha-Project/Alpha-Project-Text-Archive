 1. So uhh, is there actually LOOT in BRS? | 10/21/2004 7:28:56 PM GMTDT
Last night was my first time into BRS, along with the rest of my guildmates. Tough dungeon, much tougher than BRD in my opinion, but without the zerg component that Stratholme has so I liked it. Probably could use some shortening. Anyways, there was real disappointment in all of us save our rogue. Not just with what dropped, but with the loot tables themselves also. Here's a breakdown of our night:

Highlord Omokk dropped Fist of Omokk, disenchanted. This is garbage for the most part, only class that would use it is druids since they can't use Dreadforge.

Shadow Hunter Vosh'gajin dropped Funeral Cuffs, disenchanted. Seriously, what the !&$%? This bracer is outdone by a level FIFTY Un'goro quest item.

War Master Voone dropped Talisman of Evasion, rogue looted but I doubt he'll use much over Emperor neck. Aside from his dagger, all his other drops are pretty much junk. His bracer is marginally better than a "of Eagle" green but I'm using a +15 int cloth bracer and doubt I'd switch out for it.

Mother Smolderweb dropped some crappy boots, disenchanted. Her loot table just flat out sucks.

Halcyon dropped Pads of the Dreadwolf, rogue liked these. The rest of the table is total ass.

Gizrul the Slavener dropped Bleak Howler Armguards, disenchanted. This drop is just terrible, no one would ever use it. Shield is "Ok" for shaman, nice cloth caster legs.

Quartermaster Zigris dropped 3 cool greens. Cloth armor with "of Monkey" suffix rocks! Why is he even in the game?

Urok Doomhowl dropped Ribsteel Footguards. Fairly modest improvement over the BRD boots for our warrior though. Rest of table is bleh.

Overlord Wyrmthalak dropped Trindlehaven Staff, mage took it. Nothing but INT/STA on it, kinda boring for a high level weapon...Relentless Scythe needs an upgrade, fast 2H weapons just aren't worth using.

Overall a successful night for our group, there needs to be a severe change in current loot tables though.

First of all, each dungeon needs to cater to all classes. The amount of leather drops here is kind of ridiculous, dungeons should have a similiar amount of GOOD drops for each class. Currently there is almost nothing in lower spire that I would wear, why? Every class should feel compelled to want to do a dungeon, especially the first time.

Second, loot in harder dungeons shouldn't be inferior to the stuff found in easier dungeons/quests. We can do BRD Emp runs in 45 mins or so, why is his axe better then the sword that Wyrmthalak drops?

Fix this crap.
