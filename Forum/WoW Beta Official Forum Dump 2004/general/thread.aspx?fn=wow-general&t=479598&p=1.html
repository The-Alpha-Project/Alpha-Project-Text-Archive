 1. Comparison of Horde/Alliance Starting Areas | 10/26/2004 6:22:32 AM GMTDT
So last night I decided to put on my analytical mask, and pose as somebody with a mission. I was determined, I was intense, I was bored.

I levelled 3 Human Rogues, Warriors, Mages, all to level 5. My goal. To see who could do it fastest. I was trying to perfect my run come retail day one.

Before I get in to who did it fastest. Let me just compare the Horde and Alliance area's. I took these screenshots, since they sum it up nicely:

http://members.cox.net/klaedsbane/alliance.jpg

http://members.cox.net/klaedsbane/horde.jpg

Now, any genius can see the difference there. I count 3 MOBs in the Horde zone, not including the one I am fighting, and I could 21, yes 21, on the Alliance side. That does not include behind Northshire Abbey, or the hundreds of Defias to the East.

Moving on.

Let's release my findings on the speed of characters levelling.

I made 3 Rogues, and even made 1 on the Horde side tonight. The longest run took me 28 minutes to hit level 5. I got it as low as 19 minutes once I perfected it. This was the fastest.

The Mages took substantially longer. Too much sitting and drinking. They were upwards of 30-36 minutes.

The Warriors were also very slow. The fastest being somewhere around 32mins if I remember correctly.

What did I prove by doing this? Nothing. Just that Rogues level fast level 1-5 :) I already knew that.

I do find it a little silly in those pics though, the amount of MOBs available to the Alliance side, as compared to the Horde.

So enough typing for me tonight. Good night all! Here is to hoping Open Beta comes sooner than later.
