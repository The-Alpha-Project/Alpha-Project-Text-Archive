If you had high ping, you could be perfectly well aligned, but you'd miss anyways. Your target was elsewhere, and you were aiming based on character position that was outdated 0.3 seconds ago.

WoW seems to have taken that step, and I think this especially applies to paladins. If you're not "perfectly" aligned with your target, your stun won't work, your holy strike won't work, nothing will go off at all =\

Those changes, coupled with the small delays they added after every single thing you cast, makes my character feel STIFF.

I used to be able to press crusader strike, and holy strike at the same time, and they'd go off pretty quickly...Now I have to wait...And half the time, I'm never "facing" my target, just because I'm half an inch off.
