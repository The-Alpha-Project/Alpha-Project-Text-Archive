When I played Horizons I noticed a trend with their vendor system (kind of like the AH with buyout only). The first people who could make an item sold it for a horribly inflated price, then as many people were soon able to make the same items, they would under price each other causing the price to drop drasticly. Eventually the price would balance out, but it took a while.

I predict the same thing will most likely happen when WoW is released. We can try to talk as many crafters as we can into not undercutting each other, but there will always be someone who will sell lower causing everyone else to do so as well.

The best thing to do is try to sell something that isn't flooding the market. It doesn't have to be something super popular as long as there isn't a lot of competition.

Just what I've learned from a crafting only economy, hope it helps.
