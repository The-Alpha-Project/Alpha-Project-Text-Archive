 2. Re: Intercontinental Travel | 10/16/2004 11:47:56 PM GMTDT
Easy way: Beg for a Mage portal or Warlock Summon.

Hard Way: Take a boat from Auberdine to Menethril (sp) Harbor. Run to the east and then to the South through the Wetlands to Loch Modan, dying a few times to roaming Crocolisks and then again to Orcs blocking the path. Then run all the way through Loch Modan into Dun Morgouh (sp again) and then get to Ironforge.

You only have to do this once (tag the Wetlands, Loch Modan, and Ironforge griffins on your way), but boy oh boy does it suck.

I did it with a level 1 about a week ago, its hard and does involve many deaths.

The crocs are a pain in wetlands, they have a HUGE aggro range on a level 1, I died about 12 times to them. (Keep in mind, when you rez, get as FAR away towards loch modan as you can)

Once you get to the orcs in bewteen the 2 tunnels, chances are you will die. If you get lucky, you will be at the loch modan graveyard .. use the spirit healer, pick up your gryphon route ... and keep moving.

Once in Dun Mourgh, die and take another spirit healer ... you will now be at Kharnos ... then you are set.

It took me about an hour to pull off. I wanted to do it, because I can :P
