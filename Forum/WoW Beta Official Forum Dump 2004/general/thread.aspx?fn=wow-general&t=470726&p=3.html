a) pets are designed to tank for SOLO PLAY
b) pets health bars don't show up in group windows
c) people usually won't heal your pet even if you explicitly state that it will be tanking beforehand

Let's make sure you understand this basic concept with your gameplay mechanics:

1. There's no area that requires any amount of skillful pulling;
