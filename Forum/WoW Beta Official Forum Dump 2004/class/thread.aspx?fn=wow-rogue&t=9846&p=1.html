So not only do warriors get a greater benefit from dual wielding, they get a greater benefit from daggers than rogues. What gives? It's not like warriors have no other choice than to dual wield.

Why does Cheap Shot fail to generate two combo points 20% of the time? How about if charge failed to generate rage 20% of the time?

How can Blizzard justify continuously nerfing rogues, yet remain completely silent about fury/weapons specced warriors being the second highest DPS class in the game?
