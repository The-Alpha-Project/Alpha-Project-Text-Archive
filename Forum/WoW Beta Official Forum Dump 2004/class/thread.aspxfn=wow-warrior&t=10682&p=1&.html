 4. Re: Warrior Balance! for us, not other classes. | 10/18/2004 4:58:37 AM GMTDT
I like how they changed improved shield bash from:

increase to-hit chance by 10%/20%

to:

50%/100% chance of silencing target for 3 seconds.

It's not a huge change, but it's step in the right direction, at least.

I think protection talents definitely need some improvements, because at this point, there's no real incentive to go past improved taunt, aside from concussion blow, and possibly improved shield wall.

Last stand would be more useful if the hit points weren't temporary. In this case, the cooldown could be changed to 15 or 20 minutes (to make it less overpowering).

Also, I think improved shield block would be better if it increased the damage blocked, say by 10% per rank. As it is, shield blocking doesn't seem to make a difference on high level instance bosses anyways.

You could also put in an ability that increases resistance to melee/ranged attacks by some % ( say 75%, for 10 seconds) but while using the ability you can't move or attack. Every time you got attacked while using the ability you would instantly taunt the attacker.

Fury line should get a combat usable tackle move that causes the target to fall down. It could cause damage to both the target and the user (in going with the berserker theme). If needed for pvp balance, make it so that you can't attack right away after doing a tackle, much like the current charge bug (so that people don't complain about being completely helpless in pvp). This would give the fury users some way to close the distance, and interrupt spells.
