I had 5 PvP fights today. The one against 2 hunters was easiest; chase them down and chop until dead. No crowd control means that they are pretty easy; once you get into charge range and are smart (or have more runspeed potions, an absolute necessity for any warrior in PvP), they're dead.

Another instance, a druid tried to run away, and apparently forgot that he could root. Ended up critting him for 300 (level 25 here) and pretty much ruining his day (or at least 5 minutes of his day).

Another with a priest and I vs. a paladin, rogue, and a priest. We were a few levels higher than them, but the uneven sides balanced out. Their rogue sapped my priest, I charged and killed their priest, and beat their paladin half to death. By this time, the rogue ran up to BS me and I swapped to defensive stance and turned to face him. Now, I'm undead, so I have a bad choice here: paladin or rogue. My priest woke up, so I turned and chopped down the paladin, and was promptly finished off with eviscerate from the rogue. Priest finished him off though.

One a rogue 20 levels higher than me came up and ganked me while I was fighting a mob. Then stood around camping my corpse. Those wacky rogues.

Fifth some elf ganked me as I was fighting a boss mob in ashenvale. He was fair about it, let me loot the boss first.

So basically, the main problem is crowd control. Roots, snares, slows, etc are the last thing I want to see. Second last thing I want to see is either someone 20 levels higher feeling like they need to make a point by killing you, or someone just up and running away once the fight is joined. It isn't that I think they're bad for being a coward. It is just damn annoying. You lose, take your lumps. The sole exception to this is when you're near guards and are trying to train your enemy into them.

But I find that 75% of the PvP I do is while running. Understandable, most classes aren't melee, and most don't want to stand toe to toe. I appreciated the paly/rogue/priest fight because no one ran.

It isn't like death means all that much. Die, have a smoke, come back, spend 2 minutes running to your body, big deal. I'm not going to stop chasing you, because you'll eventually screw up.

So given all of that, I hate fighting mages and druids most of all. Anything crowd control. Rogues are fun when you go into defensive and start overpowering them every 5 seconds.

I should note, I am not an expert on PvP in any sense of the term. In fact, I usually hate PvP. I play MMORPGs for the cooperative element, not the competitive.
