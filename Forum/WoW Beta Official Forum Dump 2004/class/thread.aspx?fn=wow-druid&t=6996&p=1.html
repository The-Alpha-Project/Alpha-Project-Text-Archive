Overview of current Druid Balance skills and talents

Beta Ver 0.11.0 (3925) Oct 8th 2004



All data is assumeing level 60 avaliability.



----------------------------------------------------------

My Calculations



MinDMG+MaxDMG/2 = AD (Avgerage Damage)

AvgDMG/Mana = D:M (Damage to Mana Ratio)

Damage / (Cast Time+1second refresh-animation) = DPS (Damage per Second)



Oct 06 2004

Spell Numbers from http://www.thottbot.com

Talent stuff from http://wowvault.ign.com/View.php?view=Talents.View&category_select_id=6

----------------------------------------------------------



|=====================|

| Base Balance Spells |

|_____________________|





StarFire 06 = 485 AD 4.5s 315m -- 1.54 D:M -- 107.78 DPS 

   Wrath 08 = 250 AD 3.0s 180m -- 1.39 D:M -- 083.34 DPS

Moonfire 10 = 589 AD 1.0s 375m -- 1.57 D:M -- 045.31 DPS (32dps DOT , 205dps Nuke aka 589 for 1 sec cast)

Ent Root 06 = 270 AD 2.5s 125m -- 2.16 D:M -- 009.15 DPS (10dps DOT)

Nat Grasp06 = 270 AD 1.0s 125m -- 2.16 D:M -- 009.64 DPS (10dps DOT)



Thorns 06 = 18 reflect dmg for 10m @ 320m

F-Fire 04 = -504AC for 40s @ 115m 





Starfire, Obviously this is the best direct damage spell we have, the D:M and DPS is huge



Moonfire, IMO this is the best offensive damage spell we have, always always open with this followed by starfires until moonfire needs refresh if you are playing nuker



Wrath, If you dont have the talent points in wrath, remove this spell from your spell list, there is no reason to use this spell.



Ent Root.... basically imo they are a 1/4th life they are not for damage they are for damage potential think of them moreso like ffire



Natures Grasp, this is a must have from the druid talents, 5 points.



Thorns, well its kinda worth keeping these up on people takeing melee damage in group but IMO it needs more damage + longer duration + less mana cost.

Or better yet convert it similar to the shaman version yet allow it to be cast on others.



Ffire, I only use this when my group is melee heavy or if i am bored when soloing , its decent but I dont think AC is all that important currently.



SO in a quick summary, 

wrath = useless

moonfire = best spell

starfire = close 2nd best



SERIOUSLY need a stun/inturupt every other caster class has one.



Roots need to be indoor/outdoor we have 0 CC in instances, and the !=outdoor checking in game is pretty horrid.



If we cant have indoor roots we need a way to slowdown runners, currently only option is root or to switch to bear and charge.

IMO we need a castable snare that does no damage or is a dot.



Currently druids only AE options are 31 points into balance or engineering, need a line of AE spells. Be it ae snare, ae root, ae dd, ae dot, anything.

| Balance Talent Line |

|   58 Points Total   |

|_____________________|





Natures Grasp, Good skill, IMO this is the first talent that must be maxed with any build



Improved Wrath, 5pts, 0.5s cast time reduction

 Wrath 08 = 250 AD 3.0s 180m -- 1.39 D:M -- 083.34 DPS

IWrath 08 = 250 AD 2.5s 180m -- 1.39 D:M -- 100.00 DPS

Toss up on this, it seems to be a very good DPS spell but it is mana inefficient compared to other options.



Improved Entangiling Roots.... 100% uninterable normal cast time root.... Useless, unless you did not go natures grasp (aka stupid)



Improved Moonfire, looks good on the surface, would probably want to run some numbers on it to see how much damage increased



Nature's Reach, 20% increase distance on all offensive balance spells.... 30yd*1.20 = 36yds, 6yrd increase for 2 points... 6yrds is covered in <1s, IMO useless.



Swiftshaping, max 1.5s reduction on spell avail going into druid form.

From the limited testing I have done with this, 3 points is useless, 1 point in it means you have to wait a moment to cast, 2 points means you dont have to wait, 3 points... see 2 points.

IMO good skill, but needs reworking, Level 1 = half wait , Level 2 = no wait.



Improved Starfire, 15% chance to stun for 3s.... 1.5 chance out of 10 casts for 5 points? useless imo.



Fairie Fire Bear/Cat, cast faire fire from cat or bear form.... 

This needs to be made 1 point and cover ALL forms, cat/bear/travel imo, would never spend 2 points here, doubtful would spend 1 point even if it worked everywhere.



Improved Thorns, 25% chance to double thorns(18dmg = 32dmg).... 5 points = useless imo

Basically... 5 points to deal 18damage back to attacker in 5 melee hits. very useless.



Moonglow, 10% mana reduct for (Moon/Stare)fire for 5points

Starfire 06 = (315m - 31.5m) = 283.5m = 485dmg/283.5m ==== 1.71 D:M (vs 1.54 D:M)

Moonfire 10 = (375m - 37.5m) = 337.5m = 589dmg/337.5m ==== 1.75 D:M (vs 1.57 D:M)

Thats a large jump in Damage to Mana ratio imo, BASICALLY 10 casts for the cost of 9. 



Omen of Clarity, Hit chance buff for 1 mana free spell cast.

I have not tested this skill yet, perhaps I will, Do not have the numbers for proc chance so depending on the proc chance this could be good.



Moonfury, 10% dmg increase on (Moon/Star)fire for 5pts. This Looks good to me

StarFire 06 = 533.5 AD 4.5s 315m = 1.69 D:M = 118.56 DPS (vs 107.78 DPS)

Moonfire 10 = 647.9 AD 1.0s 375m = 1.72 D:M = 049.83 DPS (vs 045.31 DPS)

(Moonfire w/out dot = 225.5 AD 1.0s 375m = 0.60 D:M = 225.5 DPS)



Moonfury+Moonglow = 20% DPS increase , 20% D:M increase for 10pts

StarfireMM 06 = 533.5 AD 4.5s 283.5m = 1.88 D:M = 118.56 DPS

MoonfireMM 10 = 647.9 AD 1.0s 337.5m = 1.92 D:M = 049.83 DPS



Weapon Balance, 5pts 10% melee dmg increase

Good Skill IMO, dunno if worth 5pts and you do need to go far down into the line and also get omen.



Vengeance, 5pts 100% crit dmg increase (not chance)

This looks good, could get some serious crits, however I dont know formula for crits so no numbers sorry.



Nature's Grace, Crits of Nukes give you -1s cast on Regroth/Healing touch

Kinda useless, way too far down in tree, I dont often nuke then heal , dunno not for my playstyle atleast.



Hurricane, 54.5 A:D 1.0s 665m + 20%atkslow 

54.5*10 = 545dmg to multiple targets, IMO kinda useless, more efficient to cast 3-5 MoonfireMM's (due to fact you will get mana regen as well).

More then 5 monsters then yes this is prolly good.
