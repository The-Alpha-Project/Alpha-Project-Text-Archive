 17. Re: Other warlocks soloing near-level elites? | 10/19/2004 10:19:46 PM GMTDT
For me it is higely dependent upon the type of elite it is and how many other monsters are in the area. Generally when I solo an elite that is even green to me and I have lots of room then i will certainly fear and seduce if possible.

As you can tell i most often use the succubus. If I can use her seduction then I will make a point of not casting any dots that will keep me from mezzing when I need to. I use life drain and the wand. It takes a little while, but it is fairly safe. On the other side of that, if I can not mez and the area is too close to fear kite, then the only thing left to do is to keep corruption on it and try to manage your agro as best you can.

The primary goal will be to keep your pet alive long enough so that you can steal agro near the end and survive. I use weakness everytime, along with corruption. When I want to take agro, or I am sure I wont take agro I use immolate. I then tap and drain as needed and use any shadow trance that pops up. Be aware that the use of shadow trance in conjunction with drain life and life tap will draw a great deal of agro, so be ready.

If you take agro too early you will probably have trouble finishing out. The elites hit too hard and have too many hit points to drain tank effectively.

Letting the succubus build her agro is key if you want to use her without fear. Hopefully this is helpful. Try doing "The Star, the Hand and the Heart" quest solo. All three of the monsters you need are elite, but they will all be good practice and they should all be soloable by a 50 warlock.

 20. Re: Other warlocks soloing near-level elites? | 10/20/2004 3:09:59 PM GMTDT
I have been able to solo elites near my level from any level on. Before I got the voidwalker, I needed to use fear. After the voidwalker, it was all aggro management.

As others have said, the time it takes to solo an elite near, at, or slightly higher than your level is not worth the xp/time. You cannot outlast an elite's melee damage without health funnel and often using life drain. If you manually taunt the voidwalker at every opportunity so the shortest possible delay between taunts, you can keep the elite focused on the voidwalker almost the entire fight. (This is before drain-tanking.) Aggro management is still key and generally crits are not your friend in these cases.

At level 10 I solo'd Hogger (elite 11), at level 16 I killed several elite raptors outside the wailing caverns, the whole pyrewood village was elite and an issue though I did it variously at levels 15-25 (Warlock), but, like any other elite zone it's much better to go with a group. At 38 I recall killing the elite ogres in Alterac. Mostly one at a time, if I got an add and had to fear, I couldn't handle more than 3 (using banish to buy 30 seconds) 2 was the limit and that relied on fear. I did this all the way up to level 55 or so. It's not easy and it's not xp/time productive. The loot drops stink for the most part unless you're deep within an instance fighting elite groups.

I also don't exclusively rely on the voidwalker for elite tanking. The succubus can do an admirable job when fear kiting is the order of the day, but, if you need to keep the elite off you, stick to the voidwalker and his taunts. That is until we see/have new pets which may be able to do the job as well or better.

Spellcasting elites are so-so worth the xp/time. If you drain their mana and manage the aggro so they stay on your pet. As far as a tip goes, don't open with all your DoTs against an elite. The fight will go more than long enough, let the voidwalker get in at least 2 taunts before DoT'ing the target and remember, minimum delay between taunts. Do this and judge your aggro carefully and you can life drain/health funnel the voidwalker enough to beat the elite. Also, don't worry about the voidwalker's hp until he's below 1/3. At half hp he's still in good shape as a tank, this way you're not accumulating aggro for health funnel and life drain too soon. It's an interesting race to see if you can get the elite's hp to zero before the elite gets your pet's hp to zero because if the elite wins, often you lose.
