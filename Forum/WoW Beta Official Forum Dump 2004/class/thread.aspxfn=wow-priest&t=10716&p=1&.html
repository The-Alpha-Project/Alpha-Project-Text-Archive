 7. Re: UD Shackle vs. Mind control and Fear | 10/24/2004 8:07:59 PM GMTDT
Q u o t e:
From a previous post by a young priest, Iallik, disecerning the invaluable benefits offered by the Alliance versus the Undead. The young lad had the audacity to question the inherent advantages Undead speak so fondly in regards to our alliance priests.

So my question to the rest of the community is which is better and which would you rather choose:

UD shackle or Mind control and AoE Fear?


From a PvP perspective shackle is far superior than MC and psychic scream:

1.) Proximity to target: Shackle is 30 yards, mind control is 20, Psycic scream you need to be right on top of the target essentially.

2.) # of targets: Shackle is 1, MC is 1, Psychic scream is 5. I've been reading of people complaining that psychic scream hits an unlimited amount of targets which I don't believe to be true through my experience. I've never seen more than 5 players run off when I've used this when surrounded by alliance.

3.) Cast times/recycles: Shackle is 1.5 and can be cast again immediately, can be useful for switching shackle targets or to quickly re-shackle the current target when 50 seconds is about to expire. Mind control is 3 sec, can be cast again as well on a different target when it wears or if you dispel it. Psychic scream is instant cast with a 30 sec recycle

3.) Duration: Shackle is fixed duration of 50 seconds. Psychic scream is 8 sec. Mind control can last up to a minute but has a chance to break every 5 seconds. If untouched, a shackled player will sit there for 50 seconds guaranteed. Psychic scream will keep 5 feared for 8 seconds. I've read about people complaining about AOE fear being an unlimited amount of targets which I don't believe to be true. I never recall seeing more than 5 players running off when I get surrounded. MC duration is totally random. Shackle will break on damage, psychic scream has the chance to break on damage as well.

4.) Resist rates: Mind control and psychic scream are highly resistable whereas resisting shackle is almost non-existant. It is very easy to achieve sufficient levels of shadow resist through buffs and items to almost negate MC/psychic scream. I am not sure what tree shackle uses for resist, but the only time I recall ever resisting shackle was when lower level priests tried to use it on me.

5.) Mana costs: Shackle is 150. Mind control 750 mana. Psychic scream is 210 mana.

From PvPing on the beta server in my opinion shackle undead is far superior. Shackle range is greater, faster casting, lower mana cost, greater benefit and minimum risk involved. You can quickly get into that 30 yard range and fire off a quick shackle. Someone trying to use MC has to get closer to the enemy and has to deal with a 2x slower cast time and higher resist rates. Chances are you will be ganked before this even finishes casting due to its range. Even if you do get the target MCed, you will still get pasted because MC makes you vulnerable as you can't cast spells. Besides, tell me how useful MC is in PvP when it seems to last ~10 seconds and you don't get access to any special abilities/spells of the player like you do when you MC NPCs, and melee attacks are reduced. Psychic scream for the most part is more defensive. Rarely you will run headlong into a crowd to disperse them. Normally I save this for being outnumbered and being attacked by multiple targets. In PvP, alliance priests have more options available to them than an UD priest.

For me being undead has more downsides than it does benefits even with the racial traits undead currently have. Like many other undead priests in beta, come retail if I decide to go with a priest it will be definately be troll.
