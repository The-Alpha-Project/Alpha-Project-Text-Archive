 4. Re: Talent Changes | 10/12/2004 2:02:39 AM GMTDT
All patched and in game, there are several changes.

Fire:
Improved Fireball - Same as before
Impact - Same as before

Ignite - Now requires 5 pts, no Critical Mass prereq
Improved Fire Blast - Same as before
Flame throwing - Now requires 5 pts, no longer chains off of Improved Fireball

New: Incinerate (2/2) - Gives your Scorch and Fire blast spells a 2% (then 4%) extra chance to crit
Pyroblast - Now requires 10 pts
Burning Soul - Unchanged
Improved Flamestrike - Unchanged

Improved Scorch - Now requires 15 pts
Improved Fire Ward - Now requires 15 pts, no longer a prereq for anything

Blast Wave - Unchanged
Critical Mass - Now requires 20 pts, now a 3/3 talent scaling 2 4 6% chance to crit per point, also, a prereq for fire power.

Fire Power - Unchanged, save that Critical Mass is now its prereq, not Improved Fire Ward
Combustion - Unchanged

---

Frost:
Improved Frost Bolt - Unchanged
Permafrost - Unchanged

Ice Shards - Unchanged
Winter's Chill - Unchanged
Improved Frost Nova - Unchanged, still the useless 1s reduction per point and still a prereq to shatter

Piercing Ice - Now 3/3, going 2% 4% 6% more damage per point
Cold Snap - Now needs 10 pts, prereq for frost channeling
Improved Blizzard - Unchanged

Arctic Reach - Same as before, requires 15 pts
Frost Channeling - Same as before, requires 15 pts and prereq'd by Cold Snap
Shatter - requires 15 pts, still prereq'd by Improved Frost Nova, but now goes 10% increased chance to crit per point (i think it was 8% before?)
Improved Frost Ward - Unchanged

Ice Block - Unchanged, requires 20 pts
Improved Cone of Cold - Unchanged, requires 20 pts

Frostbite - Unchanged

Ice Barrier - Unchanged

---

Arcane:

Arcane Sub - Unchanged
Arcane Focus - Unchanged
Imp Arc Missiles - Unchanged

Wand Spec - Unchanged
Arcane Conc - Unchanged

Improved Dampen Magic - Unchanged
Improved Arcane Explosion - Now 5/5, 0.3s less casting time per point, no longer a prereq to Prescence of Mind
Evocation - Now requires 10 pts, not 15 <-- BIG chang imo, still 1500% regen over 8s

Improved Mana Shield - Unchanged
Improved Counterspell - NEW: 2/2 gives you counterspell a chance to silence the target for 4s... 2 pts in it gives 100%
New: Arcane Meditation: 5/5 Allows, per point, 2% of your mana regeneration to work while casting so up to 10% mana regen in combat, prereq'd by Evocation

PoM - Unchanged, save that it has no prereq now and just requires 20 pts
Arcane Mind, 4/4, 2% more max mana per point up to 8%, prereq'd by Evocation

Arcane Instability - Requires 25 pts, requires prescence of mind, now a 3/3 talent that gives 1% extra damage and chance to crit per point

Arcane Power - Unchanged


---

Hope this helps and forgive any oversights I made while alt-tabbing between WoW and typing this up :)
