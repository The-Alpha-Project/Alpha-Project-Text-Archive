I have been in the process of writing up the class role descriptions, which has taken up the majority of my time with the developers. It has taken much discussion of the thought behind the design of each class to draft them up, but I should be done relatively soon.

I did manage to get one question answered for you, however:

Will a camo or lesser stealth be added to their abilities?
The Hunter is a high-damage ranged class. Such an ability might well bring about the issues seen with Mages when they were using Invisibility and Pyroblast in conjunction. Thus there are no plans for this at this time.
