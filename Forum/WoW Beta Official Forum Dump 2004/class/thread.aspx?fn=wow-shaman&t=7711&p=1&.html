 7. Re: Shamans Without Fist Weapons? | 10/19/2004 6:06:24 PM GMTDT
1) Shaman can use Fist Weapons. You train the skill in the Valley of Honor from a roaming NPC (at least as of last patch).

2) Fist weapons use the "Unarmed" weapon skill, and that is all that shows up in your character skill screen, even after you train Fist Weapons. Ergo, you may be able to use a fist weapon right now! I'd personally like to see Blizz make this more obvious.

3) There are a few fist weapons in the game now, and I'm sure that # will increase as beta progesses or at retail. Bloody Brass Knuckles are from SM, I think, and there are 2 Fist drops in BRD, Rockfist (I have this one myself) and Bloodfist (IIRC, name might not be exactly that). Both of the BRD drops are from Phalanx.
