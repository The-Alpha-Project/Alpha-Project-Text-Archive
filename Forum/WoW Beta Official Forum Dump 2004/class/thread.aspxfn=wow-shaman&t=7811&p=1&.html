 3. Re: A few questions to some of you pvp shaman. | 10/20/2004 11:35:07 PM GMTDT
Always always always always open with purge. It’s instant cast, costs 75 mana, and removes TWO buffs. Against a priest, you can take away over 1000 points worth of damage before the fight even starts. Power Word: Shield + Power Word: Fortitude both drop, and OUCH. Against a mage, you lower their mana pool by 3 or 4 hundred points and you remove their pesky ice armor (which you need to remove so you don’t get snared by the ice effect, and also, lower armor means less damage absorption, which means, mage dead faster. You get the idea. I even cast purge on warriors and rogues, just on the off chance that they’ve got a friendly buff or two and or potion effects. You can also purge mana shield, which is a must.

Basically, you want to melee anything that’s softer than you, and you want to kite anything that can out melee you. But, it’s not really that straight forward. You want to be able to move in and out of combat as the fight progresses. When I fight mages, I try to ‘feint’ them, move in close then back up right away in hopes that they’ll fire off their frost nova and I’ll be outside the radius (that spell has a 25 second cooldown). Same thing with psychic scream on the priest. If I’m kiting a warrior or rogue, and they’re close to death, I’ll pop up lightning shield, run in and melee.

Hmm. Lots and lots of different strategies and tricks that you’ll pick up as you go. Also, try fighting on top of water when you have the chance. Pure pwnage. If you see a priest coming at you on top of the water, just purge him and he’ll drop like a rock, giving you the advantage again

I don’t think there’s really one class that just destroys us or vice versa. With a lot of the fights, it has more to do with who gets the initiative and starts unloading spells or melee hits before the other notices or responds. All things being equal, we have a reasonable chance against any class. Personally, I’ve had the most success against mages and priests, and the least success with warriors and rogues. I’m not that great of a kiter I guess.

There’s no real way to counter the totem killing macro, and to be honest, I’ve never seen anyone really focus on totems so much that it became a concern. The only totem that really matters if it gets killed is earthbind. If that keeps happening, just kite with frost shock instead.
