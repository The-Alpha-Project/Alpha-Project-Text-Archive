 7. Re: Attack Power or Agility? | 10/15/2004 8:33:33 AM GMTDT
The way I see the priority is like this:
1) stamina
2/3) agil/spirit (depending on which you prefer)
4) int
5) str

Frankly a hunter shouldn't be using anything with +str unless it has other stats (see above). Agility effects five things on a hunter which is why I generally give it higher priority myself. Those five things are: dodge percent, melee damage, range damage (albeit not that much), crit percent and armor. Strength should be the least and if possible not even used. The reason is since hunters melee is 50/50, str/agil if you have an item that gives +20 agil alone, compared to one that is +10 agil/+10 str you would end up with the same melee Attack Power, so why not use the +20 agil and get all the other benefits as well. Mana pool isn't really a problem either, so no real need to make it larger, just make it regen faster. So personally I go for +atam/+agil, and +stam/+spirit items.

 8. Re: Attack Power or Agility? | 10/15/2004 5:56:19 PM GMTDT
As far as STR affecting DPS: of course it will increase your DPS stat in your character sheet. However, the weapon information there is based on your melee weapon and not your ranged. As everyone knows, Hunters do sub-par damage in melee and really only shine in ranged combat.

Now, if you're calculating your DPS with Cosmos or something else, that's entirely different and I haven't experimented with it myself to see how the numbers match up.

Personally, I'm fond of:
1. Agility (for increase in armor and ranged damage)
2. Spirit (for increased health and mana regen)
3-5. Depends on your playstyle and server (PVP or PVE

On a PVE server, I'd probably go for INT since 80% of the time it's not all that difficult to keep from being hit, as long as aggro is controlled.

On a PVP server, I'd go for STA since a few HP is what you might need to pin down an enemy's ambush, get in to range, and do some Van Dammage.

As far as STR: do you like to melee? I dont. =P

Of course, this is just my opinion, I could be wrong.
