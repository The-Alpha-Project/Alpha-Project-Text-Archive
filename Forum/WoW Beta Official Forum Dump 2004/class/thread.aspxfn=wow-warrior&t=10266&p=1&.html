 1. Why I won’t play warrior on release. | 10/15/2004 8:24:43 AM GMTDT
This only relates to pvp servers, but some issues may also be concerned to the beta servers.

I Love playing as a warrior in most games, they have the strength and the endurance to go against most monsters in the game. But the only thing they have problem with, is taken down any actual real person. Whether it is their skills, the attributes, or that they have no chance against any other class. Whatever it is I’m sure I’m not the only person who is having difficulties going against other class players.

Let me first state that I have a 50 level warrior that I started first when I came in the comic contest push. When I first played my warrior I found it really enjoyable at first. I even had fun pvping against other players for quite some time. But of course anything can change in beta; we all know that. I’m also aware anything below is subject to change.

I guess the biggest complaint, that I have, are the warrior’s skills. I’m probably the few that went the route the route of the fury talents points. So I rely mostly on getting critical hits and being hit by critical hits. I guess I just like the speed of the fury talents. So I don’t have Mortal Strike, Improved Overpower, or Improved Hamstring. But I found myself that it’s impossible to actually kill anyone using the fury skills. It’s like warriors are discouraged to using any other talent tree other than Armory Mastery, if they want to pvp. I haven’t actually tried using the Defensive talent tree, but I assume it would not compare to Armory Mastery in pvp.

Another problem is the charge, yes a lot of complain about it but I find that it’s impossible for me to do anything without it. The patch that had charge activated even during combat was way ridicules, even when I played pvp I found myself killing mages 8 levels ahead of me. This was not fun and was not challenging enough. Now charge is deactivated even if hit by an instant cast, which totally flipped the difficulty level of combat. Now if you want to charge you basically have to sneak your way around, and you have to be sure to make the first hit otherwise your most likely to lose.

Hamstring has also seems to lost most of it’s value, lowering the effectiveness of slow. This might not be a problem for warriors who went Armory Mastery, but it’s for people like me that didn’t go that talent tree. I found myself never able to actually finish a player because they were always able to get away. If I try to get a rogue, he uses vanish and takes off with sprint. Priest able to evade me easily with psychic scream, and the ability to use absorption spell. Mage can use mana shield, and blink at of my melee range, or even use sheep, which lasts way to long. Paladins have a huge stun, and can cast divine shield and run off again. I can’t mention any other classes since I don’t accouter them as much as the others. But the fact is hamstring is the only thing that keeps any other class in battle. If this is changed too much, it’s near impossible to ever actually ever finish off another person.

Warriors also lack the ability to run away from a battle. We either use hamstring, or our timer Intimidating shout. Which both are very unaffected against most ranged units. Rogues can keep you in battle by using combinations of stuns, slow poisons, and even sprint and catch up to you. Mages can polymorph and either waits for reinforcements, or use either frost nova or a slow spell making it more difficult to escape. Priests have DOT that they can cast while running, making it impossible to ever run away from them, since they can continue to cast this on a warrior until they are dead. Warriors aren’t left much, if they wanted to flee from a battle; it’s as if though they were meant to stay in a battle until death even matched with impossible enemy.

One problem I’m not sure is true or not, is the fact that the warriors HP are to low compared to other classes. From what I heard rogues had/have more HP than warriors at the same level with out any items on. So if this is true, what is the main attribute of a warrior? I’m sure warriors aren’t the dps; although some may choose to if they were to get most items that were strength only. So if a warrior hp is not the highest compared to other classes, it makes them even more worthless.

The last thing I have to say is people’s idea of the whole paper, rock, and scissors theory. I’ve never actually heard of Blizzard mentioning this, but people are convinced that this is how World of Warcraft works. If this is true then if warriors were rock what is our scissors? I know it’s not any range units, they can bring a warrior out of combat as soon as they hit a warrior with any instant cast. It’s not rogues, because I’ve gone with several duels with a rogue from my own guild that was lower level than I was, and if the rogue uses all their timers, a warrior does not stand a chance. I’ve haven’t dueled much with Paladin’s so I’m unable to comment on that. So what is warrior’s scissors?

So coming down to it I don’t think I will play as a warrior on retail if this is how a warrior is supposed to be like. They seemed to be forced to use the armory mastery if they want to stand a chance against other players. Their attributes are don’t seem to be correct. If a warrior is ever matched with any other class they don’t stand much of a chance. Because I like a challenge, but I don’t like being unable to actual compete with other players.

- Sotanax
