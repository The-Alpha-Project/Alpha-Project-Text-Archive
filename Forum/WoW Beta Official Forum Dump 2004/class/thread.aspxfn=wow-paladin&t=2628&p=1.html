 1. Suggestions to improve the Paladin. | 7/27/2004 2:39:01 PM GMTDT
OK, I have been thinking a lot lately about what I would do to improve our favorite class. I've tried very hard to keep a balanced outlook in mind, so as not to make the class "overpowered", although many believe we are currently. I feel those people are FLAT OUT WRONG, but that isn't the current topic.

First, I'm scrapping the term Seals and all of them. Mainly, I don't like the term Seal, I'll be adding the functionality of them back in below however.

OK, first step, Auras need to be buffed up slightly. I think they went too far in the nerfage when they switched Might and Wisdom to Auras.

The Undead branch is unexciting due to the little time you spend fighting undead, but, it is defining in the WoW Universe for Paladins, and should probably be left alone.

So, the replacements for Seals.

First, while it can be useful on a rogue or with a fast one hander, Reckoning, IMO just isn't a Paladin like ability. Second, I would like to see two differty styles here, short-term, maybe an Inspiration, and longer term, perhaps call it a Blessing. So, the two thread management seals would become blessings and last 5 minutes each. I'll conceed some to balance and make the cooldown on those be 5 minutes as well.

Then as Inspirations, there would be one for a warrior/tank type class that would increase Armor and Damage for 30 seconds, BUT, it would have a hefty cooldown, and be OTHER only. The main purpose is for facing the Named mobs in the game. There would be a similar one for the casting classes that would increase spell damage and raise their casting ability to overcome some resists, althought, those don't seem to be a huge problem in WoW. Maybe it would reduce interuptions while casting. Same deal as the warrior's Inspiration, 30 seconds, long cool down (5-10 minutes), and other only. The former Seal of Protection would be put in as an Inspiration as well, and have the same functionality it currently does.

After those changes, I want to add something to the Paladin on the solo/offensive side. I have a few ideas.

The Zeal idea that others have purposed I like, but I don't think it is the only solution. I would like one more melee ability that will interupt casters, it can or cannot do damage, I'm fairly indifferent. If Crusader strike was given this ability, then it would have to have a cooldown, and I don't want that currently. A second idea I had with my Uther's Strength went off for me the other night. I have had situations that I should have died, but survived because Uther's went off when I had 98 HP left. Random? Sure, but it was a situation where I got jumped, thought I might make it through the 3 or 4 mobs, but after it was too late to run, knew I wouldn't survive. So, I just sat there waiting to die and then shawong, the ring activates and I walk out on the other side. So, maybe a skill called "Divine Hope" or something similarily sappy, hell, even "Last Hope" (note the guild tag). Basically, you have to be below a certain threshold of available HP and Mana to cast it, if you go above in either catageory, the shield ends. It doesn't have to be automatic either, maybe make it a 25% change Uthor's Strenght effect. Take the Hard-to-Kill aspect of the Paladin and make it so it applies to finishing the fight and not running away from it.

That is all for now I guess, fell free to rip it apart.
