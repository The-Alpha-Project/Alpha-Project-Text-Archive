 35. Re: Charge bug. | 10/15/2004 12:14:40 PM GMTDT
Don't listen to any of this nonsense about weapon delay being calculated before the hit takes place on the first hit.

-Easy explanation of the charge bug:

Nothing at all to do with charge, it's actually the fact that previously, if you pressed attack while out of range, it wouldn't try to swing until you were in range. (Meaning when your charge hits, so does a normal attack.) Now, if you attack from out of range, the delay on your weapon will trigger and you will have to wait around for the next swing. Clearly it's most noticable on charge because it activates from out of melee range and turns your combat autoattack on (so your first swing misses from being out of range), but it is only the rage from the initial auto-swing that is missing and nothing from the ability itself.

If you are in position, in range, and not lagging too much to cause the server to still think you are out of range, your first attack will hit immediately.

(Sorry if this has been said, I don't really read the warrior forums...I actually figured this out on my shaman. Very easy to notice what's going on with a 2h weapon.)
