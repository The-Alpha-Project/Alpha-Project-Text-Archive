 1. Petition to increase rogue damage - pls read | 9/30/2004 12:12:19 AM GMTDT
Okay. I feel that rogues only need a little bit of help here in this department. Nothing too major, and I think the below suggestions would be acceptable. Again this would need to be actually TESTED, which is what we are here for. After all, they were/are 'testing' this stealth change, so I think it's only fair that Blizzard gives us the chance to test out a damage increase in rogues, because I feel we are lacking in that area when compared to warriors and mages.
Please try to be constructive here if you don't agree with something, but provide a better alternative that does something similiar and I'd love ot hear it.

SO here it goes:

1) Increase eviscerate damage by 20%
2) Increase garrote/rupture damage by 30%
3) Increase backstab damage by 40%
4) Increase ambush damage by 50-60%

Why?

1) Eviscerate never does the damage listed. An eviscerate of rank 5 does:
1 point : 69-99 damage
2 points: 123-153 damage
3 points: 177-207 damage
4 points: 231-261 damage
5 points: 285-315 damage

However I've never seen it do above 250 damage on a mob -5 levels or higher unless it crits. I think it generally does 180-200 or so, every so often it goes above 200.

2) Garrote/rupture isn't used as much b/c they take time and some people don't like DOTS. Increasing this damage would help give people a reason to use it.

3) Backstab doesn't do much more damage then a sinister strike hit in a lot of cases. This would give those rogues a choice between using a dagger and backstabbing and a rogue who wants to spam SS with a sword. This is even with the backstab talents, that have to rely upon a crit in order to outdamage a sinister strike attack.

4) Same as above. Ambush needs to do more damage. Most people will use garrote or cheap shot rather then backstab/ambush. Why have these in here when they are being unused?

My original idea was to have backstab/ambush ignore 40/60% of the ac respectively, but that would require a lot of coding on Blizzard's part. All of these changes would take little less then a day to complete and implement and I feel it would put us in line with mage damage.

I want to remind people that Mages do not have to go out and spend tons of time/money in order to get a weapon to do the damage they do. Rogues have to spend hours camping an item or TONS of money to get a good dagger/sword in order to do damage. We are VERY much more dependant on equipment then a mage.

One more note about mages: they have all this great damage at a range. We have to do this damage in melee range, and some of it while trying to be in stealth and sneak up. A mage does not require that. I'm not trying to make us equal to mages in all aspects, but b/c of our disadvantages it would be nice to have some advantages and the above I think would satisfy that.

Warriors currently can outdamage a rogue if they go fury spec, and generally will do equal amounts of damage as a rogue as it stands right now. A warrior should not come close to a rogue in damage. Increasing the above would put us over warrior damage. I'm not crying for a nerf, more of a boost in rogue damage here.

Again, maybe these changes are little much, I really don't know, but this is something I would like to see TESTED in beta and hopefully soon.

Please /sign this if you agree, or perhaps make a suggestion that you feel would help increase rogue damage a decent amount to put us in line with mages and to be the "DPS" class that we should be.

Thank you for reading and hope that this will get to the Blizzard developers.
