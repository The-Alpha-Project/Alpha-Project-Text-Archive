Shamans want Bloodlust and that is obvious. However we are currently in a fairly well balanced state right now.

So Shamans are defined by Bloodlust... so perhaps lets put some ideas together that would not unbalance shamans but would still give Bloodlust a place in our arsenal?

My thoughts were that Bloodlust should be a buff similar to Inner Fire that priests get. A 2 minute long buff with perhaps a 45 second recast. A fairly good mana cost to it though not mana-bar breaking.

I think of bloodlust as effecting melee attacks more than anything else. It can't be a copy of other buffs and such though since that would likely cause an outcry. When I think of bloodlust... I think of a nice strength buff with an increase in the critical hit rate of melee attacks. Perhaps also an increase in the critical hit damage. Say it gives the target a +20% chance to crit on melee attacks and a +20% damage to crits. If it needs to have a negative effect perthaps instead of lowering defense it would lower the likliness to evade/block/parry.

Here's my idea for bloodlust:

Rank 1
Level 30
Increases attack speed of target by 15%, adds +20 raw damage to all attacks, increases physical size for 30 seconds.

Rank 2
Level 40
Increases attack speed by 20%, adds +30 raw damage to all attacks, increases physical size for 30 seconds

Rank 3
Level 50
Increases attack speed by 25%, adds +45 raw damage to all attacks, increases physical size for 30 seconds

Rank 4
Level 60
Increases attack speed by 30%, adds +60 raw damage to all attacks, increases physical size for 30 seconds


Now, I know my numbers might be off a bit but that would be how I would structure the spell. The actual numbers would need to be tweaked for balance.

Then, maybe a talent could be added that each rank adds another 1% haste, so that at rank 5 you get an extra 5% haste to all ranks of the spell.
