That counters a lot Roddy. Shaman are not really powerful as far as DPS goes but they have a high survivability rate and are really skilled at outlasting classes more then overpowering them.

Shaman 1 on 1 are not that scary but any good shaman hunts with a team as they seriously enhance a team to the point of making them a real pain in the butt to PvP against.

I was in a higher level group PvPing the other night and I wasn't doing much DPS with my shaman but with a group I was responsible for runners.

- 1 second wolf form (40% speed increase)

- Catch up to target instant change to caster form and instant drop earthbind.

- Instant purge on caster types and keep earthbind up so the rest of the group could catch up and finish off the target.

But back to the original question. The best thing to do against a shaman is really to keep him as close as possible and stunned as long as possible and do your best not to let him wear you down. The longer the fight the more likely it is that the shaman will come out on top. Of course you try to do that with all your targets but with shaman it really is very important to get that quick kill.
