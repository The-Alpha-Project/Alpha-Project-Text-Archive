 3. Re: Tank warrior (worth making?) | 10/24/2004 7:02:08 PM GMTDT
It's kind of pointless to say right now because *hopefully* the big changes to warriors that are coming will fix some of the current issues, although I'm not holding out much hope.

Right now there's just not enough benefit from being a Protection specced warrior to make it worthwhile. The best bet for a balanced warrior is going to be to put the minimum amount of points into Arms to get Mortal Strike, then put the rest in the protection pool to get improved taunt, increased defense and armor.

Hopefully this will change going forward, however you also need to consider what you will be doing with your group. Are you going to focus mainly on questing and outdoor fighting and just do instances once or twice to complete your quests? If that's the case then there's no need for the protection build at all, an arms warrior can tank well enough outside of an instance, especially with a Paladin in the group.

Given your group makeup, unless you pick up a full-time mage or rogue, you're going to want some additional DPS, and you can fill that role pretty well yourself if you want to.

The best thing for you to do will be to revisit the issue once some more tweaks have been done to the class, and also to evaluate what type of content you expect to be spending the most time on.

 6. Re: Tank warrior (worth making?) | 10/25/2004 6:15:09 PM GMTDT
If you are playing on the beta style server I would go with a protection/fury build (I'm going that way in pvp server actually).

Put 20 points into the typical protection talents and then get concussion blow and improved shield block. That puts you at 23 protection.

Put 20 points into the typical Fury stuff (skip enrage) and then pick up improved slam, improved cleave and deathwish.

The fury and protection combo will make aggro generation trivial compared to the arms build, concussion blow and imp shield block give you an edge and imp shield bash for 2 points lets you shutdown casters. In fact in pvp you can shut a caster down for 17 seconds using this talent, conc blow and IS.

If the paladin or druid wants to tank assuming they are balanced, you can go in fury mode and you will do more DPS over time than MS with proper use of imp slam and cleave. Make sure you hit death wish every time its up and berserker rage every time you get aggro.

That leaves you with 3 points to put wherever you want, Flurry for more offense, probably protection based for more defense.

That build is alot better than the MS build for the all around warrior on the beta server. On pvp its debatable.
