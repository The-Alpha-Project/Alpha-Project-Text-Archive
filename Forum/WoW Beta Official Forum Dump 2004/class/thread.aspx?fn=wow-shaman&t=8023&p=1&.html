So the devs read these posts apparently. I am wondering if the alliance has successfully whined enough to have WW nerfed yet.

Does it give us an advantage in PVP? Sure it does. Does it mean we win every single battle? No it does not. Perhaps when we have WW dispelled it cannot be re-cast until x amount of time after it has been dispelled? What if paladins had an aura or buff that allowed normal movement speed underwater so that alliance could mount an underwater attack? I'd really like to hear that something other than just taking the spell out of the game is on the devs table right now and I'm sure it is. But I just really want to be reassured...

There are three specific spells I thought were amazing with my Shaman; Earthbind, Water Walking and the damage shield.

Earthbind is nearly useless in PvP now, from my experience. Sure, sometimes an idiot will stand on the totem and actually get bound, but casting the totem never determines the battle for me anymore. The radius is far too small, and the Earthbound individual can move out of the totems range far to quickly, losing a step or two in the process.

Water walking has only ever helped me escape a mob of alliance. The original thread complaining about Water Walking was amazing (in a bad way). The original poster complained about Horde players in Booty Bay, who could attack from the water, and the guards couldn’t get to the players; though, they fail to mention the Alliance players who gank you on the dock, and just jump in the water to get away from the guards. The problem isn’t Water Walking, it’s the fact that the guards can’t enter the water to punish the hooligan(s). One Alliance person, forget his name, commented how Water Walking just wasn’t realistic. Um, hello; and fireballs, and lighting bolts coming from someone’s fingers are?

The last amazing trait of the Shaman is the damage shield. This spell, as far as I’m concerned, defines my abilities. Go ahead, hit me; at level 37, my damage shield does 92 points in return damage. I have read many posts from players regarding this issue, including high level Shaman, who say the spell damage should be reduced- Why? What’s the point of playing a shaman then, if you just going to give us druid thorns. Woo, we can wear chainmail. Druids have more hit points and better AC in bear form, by nearly 50 percent! This isn’t a knock on Druids, I just don’t understand what people want Shaman to be if they take our abilities away. Hell Paladins don’t even have talents yet. I can see the flames already for their class when they get tons of uber abilities.


Sadly, I fear all the Shamans great and unique talents will be watered down.
