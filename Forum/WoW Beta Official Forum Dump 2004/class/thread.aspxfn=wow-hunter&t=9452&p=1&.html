 17. Re: Melee Hunter? | 10/19/2004 2:16:31 AM GMTDT
/agree Sengar.

In 60 levels of play I have never had to wait until my pet had done x% of a target's health in damage before joining in. The key to holding agro is to ***MANUALLY*** use a pet's growl and bite/claw attacks. I think a lot of young hunters don't realize this, and early on in the game this can make a huge difference in combat.

I personally recommend assigning pet-attack to the - key on the num pad, pet-growl to the + key on the num pad, and pet-claw to the * key. This makes it easy to use those abilities seperately from your standard hunter abils, and is much more comfortable on the wrists. I hate having to use cntrl-something regularly.

Also, on a side-note, it seems like large slow hits draw more agro than fast small hits; since acquiring a Hurricane, agro has been even easier to maintain.

Oh, as for 'Melee Hunter': if you're dying to melee, just roll a warrior and buy a non-combat pet; easier, and more group invites.
