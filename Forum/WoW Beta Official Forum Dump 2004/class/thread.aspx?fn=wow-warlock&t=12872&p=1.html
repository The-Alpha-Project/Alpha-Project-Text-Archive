Maybe it could be converted into a channeling spell or something so the talent that prevents interrupt of drain spells would work with this too.

However! There are some spells that the devs nerfed that absolutely suck now (or the spells aren't very good to begin with), when I used to be able to use them.

1)Banish. Wow... I banished a demon and it lasted for 20 seconds, then it just aggro'd me like mad. This spell definitely needs to be handled better. Either make the banish last 2 minutes (It's not like you can banish players anymore with it) to make it useful in situations, or make it affect anything and bump it up to 30 seconds. Right now it is way weak.

2)Firestone. I don't see anybody using this, unless you want to make firestones and sell them to lvl 10 characters for like... 1s.

3)Mana Stone. Good idea, but yes, make it like the Healthstone, and suddenly you have an awesome item to make.

4)Soulstones. Meh, there's a whole thread about this one.

5)Searing Pain. Not enough damage done. This used to be my "cast it fast to kill fleeing monsters" but with that instacast Talent now, it's moot.

6)Enslave Demon. I'm very disappointed here. For 300 mana you get a CHANCE to enslave a demon for UP TO 1 min. Which, in english, means you have to cast it 3 times, costing 900 mana, only to grab a demon long enough to attack another demon and have both of them turn on you and kill you in 20 sec. This spell needs serious re-working. I say you make it a permanent grab (until you log out). It's not THAT powerful to have your pet and a demon as well; and when you think about it how many zones are there that have demons in the first place? Very few. This should be a well deserved Warlock treat to be able to have 2 pets. It's not like the demons are uber good in the first place and are overpowered for us to have 2 pets.

7)Curse of Weakness. This spell should be beefed up. We should be saying to ourselves "Hmmm, would CoA be better on this melee mob, or CoW?" Right now, there's no contest. I think it takes off 10 damage at lvl 30 right now, and that's definitely poor.
