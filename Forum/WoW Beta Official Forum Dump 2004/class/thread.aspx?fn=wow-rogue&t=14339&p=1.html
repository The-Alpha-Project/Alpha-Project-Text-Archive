1. DPS. No one's been angry at me for not being specced for improved sap, however I usually ask beforehand if there's another rogue in the group. Even so, apparently some groups get pissed off if you try sapping because it's "unreliable". I've had no troubles though. I don't have problems getting into group/instance runs. In fact, I've been asked to join a group more times than I've asked them.

2. Very well, especially if you can find a spot where all the mobs are yours. In most talent builds it is not difficult to control your pulls, and if you botch one you can always vanish to get rid of the aggro. Only mobs that usually eat me up are casters, and considering a rogue's healthy variety of stuns, they don't usually get more than one cast off on me.

3. Nope, but this is from the beta server. If they're a couple levels higher, equal level, or lower, I'll take them on. Only exception would probably be a higher-level warrior, since mortal strike hurts badly. I've been doing fine; I'm satisfied with my role in large-scale and small-scale pvp battles.

4. Hell yes. If you enjoy high-damage melee classes like I do (used to play a monk and rogue in EQ), rogues in WoW are awesome. I wouldn't think of playing any other class.

Hope that helped.
