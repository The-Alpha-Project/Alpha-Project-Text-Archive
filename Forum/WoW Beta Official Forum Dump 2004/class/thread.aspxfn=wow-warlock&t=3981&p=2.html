Quickly throwing a list together of things Id like to see since I dont have much time before im off on vacation for a week.. Here goes:

---------------------------------------------------
Spells: Channeling
1 - Drain life and Drain mana changed to being uninterruptable by regular attacks.

2 - Hellfire is pretty much a useless spell now. It is much cheaper in mana then Rain of Fire, but the negatives to this spell are too large. (requiring you to be right in the middle of the enemies, dealing large amounts of damage to the caster, being interruptable)

3 - I would like to see Drain soul either uninterruptable, or changed to a regular non channeling DoT. Due to its channeling nature I never let it run its full course and always use the lowest least manacost versionbecause it is too difficult to know wheter an enemy will die before or after it finishes.

---------------------------------------------------
Spells: Curses

1 - Curse of Agony. I absolutely never use this one, and the main simple problem is: It doesnt do its damage fast enough. Almost no regular fight lasts anywhere near 1.5 minutes, and even when one does (against a boss for instance) you are usually still better off casting a different curse. Its duration should be greatly reduced to make this curse of any use in solo and group situations.

2 - Cure of Recklessness. I purely use this to prevent enemies from running away, never for the statistical effects it has. If it didnt have the attack power increase but only the armor decrease, I might use it to help melee damage output from my pet when soloing.

3 - New Curse suggestion: I would like to see a curse that works as a DoT enhancer. It could reduce the duration of any DoT while keeping their damage the same. That would make for an effective and much used curse. If this were stackable by different warlocks, it could even make DoTs in groups viable.

---------------------------------------------------
Spells: DoTs and Shadowbolt

1 - Shadowbolt. Its a fine nuke, but it casts slow. It is also nearly impossible to cast if you are being hit by a fast hitting dualwielding enemy, but I suppose that is how it works. (Id love to see a distinction between slow hard hits and fast low damaging strikes when you get hit. right now a 10 damage hit from a dagger drops your casting bar just as much as a 200 damage blow from an axe.)

2 - Corruption. Too long a duration to be of much use on groups. When soloing enemies your own level and using VW, this spell can run 'most' of its course. If you use succubus or imp, enemies will drop dead before corruption is halfway through, meaning you wasted your mana. This is the main reason Id like to see a curse that reduces DoT duration.

3 - Immolate is pretty much fine as it is. Duration is low enough, and the instant damage aspect allows you to use it as a mediocre fire 'nuke' against shadow immune mobs.

---------------------------------------------------
Pets

1 - Imp damage output, especially when coupled with curse of elements, is pretty much fine. The major gripe with this guy is that he displays some erratic fleeing behaviour which is highly annoying. It often causes adds and gets you or your group killed. Using imp is a liability right now, and seeing the fleeing behaviour removed altogether would make many warlocks happy. If he needs to flee for whatever reason, at least make a max fleeing range. (No further then x yards from the master)

2 - Succubus damage output is too low currently, and she needs a melee damage increase. Lash of pain is also too weak now, it should be boosted by at least 50%. Preferably I would like it to work no matter what angle of the enemy the pet is facing, because it is too difficult to position her at the back of the enemy for every fight.

3 - Voidwalker is a great tank, but a little boring to solo with. He also needs a better way to keep aggro. My suggestion to make this a more fun pet, and since he is designed for the longer fights, is to make his damage output and attack speed slowly increase as a fight progresses. Perhaps he could even get slightly bigger as the fight takes longer, so that he really works himself up to a fast and reasonably hard hitter. But the moment you change targets for the next fight, he would be back to regular damage and attack speed.. This way, as fights last a little hile, his grip on aggro improves and it will allow the warlock to more aggresively deal damage onto the enemy without taking aggro themselves.

4 - New abilities suggestion: The ability to cast a spell 'through' your pet, which would give it a boost.
Just as an example: Imp could boost immolate.

You would cast immolate without a pet up, and hit for 50-60 direct and 70 dot damage.
Now, summon an imp and he gives you a 10% boost to immolate damage, so the same immolate will hit for 55-66 direct and 77 dot damage for as long as the imp is around. At a higher level you would be able to teach your pets improved versions of the spell boosting.

As long as theyd each boost relevant and often used spells, it would be a cool feature and not result in everyone using the same pet all the time.
Expanding on that, there could even be pet specific spells, spells you can only use if you have that pet in action. You could for example gain access to an instant cast health funnel type spell if you have voidwalker up, or gain a lifedrain proc on your weapon with succubus up.

---------------------------------------------------
Miscelleaneous:

Soul Stone: These stones are still useful, but were, for some reason, made much less useful then they used to be. I used have a stone on me at all times. They gave the warlock something unique, if you were soloing and died, you didnt have to do a corpse run. Now you spawn on top of your corpse with 5% hp/mana (which is quite often right by an enemy) and have such a severe rez sickness that you cannot even summon your pet. It takes too long to wait for your stats to recover. So long in fact, that I tend to prefer doing the corpse fun. I would like to be able to select a radius around my corpse for rezzing again, and suffer a much less severe rez sickness penalty from using a stone.
Having the option between running to your corpse and being rezzed / using a soulstone is nice, but there should definately be a plus to selecting a rez over running back. Right now, there really isnt unless the corpse run is very long, or you are in an instance. Getting the soulstone spell or a rez spell as a rezzing class should be a boost, a cool ability people will gladly accept instead of having them look at the downsides and opting to run back, that is just a shame.

Enslave Demon: I do not have enough space in my inventory to carry enough shards to let me cast this spell very often. Maximum duration is extremely low, and it breaks too often. Demons are rare to come across in the first place, making potential use of this spell sporadic. In its current form this spell needs some major buffage and removal of the soulshard cost or be removed altogether.

Sense Demons: This spell should be removed if I can gain the Demon Tracking ability for 5 skill points at the tracking trainer.

Detect (lesser) Invisibility: ITs been suggested before; This spell needs a more demonic name. Shadow Eyes? Demon sight? Burning Vision? Whatever ;)

Soul Shards: I wouldnt mind seeing more spells use a soul shard, as Ultimus has been suggesting, but if that happens I do not want them eating up my inventory space. That would simply mean tormenting anyone who is a tradeskilling warlock, and I do not see how inventory management should have anything to do with spell/class balancing in the first place.
I would like to see captured souls go to a simple soul collection item instead of taking up space.
You would have a 'soul prison', an item with its own unique slot in the character screen, specifically for warlocks (or no item at all, a mere mind prison), in which the souls are contained. You draw from that to make stones and pets, and whatetever else might need a soul.
There would be a cap to the amount of souls you can store, increasing for instance every 10 levels.

No time to write up or think of more, and Im sure in the time it took me write this, half of it has already appeared in this thread. I hope my thoughts correspond with the majority of the warlocks here.

Edit:: Cant believe I forgot DoT stacking! Immolate and Corruption from different warlocks on the same target should STACK. Having multiple warlocks around is not desirable, make them complement instead of detract from eachother and allow DoT stacking. If curse of Agony stays a pure damage curse, this should also stack.

Pardon if anything comes out disjointed. I've posted so much on this subject, that I preferred to go back and gather relevant bits from my old threads than rewriting it all :)

SOULSTONES:

In the last push, I assume it was a bug- they could be used after death in ghost form, and that allowed you to return to your body with no rez sickness - and- have range to rez around your body. Now, the lack of rez sickness was not fair- it was overpowered, and I agree with the change to that. It also didn't make "sense" that we could use the stone while in ghost form. Hands down, agreed. What I would like to see however, is a LITTLE range added on use- so that one does not have to rez RIGHT on top of the body, which is sometimes right in the middle of a very nasty spawn area ;) Granted, we can usually TRY to run away a bit, to some kind of halfway "safe" spot to die, but that isn't possible when you are bound/rooted/slept etc, and sometimes when you've cleared and area and don't know where the next POP is going to be when you rez too. Add the non-range to rez sickness, and SS's seem kind of useless now, especially considering the cost in shards. (remembering that so many of our spells and all summons but imp require shards too)

I suggest the shard costs remains the same, the rez sickness still applies- but a small limited range area is allowed upon rez- when using a soulstone. So for example, you die- you get a popup as you would anyway- "release" but also "Use Soulstone". If you release, it's as usual- your at a graveyard as a ghost & have to corpse run. But if you use your soulstone- you "pop" ontop of your body, just as you do right now- except in ghost form, not returned to life yet. From there, I'm suggesting a very small, limited radius in which to move OFF your body, and then hit the rez button. For cloth wearing casters, this would allow us some desperately needed recovery space/resummon space in some tight situations :) Part of the problem right now, is that alot of ppl (not playing warlocks, or not noticing the diff yet) still think you can hit release, and then USE your SS in ghost form, as you could in Push 2. That is no longer the case. I think rez sickness is fine- to be expected- and I'm glad that was "fixed" but I think adding a bit of range on rez would be a very helpful change too. I have heard it said that SS's are still useful in instance dungeons- but please keep in mind that not all of us prefer/enjoy/seek out instance crawls, especially some soloers- and SS's should have some value to all players ;) Not just groupers IMHO.

HEALTH FUNNEL CONCERNS

The issue we are seeing, is that the spell seems to sometimes "just quit" on you for no apparent reason. You do not get hit, you do not run out of mana, and your pet is not yet full on health. It isn't a set duration it seems, it just quits at some random point in the stream. Then, if you cast it again, it works until completion as it should. (Also it should be noted that in this push there is also a new behavior where health funnel sometimes draws INSANE agro all the sudden. Did not see this last push.) This is not to be mistaken for a case where you cast the spell, it burns the mana requirement but doesn't fill pet all the way. Requires another casting to "top it off". What we are seing is- the spell quits just a few secs into the initial casting for no reason we can understand. The second cast however, does just fine.

Here is the thread on the health funnel "quitting too soon":
http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.aspx?ForumName=wow-warlock&ThreadID=2415

Anyway, my suggestion for improving Health Funnel irregardless of the above bug is:

I suggest that the Health Funnel spell automatically continue until the pet is full health unless
A) You cancel it first
B) You can no longer spare the health to give
C) You are hit or dmged/spell is interrupted

I really don't see why anything else makes sense. It should not just stop at a random pt, like it's doing now. If the mana cost is the issue, then make it cost X mana- X=how long it has to channel to fill in what health is missing on the pet. If that didn't make sense or was confusing, my apologies :) Also- it now has a very good chance of pulling agro- just like eating & drinking, and sometimes to the extreme. Might be a bug or intentional.. I hate it though. Easier to let my pet die & use his shield in case of VW then just move to a safe range & resummon. To me.. that's dummying down my strategy.. not making it sharper.
