  • I know the devs play this game. Do the hunters over there ever use frost trap, and if so how? I have been very hard pressed to find a use for this trap.

  • Can traps be beefed up to compensate for the difficulty in using them properly? Thus far they are less effective than other class' abilities and significantly harder to use.

  • Are there plans to either improve the damage of volley or implement another component (such as stun) to make it more effective?

  • Can the taunt effect on concussion shot be increased? As a mail class, I feel I should have some ability to pull mobs off of cloth classes.

  • Since fear beast is limited to usage on beasts only, will it be powered up to make it more effective against beasts than other, broader fear abilities?

  • Will hunters see new kinds of traps? What about being able to lay multiple traps at once? I really love the idea of traps and wish it would be expounded upon further.

  • Many high-end groups already see the usefulness in the visual element of hunter's mark. Is there any chance of this ability being given an additional effect to turn it from 'kind of helpful' into the kind of ability that makes the entire class desirable to group with?   • Can flare see a range as well as area of effect increase? Furthermore, can it be made impossible to resist? Since it is easy to see lower leveled stealthed mobs and players, the ability seems pointless if anything above your level resists it. Also, when using it in PvE at the maximum range, you still must be within aggro range of a higher level mob in order for it to hit them.

  • Do 'eyes of the beast' reduce the aggro your pet draws while you control it? I could swear it does and I'd just like confirmation one way or another.

  • Can the range limitation on 'dismiss pet' please be removed? If not, what are the balancing reasons why this ability has such a short range?

  • Most people prefer large pets. What about a little love for those of us who liked our tiny, low-level pets that we put work into leveling up? My pet's size really distinguished it as unique and stood as proof that I trained it from an early level, but now it just looks like I picked it up in Dustwallow around level 30-ish when everybody was looking for a good raptor.
