 1. New idea for ghostly strike... | 9/29/2004 2:23:56 AM GMTDT
Now i think we can all agree that the subtlety line is overall the "weakest" when compared to the other two talent trees. Obviously the new hemm helped, but stuff like improved distract (why is this still here?) isn't helping you with pvp or pve in a real noticeable way. Hence my idea to tweak an ability that i think has potential, but currently is kind of ho-hum.

Currently it does 125% of weapon dmg and gives you "5" seconds of 15% evasion. Now originally i thought this was good, but for FIFTY (50!!) energy i just can't see how this is efficient... Which brings me to my idea for a new ghostly strike:
125% Weapon Damage (damage but not great)
50 Energy cost
1 Combo Point
5-10 min cooldown (whichver seems most balanced)
On hit: Pops the rogue into stealth for 5 seconds, giving them access to their stealth bar. Any sort of attack removes them from stealth, or if the 5 seconds expires without damage delivered the rogue returns to normal out of stealth.

Now think about this ability in relation to the subtlety tree. Subtlety is built around adding combo points and killing less obtrusively: improved stealth, camoflauge, improved vanish, improved ambush etc etc. What this would allow you to do is cheap shot lets say, then ghostly and ambush, vanish ambush, then preparation and do it all over again. Possibilities galore, just some food for thought, but i'd like to know what you all think...

Edit: Added cooldown.

 5. Re: New idea for ghostly strike... | 9/29/2004 3:09:41 AM GMTDT
I think this should be the next SS. Move it up a level in the tree and let it be a replacement for SS. 50 energy, 5-10% dodge increase, same Cooldown as SS is.

Honestly, this should be about 40 energy, not 50. Look at SS a second. At max level it's damage + 68. In reality, SS turns out to be a ~150% to ~180% weapon damage strike depending on the weapon your using and the rank of SS you have.

In comparison 125% is damned skimpy...then it adds a 15% bonus to dodge for 5s every 20 seconds?

I say take Ghostly strike and make it the following:

130% weapon damage + 10% dodge for 5s or till another special attack is made.
+1 Combo Point
40 Energy

This is an SS replacement. If you want to keep dodge up, (good for soloing) you keep applying Ghostly Strike. Once you apply something else, you immediately loose the 10% to dodge till you re-apply. Means you use SS for Damage and Ghostly if you need to stop taking damage.

Honestly, I think the BEST BEST COOLEST BEST change would be the following:

Ghostly Strike (2 Ranks):
125% weapon damage + 20% Dodge for 10 Seconds
5 second cooldown
50 energy
Has a 30% / rank chance of COMPLETELY wiping agro from target.

So you get the dodge bonus in case it fails or if multiple targets are attacking you, but if it succeeds you drop all agro on the *target*.

Could potentially be drop all agro on all targets too without being too overpowered.

Could also be 100% chance to drop agro on all targets but puts you in an "out of phase" state for 5 seconds (attack turned off, like a small sleep). This would be like a mini vanish.

Lots of cool things you could do with this...
