Overview of current Druid restoration skills and talents
Beta Ver 0.11.0 (3925) Oct 8th 2004

All data is assumeing level 60 avaliability. 


----------------------------------------------------------

My Calculations



MinHeal+MaxHeal/2 = AH (Avgerage Healing)

AvgHeal/Mana = H:M (Healing to Mana Ratio)

Average Healing / (Cast Time+1second refresh-animation) = HPS (Healing per Second)



Oct 06 2004

Spell Numbers from http://www.thottbot.com

Talent stuff from http://wowvault.ign.com/View.php?view=Talents.View&category_select_id=6

Regrowth 09 = 2125_AH 880m 3.0s(24s) -- 2.41 H:M -- 088.54 HPS

| Restoration Talent Line |

|     57 Points Total     |

|_________________________|





Improved Mark of the Wild, 5pts, +35% increased MOTW

MOTWI 07 = 1.0s 445m -- +22stats, +520armor, +54 resists

MOTW  07 = 1.0s 445m -- +16stats, +385armor, +40 resists

-Pretty worth it IMO, +6stats, +135armor, +14 resists, basically like adding another piece of high end gear





Furor, 5pts, 100% chance for 20 rage when you shift to bear.

-Kinda nice I suppose, means you dont need to wait 5-10 seconds for enrage to build you some rage,

-Provides ability of bearshift charge to stop runners, our only way to stop runners in under 4 seconds indoors.



Nature's Focus, 5pts 60% chance to avoid inturrupts on Regrowth/Healing Touch

-Have found this to be usefull, good skill imo.



Improved Healing Touch, 5pts, 20% mana reduction for Healing Touch

-HT 10  = 2060h 790m 4.5s -- 2.60 H:M -- 457.77 HPS

-HTI 10 = 2060h 632m 4.5s -- 3.26 H:M -- 457.77 HPS

-Not bad, not bad, comparable to H:M talent increases for priests.



Improved Regrowth, 5pts, 50% increase to chance to crit regrowth

-This goes off pretty often, some may consider it overpowered



Improved Enrage, 2pts, Reduce reuse time of Enrage by 4s and slightly lower Armor penalty

-Useless as far as a Druid is concerned, Enrage repops often enough from what ive seen

-Reduces Enrage reuse to every 16s rather then 20s (Enrage duration is 10s) so every 6s you can Enrage rather then every 10s

-We have 3 instant attacks in bear form (Swipe/Bash/Charge) 

-Swipe is an AE, Bash is reusable every 1m, Charge requires distance, Druids do not have rage issues if they use base Enrage.



Intensity, 5pts 100% chance 20energy on shift cat

-Useless, Basically what this does is save you 1.88s of time (20energy every 1.88s by my stopwatch)

-This ability should give ATLEAST 35energy IMO... AND merged with furor.

-Rake(35e) Claw(40e) Shred(60e)



Reflection, 5pts, Increase Mana Regen by 10%

-Too expensive, most level 60's have about 70-90 regen per tick

-80*1.1 = 88 = 5pts for 8 mana regen, our lowest cost spell we would use at this level is Remove Curse

-Remove Curse(100m) 100/8 = 12.5 so every 13s you can cast an additional Remove Curse

-Useless, Upgrade to 20% like priests or....

-Make it a instant cast buff like Rejuv but for mana, we have DmgOverTime HealOverTime why not ManaOverTime?

-This could give us utility in groups by supplying mana when needed say 500mana at level 5 in 10s for 250m etc..



Gift of Nature, 1pt Increase healing by 5%

-Very Nice, definatley worth 1pt

- Pretty Good, with GoN its same H:M as base Healing Touch, yet still less HPS then base Regrowth.

Nature's Swiftness, 1pt, 3m cooldown, when active makes next nature spell instant. (Req Natures Focus 5)

-Ignoring the Exploit/Cheat....

-Worth its weight of 1pt in my oppinion, althou 3m cooldown kinda long, but still figure every 2-4 encounters in an instance.



Subtlety, 5pts, 20% threat reduction from heals.

-Hmm, this skill is play style dependant, I dont have many problems with threat in groups and when I do 20% reduct wouldnt effect that.

-Good for some people.



Improved Tranquility, 2pts , 80% chance to avoid breaking channeling.

-Tranquility is used so rarely by me currently, perhaps in some PvP/PvE raid encounters this may become a consideration but currently = useless.



Combat Endurance, 5pts, 10% of your Health regen to work in combat

-Useless

-Not sure what normal regeneration is but a linen bandage is 66 per tick, so figure normal is 10-30

-10% of say 20hp regen = 2hp



Reincarnation, 1pt, 60m cooldown, SELF-REZ w/ 20% health/mana.

-Useless unless soloing/pvp but still... kinda useless.





Quick Oppinions....

Furor+Intensity should be merged , intensity is pretty useless needs 50+energy, and furor is only usefull if you have charge IMO.



Combat Endurance, just drop this not usefull unless it makes it 100% for warriors or druids.

Improved Enrage, likewise, this is kinda useless for a druid.

Improved Tranquility, dont really use tranquility as is, perhaps if it was 50/100% chance of avoiding interupt but even then, dunno.



Suggestions....

Improved Thorn+MOTW duration

Move swiftshipping into this line and take it out of balance

Shortened Duration of HoT spells ability

Rez spell / DI type spell



Overall this line seems pretty balanced I have no quams with our healing or the talent line, it provides

some very usefull abilities/upgrades in early/mid talent tree but the ending portion of the tree is useless.
