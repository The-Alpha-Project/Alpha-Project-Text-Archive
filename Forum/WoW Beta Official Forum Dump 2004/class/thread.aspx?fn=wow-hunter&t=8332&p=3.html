other classes that use range weapon get a +1 second delay to it (they get the Shoot Bow/Gun option instead of autoshoot)

This is kind of true, but its more like this:


Rogue/Warrior
<press shoot><wait weapon speed><fire><wait approx 1 sec><press shoot><wait weapon speed><fire>.....

Hunter
<press autoshoot><fire><wait weapon speed><fire><wait weapon speed><fire>.....

Thats the main difference between the two. The quiver haste time only effects the <wait weapon speed> section of the above. Mage wands work on the same exact principle as guns/bows work for roques/warriors. This is why wands sucked for so long, because their rated DPS wasn't even close to their actual DPS due to the approx one second skill lockout timer. Either way your still waiting the same exact time between pressing <shoot> and the shot firing as hunters do.
