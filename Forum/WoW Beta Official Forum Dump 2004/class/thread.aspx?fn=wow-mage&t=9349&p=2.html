FIRE

ICE

ARCANE

-Arcane Mind now has 4 ranks, for a maximum of 8% extra mana
-Arcane Meditation is back, with 5 ranks. However, 5/5 = increases mana regeneration while casting by 10%
-Improved Blink is gone completely
-Counterspell talent was changed. It has 2 ranks now, granting chance to silence your target for 4 seconds. 1/2 = 50%, 2/2 = 100%. Counterspell = $$$ now, expect a nerf.
-Presence of Mind is now the prerequisite for Arcane Instability. So it goes PoM > A Inst > Arc Power.
-Arcane Instability was reduced to 3 ranks, for a total of 3% increased damage.
