 3. Re: Level 20 Succubus Quest | 10/20/2004 5:41:10 PM GMTDT
It isn't too hard if you are sneaky and don't mind dying a few times. I obtained the quest at level 20 and completed it at 20, dying three times to Horde players in the process. Of those two were no-win situations and the third, well, there isn't a death penalty so I didn't care. I also didn't have a pet out since I was trying to keep a low profile, so fighting wasn't really an option.

The route I used was longer than the straight route, but it was also generally safer because I was in areas in which most Horde don't spend a lot of time. The only exception to this is Ratchet. I think you can get where you need to go without going through that town, but I wanted to use to boat there to get to Booty Bay and get the flight route to Stormwind while I was in the area. That town can be really rough for level 20 Alliance, but if it's really bad I think you can go around it. You just have to do the following:

1) know where the open spot in the Ashenvale/Barrens border is
2) stick to the eastern edge of the terrain as much as you can.
3) get into the water as soon as possible and make liberal use of Undying Breath
4) watch your mob aggro

As long as you know where the tent you need to visit is, once you get to the guy who gives you the instructions on summoning the succubus, the hard part's over. After that, it's just heading to the right spot in Ashenvale and fighting about one dozen mobs to get to the right spot to get what you need for the summoning.
