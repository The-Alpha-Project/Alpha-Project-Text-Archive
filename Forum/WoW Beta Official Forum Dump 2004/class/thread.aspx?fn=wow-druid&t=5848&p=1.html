 10. Re: On the Horizon | 10/1/2004 12:26:38 AM GMTDT
I'd also like to throw in my vote for a functional rez. I understand the designers have reservations (which is why they only gave us a timed self-rez), but if it's a big issue from the view of overall game balance, put it on a long timer. The current self rez is not really useful, and not worth the talent points necessary to gain it. Change it to a normal rez, and it suddenly becomes imperative.

Perhaps, in keeping with the Druidic theme, we should only be able to rez in outdoor zones (including those cave dungeons that do not require zoning)? That would also leave instance farming as the province of Priests/Warlocks/Paladins etc., which is from where I'm guessing most of the balance concerns arise.

The reason for wanting a rez? Utility. Druids are the broad spread hybrids of the game. The ability to rez (even if only outdoors) is the one useful thing missing from from our template. That and feign death, which was brutally ripped from us before I could even try it out ;)

Concerning Res ability, I think that Druids should be able to recall someone's spirit via the Emerald Dream and teleport it back to near where his body is, a shortcut. Warlock already do this, but makes more sense that Druids do. Cenarius would talk to Malfurion through the Emerald Dream when Malfurion was fighting Xavius in War of the Ancients book.

When Sargeras was about to take Malfurion's spirit into the Nether, Cenarius **Grabbed** Malfurion's spirit from the hands of Sargeras and put it back to Malfurion's body. It is there .... it is part of the lore ^_^

Druids should be able to grab the spirit of players via Emerald Dream and recall it within the area of their body.

Another aspect of Druidism I noticed from the pocketbook was that Malfurion drew energy from living things of nature. Cenarius asked Malfurion to draw energy from Cenarius, Krasus, Rhonin, Tyrande and Brox. Malfurion did draw energy in his spirit form and became very powerful. He destroyed the Tower with powerful winds and thunderbolts.

I wonder if Druids could somehow press a spell button that does the same as /stuck but in this case, your body auto-dies and your spirit stands above your body. While your physical body is on the ground, your spirit remains near you when you click this spell button. Now since your spirit is in connection with the Emerald Dream you draw energy from all the players. This may cost them either mana/rage or health. But the united tidbits of mana/health taken off each player serves to cast a massive AOE that annihilates everything within the area. This serves maybe as a way to save the team when more than 4 mobs jump on you. All mobs except bosses should stop attacking and enter in a animation of pain as they are struck and die shortly. Bosses could go on or be immune. As penalty the druid could have a countdown before returning to his buddy or maybe get a very low HP or mana.

It could have a moderate cooldown of 5 minutes. Only used as a means to save the team when we get mad aggro and the team is dying.

Druids should also gain the ability to open a portal vortex into the Emerald Dream.
