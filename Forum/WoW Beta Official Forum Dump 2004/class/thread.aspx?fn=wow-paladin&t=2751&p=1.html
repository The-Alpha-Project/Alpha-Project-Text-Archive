 1. How does a mount balance Paladins? | 7/29/2004 8:57:58 AM GMTDT
This question has been in the back of my mind for a while.

We all know we are getting a mount for the Paladin, we are all excided we dont need to save our pennies to get a mount, but I really have to ask ... why?

As I made the comparison before, I think Blizz is trying just a little too hard to keep the Paladin and Shaman balanced.

I really have to admit though, even now I cant think of a good reason why the Paladin should get a free mount. If anything, I think this creates greater imbalances with other classes. The Paladin already requires less Skill Points compared to a Warrior/Rogue to keep him effective. If the Paladin does not require the 400 Skill Points to learn horse riding, that creates a further rift between Warrior/Rogue and Paladin (for instance) with Skill Point Requirements.

I really would like to hear the rationale of this. Is a mount supposed to make up for the Paladins imbalance and lack of attack variety in battle? If so how?

I like the fact we get the free mount but I still have the very same question I had when I heard this news ... why ?
