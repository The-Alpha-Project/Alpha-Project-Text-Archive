 1. "Another action is in progress" lockout bugs | 10/6/2004 6:16:50 AM GMTDT
Well, I was finally able to pin down what is causing these. There is actually two different issues that create the problem.

Scenerio #1 - The hunter is on the move and uses one of his special attacks. While still moving he then trys to use a subsequent special attack all the time he has stayed within range of target. Result: "Another Action is in Progress" message.

What is happening is the game is automatically nocking an arrow when autoshot turns on right after the special attack fires. The problem is your moving and thus it can't fire it. If you stop briefly, it will fire and everything will work as it suppose to from there on out. The bug is that it shouldn't even be nocking the arrow for autoshot since it requires you to stand still to do so. Something that never happened, since the hunter was always moving.

Scenerio #2 - The hunter is close to max or min range, standing still. Presses a special or autoshot, but between the time the button is pressed and the shot actually fires the target moves inside/outside the 8/35 yard range. Result: "Another Action is in Progress" message.

The only relation to this bug and the first one is again, you have a nocked arrow that the game can't fire. However this bug I believe is a side effect of the game being much more strict on range. It used to be, that the game would fire the weapon if you had a nocked arrow regardless of the range. In other words it really only checked the range when you nocked the arrow, not when it was fired. Seems they have added a second check for when its fired. So you get in a situation where you can nock an arrow, but can't fire it.


One thing I did find, if you open with autoshot or get that intial queued autoshot off, and you don't turn it off, either manually or by going into melee mode (Doing a wingclip turns off autoshot, etc..), everything will work as expected with no lockout problems.
