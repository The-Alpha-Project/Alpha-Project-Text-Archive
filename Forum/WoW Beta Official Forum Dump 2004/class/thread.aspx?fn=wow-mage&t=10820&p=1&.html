 2. Re: Intellect vs Spirit vs Stamina | 10/21/2004 5:13:23 PM GMTDT
Off hand I do not know what my + is on Int/Stam/Spirit. What I do know though is that I really don't care about Spirit at the moment and mainly go for Int/Stam (Eagle) items.

The way Mana regen works off Spirit is 5 seconds after your last cast. If you were a priest or shaman this 5 seconds would elapse while you were meleeing your mob to kill it. For Mages on the other hand, the 5 seconds elapses while you run to your next mob. This gives very little, if any, time for your mana to regen between fights. With that being the case, I prefer having higher mana/health then higher regen.

At level 37 my mana is about 3350 and my health is about 1150. Granted I am still wearing 3-4 pieces of non Int/Stam armor as I haven't been able to procure any at the AH.

You do want some spirit at least above the 100 mark. I can't recall what my spirit is but it's over 175 for sure (close to 200). It seems evocation rubs off you spirit cause it's 1500% reg. more than normal so if your spirit is low you recieve less with evocation. I have to do more testing on this but it seems that when I go with my +stam and +int pure items and have less than 100 spirit I tend to only recieve like 2k-2.5k ish mana...but with 175+ I recieve at least 3k mana when using evocation. Now I'm not sure how ur playstyle is. But when I'm blasting through things in high instances you tend to run out of mana and no tiem to sit. When i'm down to about 1k mana I hit evocation and when that is done I usually have back up to 5k mana...my mana pool is roughly 6.3k right now...

So spirit does matter, as to stam + int...well stam is nice but you're not a tank so why concentrate so much on stam. I have a lot of mix +stam/+int and +spr/+int items...my main concern is the +int =)
