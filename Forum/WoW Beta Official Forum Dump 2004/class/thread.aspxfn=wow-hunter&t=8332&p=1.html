Q u o t e:
what method are you using to calculate DPS?
how do you know exactly how long each fight is?


The math is all there in the original post, but I'll make it clearer here, if you like. Let's take the example of the first fight, shall we?

First, note once more that 2.20 is my weapon's cooldown (under 'speed'). Meaning I'll fire my bow every 2.20 seconds. This time is reduced by my heavy quiver, which increases ranged attack speed by 12% (reducing the cooldown). 12% of 2.20 is zero point two six four. 2.20 minus 0.264 is equal to 1.936. Therefore, the cooldown of my weapon, adjusted to take into account the presence of my quiver, is 1.936. Meaning I will fire my bow every 1.936 seconds.

From this, I can extrapolate exactly how long a fight takes, so long as I do not at any point cease firing the bow. If an enemy gets too close for a few seconds, or if at any point I cease my autoattack for any reason, I will no longer be able to gauge the length of the fight by how many shots were fired. Arcane shot, as an instant ability, does not interfere with the autoshot.

So going back to that first battle. I fight the battle, and when it's done, I look over my combat log. I see that I opened the battle by sending my pet to tank for me and to draw aggro, which is irrelevant for the purposes of this exercise. I then hit the enemy with a serpent sting, which is followed by an immediate autoshot. Although there may be a few tenths of a second between the two shots, for the purposes of this demonstration, I am starting count from my first regular bow shot.

My first shot, dealing 115 damage, becomes '0 seconds.' Because I know my weapon's firing speed, I can deduce that my second shot will come at 1.936 seconds. From there, it is simple addition.

115 (0 seconds)
34 (pet)
83 (arcane shot)
118 (1.936 seconds)
42 (serpent sting)
28 (pet)
125 (3.872 seconds)
67 (pet) (crit)
42 (serpent sting)
114 (5.808)
8 (pet)
28 (pet)
130 (7.774 seconds)
(enemy dies)

Total Damage: 934
Total time: 7.774 seconds.
934 divided by 7.774 seconds = DPS
DPS = 120
