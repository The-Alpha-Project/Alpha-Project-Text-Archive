 1. Priests are a dying breed | 10/5/2004 5:27:21 PM GMTDT
When you look at the capabilities of Shamans, Druids, and Warlocks, you start to wonder what point making a Priest will be come release. Don't get me wrong, I do love my priest, but its hard to ignore that other classes are stealing our thunder. Shamans and Druids can heal better, get better armor, better talent trees, and have the capability for more offensive power. Warlocks are now more viable and with summons, soulstones, and health stones paired with far better DPS. Shamans, Warlocks, and Paladins offset a Priest's res. Priests used to be needed for every successful group through most instances. Not looking to be the case for much longer. In PvP Priests are still viable, but hardly last long when targeted in group vs. group.

This class needs some developer attention.
