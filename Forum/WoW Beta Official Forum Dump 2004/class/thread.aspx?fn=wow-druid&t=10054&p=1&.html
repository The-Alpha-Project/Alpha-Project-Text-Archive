1) Shifting is the only method of escape currently. Nature's Grasp is broken, and theres nothing much else. Druids are the weakest class in the game, weaker than Mages, Priests, Warlocks, and Rogues. Whereas all the other classes have some form of protection against melee, which I probably dont need to list, druids have nothing except turning into a cat and running away. If you think that is overpowered, then I suggest you nerf Priest/Warlock AE insta-cast fear, Mage Blink, and Rogue vanish.

2) Chaining Moonfire. Unlike Mortal Strike Spam, back in the day, there is an inherent cooldown everytime you cast a spell. That cooldown is approximately 1 second. So it is just as good as a 1 second cast 220-280 DD, if you choose to up Balance talents. That is about 250DPS. Quite frankly, a Shadow Priest spec'ed pure shadow will hit the 200+ DPS mark easily and without wasting the mana of Moonfire AND causing a Snare effect. If you wait 2 more seconds for the first DoT timer, it'll be 340-400 damage over 3 seconds, which consequently is *less* damage than mage fireballs with similar cast time.

Morever, Moonfire is far weaker than Warlock/Priest DoT's. The DoT on Moonfire is 120/3 seconds @ 4 ticks, max. Priests hit 210/3sec @ 6 ticks, and warlocks can stack several DoT's similar into something similar to priest's DoT damage; plus some of their DoT's have a DD and they last longer than a Priest's DoT.
