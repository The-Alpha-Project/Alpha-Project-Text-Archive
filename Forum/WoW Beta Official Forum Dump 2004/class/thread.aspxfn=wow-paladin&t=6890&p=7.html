I know many of my suggestions have been stated before. I just wanted to "put my vote in".

1) Ranged attacks - Don't need them imho. Most paladin lore states that paladins have a disdain (find it dishonorable) for ranged attacks. I do very well with body pull again mobs and Exorcism vs. UD mobs.

2) More smash buttons - It is really boring to solo always using CS/HS, or just waiting for HS to cycle. I throw in a Hammer of Justice just to offset the monotony of the whole process.

3) Tomes - good idea that would add flavor but not required to make the class more fun.

4) Snare seal effect - I can't tell you how frustrating it is to be chasing a player in PvP and not be able to do anything to stop them from running. You can HoJ once per minute, it stops them for 3 seconds, and if you can't kill them in three seconds, they are gone. If Hammer of Justice had a "daze" component that slowed movement by N percent for N seconds, it would make paladin PvP more fun. As it stands, I can't PvP to save my life against any moderately skilled player because 1) Don't have high enough DPS 2) They can run away VERY easy 3) Other classes have skills that slow me down or stop me from escaping.

5) Improved graphics - I expect these are coming. The graphics are already amazing and they continue improve from patch to patch.

6) Improved sounds - see above

7) Current seals - Duration is too short, making Seal management a pain in the @$$.

8) Auras need a refresh. Some auras should have in-combat effectiveness and out-of-combat effectiveness. Great examples are Heal and Wisdom. They work ok in combat, work great out of combat. Obviously, Domination/Devotion/Resistance have no effect outside of combat.
