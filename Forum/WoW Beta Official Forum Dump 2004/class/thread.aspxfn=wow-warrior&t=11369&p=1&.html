 1. Why I believe Bloodthirst is pointless... | 10/22/2004 3:21:35 AM GMTDT
Well, basically I haven't used Bloodthirst since it was disabled a couple weeks ago... until today. Yes, I can gladly agree that it was waaaaay to overpowered (I remember walking through level 58 elites in winterspring, killing 2 or 3 before having to med up, and most are familiar with Indalamar's video). However, the skill was changed and my curiosity as to how, got the best of me today.

After logging in, I altered my traditional fury build to include bloodthirst, and flew out to eastern plaguelands to test it against mobs around my level.

I carefully picked out a camp of level 58+ ghouls and skeletons to see how the chain-killing ability had changed. I did my normal 350-950 dmg per hit (slow weapon), and dropped the first skeleton. I raced to the nearest ghoul I could find and braced myself to see what would be expected of the Bloodthirst's previous reign. *527* The numbers put me in a state of shock. Why would they reduce what is supposed to be the best talent way at the end of the Fury talent line (for the most dps warriors can dish out), to nothing but a regular pointless attack. This is a talent that you cannot even receive until at least level 39 (if you completely spec in fury). Frankly, you are better off taking the time to med up before rushing to your nearest target while bloodthirst still is enabled on you.

The 100% bonus damage on your next attack that the ability claims to do, is either bugged, or is extremely weak, especially considering your damage range usually will outdo the damage of bloodthirst without even critting.

A suggestion to improve this ability to actually make it worthwhile would be to make the ability grant a guaranteed critical strike on your next opponent you attack in 8 seconds, instead of the 100% damage. This 100% weapon damage cannot count as a crit for 2 simple reasons.

1) It does not include the bonus damage crits cause due to agility at all, so basically it's like hitting with your weapon at the edge of max weapon damage.

2) It does not initiate any abilities that might occur when you perform a critical strike.

Is anyone else realizing this issue with the ability?
