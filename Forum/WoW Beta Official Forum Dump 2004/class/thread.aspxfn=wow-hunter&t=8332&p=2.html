 39. Re: Hunters and DPS (long) | 10/15/2004 6:07:06 AM GMTDT
Your calculation for weapon speed reduction from your quiver is off. A percentage increase in attack speed does not correspond to an equal percentage decrease in attack delay.

The correct way to calculate the new weapon delay:

newDelay = oldDelay / (1 + AttackSpeedBonus) where AttackSpeedBonus would be a decimal number

This way a 100% increase in attack speed correctly reports a 50% reduction in delay since you're getting in twice as many attacks in the same time frame. You may want to correct your DPS numbers based on that, not sure how different the results will be w/ a relatively small attack speed increase number though.
