Besides these fixes to the pets there is one more important change needed. The warlock needs a spell/ability that allows him to store a pet when he summons a mount. When he is dismounted the pet should be instantly summoned. This ability is important for the PVP warlock as they often will not have the time to summon a fresh pet when attacked.

Curses
I would like to see us get an AE weakness.
I would like to see us get a curse line to stop spell casters using a school of magic (1 curse to stop fire magic, 1 to stop ice magic, etc) (this one could be flagged PVP only)
I would like us to get a curse that does any damage a cursed target does to us also deal that damage to itself.

Utility
Our summon spell needs to get a conformation box for the 2 persons helping.
The eye of Killrog needs to be able to see enemies and we need to get a longer duration version on higher levels.
Enslave Demon needs to be made a lot better or it needs to be dropped. Its current incarnation has no use.
Banish needs to only consume a shard on a success and not on a resist and it needs to have a fixed duration. OR it needs to have its cool down time reduced to 1 minute

DPS
Currently we have no DPS to speak of. That is troublesome in a fast paced game where mobs often die in a matter of seconds. We are able to do a lot of damage when we have the time to slowly DoT a lot of targets with a lot of spells but that time is not often there.
So I suggest making both Immolation and Corruption instant cast spells and add the current cast time to a cool down timer.
I suggest adding a third DoT (with an instant casting time) and this should be a high damage, low duration DoT. Something like a 10 second duration with 40 damage per second.
Our DoTs should also be much more mana efficient then the direct damage spells a mage has to make up for the reduced DPS they offer.
I would also like to see an AE DoT.
Hellfire should be upgraded to do a lot more damage to both the caster and his surrounding. It should also be made non interruptible (both for mobs and for the player himself)

Our DoTs need to be stackable with the DoTs from other warlocks to allow us to function for 100% in a group when more warlocks are present.

I think we need to have a special line of spells that are very powerful but also very dangerous.
I think we should have direct damage spells that are better then the ones mages have but at great risk to the user. Spells that not only require a hefty amount of mana but also have all kind of non beneficial side effects.
As example I think about:
Netherblast:
500 mana, instant cast, consumes 2 shoulshards when used.
Deals 1000 damage to target.
Caster is stunned for 7 seconds after casting this spell

In my opinion this type of spell is what the warlock class should be all about. Powerful but very dangerous and only situational usable as the shoulshard requirement will prevent using it on every mob. Hellfire is a good first step but it does not take both the danger as the power to the level I would like to see.

Ok, this is a totally crazy idea, but work with me, it's definately unique... my only hope is the idea gets a fair shake instead of getting lost in here:

Notice how all your draining spells have the dramatic pose of one hand outstretched with the beam lancing from your hand to your target?

Notice how that other hand is just kinda hanging limp there, doing nothing?

Add a skill of "dual casting" to warlocks, much like dual wielding for warriors/rogues, which allows warlocks to cast a second spell while maintaining a channel. This could be a checked skill with spell failure as a possibility, or maybe a talent that has to be bought up to.

Consider: drain soul for the duration of a fight while nuking or refreshing curses, target two seperate enemies with drain life for a quicker health boost, drain an enemy and heal your pet at the same time. The strength of Warlocks as casters is their versatility, the ability to cast and command a pet, to heal and raise dead, to crowd control and nuke, all at the cost of micromanagement; dealing with inventory space, managing hate, managing life and mana balances.

Yes, I know this would up our ability to deal damage and such... but that's what we need. And this doesn't make the class too powerful, but definately adds a new flavor to the spellcasting that other classes don't have.

I suggested this in another post and wanted to add it here. There are a lot of possibilities for the warlock and like most people would probably agree, it feels like a gimped mage pet class right now.


======================

A Warlock should be an evil magic user that has made a pact with demons to enhance his magical abilities beyond the normal magician.

When I say magician, I am not refering to the mage class.

I don't necessarily agree with some of the exact suggestions he makes, but he does make some good examples. About the best thing I can equate this too is the Goblin Death Ray I heard about the other day in another post. It usually does an insane amount of damage like 900, but occassionally it will blow up and do 1600 damage to the user. This is what I am talking about. But not because Goblin's aren't crap for engineers, but because there is a price to pay for the power.

Here are some of my examples.

  • Insta Cast: Touch of Death Does 75% damage to the target with a chance for a critical that kills the target. HUGE mana cost, like all mana and at least 75% mana or you can't cast. Small chance that the Warlock will die himself instead of the target (10%) and a 24-48 hour recast. It would need to be adjusted a little on elites / bosses, but should be pretty powerful.

  • Touch of Hatred: Channeling spell that does HUGE amounts of damage to the target as a DOT in a relatively short period of time (5 - 10 ticks), but acts as a drain on everyone within 50 yards. Drains 5% health from everyone in the group, adds those amounts and does that damage to the mob each tick. Picture Green Channels to all people close (to avoid "group" abuse) and a red channel to the mob.

  • Sacrifice: Kills the Player Character Target causing a triple rez death loss of EXP. Allows the Warlock to Summon a super elemental that lasts for 1.5 minutes, but is extremely powerful and huge amounts of hitpoints. 24/48 hour recast.

  • Raise Demons: Summons a Dozen demons that are like 1/2 the level of the caster, with super taunt. They will randomly attack / taunt monsters while scrambling their hate list (randomizing) with the demon at the top. The demons would be relatively quick to die and once they die, the mob could have any member of the party at the top of the hit list and be hard to remove (unless another demon taunts and scrambles). Think of it as a distraction mechanism. Say your only priest got too much agro, you can cast this and summon the demons and give the priest a little breather, but they may get it back double when its over. Make them act like the imp and run around making it is possible to agro nearby mobs.

  • Turn Hell Fire into HELL fire. Right now, its a wimpy AE that does damage to the Warlock.

  • Buff of the Dead: Super Buffs a party member for 2 mins making them hard to hit but do super damage and maintain agro. At the end though, they go to 5% health with really bad rez effects. Could also do this with a caster making them really hard to hit, all spells take 1/2 time to cast and mana regens at 4x rate. Kills the Warlock. (For getting out of really bad situations as a last resort).

  • Demon Gate: Ports all Members of the Party who have a SoulStone to Demon Gate location (some area way atop a mountain somewhere in one of the firey zones or maybe it's own instance (i.e. Hell). All members escape the location and have a 2.5 minute timer to "recover" and re-enter the gate to return to the instance (or other location). Otherwise it closes and they will die an EXP death and be rezed automatically at the closest graveyard to the original instance. All members are returned to the exact location they were at when the gate was activated and lose their soulstone (essentialy costs - 10 soul shards)

    There are a lot of fun and wicked things you could do with this class. I just hope it gets some of it.
