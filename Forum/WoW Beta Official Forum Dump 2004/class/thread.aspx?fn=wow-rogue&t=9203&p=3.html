All the damage / energy discussions are nice, but they don't get at the heart of the matter.

Rogue HP - Damage Taken > Mob HP - Damage Taken

is the one formula you need to know in any MMORPG. This is simple, you must be able to outlast the mob.

Gouge provides several seconds of no damage to the Rogue, and also provides a FREE high damage attack. Any damage done to the mob while it's not attacking you is free damage.

Looked at from a group perspective, you have to prove that SS + Weapon does more damage in a certain time than BS + Weapon. The fact of the matter is, it does not until you get to extremes in weaponry.

Looked at from a solo perspective, I don't give a rats ass whether BS or SS does more DPE, what I care about is damage given vs damage taken because this is the formula that determines the success of failure of the battle. For SS to be better than BS+G you'd better be able to prove to me that SS does enough damage that it lessens the damage I TAKE from the mob by more than 5.5 seconds of the mob not attacking. Your SS had better make up for 5.5 seconds of damage taken.

SS alows you to kill faster, but you take more damage. In situations where you have healing at your disposal but are still tanking, this is probably better...faster kills = more xp / hour and more loot = better for everyone. In solo situations where you have no healing, less damage = better...less downtime, less chance of dieing, more kills. In group situations where you're not tanking BS > SS as it takes 3 SSs to make up the typical 300+ damage BS (4 - 500 at high level) and i'm getting less agro and can time the strikes better.

It's about tactics and situation. There are situations where SS is better (and the higher in the game you go, the better weapons you obtain, the more these situations lean toward SS...which is what one of my big problems with rogues are right now...why are we being HOARDED toward Swords and Maces at the top end of the game?) and situations where BS is better.

Keep a sword and a couple of daggers in your inventory and given a partiular situation, try them out.
