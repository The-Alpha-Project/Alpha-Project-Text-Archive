 9. Re: Upgrading Daggers, and Gripe about BOP (Why?) | 10/14/2004 4:29:49 AM GMTDT
2 reasons why they unique drops from nameds in instances are BOP:

1.) They drop them consistently (as in 100% chance of a green item, sometimes a 100% chance of a blue item, depending on the enemy). If they did not restrict them to bind on pickup, high-level characters could just farm the lower-level instances and make a HECK of a lot of money selling the gear.

2.) Since there is no worries about hand-me downs, they do not have to have items become destroyed over time.


Also, probably the reason why Toxic Revenger, for instance, drops off of a lvl 32 named, but is a lvl 27 dagger is because there is SOME chance, you will end up killing Viscous Fallout at lvl 27, if you have a group with higher-level characters. That doesn't mean Toxic Revenger is no good for a lvl 32 character. In fact, I am still using mine at lvl 41 (albeit in my off-hand).
