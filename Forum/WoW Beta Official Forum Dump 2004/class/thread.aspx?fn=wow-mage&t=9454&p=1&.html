Mages have basically 4 aoe spells

Flamestrike
Blizzard
Cone of Cold
Arcane Explosion


Flamestrike has a casting time and does 1/2 its dmg up front, could be used with combust for good front loaded AoE. Decent sized AoE

Blizzard, has the largest AoE, and does the most damage, channeling spell, 4 waves of ice. If you get the right talents this will slow anyone caught in it to 25% move speed so they cant escape it!

Cone of Cold, PBAoE in front of you. Decent dmg, decent range, best when used with frost nova. 10 s or so cooldown.

Arcane Explsion another PBAoE, 10 yrds around caster. Can be made instant cast with talents. No cooldown!


IMHO the best AoE build would be a Frost/Arcane mage with the blzzard and Arcane explosion talents.

 3. Re: ex-Mana Eldritch here -- how are Mage AEs | 10/12/2004 4:29:26 PM GMTDT
Mage AE is what makes the class worthwhile to play currently. Generally speaking, they're efficient if you have at least 3 targets. You need to have as much int as you can possibly scrounge up in order to AE elites though, and even then it's best to have a second mage or warlock helping.

This changes every patch, but right now Arcane + Fire is the way to go. You can easily get the AE talent in both trees, and also get Blast Wave without sacrificing too much in Arcane.

Frost is a little more complicated, since it has 3 AEs to begin with. Blizzard isn't a bad AE, but the talent for it does nothing to increase its dps or efficiency. Frost Nova does pathetic damage, and its talent is utterly worthless. Using Nova makes up for not taking the Blizzard talent though. Cone of Cold is nice, but not worth the talent points to increase its range and damage.
