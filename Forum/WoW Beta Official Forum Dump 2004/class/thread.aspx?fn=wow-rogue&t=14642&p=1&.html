 1. Subtle Effects of Missing | 10/21/2004 9:46:27 PM GMTDT
The miss rate increases have decreased the efficiency of all rogue abilities but they have had an even greater effect on Sap, Gouge, and Backstab.

When Sapping, in most solo situations a missed Sap means that your going to have to try to run away or vanish and try again, furthermore Imp Sap allowed a rogue to sap an add then perform an opener on his real target. When a Rogue misses a Sap with Improved Sap, not only does he not sap his target, but he loses stealth, meaning any benefits of Imp Sap never came into effect.

Backstab when soloing requires the use of Gouge, if Gouge misses then you never get to attempt a Backstab which means your stuck with Sinister Striking with Daggers until the Gouge cooldown is done.
