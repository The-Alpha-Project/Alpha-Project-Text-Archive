 4. Re: Dual Wield DPS versus Single Wield | 10/12/2004 5:35:04 AM GMTDT
I might not buy Dual Wield during beta, I don't have it yet.

Let's say you had 2 daggers at 100 damage each, same delay. Dual Wielding would apply 150 damage (without talent) but only 76% of the time, which would average out to 114 damage. As mob level goes up, the accuracy scales even more poorly. Personally if accuracy vs. damage was the only factor, I'd never get DW ever, because I value accuracy more than a bit of DPS, because streaky misses = dead rogue. By keeping a higher accuracy, you lower your chances of several misses in a row. (Would I be correct in assuming the accuracy penalty does not affect specials? I'd have read something if DW caused Ambush to miss 1 in 4 times.)

However, there is the nice bonus of another poison proccer, which would be a huge benefit, and more stat bonuses, which aren't that huge of a benefit. And there are also stat bonus offhands that aren't weapons.
