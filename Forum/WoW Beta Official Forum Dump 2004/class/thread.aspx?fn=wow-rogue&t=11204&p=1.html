 1. Backstab Damage Bugged? - Stats Analysis | 10/4/2004 4:42:44 AM GMTDT
All testing performed under these criteria:

Character: Matricia, Level 57 Rogue
Weapon: Level 3 Stiletto, 2-6 Damage (main hand); floweres held in off-hand
Damage Range Listed in Character Sheet: 54-59
Targets: Mobs surrounding Goldshire - level 1-6

I first started by using only standard attacks and documenting all damage done and the level of the target. I have excluded critical strikes from this particular analysis (although the results were recorded) since we can't say for sure what the crit calculation looks like and I didn't want to contaminate the results. Also, I used a the lowest base damage weapon I could buy as I wanted to isolate the base damage from the bonus damage in the backstab. Results as follows:

Standard Attack Results
Number of Attacks Recorded: 70
Damage Range: 53-59
Damage Distribution: 53 (6%), 54 (19%), 55 (26%), 56 (20%), 57 (19%), 58 (9%), 59 (1%)
Target Level Distribution: 1 (43%), 3 (0%), 5 (39%), 6 (18%)

The damage range was 1 point below the listed minimum, but that could be due to the minimal armor a couple of the level 6's had. I carried the 53-59 range into all the other tests as the "base damage" range.

Sinister Strike Results
Number of Attacks Recorded: 72
Damage Range: 121-127
Damage Distribution: 121 (1%), 122 (24%), 123 (31%), 124 (18%), 125 (13%), 126 (12%), 127 (1%)
Target Level Distribution: 1 (61%), 3 (0%), 5 (26%0, 6 (13%)

My sinister strike is listed as 100% weapon damage + 68. As you can see 53+68 = 121, 59+68 = 127. This matches the test damage range perfectly.

Backstab Results
Number of Attacks Recorded: 74
Damage Range: 218-227
Damage Distribution: 218 (5%), 219 (7%), 220 (5%), 221 (16%), 222 (11%), 223 (15%), 224 (21%), 225 (8%), 226 (9%), 227 (3%)
Target Level Distribution: 1 (61%), 3 (1%), 5 (32%), 6 (5%)

My Backstab is listed as 150% weapon damage + 165. We would expect (53 * 1.5) + 165 = 244.5, (59 * 1.5) + 165 = 253.5. As you can see, the highest damage recorded was 227 which is 17 points below the expected minimum. Either the way the 150% is calculated is different than it would appear...or it is bugged somehow.

I thought of a few different ways that the damage could be calculated that would produce the results seen. To start with let's see what the damage range -165 is. It's 218-165=53, 227-165=62. 53 seems familiar because it's our original minimum base damage. 62? I can't say where that came from, but it's eqaul to our weapon max damage times 150% plus my agi/str bonus.

AGI/STR bonus = 59 (max actual) - 6 (weapon max) = 53

6 * 1.5 = 9 + 53 = 62

Based on these preliminary results, it appears that the 150% damage bonus is being applied before the agi/str damage bonus and only to the maximum damage value. You'll also notice that the distribution wasn't as "bell curved" as the rest. I suspect this could be due to the backstab being calculated 2 ways, one way with no bonus and one way with the bonus. These two overlap and give us the "2 humps" we see in the distribution.

I'm going to grab my high base damage weapon and conduct more tests to further verify my conclusions. I think that the rogue community does deserve to have our class dev at least tell us that this is "as intended" or if this is, in fact, a bug in the calculation. I believe the effect seen here is part of what makes sinister strike so superior to backstab currently.

I invite any questions regarding my process and open discussion of my conclusions.
