Thanks for listening ; )

I have a long history of playing a support class in many games. EQ Cleric, Shaman, DAOC Cleric, Druid and Healer, etc etc, the list goes on. I also like PvP, and that was a focus for me in each of these games. I was on Rallos Zek in EQ. (PvP server).

Having played my priest to 44 to date on WoW, I am enjoying the class, but to a limited degree. I play solo, grouped, I do instances, and I pvp when ever I get the chance. I have played full shadow spec and full holy spec. Other classes seem to have more dimensions to them.

I think WoW has three healers in game right now, with similar healing abilities if equipt correctly, and played with the right classes. I think priests can probably lay down more healing in a pinch and survive because of fade. Two healers (druids, shaman) have many options and utility, one (Priest)that has limited utility and a very defined play-style (like most MMORPGs) - the heal bar / mana balance game. Most of the time priests take on the main healer role for the simple reason that the Shaman/Druid has other options to play and the priest has none.

In previous games I avoided cloth caster types - in this game I have to play a caster/healer to be what was billed as the main healer class - Druids/Shaman are hybrid healers.

When I first started playing the Priest it was fun, there were options! Choices - I could make a difference to a group in a variety of ways. Gradually I feel I am being pushed from a group play perspective to a very narrow role.

Here are my wishes:

PvE

1. Give us some more options in group PvE - not more damage either - some other ways to mitgate damage- debuffs (slows, stat reduction, resist debuffs etc), some form of crowd control, anything so that we can adapt to different situations with a different response.
2. Make inner fire like the other caster armor buffs and last ALOT longer - 30 mins would be my preference
3. Power Word Shield used to be fantastic - now I use it in kind of as a spell of last resort to buy time for a heal. I actually dont use it that much. Can we have it last until its beaten down - I find i am casting it and it wears off before its useful and I have to recast.
4. Durability - dont forget that priests tank to solo - my cloth gear has already nose dived - make it have the same resilience as other types of armor.
5. Fix the interface so its easy to look at the group - make my health bar the same line and size as the others - add pets onto the list.
6. Ideally I would actually like to wear better armor - I kinda liked taking a beating, but at this stage in the game I suspect we are stuck. A talent that lets us use armor might be fun - though imprcatical I suspect for you guys.

PvP

1. Shadow spec is best and upto last patch was good. The changes to Mindflay (I know it was a bug that we could kite with it!) and the Face target change made pvp life very difficult for me in PvP. When we channel or cast now our opponent can just step through us and interupt the spell. Its still a fun spec, but its not the support role I wanted to play.

2. Holy is very weak in pvp and I think you need to add some survival abilities into the talents. I'm not asking for more damage. Make a Holy priest tougher to kill. Let us do our healing job. At the moment I am the first person in the group to die - make it much harder for me to die.

3. Interfacewise - when you repatch, I loose Cosmos and its a nightmare. I feel handicapped. You need to look at Cosmos and put alot of that into the game.

4. Talent respec - I doubt you will allow the free respecing of talents we have at the moment in live game. I like pvp, and this would be very useful for me to have access too.

5. Healing in PvP is very hard, big heals are too slow, flash heals and HOT dont heal enough quickly enough to counter the damage. A big insta-cast on a timer would be handy.

In summary, the priest class is a bit boring. Make it more interesting please.

 45. Re: 1 simple question, that is several questions | 10/13/2004 11:29:39 AM GMTDT
Oh geez, where to start...

1. What does Blizzard exactly feel is the role (are the roles) of a priest? Obviously we *can* heal, and *can* nuke, but how does this play out versus other classes? In terms of class balance, where do we really stand?

2. It has been surmised by several players of the priest community that the holy tree is not overly powerful (compared to other healing classes at least) so that priests maintain some option in their talent choices. Can you confirm or deny this, and expand briefly on the nature of the three talent trees from a developer's standpoint?

3. Will Mind Blast ever be restored to being on par with other nuke spells? The cast time was increased from zero to something (which is a bigger difference than something to three-times-something,) the damage-per-mana was reduced, so that the only thing this spell has in favor over Smite is one second casting difference and support of shadow talents, and yet it still creates a HUGE amount of aggro and has a recast timer that through talents we can reduce to the point of almost perfect negation of our mana regen. (Note that many players refer to that talent as the worst the shadow tree has to offer.)

4. Was the five-second rule conceived with healers in mind, or nukers? Have the developers realized that this effect in the game paralyzes a priest in group play, as they can do nothing but heal and sometimes melee? (or wand if it ever gets fixed.) Are they also aware of the emphasis this change puts on instant-casting spells and significantly encourages casting as many spells in a short span of time rather than actually including the use of strategy and tactics in our gaming? Could there at least be some spells that do not create the "regen void", say Dispel Magic and Nullify Disease?

5. What can we expect from itemization? Will there ever be enough cloth to balance the disproportion of players that use it and the ability of other classes to use cloth but not vice versa? Since the total nerfage to +damage items, notably Shadoweave armor for us, will there be any effects on equipment that we can look for besides simple +spirit and +intellect?

6. My armor seems to lose durability very quickly - moreso than a tank's would - when I solo and am therefore tanking for myself. While it is reasonable to assume a mage's robes would be ripped to shreds before a warrior's steel breastplate, is there anything we, as priests, can do when soloing for ourselves to prevent this, as we have no frost spells and no pet to tank for us? Is durability supposed to decrease even while Shield is in effect?

7. Regarding the three "branches" of our spells, what can we expect to see added to Discipline to give it more than just shield and mana burn? (For that matter, what other skills/spells will we have to look forward to?) Will Discipline be made a true branch of its own so that effects that negate one school of magic do not blot out Discipline and Holy, or Discipline and Shadow? Will there ever be any significant offensive spells outside of Shadow, or is that supposed to be counterpoint to healing spells being Holy?

8. While Blizzard claims that the developers regard the class forums more than the general forum, will we ever even semi-consistently see blue littering our thread listings? It may very well be that the programmers and designers and "non-PR" people that don't comment at all on the boards do read these threads, but without anyone posting, as near as we can tell, we are being nearly ignored.
