 1. Demonology talent list here. | 10/12/2004 1:51:36 AM GMTDT
First tier

Demonic embrace (0/5) increases total stamina by 3%, lowers spirit by 1%.

Improved imp (0/3) increases the effects of firebolt, fire shield, blood pact by 10% per level

Improved healthstone (0/2) increases the amount healed by 10% per level.

2nd tier

Fel intellect (0/5) increases total mana of imp/vw/suc/felhunter by 3% per point.

Improved VW (0/3) increases VW spell effects (torment, consume shadows, etc) by 8%

Improved health funnel (0/2) improves the health transffered by 10% per point.

3rd tier:

Improved stamina (0/5): increases pet health by 2% per point. (imp, succ, vw, felhunter - nothing about infernals)

Fel domination (0/1) - 15 minute cooldown, instacast, lowers pet summoning time by 5.5 seconds and halves the mana cost.

Improved succubus (0/3) - spell effects and duration increased by 8% per point.

4th tier:

Master conjurer (0/2) reduces the summoning cost and time of stones by 20%

Master summoner (0/2) reduces the cast time on summoning spells by 2 seconds per level

5th tier:

Enslave demon (0/5) - reduces the penalties associated with the spell by 2% per point.

Demonic sacrifice (0/1) - sacrifices the pet to obtain one of the following effects for 30 minutes:

imp: +15% fire damage
VW: +15% total health
Succubus: +15% shadow damage
Felhunter: +20% total mana

If a pet is summoned, this effect is instantly cancelled.

Improved firestone (0/2) - increases firestone damage by 15% per point.

6th tier:

Unholy Power (0/5) - pet melee attacks are increased by 3% per point.

7th tier:

Soul link (0/1) - no cooldown, insta cast ability, when used it allows you to split 25% of the incoming damage with your pet. Lasts 30 seconds.

Improved soulstone (0/2) - 15% more damage absorbed per point.
