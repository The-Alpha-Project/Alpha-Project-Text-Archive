    Actually, this isn't really a good patch for rogues...

    Master of Deception, after some tests, seems to have had it's effect reduced. Now it appears the 5 additional MoD points grant you the ability to close with an an even levelled opponent by one extra yard before being spotted. It is really, in my opinion, not worth the 5 points.

    Stealth is also acting strangely, and lower level mobs are spotting through it. Walk out out of IF and stand near a level 8 bear stealthed, he WILL attack you. This happened to me, and I was level 58 with 5 points in MoD. During a PvP back in forth in Hillsbard, a nearby level 22 bear attacked me revealing my stealth to a nearby level 60 player. I haven't tested this thouroughly, but it almost seems as if the lower level mobs are MORE agressive when you are stealthed, as they would otherwise be ...liek their agro checks are made quicker if you are stealthed. They are without a doubt able to see stealthers better, and much higher levels.

    Rogues that pop into stealth on a players screen when they are finally detected pop in fully colored, then shift a see through siloeutte after a second. This may have been like this for a while, but was quite noticable during my stlath tests and worth mentioning. It CLEARLY gives away the rogue.

    Range of sap (and other specials have been reduced). This is extremely noticable, and somewhat problematic in PvP when you are pursuing an oponnent, and his exact postion is a bit dodgy due to lag. Essentially, for most oponnents in PvP, unless you are standing RIGHT on top of them, you cannot use your special ...a particular problem for from stealth abilities, such as sap. If the opponent is running, you have a more difficult time hitting him.

    Specials are missing more. In one PvP engagement I hade a warrior escape from a sinister strike, gouge, then blind all in succession, by blocking, parrying, or simply having the effect miss. Not sure if this is just luck at this point, or the blocking and parying are of specials are constituting a huge factor, but I definatley felt less effective then in the previous patch this one night --but more testing is reuired.

    Camouflage has had it's speed increase reduced. Previously you could almost, but not quite keep pace with a running opponent. Now you are are only getting a 15% speed increase for 5 points. The difference of having 5 points on camouflage and not having it is really, not too noticable any more. I had to rece Tibsrogue sevral times to see it, and when I was out in the feild with Malaka I had to ask him if he had it because he coudl keep up with me so well (he didn't have it).

    So there have been some changes...
