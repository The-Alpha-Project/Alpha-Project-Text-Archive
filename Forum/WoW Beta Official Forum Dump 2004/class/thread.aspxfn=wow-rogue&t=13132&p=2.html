 36. Re: Upgrading Daggers, and Gripe about BOP (W | 10/15/2004 2:46:49 AM GMTDT
Just a note about Blues...there are several blue daggers which are not Bind on Pickup. You see em on the AH from time to time...

Generally speaking, the rules go like this:

If the item is an item which ONLY drops off of one specific MOB, or is a Quest Reward, it is Bind on Pickup. This both prevents farming and makes it so you actually have a feeling of accomplishment when you go out and get it.

If the item is a random drop (either off MOBs in an Instance, or a World Drop), it is Bind on Equip.

Going down the list of blues which are available as BoE, lvl 20-35:
Blackfang (20)
Vendetta (26)
Claw of the Shadowmancer (27)
Howling Blade (31)
Stonevault Shiv (31)
Silverblade (32)
The Ziggler (34)

Going down the list of greens which are BoE in that same range:
Deadly Bronze Poinard (20 - Crafted)
Wyvern Tailspike (21)
Naraxis Fang (22)
Cross Dagger (23)
Flesh Piercer (24)
Daring Dirk (29 - Bought from Jutak in STV)
Glinting Steel Dagger (31 - Crafted)
Tigerbane (34)


So, I think there is a fairly good range of BoE daggers...problem is in the AH, you generally will only see DBPs and Glinting Steel Daggers.

I think an easy solution to this would be just to add another craftable dagger, say lvl 25, which can be a bridge between the DBP and Glinting Steel.
