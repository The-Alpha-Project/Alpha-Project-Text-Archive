There are more uses for our spells than just PvP. Please remember that when analyzing things. Our DoT spells are very potent in PvE since the vast majority of mobs have no way of dispelling them.

Warlocks aren't supposed to be nukers. Shadow Bolt and Searing Pain are in place to supplement our other methods of dealing damage. Searing Pain, though inferior to Shadow Bolt in most instances, is useful against shadow resistant mobs or if you need the faster cast time.

The imp isn't a tank. I don't know why you listed his feeble HP as a drawback. Any warlock that's used him knows he can't take a hit.

However, I agree that Sleep would be a great addition if Blizzard wants warlocks to be the crowd control class, as it would work around the weakness that was recently put in for the succubus (though it would all but remove any reason to use her for CC). Ultimately that's up to the devs to decide, and they're more than entitled to change their minds on what role they want the warlock to play in the game.
