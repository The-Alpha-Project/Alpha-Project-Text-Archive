I feel this is testament that rogue talent trees are quite balanced. (some tweaking could be done... like premeditate and vigor being not as strong as adrenaline rush, even situationally).

There are several viable combinations, and for the most part many of them work quite well. However, they do not all work as well in different situations.

A rogue who specs mainly into openers, ( imp cs, imp ambush, initiative, premeditate ) combined with assassination has a huge opener/finisher in the first round of fighting. However, this same rogue caught unstealthed and not attacking first is at a disadvantage compared to combat rogues, whom skills mainly do not depend on their stealth status, or that they open an attack.

Blade fury, imp snd, adrenaline rush, allows a rogue to have a very powerful sustained dps for 15seconds.

Thats just two concentration of builds, there are many others that combine their advantages and disadvantages. Are there builds that completely suck? there certainly is, and im sure people could think of ways to crapify things.
