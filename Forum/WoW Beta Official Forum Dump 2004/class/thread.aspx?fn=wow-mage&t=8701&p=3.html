I agree with the issues that Tikk brought here.

I'm on the Beta server now as you can see, but I'm planning to roll a mage PvP.
Mage definitely needs an improvement atm.

** Right now meele classes can easily heal themselves using firs aid and avoid down time all together pretty much. There is no "first aid" skill for us to replenish mana that way quickly and consistently (I don't count Evocation in, because it is a talent and not as effective as first aid).
** Water definitely needs to be improved more. There are ppl with mana pools close to 10K now. Currently water doesn't replenish this mana pool even if you sit and drink for the whole duration.
** I think that int should be direct damage modifier as it was already stated in this thread.
** I would like to see critical spell damage doing 100% of additional damage - same way it is done for melee.

As many ppl stated I would like to see an improvement to the frost line.
* An additional high damage one target spell should be introduced (not as high dmg as fireblast though).
* Frost nova talent should reduce cooldown of nova by 3 seconds per each talent point.
* Ice block should be swapped with Ice barrier again. And the mage should be able to cast frost spells while under the ice block.

Improvements to the arcane line:
* Arcane meditation should allow to regenerate at least 30% of normal rate with full talent investment.
* Invisibility should be at the bottom of arcane line. The way to fix invisibility for pvp is to make it same as an effect spell. As many ppl suggested invisibility effect should drain mana may be around 100 mana/sec. When you cancel invisibility all you mana gets depleted and you cannot regenerate mana or cast spells for 10 sec. (Evocation gets disabled for this duration too).

Fire line:
* So far it seems good to me. May iproved scorch needs to be upped. Reduce damage period to 2 seconds with 50% of the spell damage added. And move it close to the bottom.
