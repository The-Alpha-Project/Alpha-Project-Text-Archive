 1. Enchanted Gold Bloodrobe | 10/20/2004 9:32:53 PM GMTDT
I just spent the last couple hours doing the majority of this quest.

I was lucky and found 2 gold bars in a chest a day or two ago, so that part was extremely easy.

Now, here's How the quest works.

At level 32 you can get this quest.

Here are the components needed.

1) Robes of Arcana
http://www.thottbot.com/?i=1542
The stats of the robe are +8 intellect +6 spirit.
The thottbot link shows the rarity of the pattern, and the stats of the robe in question. In fact, this pattern can only be recieved off of mobs found in alliance zones. So, come retail, you probably won't see any horde warlocks doing this quest.
If you don't have the pattern but know someone who does, this requires
16x Silk
2x Spider's Silk
2x Fine thread
Or if you don't have that they tend to usually sell for anywhere between 3 and 6g at the AH.

2) Gold Bar
For some this may be easy, for others not as much so. Gold bars can be hard to come by.

3) 10x Vial of Hatefury Blood from level 31-33 Satyr in NorthEast Desolace.
It took me quite a few kills to get this. In the 20-30 range.

4) Lesser Infernal Stone
These are level 36/37 mobs in Mannoroc Coven in Southernish Desolace. Not an easy mob at level 32 when you can first get this quest.

5) Soul Shard - Easy enough, you make those.

6) Golden Thread - Easy enough, you already did that part, you just have to pick it up.

7) Smoldering Coal - Here's the problem, the mobs in question that drop this, are level 38/39.
This is quite the difficult task for a solo level 32 warlock to walk up to a 38/39 to try and get these smoldering coals for their level 32 warlock quest.

Granted, you don't have to do quests as soon as you get them, but in this case you do, and here's why.

http://www.thottbot.com/?i=3967

You do all that work, and turn a +8 intellect +6 spirit robe into a +8 stamina and +11 spirit robe. That's pretty lame IMO.

Now, let's compare this robe, to other robes available.

http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=2761
IMO much better than the enchanted gold blood robe, and takes much less effort.

The named that drops these is near the front of the instances, and has a 38% chance to drop those robes.
http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=57744
