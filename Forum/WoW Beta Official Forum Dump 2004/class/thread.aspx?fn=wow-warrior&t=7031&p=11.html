Also, warriors have now lost one of the most important tools in their arsenal, which is any ability to keep classes with snare and stun abilities in melee combat.

Taking a deeper look into this PVPish issue its odd that caster types that can attack from a distance get snares that are devastating to a melee class basically removing them from the fight for quite a while letting the casters pound on them again and again. Yet after a nerfed charge and hamstring now warriors can only watch in slow motion as the casters move away to hit them. It just makes more sense to me that casters get snares as a form of offense and defense and melee classes should get stuns or even a snare for the same reasons.

It seems to me that blizz has been trying to balance warriors more for PVE than PVP. What blizz has to take into account is the difference of awareness of advantages and disadvantages between npcs and players. A caster or melee type mob will fight a mage the same way they will fight a warrior oblivious to the any advantage or disadvantage they have. A player will always try to maximize his or her advantages and minimize their disadvantage right from the start. In this case a warrior has lost one of their only ways to maximize their advantage.

This hasn't been as specific as I want because many of the skills a warrior has aren't broken and have usefulness in PVE, but not PVP unlike other classes like mages where their skills are useful in both. Some suggestions to improve warriors:

1. Change hamstring to keep the target from moving for 10 secs (no stun they can cast and use melee just no running away.)

2. Change charge to have no stun affect and than have it work in 1 of 2 ways
A. Produces rage in some amount and hits for a defined amount of damage.
B. It instantly hits a target with a normal melee hit and warriors just get the rage produced from hitting the target with the disadvantage of being able to miss and gain no rage.

3. Battle Stance should add +10 attack and Berserker Stance should have a similar affect be it increased attack power or rage production.

4. Rend and Thunderclap need to be changed to be more useful or cost less.
