 5. Re: Coolest pet level 1-10, 10-20, 20-30? | 10/18/2004 4:31:16 PM GMTDT
At the lower levels, especially as new servers start up, coolness can often be achieved by getting a pet from deep within the other faction's territory, from a place that is very difficult for other hunters around your level to reach or that new players with hunters might not even be aware of.

For example, when I first starting my NE Hunter, right after Hunters became available, as soon as I hit level 10, I made my way to the Echo Islands in the Horde lands. Getting there involved many deaths but once I got there, I tamed a level 8 bengal tiger (classic orange with black stripes). Every other NE hunter my level had the typical cat mobs from their starter area or Darkshore. I got a couple of tells an hour asking where it came from. Most just assumed you could not get an orange and black striped tiger until you were high enough level for Stranglethorn Vale. Another good one to grab at low level is the red and black named scorpion near the Orc starting area. And if you are really feeling bold, make your way from the Alliance lands at level 10 all the way to the Undead lands and grab yourself a bat as a pet, then head back over to Darkshore or Elwynn and you are sure to raise eyebrows.

Once you get to the higher levels, you can achieve a strong coolness factor by getting pets that can't be tamed until you are in the level 58-60 range. I know of one particular creature that my Hunter will be taming if he ever makes it to 60. It's really my strongest motivation at the moment for playing the character.
