 1. Windfury bugged? | 10/1/2004 4:01:07 PM GMTDT
I've been doing some tests with windfury the last two patches and I've noticed some problems.

The desciption of windfury is:
Imbue the Shaman's weapon with wind. Each hit has a 20% chance of granting you 2 extra attacks with 665 extra attack power. Lasts for 5 minutes.

This would lead me to believe that the next attack round I would get a bonus 665 attack power for a total of 3 melee hits.

After doing some testing I've come to the conclusion that this is not working as intended, or described. What happens is that the attack round that you proc Windfury on will get the bonus attack power mod on that melee hit, the next attack round the first melee hit and rarely the second melee hit will get the bonus attack power, but the third melee hit will not. As it stands now, windfury is hardly worth it compared to Rockbiter.

According to the description the bonus attack power should be added on the next attack round, not the "procced" attack round and should then last for that entire attack round that should be a total of 3 melee hits (or misses).
