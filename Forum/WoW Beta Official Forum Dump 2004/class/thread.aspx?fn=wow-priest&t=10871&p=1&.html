Just trying to answer the original question.

The healing tree lets you heal mabye 10-15% more efficiently than a shadow talent spec. (lower mana cost and your regen chance on criticals). What this translates in terms of group efficieny (being BETTER) really comes down to two cases.

A. If your not that great of a healer, or your group sucks. Then these improvements will help save your group. Combat ress will save you possibly if you mess up. Holy Nova will help you loose aggro if your warrior sucks. (Or if you get Inner focus, that one free greater heal or prayer of healing may be the last heal you need to keep the tank alive the second before the boss dies.) If your good, your group is good, see case B!

B. If your group is right on the cusp of being able to do a particular encounter, these improvement in healing MIGHT help keep your group alive long enough to push you past. Your group are just doing a challenging encounter, and need all the extra heal juice you can get...How often does this happen? That depends on you... Are you trying to 5 man Strath? Then you might need it! :) haha


People rant and rave about Inner focus as an instance necessity talent. So you get 1 extra spell off, just defeat the encounter before that happens, Or get a mana potion, or get some mana plants from felwood, or get some demonic runes.

This same concept applys to the whole holy tree. If your having difficulty with particular encounters, your group might NEED you to be holy / disc specced.
