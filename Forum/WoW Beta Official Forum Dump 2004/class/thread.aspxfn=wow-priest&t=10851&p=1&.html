0 mana regen because you will not regen mana
1) while casting a spell
or
2) for 5 seconds following the casting (or partial casting and subsequent interuption) of a spell. (refered to as the 5 second rule)

Casting a spell 5.5 seconds after casting anything would not allow you 2 seconds after the end of the 5 second rule. Hence zero mana regen.
