 1. Cool DUAL WIELD Builds! | 10/22/2004 8:21:51 AM GMTDT
I saw a couple of questions. People claiming that high damage 2hded weapons with mortal strike is the only way to build an offensive warrior - wrong! There are some VERY effective dual wield warrior builds avaliable. The problem is everyone wants to feel like they have 1 UBER ability like mortal strike - you don't need it to dish out damage.

First off. The key to a dual wield warrior...

HEROIC STRIKE..

It's cheap and very effective and it works on your NEXT weapon strike (at rank 8 its +128 damage per strike with NO COOLDOWN). With a fast weapon you can spam this ability very quickly for a huge amount of burst damage. Something that slow 2hders don't benefit from. It doesn't neccesarily BEAT mortal strike but it's damn effective.

Look at a highlevel warrior with 50 rage. Over 10 seconds.

With a 2.2 speed 1 hand weapon - thats four heroic strikes. At +128 per strike. +500 damage.

With a 4.0 speed 2hded weapon - thats 2 mortal strike (20 rage). 380 for each mortal strike. That's +760 damage.

It about 260 damage difference. I think this would be covered in the STR increase you get on your DPS for 2 one handed weapons. Right now with the miss factor it might seem to be a bit broken.

But best part about all of this? You saved yourself 6 talent points. Opening up a lot of fun abilties.

So here's some fun builds. Just remember to mash your heroic strike. I put it in ability slot one and just finger the "1" button during combat.

"Vapula's stun lock of death!" - 51 points ARMS/PROT - Dual wielding Maces.

ARMS (30 points) :

Improved Heroic strike x 3 (duh)
Deflection x 4
Improved Charge x 2
Improved Rend x 3
Improved Overpower x 2
Tactical Mastery x 5
Deep wounds x3
Anger Management x 1
Impale x 2
Mace Spec x 5

PROT (21 points) :

Anticipation x 5
Toughness x 5
Improved Bloodrage x 3
Last stand x 1
Improved revenge x 3
Improved taunt x 2 (want this for off tanking)
Imporved sunder armor x 2
Concussive Blow x 1

You're tanking a mob or a player so have STAM/STR. The key to this build is switching stances during the fight. He dodges - switch to arms and hit him with overpower. You parry - switch to protection and revenge him. Open up with concussive blow and watch him squirm when you are racking up stuns with your maces. This build is great against casters of any kind.


"Vapula's Flurry of Death" - 51 points - ARMS/FURY - Dual wielding swords and daggers and fist weapons.

ARMS (20 points) :

Flurry x 5

Ever wanted to play a rogue in plate armor with 3000 hitpoints? Here's your chance. Go ALL STRENGTH. Get the fastest weapons you can get. Sit in arms stance and spam heroic strike while waiting for your 3 minute Inner Rage to cool down. When you really want to hurt someone and they cant hurt you back switch to Zerker click on death wish and watch the fireworks. I'll warn you now...if you like to use a bunch of abilities and have 1 super powerful ability - THIS IS NOT THE BUILD FOR YOU. With this build "the devil is in the details". Lots of rage - expended quick on heroic strikes every 2 seconds...for teh win!

don't forget double the mitigation!

Two handed weapons are mitigated once per attack (every strike). One handed weapons are mitigated for the SAME ammount (or so I understand), so having each strike for less and be mitigated for the same amount means less damage overall.

Lets say the 2H strikes for 200 and the 1H strikes for 100. The enemies armor mitigates 20 physical damage on average.

2H = 180 (10% reduction)
1H = 80 (20% reduction)

If that's how it works (that's how I understand it to -- if it's percent based, my arguement is invalid) then 2H > 1H in damage. What you gain by dual weild is more hits, which generates rage a bit faster -- and interrupts casters.

If Dual Weild and 2 Handers had the same damage, I think a lot more people will be going Dual Weild, because they get the same damage PLUS rage in smaller and faster portions PLUS better interruption rate vs. casters.
