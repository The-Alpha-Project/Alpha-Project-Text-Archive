 4. Re: Opinions on these two templates please | 10/21/2004 2:23:12 AM GMTDT
Fire does more damage if you are taking your newest fireball compared against your newest frost bolt and doing one cast each.

However, fireball takes longer to cast. It uses more mana. In the end, those seem to balance out fairly evenly unless you try to throw in extra tricks like a 100% crit or pyro as first hit.

Try pyroing every mob in an instance. You cant.

They all seem to do fine soloing. It all depends on if you want more CC in groups, are going to PVP, and whatever else you might be concerned with in personal play style.

Dont be lured only by large damage numbers or a mana regen talent. Using less mana with faster casting spells & slow provides a balance that some people dont consider.
