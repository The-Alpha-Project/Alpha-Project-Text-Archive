 1. If I could change Priest spells.... | 10/23/2004 6:47:00 AM GMTDT
First let me get Fire Ward up and going, and grab a Fire Prot. Potion. :)


Anyway, this is what I would do, strictly spell wise. Of course, it's also mostly brainstorming. :p If I really had to pick something to do, it would be Brainwash, and Mind Tap. :)

  • Essentially, my plan is to make Holy be mostly healing, some damage, minor utility; Discipline mostly utility, some damage; Shadow mostly damage, some utility; minor healing (Vampiric being the minor healing).

    General

  • Healing spells now awards threat based on the actual amount of hitpoints healed. For example, if a 1000 hitpoint spell would normally award 1000 threat if it fully healed, that same spell would only give 200 threat if the spell healed for 200 (the 200 was all the target was missing), instead of the full 1000.
  • Undead now are divided into two types: Undead and Spirits. "Undead" are classified as zombies, skeletons, and ghouls. Spirits are classified into apparitions, banshees, and wraiths/shades. (new) The idea here is for Banish to be able to deal with Spirits also, hence the subtypes.

    Holy
    - Smite renamed back to Holy Smite :p
    - Holy Fire cast time decreased (3 seconds)
    - Holy Nova now heals the party for X% of the total damage it does
    - (New) Divination -- reveals target's resistances and armor, and prevents the target from going into stealth or invisibility
    - (New) Cure/Abolish Poison
    - (New) Cure/Abolish Curse
    - (Add) Word of Condemnation - blasts the target Undead/Spirit/Humanoid with holy energy, causing it to be unable to act for X seconds; damage will dissipate the field and cancel the effect
  • (New) Holy Storm - nearby Undead/Spirits will take X-Y holy damage per Z seconds, and allies will be healed every Z seconds

    Discipline
  • (New) Word of Sanctity - provides a massive increase to resistances to the target over a short period of time
  • Power Word: Fortitude renamed to Word of Fortitude; the effect now provides a massive increase to armor over a short period of time (think of a very high powered Guardian Talisman effect)
  • Power Word: Shield renamed to Word of Shielding and is now self-only with a cooldown (Weakened Soul removed); talent increases the absorption of it (and then I'd remove Focused Casting from Discipline; Martyrdom I'd change to Clearcast while active instead of prevent interruption)
    - Inner Fire now 5 minutes in duration (from 3)
    - Shackle Undead renamed to Word of Condemnation; returned to Holy
    - (New) Word of Attraction -- causes nearby NPCs to tend to attack the target more, increasing the likelyhood that their next attacks/spells will target the recipient of this spell instead of their original target (think "melee/spell grounding totem", only a chance to occur)
    - (New) Humility (channel) decreases the Priest's threat with nearby targets while channeling
  • Divine Spirit renamed to Word of Spirituality; provides a massive increase to Spirit over a short period of time. (Should this be a core spell or talent spell, you think?)


    Shadow
    - (Removed) Fade (give to Mage)
    - (Removed) Dispel Magic (give to Mage)
    - Shadow Word: Pain renamed to Word of Pain
    - Psychic Scream now dazes (snares) the afflicted, instead of fears
  • Mind Blast now has a chance to interrupt spellcasting
    - (New) Mind Shock -- damages the enemy for X-Y shadow damage, and has a chance to stun Humanoid targets for Y seconds (not counting Blackout)
    - (New) Mind Tap (channel) -- invades the target's mind; if the Priest channels for the full duration, all resistances decrease by X, armor by Y, and reduces the likelyhood of Mind Control breaking; causes a very high amount of threat
    - (New) Brainwash -- decreases the Priest's threat with the target over time (threat reduction "DoT" in other words)

    EDIT
    - All new stuff is denoted by the dot.
