The Shaman has three great PvP abilities: Shock(s), Insta Healing, and Improved Ghost Wolf (to run down stragglers). That being said, I wanted as much critical damage as possible, especially Lighting based Criticals:

Elemental Combat Talents (23 points)

# Concussion - 5/5 points

Reduces the Mana cost of your Lightning Bolt spell by 15%.

# Convection - 5/5 points
Reduces the Mana cost of your Shock spells by 10%.

# Elemental Fury - 1/1 point
Increases the critical strike damage done by your Fire, Frost, and Nature spells by 100%.

# Improved Magma Totem - 1/2 point

 4. Re: Opinions on this template please | 10/20/2004 11:07:15 PM GMTDT
I'm an advocate for a well balanced spec, but if your friend must turn his shaman into a one trick pony, he does have a pretty nice burst DPS if the circumstances are ideal.

At mid-range, you can open with a lightning bolt, then a shock, then an instant cast / auto crit chain lightning, then another shock, plus you can drop an improved fire nova. At 60, you're looking at close to 3000 damage without counting the potential for regular crits in 5 seconds (after your initial lighning bolt cast). I wouldn't count on getting that combo off on a regular basis, especially in group pvp, but if you do, you'll be standing over someone's smouldering corpse, giggling like a school girl.
