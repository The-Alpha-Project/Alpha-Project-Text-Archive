How fun are warlocks?

This class is great. Maybe not the most powerful but definately one with excitement.

Example:

I was partnered with another Warlock in Westfall. We were hunting the Murlocs on the coast when we had the great idea to take on Old Mad Eye (level 20 elite). We were 18 and 17 each.

He had the imp and I had the voidwalker. We pull and dot him up all nice but he is just not falling. Soon my voidwalker falls and because of how it worked out, he went to smack down on my partner. I feared him (luckily) and then started summoning my voidwalker. tick tock, tick tock, watching the casting bar, watching to see when the fear would break. Partner was out of mana...I only had about 50% left. Fear breaks. Hear comes the big guy. I see him running right at me. In pops my void walker. I send him to intercept and taunt. They duke it out and finally we knock him over. Both of us near death, oom, and completely stoked that we just felled an elite mob that took both of our combined strengths and skills.

Between all the resists and near deaths that was one of the greatest battles I ever had in WoW.

Sure a mage could've done it easier but to me, that would not be as fun.

Since then when I group, I long for these types of battles where one gets feared, another is polymorphed, all the while spells, swords, and souls are flying every direction....WEEEE!

So yeah, Warlocks are fun.
