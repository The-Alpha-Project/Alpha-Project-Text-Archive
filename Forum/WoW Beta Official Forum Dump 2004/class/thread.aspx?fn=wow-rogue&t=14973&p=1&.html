 3. Re: Criticize this build! | 10/24/2004 12:28:05 PM GMTDT
As previously stated, you are going too much into the Combat tree. Blade Flurry is not a talent you will need if you are going with Ambush/Backstab. It is a good talent but not for what you are aiming for. You may not have been aiming for an Ambush/Backstabbed focused build but you said "up-front" and you can't beat a good Ambush/BS build in that area.

What you'll be aiming for will be something along the lines of this:

Subtlety Mastery
Camouflage Rank 5
Opportunity Rank 5
Improved Ambush Rank 3
Subtlety Total: 13

Combat Mastery
Improved Gouge Rank 3
Improved Sinister Strike Rank 2
Improved Backstab Rank 3
Combat Total: 8

Assassination Mastery
Improved Eviscerate Rank 3
Remorseless Attacks Rank 5
Malice Rank 5
Ruthlessness Rank 3
Relentless Strikes Rank 1
Lethality Rank 5
Improved Instant Poison Rank 2
Cold Blood Rank 1
Seal Fate Rank 5
Assassination Total: 30

Total
Total Points Spent: 51
Level Required: 60

Make your own adjustments but certain skills are key like Malice, Lethality, Opportunity, Imp Ambush, Imp Backstab and I suggest Imp Gouge as well if you plan on using Backstab often.

You don't need to take Camo, stick with MoD if you wish. I just picked that because I'm currently using it to see which I would prefer.

Cold Blood is a very useful skill, especially for Ambush/Backstab builds. *If* you hit, it'll be for double your normal damage, if you crit along with that then your gold.

Remorseless is up to you if you want that or not. I found uses for it but not everybody likes the skill anymore. For me, mainly because I consatntly take 2v1 on PvP and having 100% damage bonus after I vanish and Ambush again is great. Thats just me though and you may not use it as much as I do so picking up a different skill may benefit you more.

The 2 points in Instant Poisons is up to you. I prefer Instant Poison over Vile Poisons mainly because with the speed that I end most fights I rather increase the chance the poison will proc rather than the damage it'll do. Of course you could stick these 2 points into something like Imp Kidney Shot or something.

Seal Fate is a great skill when used with a dagger build. If your Ambush crits and it should most of the time, you'll get an extra combo point. Same goes for Backstab and for daggers in general you'll crit pretty often.

This is just a rough build to give you some ideas and nothing really that was worked on. Think of it as a guide.

If you were looking for Combat type of talents then Backstab isn't really for you. Combat is much better suited for swords/maces than a little dagger. Of course that is damage over time, not a big amount of damage your first hit.

 5. Re: Criticize this build! | 10/24/2004 2:36:06 PM GMTDT
Actually I disagree with the guys above and will say that it isn't a terrible build at all... but I'll point out the weaknesses of it.

1. Blade Flurry is a 20% attack speed increase. Compared with SnD 30% increase (with several seconds per combo point), it's not as good. What it IS good for is getting mace stuns, sword extra attacks, and pulling some aggro to you and popping evasion. Remember that it's only 12 seconds, it's not a major player but more of an extra edge.

2. With your build, where you have medium openers and medium constant dps, you are going to need improved kidney strike. Consider it.

3. Camoflage is far superior to MOD, esp. if you plan to cheap shot ever.

4. Maxing out eviscerate means to make it effective you need lots of combo points. So not taking the improves Sinister Strike is a mistake.

Overall, I'd say your build is very unfocused, which also means it's adaptable. With some tweaks and a focus on +agi +stamina, you could do ok. But if you want to ambush, ambush. If you want combat, do it. Don't take things like improved sprint that really help neither. Both ambush and combat are good in PvP, but in different ways, so try both and decide for yourself.
