Rogue have many detriments.

Poisons -> Seem great. However, a simple fact is neglicted. All poisons that go on weapons need to be done before combat is started. If you want to prepare for a mage, apply poions, want to prepare to slow an enemy, prepare poisons, want to prepare for Dot damage, etc. We need to apply our poisons Before combat ensues. And we can't flip em at a moments notice. Unlike a casting class that can bust out what is needed the moment is needed, we need to prepare before, and if the situation changes, oh well. I compare this mostly to the shaman, they bust out the totem they need, when they need it. (Bet example drawn to the gounding totem.) So in the end, this doesn't put us up on much of anyone.

Stealth -> Curretnly our trademark. However, with all the detect Stealth possabilities (and more bveing discovered.) This main advantage can be mostly negated. It is now more of a fuzzy impage. If the person is paying attention, we can't relly surprise em, if they are unwary, afk or sleeping, we have a chance.

DPS -> We are melee. As such we suffer to large armor classes. Relegating us to caster killers. Where as casters don't much worry about the oponnants AC. On a damage basis we are out damaged by mages. IN addition, a majority of our DPS is Melee, meaning we need to be up close and personal, where as casting classes can sit back and nuke. When the mob aggros them, it takes a few secs to do so. Not a quick turn and pounding as rogue recieve.

Grouping -> Why have a rogue when any roll they can fill is better filled by a mage (DPS & CC), Generally the reason to have a rogue is they are a guildee or they are a friend. Not much reason to search out a rogue for a group.

CC -> Only on humanoinds. Only from Behind (sorta). Variable Duration. Do not always remain undetected after sucess or failure. (which draws all the aggro nearby and has a high propensity to end in wipe out)

Equipment -> We rely on equipment greatly and weapons even more so. Like the warrior. Equipment costs money. Where as the mage is not nearly as dependant on Equipment for damage.

Gold -> Equipment, POisons and training. We are a majro gold sink. You can't continuously use poisons without ending up in the poor house. (UNless you have been max level for a whiel and had nothign else ot do but farm gold). Again caompared to other cloasses our gold costs are much higher.

I have actually been trying to think about what are the major benefits to rogues,a dn well, I haev kind of run dry. Not sure. I am sure we are better at something than a mage, or other class we vie for group spots with. Not really sure what though. I am kinda tired, and only woke up to start this patch.

Wish Blizzard would impart to us what they plabn for the rogues. I left DaoC because I got tired of waiting for Thane love. It did not come in two years, and I was gone. Now i am here, and for some reason, I am starting to feel that the Rogue will be the Thane of WoW. The class that people want to like, but the company just makes it impossible to do. A class that people play, and kick themselves every day and ask why they are being usch masocists.

Again, I have faith that Blizz has some plan to make rogues group friendly (was one thing I loved hwen they first came out, and is a major reason I have stayed with them.
