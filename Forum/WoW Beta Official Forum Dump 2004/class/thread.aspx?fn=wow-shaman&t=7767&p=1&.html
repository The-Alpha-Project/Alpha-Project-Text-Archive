Hm I would definately suggest going with 1h + shield since you'll have a DPS partner. You might also consider going more Agility + Stamina and less Int + Spirit for the same reason.

You should definately take first aggro and the Mage would back you up. You'll basically be a tank.

You'll probably want to play a little more defensively and not dump all your mana trying to kill, since your pool won't be as big as the Mage and you will regen slower.

Just jump in there and smash heads, toss a few totems here and there and maybe a nuke or insta nuke sprinkled around.


You might consider making your best damage tools melee + lightning shield. Just make sure to get the talents to back that up.

 5. Re: Shaman PvE build w/ Mage Duo | 10/20/2004 6:36:47 AM GMTDT
Go full elemental with maybe some in restoration.

There's no way you're going to be able to hold the aggro unless the mage lets you get in first and deal damage, which slows you down. Slow is no fun! Your best option is to use earthbind so that you and the mage can easily kite.

Once you get high up into elemental you get 2 second cast time lightning bolts that hit hard and heavy damaging shocks every 5 seconds. You'll both be dealing so much fast damage that regular monsters won't get to you, and if you get adds, just make it into an area of effect party. The mage gets all of the aggro with their various area of effect spells, you drop a fire nova totem and throw a 1.5 second cast chain lightning.

Plus, with a mage nearby you've got plenty of free drinks which eliminates the biggest downside of going elemental.
