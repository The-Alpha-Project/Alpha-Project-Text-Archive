 92. Re: Missing like crazy. | 10/14/2004 5:39:53 PM GMTDT
Here it is, straight from the QA.

The miss ratio against an even level NPC with maximum weapon proficiency for the level has not been changed. What can be misleading is that if an NPC rolls a successful block but has no shield, it will be reported as a miss. The same holds true for NPCs that parry but are not holding a weapon. The combined total can be perceived to be double or even triple the normal miss rate, when in actuality up to 2/3 of that percentage is the NPC’s inherent avoidance skill and not reliant upon the player’s ability to hit.



We hope this clears up players’ concerns regarding this issue.
