 2. Re: Track Humanoid (lvl 32 spell) - useful? | 9/29/2004 8:17:08 PM GMTDT
Track humanoid is extremely useful, especially once you get to the zones that contain both alliance and horde quests.

The range is so-so. Basically, zoom out all the way and then zoom in two clicks. That's your range on track.

If you take the speed talents for cat form, you can play as an effective scout both for setting up ambushes as well as avoiding them.

Humanoid characters (both PC and NPC) will show up as red dots on the map (much like herbs will show as yellow ones). I'd really like to see them add a different color for your faction vs opposing faction, and one for PC vs NPC. Currently, everything is the same red, and can get really confusing in a crowded NPC locations.

I haven't decided if I prefer the mini map track approach of WoW or the named-list track from EQ and Shadowbane.
