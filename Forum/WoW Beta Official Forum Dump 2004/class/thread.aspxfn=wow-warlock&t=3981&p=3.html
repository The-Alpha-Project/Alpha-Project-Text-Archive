Increase their DoT effectiveness by a lot. Warlocks power come from pets and dots mostly, and the pets are situational, like the voidwalker for example which is there to tank not deal damage. Decrease the duration of dot spells and increase the damage.

Give them more effective curses. Give them Curse of Mending to heal them when hit in melee, and Curse of Slow to slow enemies. Both would help them against melee, which is their huge weakness.

Disclaimer: A lot of this is my personal take on the Warlock class up to level 45. Other ideas were taken throughout the boards and credit has been given where I remember who said it =)

1) Curse of Recklessness
  • Should be given a snare effect.
  • This will make Fear more useful without overpowering it (they will hit you harder if fear resists).

    2) DO NOT make soulshards stackable.
  • Aggro management just like Soul Shard management defines a good Warlock and a great Warlock.
  • Stacking soul shards turns the Warlock into a stone factory. We don't want to pump these out all the time.
  • If anything, let them make 2 per stack.

    3) Warlock specific animations such as the ones Ultimus specified.
  • Drain Soul actually draining a soul from the mob to the Warlock (like the Vale Screechers quest, only their ghost appears and enters the Warlock's body)
  • Banshee screams and other ghastly sounds (Curse of Tongues is good!)
  • Phantom Skulls coming out of the Warlock's hand towards the target (or vise versa) on Drain Life / Drain Soul

    4) Voidwalker
  • Should look like a Shade and not a Blueberry (as Ultimus has stated).
  • Give the VW a defining ability/spell other than Torment.
  • Give the VW Consuming Rage (?) like the summoned one you have to kill for the scroll quest.
  • Make Sacrafice Autocast when the Warlock is at 5% health. With option to turn autocast off. Have yet to use this ability because I always forget its there!
  • Torment (rank 4) is great. But it needs to scale a little as you level. Or a new rank become available every 5 levels. Rank 3 did not work well at level 39.
  • DPS should be half the rate of the Imp. (at Level 44, imp hits for 53, VW should hit for 26 instead of 17-19)

    5) Succubus
  • Needs to get her damage output raised.
  • Should be the highest DPS pet (out of the Imp and VW)
  • Lash of Pain's "backstab" requirement should be removed so that it works in front of the mob as well. It is a whip, not a backstab.
  • New animation for Soothing Kiss
  • New animation (channeling/lease) for Seduction (floating hearts?)
  • Stands aroud too much looking dumb when she should be attacking. Animation problem or melee code problem? Needs fixing.

    6) Imp
  • Needs AI improvements.
  • Get rid of the new green aura look. Go back to push2/push3-prepatch.
  • Imp should be the best "utility" pet for group situations (That warrants using it over the Succubus's Seduction)
  • Want to see Imp get a "fade" spell so they don't always out aggro the Warlock.
  • More group-friendly utilities or improved current ones.

    7) Enslave Demon
  • Remove the soul shard requirement

    8) Ritual of Summoning
  • Make RoS kill your target and then summon their ghost (and body) to you.
  • Make them suffer ressurection effects when they get teleported to the Warlock.
  • Give RoS a confirmation box. Make the default timeout YES (if a player is AFK and you need to get them out of a dungeon on raids).
  • Reduce the 3 person requirement (caster plus 2 others) to no extra people. Not like Warlocks can feign death/invis into the very end of a dungeon anyway to summon people.

    9) Channeling
  • Make channeling less interruptable from regular melee.
  • Channeling spells should have an initial damage dealt (life drain instantly transfer 50 health, then starts channeling 41 per tick)
  • Lower the overall channeling time with each melee hit.
  • Hellfire should not be interruptable. If we have to give up our health to cast the spell, it should cast the whole way.

    10) More utility spells that we can transfer onto our pets.
  • Should be worthwhile in different situations.
  • Transfer our strength to our pet.
  • Transfer intellect.
  • Give our pet a % of our mana pool.
  • Give our pet Demon Skin
  • Make our pet hit faster and harder for X seconds and require a soul shard.

    11) MORE SPELLS MORE SPELLS MORE SPELLS.
  • Not just upgrades. I mean different spells. As it stands now, warlocks use 3 main spells. Curse of Weakness, Corruption and Immolate. Not much variety in battle.
  • Warlocks are supposed to be the masters of damage over time. Why are they limited to only Fire and Shadow damage? Add Frost, Nature (disease)..
  • Group spells.. channel life/mana from a mob and give to the party.

    12) Curse of the Elements
  • Should lower resistances instead of raise damage.

    13) Summon Nightmare
  • Give some specific abilities. Perhaps invisibility during the night hours.
  • Make it cost more and be more than just a "mount" - after all it is our level 40 pet.

    14) Death Pact / Death Coil
  • Instant transfer of warlock's current health to its target (group member, pet, etc).
  • 3 minute cooldown.
  • Handle aggro so that the Warlock doesn't die each time it is used.
  • Usable on other mobs, like WC3's Death Coil. Take Warlock's health to steal the enemy's.

    15) Fix it so that level 1 rabbits and other low level mobs miss a level 44 Warlock (and I supposed all classes) more often.
  • I fought some Zanzil guy in Stranglethorn that summoned level 10 pets. Every one of those blasted pets hit me every time they swung. Made it impossible to cast anything (375ish AC).
  • Remove the "zerg" effect.

    16) Make curse of agony worthwhile to use in place of Curse of Weakness.
  • No one in their right mind will sacrafice lowered melee damage for a long-duration DoT.
  • The damage saved to your pet with Curse of Weakness is greater than dealt from Curse of Agony.

    17) Summon Healthstone
  • Should TAKE your health instead of your mana to summon it. We bottle our health into the stone for others.
  • Takes 50% of the health amount that it will heal? Scalable with levels?

    18) Instant Lifetaps
  • Make them worthwhile but not overpowering that Life Drain is not used.
  • Make them require a Soul Shard if needed.
  • Possibly combine with Life Drain where when the spell is initially cast it transfers X health to the Warlock, then ticks at Y per tick.
  • Make it useful.

    19) I'd like to see a warlock able to cast two curses on a mob, with the restriction that the second curse doesn't last as long and doesn't do its full effect.
  • Example: Curse 1: Weakness, Curse 2: Agony (lasts 60 seconds, does 75% of its normal damage per tick).

    20) Reverse Ritual of Summoning.
  • Allow a Warlock to open the portal and step through it himself to go to a group member or nearby graveyard.
  • Usable to enter instances? Balance issue here with Warlock summoning himself to a mage who invis'ed through a portion of a zone.
  • Requirements unknown (4 soul shards, warlock gets killed, must have another warlock present to open the portal).

    21) Shapeshifting ability. Transfer your soul to a demon from the underworld.
  • Shapeshift into a Shade, or something demon-like.
  • Raises melee/tanking abilities but removes spellcasting abilities (for most spells?)

    22) Warlocks fight toe-to-toe with other mobs. Due to this we should be able to stand against them longer.
  • Make stamina scale as you level. As it stands now, I believe 1 stamina = 7 hit points for all levels (of casters?).
  • Give Warlocks more AC than mages. Mages get an AC buff similar to Demon Armor, it makes the two classes too alike.
