 1. Woud you give up undead bane abilites for.... | 7/27/2004 5:56:49 AM GMTDT
Would you give up your undead abilites for a ranged (min 25 yards)nuke that worked on all mobs?

Consider this scenario that happened to me at lvl 37:

I had been waving by low level undead almost all day long while simultaneously being mutilated by lvl 50 UD mages. Toward the end of the evening, I was running through Arathi highlands when a lvl 32 UD warrior and a lvl 37 UD warrior were spotted in the raod. I stopped aobut 100 yards short to try and let them by. THey of course, decided to gank me. They opened the battle with a charge. I then proceeded to give them a serious taste of paladin asswhooping. (I regret to say that I did have to use a greater healing potionduring the fight.)

After they were both dead and I had not even expended my lay hands, the 37 warrior logs onto his Alliance verbal abuse mule to tell me how that was so unfair. He continued to rant about how paladins were so overpwoered and that there was no way anyone should have been able to do that. He also siad that paladins wre gonna get "nerfed hard" I guess he forgot that there was such a thing as skill and tactics as opposed to numbers.

I told him that perhaps blizzard would tweak what our undead skills did in pvp but that that was no reason to nerf us as a whole. Our undead abilites are part of the core of being a paladin, so there's no reason to take that away form us.

Sad to say, but I rarely ever even use these abilities. Anyway, it got me thinking. If the UD think it's so unfair that we have these abilities that help us kill undead faster, then why not remove it entirely? The undead abilites are entirely overrated. Plenty of times i don't use exorcism at all when doing an undead quest because I'd rather save the mana in case I get an add and need heals. Seal of righteousness duration is so short it's usually not wortwhile to keep up for the mana cost or the hassle of having to switch over your hotkey pages. Turn undead...so what, they run away and aggro other mobs or they run away and you can't catch up to them to beat on them while they are feared. Pretty much useless w/o snare.

So, to answer my own question...Yes, I would absolutely positively give up undead abilities entirely for a ranged nuke that works on all types of mobs.
