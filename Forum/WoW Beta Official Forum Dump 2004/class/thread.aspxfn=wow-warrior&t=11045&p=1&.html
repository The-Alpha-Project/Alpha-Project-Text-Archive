I think the trouble with druid tanking is more related to holding agro, and lacking the defensive abilities that warriors get (stuns, blocks, ac/defense bonuses, etc), than a relation of AC and HP.

The use of abilities also plays into how long a warrior can stay up. For instance, Improved Revenge can give you a 3 second stun, there is 3 seconds of no damage. Concusion Blow gives you 5 sec of the same. Disarm / Improved Disarm provide a dps break against enemies.

There is alot more to being an effective tank than AC/HP.

edit: That is not to say that a druid cannot be a viable tank in some situations, I've seen some very good bear form brawlers.
