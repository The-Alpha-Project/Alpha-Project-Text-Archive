 1. The rediculous rarity of rogue viable daggers | 10/18/2004 6:47:16 PM GMTDT
I just got my rogue to 21 the other night, and I felt it was time for an upgrade to his weapon set up. Now I know alot of people swear by swords of maces, but dammit I like a rogue with daggers. That's just the way it "should" be. So off I go to Thottbot to search for what kind of daggers are available to him.

Wow, some pretty nice stuff around this level. Couple blues that are nice. Currently I'm using a Wingblade and hook dagger. Pretty decent combo, but I really want a dagger in my main hand, and my offhand dagger could use an upgrade. So first things first, I head off to the auction house in Orgrimmar.

40g buyout price for a level 27 dagger?!?!!?!?!?!?!? WTF!!
4g buyout price for a Deadly Bronze Poinard? You gotta be kidding me! A hook dagger for 1g? Those things drop like candy in the barrens!

So I message a friend of mine who's a smith. He needs a couple items and he can make me a pair of DBPs, so I do that. They're not bad daggers, but there are better out there. I'm really bad with names, and right now Thottbot isn't working so I can't get the actual names of the daggers or their DPS ratings, so bear with me.

DBP is what? 12.5 dps or thereabouts? Well there are 4 other daggers that I can get which are better.

My friend suggests to me to go kill Dragonmaw orcs because they drop one. I think that one is like 15 ish dps. So I go and start killing them on my level 50 warrior for a good 2 hours. Nothing

There's an awsome dagger that comes from Rohh the Silent in redridge mountains. Has a 9% drop rate. Now that's pretty reasonable for a blue item that's not in an instance. So I go to kill his arse. But where is he? The map at Thott shows where he is. Looks like he wanders. So I hop on my trusty wolf (still on the 50 warrior) and go searching for this mysterious person. Can't find him anywhere!!!! I search every part of the zone, including the alliance city. Which was fun for the newbie alliance people as they all ganged up on me :) Still no Rohh, still no dagger.

Ok, so I'm gonna do this the good ole fashioned way. I log on the rogue, level him up to 23 and get into some SFK groups. 4 times I've gone through that zone now, and not a single dagger dropped. And Arugal didn't drop the meteor shard. And to top that all off, while I was not looting a single item and kept telling the group how much I needed a new dagger, other members (like the priest) were talking about how cool that dagger was going to be for them........ So not only do I have a horrific drop rate on viable daggers for my rogue, but I also get to compete with almost every other class in the game for it too! But their abilities don't depend on a dagger! WANKERS!

So I still haven't seen a dagger drop, not even ones that are below my level. So I see some rogue, level 26, with this dagger called Flesh Piercer. So I go search on Thott, and it's got a 1.4% drop rate, off of level 30 mobs in Hillsbrad. Well that's not so bad! I can mow through those suckers in no time on my warrior. So back to the warrior I go, to slaughter some nagas.

Now you must realise that I played EQ for years. So really, this is a cake walk compared to the 3 weeks I camped Kalayia in Lesser Faydark just so my human cleric could get an item to see in the dark.

So I get there, and start the slaughter. Over the course of the night I log on other toons as I get bored, then back to the warrior to slaughter more, I've killed those nagas for a good 5 hours.

Not a single dagger has dropped. Not even a grey dagger!

Now just to put this in perpective, I've gotten 12 green items, mostly armour. and 3 blue weapons. Some 2 handed sword, which was actually really nice for the level and sold for 10g easily. And 2 Killmaims. Killmaim is a 2 handed blue axe, with a drop rate of like 0.1% according to Thott. I got 2! Both sold for 10g in the auction house. The dagger I want, has a 1.4% drop rate........ over 10 times more likely to drop, and it still won't drop.


Now I realise that there are other alternatives for the rogue. That swords generally do more damage, and mace specialization is teh pwn! But how or why or who thought that rogues wouldn't like daggers and made them this rare? These things are like the holy grail. If a decent dagger goes up for auction, everyone and their grandmother is in a bidding war.

There REALLY needs to be some quests for rogues that give them decent daggers for their level. Maybe one quest every 10 levels? Because this really is retarded. It should NOT be this hard to get a decent dagger for a rogue.

Many people are confused, from what I have read on this thread so far, about daggers available. There are daggers bought from merchants at 19, 29, and 39 and usually not hard to find Bronze Poniards for level 20 and Glinting Steel Daggers for level 31 that are smith made. From the sounds of this thread, people are upset they can't have their uber blue dagger, which was intentionally made hard to get.


As to daggers in the AH with high buy out numbers in gold, you should feel fortunate to even have a chance to buy one. When you play the AH as a seller, items have no set price, its the supply vs the demand for the item. 40 Gold might sound steep for a buy out, but many will never put a buy out number on a high demand item, or if they do its at a very high number intentionally met for only the desperate or a number the seller would settle for.


Currently feel fortunate if your still leveling a Rogue now then vs when retail comes out. Currently there is many items to buy at the auction house due to the economy existing for 7+ months. When retail comes the first few generations of level 60s will have to earn most of their items with little to no chance at buying them until the economy becomes saturated with far less demand, which will be months after the retail comes out.
