On a positive note.

Curse of Agony has been fixed. It no longer counts as melee damage, and no longer cancels drains.

On another sadder note.

Improved Curse of Exhaustion is bugged. I have noticed the same bug with some priest spells (improved renew being the extremely important one).

Basically, if you cast Curse of Exhaustion on a mob, you can NOT cast it again until AFTER the debuff as run it's course (15 seconds).

It gives the error "a more powerful spell is already active".

So, what i think the issue is, is that the improved curse of exhaustion works fine, but it adds the talent improvement AFTER you cast it. So, when you go to cast a new one, it considers that one a lesser version, and thus you aren't able to "overwrite" the current one.
