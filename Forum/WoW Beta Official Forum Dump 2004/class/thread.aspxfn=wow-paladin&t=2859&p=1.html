I think its fair to say I am one of the highest, played warriors who post in this forum. Comming from someone is known for playing tanks and does actively play a Paladin and a Warrior, I can tell you hands down, without a doubt Warrior is best even without talents.

The Warrior is just so well designed, words just don't do the class justice.

The warrior has a number of attacks that cause a great deal of threat beyond the simple taunting. Thunder Clap, Cleave and Punishing Blow are great for grabbing aggro. Beyond that, the warrior has so many debuffs they can throw in that makes their hits have a greater chance of grabbing the top of the hate list.

A good warrior will never rely on taunt/challenging shout to gain, keep or maintain threat and hate. Taunt and Challenging Shout are just the classes safety net to allow it to do what it does best if things go bad. There are a great deal (probably most) situations where a warrior doesn't need to pull out taunt and CAN keep the creeps sqaurely focused on the warrior the entire battle (IE no one else taking damage).

The neat thing about the class is if someone has no idea what they are doing or things get bad they can always, shutup, pull out a shield, jump into defense stance and taunt.

Paladins can cease aggro but the reality is, Paladins make a far better backup tank in virtually every scenerio. In any situation, I never sit back and say "Gee, I wish I had my Paladin to pull/tank this pull".
