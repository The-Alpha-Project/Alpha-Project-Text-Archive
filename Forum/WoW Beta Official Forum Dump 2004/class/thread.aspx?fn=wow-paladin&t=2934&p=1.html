What I am finding in the higher level instance groups, I'm seeing paladins used more often in a true support role when the boss level's are getting tanked. Unfortunatly, the seal duration is too short for that role. As was stated earlier the longer seal duration would make them more useful. What I've been called on is to keep fury on the tank, the threat-reduction on the priest and mage, and to keep devotion or resistance aura up. Keep a seal of protection ready, and like some have pointed out, the "run for the hills" technique. These are what the groups I've been with since level 45 doing uldaman, Zul, and the temple have been asking for. What this does do is give a lot of very unsatisfying button pushing, but it has been effective.

The downside of course is we tend to be a damage dealer for the normal elites, but a supporter/rezzer for the boss battles. Heck one time due to pathing bugs, I stayed back in the Uldamen final room, only to be shut out of the room. Upside was I could still heal & seal through the door. Was the easiest we've beaten the boss there yet, but it is very un-paladin.

Where that brings me is to many of the suggestions here. Most tend to make the Paladin more offensive oriented. However, players are going to find what works best in a group, and head down that path. Combat feats such as the much talked about sacrificial strike will make the Paladin, IMO, more of a group liability that he is now. Yes, he may generate more threat, he may do more damage, but he/she is also going to require more healing, which to me seems like it will offset the threat increase from using the strike.

How to make better? dunno, I've yet to be bored or not have fun with the Paladin since beta1. That said, making wis/str auras definatly seemed to go in the wrong direction. As a player I feel less useful in a group. However, I have learned to use my other seals better to the benefit of those I party with. What I don't find though is any use for the wis or str aura's. Unless I am with more than two paladins, they've become near useless aura's. I keep devotion or resistance on in combat, and healing on out of combat.

Specific changes I would like to see/try out.

Wisdom Aura made an out-of-combat aura that regens 30 (level 1) to 60 (level 2) MP's per second to party or self.

Crusader strike/holy strike Need to make crusader more useful. Perhaps a chance to debuff the target, or something. Holy should have a chance to stun. Crusader needs a mana cost reduction.

While I think it would be cool to allow the paladin to "Bless" weapons, I feel that should remain the realm of the enchanter, unless it was a party seal/aura effect. Something along the lines of makes all weapons used in group have a 5% chance of firing off a holy smite or something similar.

If there must be another attack type. Instead of the stun chance on Holy Strike, Change the current hammer of justice from a always stun once per minute spell to a 15-20 second timer increasing chance to stuff attack. Does x-y damage boost + a 20% 40% 60% (levels 1-3) chance to stun target.
