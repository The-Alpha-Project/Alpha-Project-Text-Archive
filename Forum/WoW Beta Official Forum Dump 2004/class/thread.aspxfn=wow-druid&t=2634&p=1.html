 1. Suggestion: low level travel form | 8/1/2004 12:44:49 PM GMTDT
After having played a Druid for 10 levels, I think it would be more useful and fun if we were granted a low level travel form earlier than level 30. Faster movement is one of the class defining abilities of a Druid, and having it at low level would enhance the playing of the class greatly IMO.

If necessary the speed of the form could be scaled up each level until it maxes at 30? Not only would that give a bonus to each level - particularly the odd levels when we get no new spells and abilities - but it would be such a boon for the lower levels when you are doing all the travel quests. Everquest did a similar thing with Bard speed, and it helped to make each level up meaningful.

My suggestion would be to swap the early travel form with Bear Form at level 10.
