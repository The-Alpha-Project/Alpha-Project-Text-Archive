I wonder why every MMORPG designer felt like warriors should just be tanks. In my mind paladins have a better claim lore, realistically, and theme wise to be tanks than warriors ever had. In movies or stories does the "warrior" decide he is going to smack some guy with the flat of his blade in order to piss him off? No, he goes straight for the kill. Warriors just seem like they should be more direct damage than tanking. Rogues may whine over this, but when I think of warriors I think of whirling swords and crazed combat moves, not ducking behind a shield in order to take the hit for some wizard across the room.

Paladins are supposed to be stalwart defenders of your comrades. Not suicidal guys screaming and swinging wildly, that is a zealot, paladins are just the noble defender, knowing that he should take the hit for his friends. I don't want taunt. I want mass aggro management in order to protect my friends. After all is it more noble to see your friend get cut to pieces and THEN heal him? Or does it seem more paladin like to charge into the fight, bashing with your shield in order to stem the tide of enemies charging at that friend?

The problem with MMORPGs is they seem to be set in this idea that warriors HAVE to be tanks. Paladins HAVE to be a hybrid warrior/priest. Why not just switch warriors to mainly damage and turn paladins into tanks? It makes more sense.

Warriors don't train their bodies and learn crazed moves in order to tickle a monster. They learned those in order to hurt someone. Paladins don't learn how to heal in order to help someone they watched get the living hell beat out of them. They learned how to heal in case their vigilance over their friends was pierced, and they needed to help them.

Now some of you may be thinking, "What about rogues?" Good question. They should still be direct damage. When it comes to pure melee there has always been those more like rogues, i.e. swashbucklers, highwaymen, and you standard blaggard, and the grunts. Rogues should stay, but rogues have always had other uses. Just slacken their dps and up their stealthiness.

Meh, I am just rambling now.
