Mentioned in another thread, but traps should be usuable in combat. That would allow us to do much more in combat and make us more versatile:

Hunter would be alot better I think if you could use traps in combat. Most of the time you need to use a trap like freezing/snare is when you're already in combat (ie, get jumped or add joins in).

Give the traps longer cooldowns but allow their use in combat. That would give us more utility, escape mechanisms, and more dps (fire trap).

It'd be a good way to fight,if you wing clip, back up, lay down a fire trap in the mob's path followed by a snare trap. That way you could get roughly 20 seconds of snare + some extra dps.

Or for crowd control, draw a mob off with a few arrows and then lay down a freeze trap and back off to the main target.

Traps can certainly be a cool addition/ability for hunters but with the out of combat restriction you have to be a psychic to use much more than the fire trap.
