 14. Re: Hunter Questions for the Developers | 10/12/2004 10:21:56 PM GMTDT
Q: Hunter DPS, as it stands is low(er) than the other dps classes, What are you planning or considering as methods for correcting the DPS gap and where are you aiming for Hunter DPS to fall in the scheme of WoW? Also, hunter dps currently depends on a number of factors in combat including:
1. pet damage(0 if dead or CC),
2. Aspect in use (ranged damage reduced if not in hawk aspect),
3. Hunter's mark applied (takes a round to cast, and have to be facing target, and its dispellable and thus we lose more DPS)
4. Not only Weapon lvl/dmg(which affects all melee class)
5. Ammunition Level/dmg (of which only gun ammo is craftable (and thus "better").
6. RANGE from target! (we are the only class penalized for being "too close" forcing us to fall back to a melee weapon(which depending on if we can afford 2 "good" weapons will be good.. or lousy) Our available "specials" also become null depending on range (arcane missile, sting)
7. Stats selected(Bow/Gun AFAIK depend on combined STR/AGI, Melee still based on STR)
8. "Missing" our shots can be blocked, absorbed, dodged, missed, and deflected.
9. Armor of target we are shooting and etc. the remaining factors are general factors all the classes must face

AFAIK there are far more variable factors for hunter damage than other classes. And our DPS suffers if any of the stars are "not in alignment". Overall the hunter feels like a very flexible class, But some alleviation of all the "right situations for max dps" need to be looked at.
