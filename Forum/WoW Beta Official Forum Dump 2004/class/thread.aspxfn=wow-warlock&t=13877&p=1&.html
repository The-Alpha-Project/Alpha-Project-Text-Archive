 1. Soulstone / Healthstone Suggestions. | 10/23/2004 10:03:15 AM GMTDT
So Blizzard evidently wants to use the Demonology Tree to buff our "stones". Currently I think (and I seem to be in the majority) of Warlocks who think the majority of the buffs in this category are rather mistargeted.

Here are some general suggestions I think are more on target. Thoughts / comments absolutely welcome.

Healthstone Ideas

Improved Healthstones Transferrable: We should be able to give away Improved versions just as we give away standard ones.

Increased Chance to Critical Heal: Increase the proc rate on critical heal when we use a Healthstone.

Double mana / no shard requirement: Self explanatory.

Alternate Critical Heal: Instead of critical healing, the Healthstone has a small chance to function as a regenerate spell. It would first restore a burst of life, than restore a set amount every few seconds for a time period.

Soulstone Ideas:

Improved Soulstone: Give us a talent that decreases or eliminates ressurection sickness and increases base life / mana. The ONLY problem I see with this is that it will be an ABSOLUTE requirement for any Warlock wanting to group.

Progression could work as follows:

1/5 = Rez Sickness Reduced to 60%, HP / Mana at 25%.

2/5: Rez Sickness 45% / HP + Mana at 35%.

3/5: Rez Sickness 30% / HP + Mana at 45%.

4/5: Rez Sickness 15% / HP + Mana at 55%.

5/5: Rez Sickness 0% / HP + Mana at 65%.

These "Improved Soulstones" would function as a spell rather than a single upgrade. Possible ways to control it include requiring higher number of shards, higher mana costs, and putting casting timers on the spell itself.

Soulbind the Soulstone: Soulbind the Soulstone. Offer Improved Soulstone upgrade that requires three shards but gives Warlocks a guaranteed full health + full mana resurrect with no mana sickness.

This is not as unbalanced as it might seem. We are still Pet-less, and still within 5 seconds of whatever killed us.

I HATE the chancge this makes to our groups, but given the current uselessness of Soulstones, it does improve the situation for us.

Lifestone:

One other option for a late-game "Stone" talent is the Lifestone; a stone that gives a full resurrection but is soulbound and drains 5% of a player's health / mana as long as they carry one. Your totals in both are 95% as high--that's the price. Four shard requirement, 20 minute cooldown.

That sums up my thoughts on these two areas.
