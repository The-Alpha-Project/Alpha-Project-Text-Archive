 1. My Combat/Subtlety build; Suggestions? | 10/21/2004 5:45:01 PM GMTDT
Ok. I've caught the "build bug".

I have a lot of fun with stealth in WoW, and I plan on using it extensively in Retail. I also want to be able to hold my own in direct combat, though. I've split my planned build down the middle, picking up Combat and Subtlety Talents, ignoring the Assassination talents except for a few minor skills.

Is this a feasible build, or have I overlooked something?


Assassination Talents (6 points)
Malice - 2/5 points
Increases your critical strike chance by 2%.

Improved Eviscerate - 3/3 points
Increases the damage done by your Eviscerate ability by 15%.

Murder - 1/2 point
Increases your chance to hit while using your Sap, Ambush, Garrote, or Cheap Shot abilities by 3%.

Combat Talents (21 points)

Improved Gouge - 3/3 points
Increases the effect duration of your Gouge ability by 1.5 seconds.

Improved Sinister Strike - 2/2 points
Reduces the Energy cost of your Sinister Strike ability by 5 Energy.

Improved Backstab - 3/3 points
Increases the critical strike chance of your Backstab ability by 30%.

Precision - 5/5 points
Increases your chance to hit with melee weapons by 5%.

Deflection - 5/5 points
Increases your Parry chance by 5%.

Riposte - 1/1 point
A strike that becomes active after parrying an opponent's attack. This attack deals 150% weapon damage and disarms the target for 6 seconds.

Blade Flurry - 1/1 point
Increases your attack speed by 20%. In addition, attacks strike an additional nearby opponent. Lasts 15 seconds.


Subtlety Talents (26 points)

Master of Deception - 5/5 points
Reduces the chance enemies have to detect you while in Stealth mode. More effective than Master of Deception (Rank 4)

Camouflage - 5/5 points
Increases your speed while stealthed by 15%.

Opportunity - 5/5 points
Increases the damage dealt when striking from behind with your Backstab, Garrote, or Ambush abilities by 20%.

Improved Ambush - 3/3 points
Increases the critical strike chance of your Ambush ability by 40%.

Initiative - 5/5 points
Gives you a 75% chance to add an additional combo point to your target when using your Ambush, Garrote, or Cheap Shot ability.

Preparation - 1/1 point
When activated, this ability immediately finishes the cooldown on your other Rogue abilities.

 8. Re: My Combat/Subtlety build; Suggestions? | 10/21/2004 11:44:47 PM GMTDT
Parry certainly wont save you from a warrior, and dodge will hurt you more fighting a warrior.

Though, last time Riposte proc'd against a warrior 3 levels above me, that 6seconds completely turned the tides of the fight. It took him a good 3 seconds to figure out what was going on.

Soloing, I dont believe Riposte does anything, its as if the "weapon" mobs use are cosmetic only.

And if you're building a defensive rogue, you might as well go for setup, dodge, ghostly strike, parry riposte. Thats about as defensive as it can get. While you might be hurting against a warrior and have lower dps in general, you'll likely win most 1v1 fight against rogues.

Backstab/Sealfate combo's are nice and all, but not against someone with up to 95% dodge GL hitting them in that 15seconds, let alone build combo points or get crits. (25% max from agility, 15% from ghostly, 5% from lightning reflex, 50% from evasion) AND they are getting combo points, and still can disarm you.

Subtlety rogues are also very hard to kill, preparation allows you to run away very very quick, vanish twice, evade twice, blind twice, at the price of generally lower dps.
