 1. Holy spec with a little Shadow and Disc | 10/23/2004 6:10:41 AM GMTDT
This was made for a friend of mine who really wants to be a Holy Priest, but admits that he wants some Shadow for Mind Flay and soloing.

He called it, "goody two shoes with a little bit of evil."

---------------------------------------------
Discipline Talents (10 points)


Unbreakable Will - 5/5 points
Increases your chance to resist Stun, Fear, and Silence effects by 15%.


Improved Power Word: Shield - 3/3 points
Reduces the duration of your Power Word: Shield's Weakened Soul effect by 15 seconds.


Improved Power Word: Fortitude - 2/2 points
Increases the effect of your Power Word: Fortitude spell by 30%.




Holy Talents (30 points)


Holy Specialization - 5/5 points
Increases the critical effect chance of your Holy spells by 5%.


Spiritual Healing - 5/5 points
Increases your Mana regeneration rate by 50% after getting a critical effect from your Flash Heal, Heal, Greater Heal, or Prayer of Healing spell. Your Mana may regenerate at a 50% rate while casting. Lasts 10 seconds.


Subtlety - 5/5 points
Reduces the threat generated by your healing spells by 20%.


Improved Resurrection - 2/2 points
Reduces the resurrection sickness penalty of your Resurrection spell by 25%.


Improved Healing - 5/5 points
Reduces the Mana cost of your Lesser Heal, Heal, and Greater Heal spells by 10%.


Combat Resurrection - 1/1 point
Brings a dead player back to life with 50% of their health and mana. The target does not suffer from resurrection sickness.


Improved Prayer of Healing - 2/2 points
Reduces the Mana cost of your Prayer of Healing spell by 20%.


Master Healer - 5/5 points
Increases the effectiveness of your healing spells by 10%.




Shadow Talents (11 points)


Spirit Tap - 5/5 points
Gives you a 100% chance to gain a 100% bonus to your Spirit after killing a target. For the duration, your Mana may regenerate at a 50% rate while casting. Lasts 15 seconds.


Improved Shadow Word: Pain - 2/2 points
Increases the duration of your Shadow Word: Pain spell by 6 seconds.


Shadow Focus - 3/5 points
Reduces your target's chance to resist your Shadow spells by 6%.


Mind Flay - 1/1 point
Assault the target's mind with Shadow energy, causing 75 damage over 3 seconds and slowing the target to 50% of their movement speed.
--------------------------------------------

Questions? Comments? Concerns? Post 'em.

 5. Re: Holy spec with a little Shadow and Disc | 10/23/2004 6:38:19 PM GMTDT
Your shadow is clearly just to get mind flay. I have nothing against that assessment, however I think you will get better mileage from Blackout than Shadow Focus. SF is one of the less useful skills in the tree, in my opinion. Further, if your friend wants to use Mind Flay for damage in a group, Shadow Affinity might be a better choice for reducing the threat generated.

Your discipline is just for improved buff and shield. That's fine too. I won't argue the choice between Unbreakable Will and Silent Resolve.

Your holy is to get to Master Healer and max it. Working backwards up the tree, I can see your logical choices. Combat Res is a favorite of holy proponents and 20% off your group heals is nice, but I'd argue the usefulness of either. I think your friend will need to decide what he uses a lot of when it comes to healing: you might consider Improved Renew rather than either or both of those choices.


Your other option is to drop one of the trees. Your friend wants holy and mind flay, so I guess that means discipline. While I fully support your decision to use improved shield and fortitude, hypothetically if you dropped it, you'd have 10 points to toss at Improved Renew, Inspiration, Holy Nova, or just about anything you could reach in the holy tree. Shadow Reach is a godsend for Mind Flay with it's shorter range, and Improved Fade is the one redeeming group skill in Shadow.
