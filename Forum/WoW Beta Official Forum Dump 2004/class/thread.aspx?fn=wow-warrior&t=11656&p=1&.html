 14. Re: What are the warrior essential quests? | 10/27/2004 11:31:16 PM GMTDT
At level 5 buy the armor and weapons in the town in your starting zone. At level 10 do the first Warrior quest for a decent weapon.

For Alliance at level 22 there is the Legend of Stalvan series for the Crecent of Forlorn Spirits axe (18.1 DPS) http://www.thottbot.com/?i=1157 although you would need a good group to help you complete it at that level.

At level 27 you can take the In the Name of the Light series, but you would need a lot of help to finish it before your late-30s. You can go 2H with Bonebiter 38.8 DPS) or 1H or dual wield with Sword of Serenity (30.0 DPS).

At level 30 there is also the Green Hills of Stanglethorn series for the Olmann Sewar sword (23.0 DPS) http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=1957 This is much easier to complete.

At 30 you can also get the Cyclonian series for the Whirlwind weapons, but you would need a lot of help to complete it before 40. http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?s=Whirlwind

The optinal path is to get the Crescent of Forlorn Spirits at level 22, then at 27 do In the Name of the Light for Sword of Serenity to use as 1H + Shield. At 30 complete the Green Hills of Stranglethorn for Olmann Sewar to dual wield with Sword of Serenity and complete the Cyclonian to get a Whirlwind 2H weapon. At that point your warrior is good to go with weapons until he is 47 and ready for the craftable Truesilver Champion.
