 1. Blizz changes to Enslave Demon scare me. | 10/21/2004 8:16:30 PM GMTDT
I'm bothered by the changes Blizzard made to Enslave Demon, because they suggest a development team deeply out of touch with what would make this spell useful to a Warlock.

Here are the problems.

Unlike priests, Warlocks HAVE a Pet out.:

In order to even cast this spell, we must dismis our Pet.

Well when do you NOT have a Pet? I typically don't run around in a dangerous area without one. So the value of the spell should make it *worth* getting rid of our Pet.

Spell duration far too short.:

If you manage to enslave a demon, you get a demon for UP to 60 seconds.

As compared to my indefinite, last-until-dispelled or killed other Pets? I'd rather have an imp, even, kthnx. At least Timmy the Crackhead doesn't try to kill me without warning.

When are we supposed to want to use this?:

This is one of those spells that seems purposefully designed to be NOT USEFUL. When is it useful?

Oh. Well its useful in a group, with other tanks, when not in danger of being killed by a suddenly non-charmed demon. In short, its useful PRECISELY WHEN NOT NEEDED.

If I'm in battle, I'm not going to dismiss my VW to cast "Enslave Demon." If my VW dies, chances are I'm following fairly quickly no matter what. Even if I manage the enslave demon spell, the little sucker has a chance to shake it off 20 seconds later.

In EP earlier today my VW got killed and I was at very low life by the time I managed to enslave the fully-powered Plaguehound add that came along.

I enslaved it and sat down to eat. It broke enslavement 10 seconds later and killed me with a single critical hit. Since I was at v. low life AND mana, I had no choice but to sit for food--even had I kept running, this thing would've flattened me.

What bothers me most is that there is actually a Demonology talent devoted to this spell, as if anyone will EVER use it..

That's the part that bugs me. That's the part I can't figure out.

Its not just that Blizzard tweaked a spell and destroyed any chance of anyone actually using it for anything but a joke...but to actually add a TALENT to it?
