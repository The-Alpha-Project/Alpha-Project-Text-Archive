 244. Re: Warlock Questions for the Developers | 10/27/2004 4:19:31 PM GMTDT
For lack of a better term, I was calling it a rez or death portal, though I imagined it as a pentagram that remained on the floor where it was summoned.

Limiting the time makes sense so it cannot be abused for travel. I think a 30 minute limit would be sufficient, with the ability to summon a new one as needed - but with only one ever existing at a time.

To avoid solo abuse, you could require the entire party to participate in the ritual.

With all that in place, anyone in your group that has your soulstone would end up there on death. It's not guaranteed to be a wipe prevention because you could make a bad choice about where you placed the pentagram. Or, a roamer could run across you when you popped up.

Also, if you're not in the same group as the Warlock, then no pentagram rez. If the pentagram expires, no pentagram rez.

I like being able to do some useful ritual magic and think this could be a nice feature without being over-powered. Certainly it's less exploitable than the current solution where you can still skip content if you are careful.

I just think the current state of soulstones makes them pretty useless.
