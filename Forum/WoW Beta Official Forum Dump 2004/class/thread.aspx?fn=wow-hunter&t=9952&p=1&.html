 1. Pet Ability: Bat out of hell! | 10/20/2004 9:30:12 PM GMTDT
Ok, so that's not what I'd actually call it...

Pet Ability: Sacrificial Charge
Pet runs from the group and never stops, like a bat out of hell, constantly growling and aggroing everything around it and leading all mobs aggroed this way away from the party.

So long as the statement that pet aggro no longer transfers to the hunter is in fact true, this would work wonders for instances when the pet has just aggroed 3-4 extra mobs which will surely cause a group wipe.

Better still, in any case when a player has accidentally aggroed several adds, the pet could very well be what saves the party. Ideally, the pet will navigate its way through passageways so it isn't just running into walls. You might even be able to save the pet by gaining enough distance from it so it disappears.

In this fashion, pets might actually be able to SAVE a party rather than leading extra mobs back and causing a wipe. Right now, they're a liability and a big one at that. With this, the hunter could potentially be one of the most valuable tools for instance fighting, and I think groups would then think twice before passing us up.

Decent DPS + hunter's mark + a pet which can pull a group of mobs away from the group would, god willing, = a need for hunters in high level instances.
