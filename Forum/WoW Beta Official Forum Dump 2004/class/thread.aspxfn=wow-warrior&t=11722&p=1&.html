 1. DUAL WIELD Basics! | 10/26/2004 6:26:24 AM GMTDT
I know everyone out there thinks I'm crazy for liking dual wield builds. Mortal strike does more damage! Yes I said it! But....

If you are going to dual wield heres how to do it right.

1. Get the best damage enchants you can on your weapons. +7 on both is huge especially on an ultra fast weapon.

2, Become a blacksmith and ALWAYS use your sharpening stones. Again this is an additional +8 to each weapon. With enchants thats +15 to each weapon. On a 1.5 speed weapon thats 10 MORE dps per weapon for 20 more dps...that's a lot.

2. Don't get high damage weapons - get really fast weapons. Again this will improve your dps.

3. Get a combo talent build. Arms and protection. Arms and fury. You don't need your final tier abilties.

4. Go ALL strength - take +25 str over +20 stam/+ 10 str. If you want to be a damage dealer you are going to have to sacrifice some stamina. This has been refuted but I gaurantee you it makes a HUGE difference on ultra fast weapons.

5. Pray that the miss ratio gets reduced.

6. Since you don't rely on mortal strike - don't always sit in arms. The key to a dual wielder is that they benefit from all stances. Don't be lazy. Switch your stances.

Here's what a gneral fight for me looks like. Mob jumps on me. Switch to defensive - Concussive blow. Sunder armor. Sunder armor. Switch to Arms. Heroic strike. Heroic strike. Switch to defensive. Concussive blow.

***Check my dual wield builds out. They are effective - if you tell me they aren't you might have to duel me***

Here's how you dual wield.

1. Be a rogue.

2. Remember that you're stuck with dual wielding at level 10.

3. Use poisons.

4. Get a really really slow high DPS dagger in your main hand. And anything else you can use with some nice proc effect or stats in your off hand (It doesn't really matter, as you're doing 50% damage from your off hand).

5. Spend some talents to beef up your Back Stab.

6. Learn how and when to Gouge, Move and BS. Even if you have to do this between other people's swings and DoTs. (You can do it, if you're skilled(Which involves practice)).

7. My BS crits 50% of the time for 600 to 900 points of damage, (And remember to use feint in groups after taking an elite mob down to red in 2 hits).

Anyways, Cosmos reports my DPS at around 250 dps, and 24% crits against mobs slightly lower level than me. So I dunno what you're smoking by telling people to make a dual wielding warrior. Personally, I can't wait to hit level 60 and check out how my dps would be with a 40 dps dagger.
