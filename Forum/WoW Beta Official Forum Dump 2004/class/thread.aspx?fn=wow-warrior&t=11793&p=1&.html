Thats not the point, go to the auction house, buy a green 2h weapon that is your level and two green 1h weapons your level and go test the DPS without using any special attacks, see how the DPS compares. When they balance DPS for weapons they take the miss rates into account, thats how they set 2h weapon dmg, they don't just pull numbers out of thin air.

To use an example...

At lvl 24 assuming you are using quest or forged weapons which are the easiest things to get.

You would likely be using something like a Orc Crusher at 18.2 DPS for a 2h weapon and mighty and iridescent hammer combo for 15.5 and 14.4 DPS dual wield. (numbers from thottbot, not 100% sure they are still accurate but you get the idea)

18.2 * .95 = 17.29 DPS
(15.5+7.2)*.76= 17.25 DPS

They end up very comperable for DPS. Generally speaking at all levels there is a 1h combo that equals out with the 2h available because they balance weapon DPS based on the miss rates.

Lvl 60 blue weapons

Dreadforge Retaliator - 51.4 DPS*.95=
48.83 dps after misses

Various 1h weapons have around 41.5 DPS

(41.5+20.755)*.76= 47.31 dps after misses
