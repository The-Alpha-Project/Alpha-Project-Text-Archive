 2. Re: Improved Arcane Explosion "vital" for mage? | 10/21/2004 4:47:45 PM GMTDT
Improved AE is an instant damage spell that does about 275-300 damage with no cool down. Even if you were not going to use this for pve, it's use as an instant spell with no cool down in pvp is tremendous. Blastwave is good yes, but it has a long cool down. Flamestrike is good yes, but its cast time makes it impractical most situations.

When in a PvE situation AE is the most reliable spell to do when facing multiple groups of large monsters. Take for example the Lyceum in BRD. You fight groups of 10-12 at a time every 15-20 seconds about. You could use flamestrike, but what if your tank moves? Also you're tank is taking damage while you're waiting for a cast time. With Arcane Explosion, you're instantly doing damage and you have full control of exactly where that damage will be best used. Not to mention you don't get interrupted. You could use Blastwave, but the cool down makes it effective only every other fight, and it's not enough damage alone to do the job.

I don't deny others can do Lyceum without Arcane Explosion, or other AE situations, but I don't think anyone will disagree that Arcane Explosion is the most reliable spell for the job in addition to being an awesome PvP spell.

I'm an end-game Undead mage that has played each Talent tree for at least 2-3 weeks each. Improved Arcane Explosion is NOT required provided you have spells like Blast Wave or Improved Cone of Cold.

Its been a while now since I switched to Mostly Fire talents. Here's what I do against a big mass of Critters.

1. Flamestrike right on them.
2. Blastwave when they're close.
3. Cone of Cold as I back up away from them.
4. Frost Nova to keep them stationary and in a tight bunch.
5. Flamestrike right on them again.
6. Fire Blast or Cone of Cold depending how many are left.

If you're a Fire Mage, use Combustion before the first Flamestrike or BlastWave for a really quick fight.

This is kill just about Anything and is FAR more safer and Effective than spamming Arcane Explosion. What people forget about Arcane Explosion is, you better be Shielded/Healed by someone else because you'd getting kicked in the Butt by a LOT of mobs. My way deals Tons of damage without taking much Melee damage.
