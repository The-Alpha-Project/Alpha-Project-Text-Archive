 6. Re: Tailoring/Enchantments OR Alchemy/Herbilisim | 10/22/2004 8:08:46 PM GMTDT
I did enchant+tailor and I've never had a big problem funding either of them. My mage at 35, my tailoring is at 205, and my enchanting is somewhere around 170. I never have to scrape for cash to buy spells. I even buy often on the AH for mats. (I seem to get WAY too much soul dust lately from disenchants...and very much needing other essences) so I buy on AH...you just cant let people rip you off...they try to do it around every corner. Things that go for 50s or so, people are trying to sell for 5g+.


I never bother trying to sell the enchants, because usually in IF there are always people like Pixy who have the much higher enchant skills and have the enchants people want. So I'll enchant for free when I'm in a group and I have the mats, or I just buy a POS bracer or shield to build up skill on casting the same enchant over and over. I can imagine the curve getting worse though for the higher levels, but for the lower ones, enchanting was fine.

Tailoring...is so easy. Mats are abundant...just make sure to KEEP AHOLD of stuff like pearls and spider silk and not sell it to vendors or on the AH....cuz you'll be using it later. When you get a new item you're not used to getting, look it up on thott and make sure its not used for any tradeskills. If its not one you will ever use, put it on the AH, you'll get 2 or 3x the amount from a vendor. I'm hitting mageweave now with tailoring, and got through the silk stage in about 1 day (wanted the robe of power really bad) ...by going to Gnomeregan and farming the Gnomes outside the back door. In about 4 hours of farming, I'd end up with about 8 or 9 stacks of silk, a few blue items, and 9 or 10 green ones. Attempt to sell in AH (blue always sell quick, no matter how bad it is compared to green drops) and disenchant what you wont use. Cake.

Sell bags and items from it on the AH, and disenchant others depending how hard you want to build up enchanting. I've never had so much fun with tradeskills. I'm actually getting something out of what I make instead of making items that are 10 levels below me and not being able to go any higher (due to funds.)


As for alchemy/herb...I didnt really enjoy it much on other alts. I cant get too excited about it. I'll let other people do that.
