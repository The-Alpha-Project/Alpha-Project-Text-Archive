I use stoneclaw all the time in solo situations.

As Squib points out, it works OK for dealing with 1 add if you can burn through the original MOB fast enough.

I also like it when I get a bad pull and have to run, or when I get jumped while running through an area. In both of these cases the MOBs will instantly switch to the stoneclaw leaving me time to getaway. I personally find it more effective than earthbind, although I sometimes use that as well.

Finally, it is nice in a small group setting as a way to hold an add for a few seconds until a mage can poly it, or whatever. It has the advantage of taking the pressure off the primary CCers. Also it keeps any initial totems you put up from being killed by adds before they are sheeped.

It's not the best totem we have by a long shot, but it certainly has its places.
