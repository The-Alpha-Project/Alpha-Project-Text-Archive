 2. Re: Prayer of Healing Questions. | 10/8/2004 9:22:08 PM GMTDT
Every effected member has a chance to crit (it also heals pets)

If the crit goes off, whoever it crit ON gets Insipiration, and you get Spiritual Healing. It also 'heals' people who are at full health, so it's always attempting a crit on every party member in rage.

It's also the best spell via talents, upgrade wise (10% healing from Master Healer, 20% mana cost from Improved Prayer of Healing.. as opposed to heal, which ends up 10%/10%)
