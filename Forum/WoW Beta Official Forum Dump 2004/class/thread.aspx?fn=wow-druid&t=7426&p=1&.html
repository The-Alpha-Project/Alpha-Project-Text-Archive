Q u o t e:
Funhooves, can you clarify which forms are appropriate at what times?


Usually I sneak around in cat form, find my target. Open with pounce usually to stun/get the extra combo point (since I have the talent) and start dealing damage.

If it's a warrior/rogue, I deliver the combo points ASAP, shift into druid form, cast nature's grasp, and start jogging away. When I'm far enough, cast ER, heal, shoot 'em with something, shift into bear form and charge 'em (since I have a wolfshead helm). Fight for a bit, and if needed do a bash, shift out, ER, heal, and so on.

If it's a mage, I'll probably get instantly nova'ed when they can do it, so I shift out, cast ER so they can't get far, heal, shift into bear form, enrage (who the hell needs armor vs a mage?), charge, hit 'em a few times, bash them, shift back to cat form, build up combo points, and deliver a rip, and probably kill them.

Priests are a bit more annoying, I usually just deliver my rip with 3-4 combo points, as it deals damage while they have HW:S up. Shift out, entangle, bear form, enrage/charge, hit 'em for a bit, bash, shift back, etc.

There's not a form I use against a certain target, I use all of 'em against everyone pretty much.

1 on 1 pvp with druid is tough, I have seen many 60 druid vs 60 other class duels, and basically such a situation shows all of the druids weaknesses. The chance of winning? hmm less then 50%, depending on skill, and gear ...

In my opinion high gear dependency just means that pvp is flawed ... seriously if you win duels becouse you do have xyz item, which gives you certain advantage .. it doesnt leave too much room for the skill. EQ style pvp is very good example of flawed pvp system, and thats why pvp in EQ was almost non-existant.

Saying all of this, I have to admit that I am having lots of fun pvping with my druid.

In mass pvp this is where druids really shines.. eheh and this it the "real pvp", pvp is not dueling, dueling has nothing to do with pvp, pvp is all about "ganking". I have seen many of my respectable Alliance enemies, who always do great in 1 on 1, but in mass pvp they perform very poorly :).

So whats the role of the druid in pvp?. How we can contribute to the mass (group) pvp the most?

Root the enemy scouts, once they get in root range ;), let the warriors do the rest. Keep eyes open, root the enemy warriors to protect your mages, warlocks, and priests. Keep eyes open drop a heal if someone in your group needs it. All of this while looking for "good" druid targets (like mages or priests), and trying to survive if you get targeted by enemy warriors (druids are squishy).
Its very important to keep your eyes open, always apply the "finishing touch" on any enemy who goes low on health - our insta nuke works great for the purpose. And when the enemy starts to flee, this is when the "fun" part starts. Its the druids responsibility to make sure that not a single enemy gets out from the fight alive :).
