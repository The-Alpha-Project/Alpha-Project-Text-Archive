 1. Hamstring fix - remove the snare. | 10/26/2004 8:18:25 PM GMTDT
What I propose is a total revamp to hamstring.

The current form is obviously impossible to balance. You either have the enemy that is unable to get out of range of the warrior, who grinds them into dust, or the warrior never able to reach his opponent.

My solution is this:

Make hamstring actually hamstring the opponent. If an opponent is hamstringed, their leg is injured and they now have a chance to trip while moving.

By trip I mean a simple knockdown mechanism. This alleviates the problem of either being always in range, or always out of reach, by making it some of both.

That mage that is running around casting stuff just crumpled on his lacerated leg, that means he is down for a few seconds. This gives the warrior time to get in and nail the mage for a few hits, but then the mage is up and running again at normal speed, out of range of the warrior. It provides the best of both worlds.

Simply make it a % chance to trip for every second or unit of movement. You could even have it wear off after one "trip".

There are a lot of ways to balance this idea, but at the core I think it is a good one and should be considered.

There may be some glaring issues that I am not considering, feel free to discuss.
