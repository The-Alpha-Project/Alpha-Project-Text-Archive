Spending months and months to get to Hero rank should not get you an "I Win" button. (Not being literal...but you should be able to get the point.)

Part of the FUN of games is the challenge. After you put godmode in on Doom back in the day, how long did it take you to get bored of it because it was too easy? The hero classes should be just a couple steps above...mostly there for coolness with interesting abilities...not game breaking ones. You shouldnt be able to walk into some dangerous place and solo it just because you're a Hero...you should still require help and friends and etc etc. You shouldnt be able to singlehandedly destroy an entire group in PvP. Whats the point of playing if you've just "beaten" the game by being too powerful to bother. NEVER should a game allow the player to become mini-gods. BALANCE must remain or the game breaks.

Even a solo lvl 1 hero in WC3 could do little against 3 or 4 basic creeps. Maybe they should make people sacrifce level 60 and start over as a baby hero. Not lvl 60+Hero. Hero should just be the next stage in working your way up the ladder.
At the very top...you still shouldnt be able to solo the 95 Knight at solo, or bust your way in to the depths of UC with just your own badass hero self. That doesnt promote community...and community is what MMOs are all about and they are what keeps people playing.
