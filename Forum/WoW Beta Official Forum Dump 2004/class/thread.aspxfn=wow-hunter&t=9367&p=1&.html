 4. Re: Whens time to change pet ? | 10/18/2004 1:11:10 AM GMTDT
I believe there *are* certain differences between pets. Some have more armor/hp than others. As well, some attack quite quickly (scorpions are a good example) and others attack fairly slowly (Apes, turtles..etc).

My scorpion pet seemed to have an extremely fast recharge speed on his pet abilities like Growl and Claw. He could claw almost every 1-2 seconds, and Growl fairly fast as well.

Cats can learn Growl, Claw, Bite and Cower.. which is everything pretty much. This can be considered an advantage, because many animals are only able to learn ONE of the attack abilities (plus growl and cower of course).


as for when its good to get a new pet.. I generally get a new one simply to have a new look =)

If you plan on getting one just to learn new abilities, ditch it and train them to the old pet, make sure you do so rather quickly, as it is a bit of a pain keeping pets at the same level as yourself.
