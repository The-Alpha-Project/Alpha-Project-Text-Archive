 1. Warrior PvP Role | 10/19/2004 4:36:13 AM GMTDT
All comments below do not apply to undead warriors, because they are immune to fear.

Most people seem to think there should be some "Rock Paper Scissors" rules in PvP. From what I gather, this means we lose to casters, casters lose to leather (rogues/hunter/etc), and they lose to us.

Right now, I'd say a warrior is fairly certain to lose against a mage that knows what he's doing. Same with a warlock (kite gg), or even a shadow priest (I haven't dueled one recently, but I know psychic scream and bubble+greater heal are almost insurmountably rough).

Furthermore, a druid can somewhat easily beat a warrior being able to just wingclip/shift forms to run/root/etc.

I doubt a warrior can take out a pally (I haven't dueled one in a while), given that they have bubble, heals, LoH, and as much AC as we do.

That leaves rogues, shaman, and hunters. I have to be honest and say I've not fought any hunters. There just aren't many high-levels, esp. ones that duel. I will also say that almost every shaman I've seen has run (or died running), so I can't say what they're capable of in an all-out fight. I know they were VERY tough before, but I think a warrior could probably take one out.

We're left with the rogue. A sneaky, lightly armored target. I'd think this would be an almost guaranteed win for a warrior. In fact, it usually was. However, tonight I dueled Fei, and it was hands-down his fight every time. By the time I came out of stuns I was usually around 1/2 health, and it was downhill from there. If I ever got him close to 1/2 health, he'd just blind/gouge/sprint away and first aid, stealth, cheapshot, and blast me all over again.

I haven't seen all rogues, but I'm pretty sure Fei has the best gear of any rogue on the server--at least, alliance side. Maybe this means he should be able to destroy a warrior, but that leaves us with only a chance against shaman. WTH?

BTW I know many of you (under level 60's mostly, I'll bet) are going to reply and say how I am a noob and my gear sucks, etc. Well, I've never lost in 1v1 to a warrior, and I doubt there's more than a small handful of warriors who could beat me, so I'm going on the belief that while not the best, I'm at least "above average" in PvP.

What do you guys think? Are we just broken as hell atm, or should we all be undead?
