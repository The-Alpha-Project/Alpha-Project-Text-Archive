Uh... lets see...

Eyes of the beast: When I tame a pet, I just don't name it. This way when I'm in an area where the pet can usually be, It's a greaty way to spy on the other faction (they usually don't see my name under it, which I think should be removed anyways).

Eagle Eye: Again, great for scouting out mob's and other faction members. This has helped me track down Alliance more than once when they tried excaping from our blocking position :)

Scare beast: This is critical when fighting beast mob's. This is also very useful for PvP'ing against hunters. Fear their pet... makes your mages cast easier!

Mongoose Bite: This is needed for Melee. This takes us from horrid DPS to just bad DPS :)

Disengage: This only works if used properly. You have to build up some aggro on _something else_ for this to work. All it does is lower your aggro, not remove it all together. Growl a few times and it works like a charm.

Beast Lore: This is only useful for deciding what to tame... but since you never name any new pets (unless you want something different looking) it's kinda useless.

Multi-shot: Best group PvP skill ever! (Except for every other classes skills...). Seriously though, this is the bread and butter of our group PvP attacks. Also, if you are doing an instance with a descent tank (that can grab aggro) you can use this freely.

Most of our skills have uses right now... It's just that some of them are more nitch than other. Play more PvP and you'll find yourself using some of those skills that are 100% useless for solo'ing or group PvE :)

I kind of disagree.

Eyes of the Beast
Awesome for scouting. Beast aggro range seems to be very small, and controlling pet its much easier to run into a very hostile area, and see where a named mob/boss might be if you are unfamiliar with it. Also great for scouting in pvp and dangerous areas, risk free to yourself.


Eagle Eye
Seems fixed in recent patch, works beautifully. Can see far areas no problem, granted you are vulnerable as you use it. Great for spying, to see who you might be up against in a pvp match, or what monsters/ an area looks like. A pity it is outdoors only, however.


Scare Beast
Situational ability, at least worth level 1. Can save your life in a pinch, if you are low on health/mana and have one too many beast adds. Also useful while you're traveling and don't want to bother with a beast that is interrupting your progress.

Mongoose Bite
Personally, I didn't buy this skill. I am sure it is useful, but my personal play style, and hotkey set up has no room for it. I just do not melee enough to want to have an ability only usable after a dodge hogging up a better used slot on one of my hotbars.


Disengage
Works fine, although it doesn't always seem to be reliable. Use in conjunction with growl. Usually used when I over-aggro a pull, and want it on my pet/tank. Also can save you in groups, if you're low on hp after tanking, can sometimes pass it to another party member (preferably not a healer or caster :)) - You're no use to your group dead, after all!

Beast Lore
Well, not that useful. Can see max hp of beasts, yippee skip. Ok to see what you might want to tame (diets, etc). Otherwise, just a pure rarely used utility.

Multi-shot
Um, this is one of our very few burst damage skills. Its damage is actually decent, though steep on mana, PLUS it's area effect. Very useful ability, good damage, an extra attack, and AE. Don't know why you wouldn't pick this up.


I do not use every skill, but I don't believe any to be 100% useless. Not all skills are for pvp, or for groups, or for soloing. Some people have different playstyles, where a few skills become "useless". That doesn't mean the skill is useless to the hunter as a class overall, however.
