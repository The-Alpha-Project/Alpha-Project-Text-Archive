I'm not unhappy with my warrior right now, lots has been changed, but except in a few glaring areas, it's still the best class in the game, in my opinion. Here's my take on how we stand now:

Overall, the talent trees are decent. Some are definitely not worth the points, some are pretty much must haves. You can be a DPS machine or a defense machine. That's the main problem, I think. The optimal talent builds for each role tend to vastly reduce your effectiveness in the other. With a defense oriented talent set, I have no trouble in an instance keeping aggro and, in general, keeping rage up. Trying to solo with that build is like pulling teeth. Similarly, a talent set for soloing is bad news in an instance. You just spend even more time controlling aggro and virtually no time for anything else. A talent build in the middle just makes either role a bit better than no talents at all, but nowhere near as effective as building for one or the other. It's not a really good middle ground.

The biggest change I'd like to see is a "narrowing" of the difference between a defensive spec warrior and a DPS spec warrior. I like the idea of individualising your warrior through talents, but it's a bit too extreme right now. Yes, a protection spec warrior should be better in an instance than a damage spec warrior, and the damage spec warrior should solo better than the protection warrior. It shouldn't be as big a difference as it is now. Damage warriors should not be shunned for instances because they have chosen that path.

Also, rage generation is too widely divergent as well. When soloing, I make so much rage that I am always at at least a half bar of rage as I go, usually full (even without bugged MS rage generation). In an instance, I am bloodraging constantly. I'm never at a point where I don't have enough rage to keep aggro, but I do find myself without enough rage to do anything but taunt sometimes. Yes, I do solo with 2h and tank with 1h/shield. I imagine that it's a problem with 1h vs. 2h weapon rage generation combined with elites vs. non elites. Too widely extreme, again.

Other thoughts:

Abilities - Make them worth what we pay for them.

* Charge. Today's whipping boy. I agree with removing the stun completely, making it a spell interrupt only. Interrupt, distance closure, rage generation. Perfect. Oh, and not being able to charge the moment you get on a mob's hate list really kills its usability.

* Taunt. I agree, it's too weak right now. The talent is almost a necessity for instance adventuring, which is wrong, in my opinion. Too much mashing, to little of anything else.

* Thunderclap. Take the damage right out of this thing, leave the slow at 10%. Each rank adds 3 seconds to the time the enemy is slowed.

* Hamstring. Take the damage away, leave the slow at 40%. Each rank adds 2 seconds to the time the enemy is slowed.

* Rend. Too little damage over too much time. Each rank should increase damage, but keep the time to 12 seconds. I'd make it so it deals damage as a normal hit, then bleeds the same amount of damage over 12 seconds. As it stands now, it becomes less and less useful as you level.

* Heroic Strike. Uncouple it from weapon delay and make it an instant, just as Mortal Strike is. (Then move MS to Berserker Stance.)

* Pummel and Shield Bash. If the cooldown is going to be this long, it should not miss as much as it does now. As it stands, it isn't worth spending the rage, especially in an instance.

* Shield Block. I've said it before, I'll say it again - shields are a binary defence. As it stands, the amount of damage blocked by a shield is negligible, and the idea of a partial block is silly to begin with. 75% chance to block the next incoming attack is awesome.

* Revenge. Far, far too little damage. Given the same restriction on when you can use it as Overpower, it should do at least as much damage as Overpower. It's not like we have even half of the DPS in defence stance as in Battle Stance to begin with; the damage from this is laughable. As a possibility, with decent damage dealing from it, it could become a source of rage.

* Battle Shout, Demoralising Shout. <3

* Sunder Armour. Pretty good as is. I find it vastly unfair that General Angerforge sunders me for 3360 though.

* Berserker Stance in its entirety. Never, ever used it. Why would I?

* Mocking Blow. Does anyone use this? I know I don't.

* Challenging Shout. Does not taunt the enemy enough, they very quickly turn to new targets, often before I can propery taunt them. Cooldown is tooooo loooong.



Talents - Not going to list every one here.

* Improved Taunt. This should not be a necessity to make doing an instance reasonable. This should be something you take to allow you to play more with other abilities while you tank. Right now, it is a necessity to make doing an instance reasonable.

* Mortal Strike. I love it for soloing. The biggest problem I see is that if you want it, you struggle too much in an instance. All covered in my opening comments. Why isn't this a Berserker Stance ability anyway?

* Deflection, Impale. I love them.

* Improved Rend. Combined with what I said above for the basic ability, each rank adds 10% extra damage to the rend.

* Improved Charge. Combined with what I said above for the basic ability, each rank now adds 1 second of stun.

* Tactical Mastery. Nice to see it easier to get. Changing stance as you go really adds depth to playing the warrior. Every warrior should be able to keep 5-10 rage when switching stance without the talent to begin with, and this talent then increases the amount of rage you keep from there.

* Improved Thunderclap - Combined with what I said above for the basic ability, increase the slow 5% per rank for 2 ranks

* Improved Overpower. While I loved having guaranteed critical, it's quite good as it stands now.

* Improved Hamstring. Combined with what I said above for the basic ability... increasing the amount of slow is still useless. I'd honestly just do away with the talent if the basic ability were changed as above. If not, the talent should increase the time of the slow, not the amount.

* Improved shield block. Please make shield blocks binary. Then make this add one more block per rank, so that at rank 5, the next 5 attacks have a 75% chance each of being blocked. If shields cannot be binary, I'd make this talent have 4 ranks, each increasing the amount of damage any block the shield makes by 25%.

* Iron will, Improved Intimidating. I guess they're useful in PvP?

* Combat Endurance. Not bad now, but I get 82 Health/tick regen out of combat, which seems kinda high. If spirit regen is going to be changed, bring it back to 20%, even 30%.

* Improved Battle Shout. Winner. My rogues love shout, I love shout for threat and rage. 5 minute shouts = yes.

* Unbridled Wrath. Lukewarm at best. Too godly with dual wield, not good enough with single 1h or with 2h. A non starter for an instance.

* Booming voice. Yay?

* Improved Inner Rage. I love this for instances. Be nice if it had fewer prerequisites, or even if it were moved to follow after Improved Bloodrage.

* Improved Shield wall. Decrease its cooldown instead.

* Toughness. Armour increase as percentage = win.

I would add one new talent: Tough as nails. 5 ranks, each increases the health you get per stamina point by 1/2/3/4/5. We should already have better Health/stamina than other classes before this talent is applied!
