On this topic of speccing.. Just something to keep in mind.. spec for your level. Basically only spec in stuff that is going to currently help you. For example I WAS specing in improved drain life.. however at 27 I currently only have rank 2 (base 17 per sec). Well I speced up to improved drain life 4/5 until I realized.. I am getting absolutely nothing out of point 4 and very little (only 1hp per sec) for all my points 1-3.

Basically when at lower levels I think its much better if you don't focus on talents with flat +% unless they a hefty amount. I think specing in more utility lines that have much bigger effects are more worth it until you level up enough to have that little +10% or such really mean something.
