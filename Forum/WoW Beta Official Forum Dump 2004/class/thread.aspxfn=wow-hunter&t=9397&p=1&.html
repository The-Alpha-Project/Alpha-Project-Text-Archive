 7. Re: Hunters and Stun Abilities | 10/18/2004 11:24:57 AM GMTDT
I'm more in favour of a shot stun then a pet stun. Pets are imo useless in Pvp vs most classes.
I'll use the mage as an example.

Mana pool over 6k. thats 6 viper stings to drain them, more realistically 3 or so since they will use mana to nuke us as we are waitting for the sting to drain their mana. 3 stings = 24 seconds, for convenice sake say its 30 secs. more then enough time i think for a mage to kill us. A shooting type stun will help us survive a little more. Why i say shooting stun vs pet stun is because of this. Our pets are easily negated. A mage can polymorph me, kill my pet (easily), re polymorph me before i can come out, sit down and drink to full mana and health, then open up with a big nuke. This has effectively made my pet useless except to prolong the encounter, so if we got a pet stun, it will be useless. Other caster classes have similiar abilities to negate our pet, warlocks can seduce/fear pet or us and take us out one by one, priest can fear our pets, rogues can sap us and take out pets, etc etc. These all negate pet stuns or abilities in pvp, which is why im more in favour of a shot type stun.

Another issue i would like to bring up is Viper sting will be less useful as time goes on and this game evolves. Casters get better gear from better instances boosting their huge manapool to even bigger heights, maybe say 10k mana, whats a 1.1k mana drain in 8 secs gonna do to that? Of course with gear we get more hp and mana too, to fight the caster, but the point im trying ot make is viper sting will deteriorate in usability with introduction of more "uber" gear, so hopefully theres a talent or if blizzard can think of something else to boost viper sting .(our most in my opinion useful sting vs casters atm) accordingly so it does not become another useless ability like 1/2 our spells.

so with that said :P im in favour of a shot type stun vs pet stun (the pet is too easily negated in pvp)
