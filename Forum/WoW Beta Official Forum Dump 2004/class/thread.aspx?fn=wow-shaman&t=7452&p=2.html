"2H is the way to go"

As my good friend Diivox would say, this is pure and utter theorycraft. The WoW combat system is inherently biased against a slow weapon. In PvE especially, you do not want to be focused and reliant upon your weapon procing, because the DPS is inconsistent.

If go with 2H you have a greater chance of falling in combat waiting for your weapon to crit. Higher DPS 2Hders are higher only on paper and not in-game.

In conclusion, up & coming Shamans, don’t listen to what some of these pure theory crafters say. 2Hders work for Warriors, they work for Paladins, and they work for Hunters.

In PvE the smart choice is Shield + Dagger + Rockbiter. It has been a proven, fast, and consistent DPS setup time and time again.

Note: That doesn’t mean you shouldn’t have fun with a 2H, especially in PvP! Because this is Closed Beta, you can switch out your Talent Points any number of times. So grab a 2H and have some fun, but always remember what the smart choice is for a long term combat: Dagger + Rockbiter > then anything else available to the Shaman.
