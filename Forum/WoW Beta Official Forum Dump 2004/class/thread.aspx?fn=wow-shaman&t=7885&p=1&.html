 3. Re: Shaman/Druid duo for retail. Group friend | 10/21/2004 5:18:50 PM GMTDT
This duo should be able to easily handling healing for any instance I have been to. I have been the primary healer in Zul'Farrak with a druid backing me up a couple of times, and had no problem. Since healing tends to be the most sought after role, you should be good to find instance groups.

This is also an effective duo for small parties because you have all the class roles covered pretty well. The only one you lack is crowd control. There you are limited to stoneclaw and root. However, a druid and I have taken out groups of 4 MOBs averaging about one level above us. We settled into a tactic where he cast hurricane (big AE DOT/debuff) then shifted into bear form, and I did some AE then chain healed him. In retrospect, I should have been using magma totem, but I never think of it.
