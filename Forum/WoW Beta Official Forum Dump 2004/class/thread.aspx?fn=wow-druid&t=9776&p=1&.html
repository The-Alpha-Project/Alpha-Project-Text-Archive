How do you solo?

I have just played my first druid up to level 30. So far, I have enjoyed the class. It soloed quite nicely from 1-28 without many complaints. However, I am starting to feel a little decrepit. Compared to other classes in efficiency I think the druid falls behind as it approaches 30. Even-level monsters are getting harder and harder for me to solo and I am wondering how other druids approach combat.

Here is how I have leveled:

1-15:
Basically, I used wrath and melee attacks in druid form to kill mobs.

15-20:
This was an awkward period. My melee damage was horrible and I was dead broke. I do not think the quests around Darkshore adequately equip a druid. Basically, I nuked as I had before and healed myself while mobs beat themselves to death on my thorns.

20-30:
I tried to focus on my shapeshifting abilities.

My standard solo fight was:
Starfire -> Wrath -> Moonfire -> Faerie Fire -> Rejuv -> Cat Form and then I would melee the mob to death using claw and rake. The Cat Form finisher is not worth the energy.

This worked well, but as I leveled the mana requirements for spells increased much faster than my mana pool. This led to increasing down time as I moved closer to 30. I have been really annoyed by the 300+ mana required to shapeshift. It greatly reduces my ability to be flexible in combat, especially if I do not begin a fight with full mana.

Now, I have hit level 30 and moved into new areas and I am having a tough time. Cat Form is not cutting it for damage. Battles are becoming much longer, and because Druids do not make decent tanks, I am finding that I have to shift multiple times per fight to keep myself alive. This eats a ton of mana and slows me down a great deal.

I know it is a faux pas to mention it, but compared to the other classes in the same area and at the same level I have fallen behind and the gap seems to be widening. The other classes move through combat much faster than I can, and because of this suffer less damage and accrue less down time.

So, I ask, is there something I am doing wrong? Is there some talent I am missing or trick that I do not know that would lead to greater efficiency?
