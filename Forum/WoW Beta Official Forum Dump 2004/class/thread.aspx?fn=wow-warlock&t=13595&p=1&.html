 1. The CoW Saga Part IV: How CoW REALLY Works. | 10/21/2004 10:30:08 PM GMTDT
First off, this post wouldn't be possible without input from lots of sources, help from Kracow the Shaman, and Karodoc's mathematical analysis. Much thanks to all.

Much testing has gone on comparing CoW to Stoneskin Totem. Here's what we've discovered (in a nutshell): Assume the Warlock has DR of 10%, Voidwalker has DR of 50%. Stoneskin totem lowers damage by 27.

How Stoneskin Totem reduces Damage.:

Base Damage: 100
Warlock Damage Taken per Hit: 100*(1-.1)=90.
VW Damage Taken Per Hit: 100*(1-.5)=50.

Now we cast Stoneskin Totem:

Warlock DPH: 100*(1-0.1)=90 (base)-27 (Stoneskin Totem) = 63.

Voidwalker DPH = (100*(1-0.5)=50 (base)-27 = 23.

In both cases, damage is reduced by a literal 27 points.

How Curse of Weakness Reduces Damage.

Assume the same numbers, the same stats. Here's what happens:

Base DPH (Warlock): 90
Base DPH (Voidwalker): 50.

Curse of Weakness Applied (-31 damage reduction):

Base Damage: 100 -31 COW Effect. = 69.

Warlock DPH: 69*(1.0-.1) = 62.1.
VW DPH: 69*(1.0-0.5)=34.5

Damage Mitigated for Warlock: 27.9
Damage Mitigated for VW: 15.5.

This corresponds very well to testing and unless significant new information comes in, all but proves the hypothesis that Stoneskin totem lowers damage AFTER armor is calculated, COW lowers BEFORE.

Now the question is: Which is intended? Are both "right?" If they are, can we get this clarified in text?

 14. Re: The CoW Saga Part IV: How CoW REALLY Works. | 10/22/2004 10:20:43 AM GMTDT
Here's how they would stack.

Assume 10% Damage Reduction:

Mob doing 100 damage.
100 -31 = 69. This is Curse of Weakness Applied.
69*(1.0-.1)=62.1.
62.1 -27 (this is Stoneskin Totem).
Total Damage: 35.1 DPS.

Assume 50% DR
100-31=69 (CoW).
69*(1.0-0.5)=34.5.
34.5-27 (Stoneskin).
Total Damage: 7.5DPS.

From the Warlock's perspective, we reduced damage from 50 to 34.5. Damage reduction was 31%.

Shaman spell reduced damage from 34.5 to 7.5. Damage reduction is over 80%. In raw DPS terms, we prevented about 15.5 DPS--they prevented nearly double.
