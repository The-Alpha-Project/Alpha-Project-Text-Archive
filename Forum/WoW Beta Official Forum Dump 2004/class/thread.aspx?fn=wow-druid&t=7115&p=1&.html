Lots of reasons.

1. Rogues probably have higher STR/AGI than you which drives up their DPS.

2. Rogue DPS is weapon dependant. Our DPS is not. We are stuck holding vendor daggers while he may have blue ones.

3. Rogues specials are all based on this baseline DPS, so the overall DPS differential is exacerbated.

4. Rogues have higher innate Dodge and Crit % so they are avoiding and inflicting higer damage.

Our Catform DPS is actually more comparable to a Warrior than a Rogue.
