 1. A couple fun and unlikely ideas | 10/15/2004 4:45:15 AM GMTDT
Here are my ideas. The less likely of the two:
-Un-Constrained Shifting
You find a beast, kill it, loot it, skin it (if you can) and as it fades out, which is a cool effect by the way, The essence escapes and you have a chance to catch it, adding it to the list of things you can change into. You'd gain the new image and the monster's skills available while you're in that form, much like the cat and bear skills are available only in their forms.

You'd really only want it for the fun factor (I would SO love to fly around town as a Fay dragon, even if it was 5 levels below my natural level) and to use the beast's skills for a specific purpose.

Probably make it a talent deep in the feral tree. Make the chance of actually catching the animal you've killed taper off quite sharply as it approaches and passes your level. Give the canging a good 10 second cast time (I hate cooldowns) so it's not the sort of thing people would shift into in a battle then pop out for healing as we do our familiar forms. Likely many people wouldn't bother getting it since it would mean giving up talents in another tree, but for those of us mostly specced in feral it'd be an excedingly fun 1 point ability.

-Reincarnation
I admiit I got the idea from discussions of the DnD Druid spell
The half-assed answer to the calls for a res spell for the druids. Personally, I see why many people want a res, but I'm somewhat indifferent on the issue. Reincarnation would bring back the subject (after ofering them the choice as res does) in a body of random race and class. They pop back up with full health and no (or at least lessened) res sickness. The clear downside is that they'd have 1 in any new weapon skills, and depending on the new/old class most of their equipment might be useless. The effect wouldn't be permanant of course, and the next time they're res'd normally or claim their corpse they'd be back in the correct body.

It's another skill that would be loads of fun and somewhat usefull, but still wouldn't give the res that would make Druids quite the primary healers everyone wants and bliz clearly doesn't. Make it useable on self as well (with quite a long cooldown) to allow the soloing druids seem to be built for.

er there they are. They're big changes and I don't imagine they've got a chance of happening but I'd love it if they did. I play a druid because I love versitility and playing with all sorts of stuff (as a tauren druid, try this one. wander around the human starting areas, find one who looks suitably dazed. Stalk him in cat form for a while then at some point, perhaps just as they're getting up from looting or walk in a building, shift to GIANT COW OF DOOM form a few inches in front of/behind them. Great times are had for all. /no1)
