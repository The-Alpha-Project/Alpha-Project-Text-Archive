If someone wants to go pure melee, go Warrior or Rogue. A shaman is one of the truest melee/caster hybrids in the game. While your strength can lean to one side or the other, the true power comes from using both of them together.

Melee strong with casting supplement is viable, and casting strong with melee supplement is also viable.

Each are strong in different situations. The burst casting Elemental Combat build is good for short duration battles. The 5 minute timer on the uber nuke is somewhat limiting.

The Stormstrike/Flurry melee build has decent front end damage, and tends to be better for ongoing battles that don't give the luxury of downtime.

Over a duration, it is my opinion that the melee strong build will outdamage the casting heavy build, but it all depends on the types of battles you see yourself fighting.

I like to be in my enemies face beating them with my staff. All the while casting my shock spells as supplemental damage/interrupts.
