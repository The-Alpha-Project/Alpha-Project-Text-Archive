 8. Re: 41 Mage Need Advice on Soloing 37 Elites | 10/21/2004 5:29:40 PM GMTDT
Different classes are better at killing different things.

Elite mobs just are not efficient solo material for mages. This was not always the case, but it is the case now.

Regular mob - usually dies before it can even get to the mage. That's efficient (take no damage, spend no time running around)

Elite mob - will not die before it reaches the mage (must waste time kiting, or root + run away, etc.) all of this is a waste of time.

Shamans/Paldins etc. don't have to run away. They can continue to deal damage. This is why it's more efficient for them to kill elites than it is for us. Mages just don't tank, and killing elites efficiently requires tanking.

The key with mages is to kill as many mobs as you can, as fast as you can, drink, repeat.
