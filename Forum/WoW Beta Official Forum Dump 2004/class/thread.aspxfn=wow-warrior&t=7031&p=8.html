 160. Re: Feedback Request: Warror Changes | 9/29/2004 3:38:45 AM GMTDT
I understand that Bloodthirst has been changed in the latest patch to make it unusable with any of a number of other warrior skills/talents, due to some unintended stacking. This makes Bloodthirst rather worthless, as a critical hit by the Warrior or on the Warrior or the use of certain skills means it doesn't activate at all, and as we can see from the video that brought up the problem in the first place (find it here http://www.bloodscape.net.nyud.net:8090/perso/Indalamarwow0925040727530.rar ), those things happen a lot.

The Proposal:
Weapons that have procs have been given timers, so that they only proc a certain number of times per minute, which evens out the difference between fast and slow weapons with procs. How about allow Bloodthirst to stack with the skills/talents, set it up so that Bloodthirst is a passive proc on the Warrior's attack that can only be used about once to three times per minute? At least that would mean that the guy in the video would have to wait a while between every couple of ass-kicking runs, and since the Bloodthirst effect only lasts for eight seconds, he'd have to start over, like his first kill, after every rest.

The Benefit:
The Fury tree of the Talents is once again a viable option, even preferrable over Mortal Strike in certain specific situations, but it is no longer over-powered like that video made it look. A rest and re-start after every third kill in that video would not have made what was done impossible, but it would have made him stop to heal/wait for proc recharge for a minute after every 6-second encounter.

I know the system isn't perfect, but with some tweaking it could be much better than complete incompatibility.
