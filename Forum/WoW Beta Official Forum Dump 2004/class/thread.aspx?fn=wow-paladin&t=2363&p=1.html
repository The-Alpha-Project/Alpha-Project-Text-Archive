 7. Re: Divine Shield and Polymorph | 7/27/2004 4:59:08 PM GMTDT
If you time it right, you can get rid of sheep right after it hits. The sheep spell chance to affect you is calculated the moment the spell is cast, but the animation and consequent inability to do anything but regen doesn't take place until the casting animation is over and the spell hits you. If you cast shield in between the beginning of the spell and when it lands, you will cure yourself of sheep.

Once you are a sheep, a priest or another paladin can dispell it.
