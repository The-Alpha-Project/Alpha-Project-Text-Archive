Objectives:
1) To ensure that Berserker stance is the best 'shock' DPS against soft targets.
2) To ensure that Battle stance is the best 'sustained' DPS against tough targets. (warriors and paladins)

Two categories of changes are needed::
(nerfs): Damage reduction from 220% weapon damage to 100%.
(nerfs): Cooldown increase from 6 seconds to 10.

(buffs): Add 2 more levels of MS for a total of 3.
(buffs): Increase heal debuff duration for each level.(10, 17, 25)
(buffs): Reduce rage cost to 15.
(buffs): New effect: health debuff multipies bleed damage per level of MS.(2, 3, 4)


Please take a minute to review the changes I suggested and understand the tradeoffs. MS would be significantly less 'shock' damage and significantly more 'sustained' damage and would grant much more damage per rage point.

The effects this would have on the warrior class:
Arms talent warriors would much better against other warriors and paladins, while Fury talent warriors would be much better against all the other soft targets.
