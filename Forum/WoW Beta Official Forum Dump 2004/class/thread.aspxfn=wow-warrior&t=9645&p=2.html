 40. Re: Hit/Miss ratios | 10/14/2004 5:40:24 PM GMTDT
From the QA team:

The miss ratio against an even level NPC with maximum weapon proficiency for the level has not been changed. What can be misleading is that if an NPC rolls a successful block but has no shield, it will be reported as a miss. The same holds true for NPCs that parry but are not holding a weapon. The combined total can be perceived to be double or even triple the normal miss rate, when in actuality up to 2/3 of that percentage is the NPCs inherent avoidance skill and not reliant upon the players ability to hit.

We hope this clears up players concerns regarding this issue.
