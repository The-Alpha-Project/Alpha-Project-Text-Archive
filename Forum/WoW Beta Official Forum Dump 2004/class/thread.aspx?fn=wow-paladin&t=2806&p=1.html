Based on how talents baned out for Warrior and Mage, talents will likely fortify what you already have. I've seen many suggestions that say Paladins should additional attacks within their talents. Although they might get 1 or 2, they wont get 4 or 5.

Here's a URL that gives you a pretty good idea of how the talents panned out.

http://www.thottbot.com/wow/index.cgi?ti=Warrior
http://www.thottbot.com/wow/index.cgi?ti=Mage

Based on in game captions that are established now, we will likely have <Combat>, <Holy> and <Protection/Defense> as our captions.

To put this in perspective, here's a quick line up of what Talents did for warrior

Class Passives: Added 15 Class Passives
Class Passives are abilities the class is iniately proficent in or can be trained via skill points. Class passives include such things as specializing in a specific weapon wielding to rage generation. The vast majority of these increases are in the top of each caption.

Abiliity Increases: Added 23 Ability Increases
Abiliity Increases are abilities trained at the Class Trainer. There are improvements for every abilitiy a class trainer trains that is an activated ability. (IE There is no increase for Parry) The vast majority of these skills are in the top of each caption.

New Passive Abilities: Added 7 New Abilities
Passive Abilities are absolutely new things that can now passively occur when you use an old attack. The percentage of how often these passives occurs increases based on the level of the skill. There is an average of 3 per caption and in order to obtain them, you generally need to specialize in that caption by investing the vast majority of your talent points into that caption.

New Active Abiliites: Added 5 New Abilities.
New Active Abiliites are abilities given to you through talents that you did not have before. There is an average of 2 per caption and in order to obtain them, you generally need to specialize in that caption by investing the vast majority of your talent points into that caption.

To try to put this more into perspective, I've broken down the likely captions with the spells associated with them. This should give you a constructive end to suggest what caption should get what kind of increases. Understand the straight ability enchancements trained at the class trainer are a more or less a given and that the increase in procephies is also a given.

    Ok, I'll be a bit of a nerd and suggest some talents...

    Weapon Traits:
    - Mace Bash (each point will give a mace-wielding paladin 1% chance to stun the opponent)

    - Holy Sword (each point will cause a sword-wielding paladin to deal 1 point of additional holy damage)

    - Shining Spear (each point will cause a polearm-wielding paladin to regenerate 1 point of mana)

    - Axe of Justice (each point will cause an axe-wielding paladin to gain 2% higher chance for a critical strike)

    - Shield of Hope (each point will increase a Shield's block with 1% and the defense with 2%)


    Abiliity Increases:

    (DPS, ie. Solo)
    - Holy Strike (reduced mana cost, increased damage)

    - Crusader Strike (increase damage-add on with 1% per point)

    - Hammer of Justice (reduced cooldown)

    - Dominance Aura (+2% per point spent)

    (Healing)
    - Holy Light (Reduced mana cost)

    - Healing Aura (Increased health regeneration)

    - Wisdom Aura (Increased mana regeneration)


    (Tanking)
    - Seal of Fury (increased aggro, increased duration)

    - Seal of Salvation (decreased aggro, increased duration)

    - Seal of Protection (decreased cooldown)

    - Devotion Aura (1% per point increased defense)

    - Resistance Aura (+2.5 per point increased resist)

    - Retribution Aura (1% per point increased "feedback")


    Basically I feel that the Paladin's Talent-tree need to show a bit the different roles that you can play as Paladin. You can invest talent-points to make you either a more effective tank, or a more efficient healer... or to make your class more able to solo.


    New Passive Abilities:
    - Holy Strike cause aggro for each point spent.

    - Holy Light cause less aggro for each point spent.

    - Crusader Strike will drain a target's mana with 1 point per talent point.

    - Hammer of Justice will give an additional slow movement for 2sec (after stun) per spent talent point.

    - Exorcism will work on demons.
