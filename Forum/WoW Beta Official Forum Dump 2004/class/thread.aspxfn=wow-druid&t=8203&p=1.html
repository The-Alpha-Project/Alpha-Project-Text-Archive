Unless I'm healing to capacity, I'll at least use two of my forms. While solo, I use them all.

Caster form heals and can trade mana for respectable damage.
Cat form does the most melee damage and does not drain mana.
Bear form takes the least damage while doing respectable damage.

Caster form is something I use to pull, fight at range, heal after combat, heal during combat, and burninate.
Cat form is my non-mana-draining fast killer, but has the worst defenses of all of them.
Bear form is my form for trying to outlast something(s).

For example, I was in Cat form, and I pulled two (a mistake, otherwise one would have been rooted, I was outdoors). I shifted back, rooted one while fighting the other a couple of times, dropped one last heal and went into Bear form in hopes that my armor would see me though.
