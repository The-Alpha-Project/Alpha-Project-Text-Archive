I agree that the Paladin is too run-away-friendly. Why stay and fight when you have so many invulnerability buttons you can hit?

The major problem is that Seal of Protection and Divine Shield are blatant run-away spells. You can't even melee while under Protection. You can heal yourself while invulnerable, but if you're out of mana, all you can do is run.

IMHO, the problem would be greatly solved if those two spells were changed as follows:

Sanctuary (this should replace Seal of Protection)
Target player is invulnerable for up to 15 seconds. (player can 'click off' sanctuary)
Target player may not move, attack, or cast spells.
Target player cannot be buffed or healed.

Divine Shield
Invulnerable for 10 (15?) seconds.
Cannot be buffed or healed.
Slightly reduced movement speed (~70%)
Normal attack speed

Divine Favor could be left exactly as it is. A 5 second "run away" spell is fine, the problem is having 15 seconds' worth of running away.

Once the paladin's shield wore off, the warrior could step in with some taunts and take over tanking having a priest and paladin with tons of mana to heal him as he tanks the remaining life out of the dragon.
