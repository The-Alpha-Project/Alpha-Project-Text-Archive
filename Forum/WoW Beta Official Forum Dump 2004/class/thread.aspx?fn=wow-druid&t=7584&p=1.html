So here it is: I have read this forum for a long time and seen so many complaints about my class that i have not always agreed with. I have also seen a lot of suggestions (some good, some not) written by many of my fellow druids be ignored. I have been contemplating writing something for some time, but have been waiting and waiting for druids to be fixed.
I never thought druids were broken, rather that we added a different 'flavor' to a group. We lack the desirability of a priest, and the capability of a shaman(don’t know much about Paladins, as i play exclusively for the horde)
We received our talents not last push, but the one before that. I’d say 80% of our talents are useless. The ENTIRE feral combat line doesn’t make up for our shortcomings in the shape shifting forms. The forms flat out don’t cut it. Cat form is only useful for prowl and track. Bear form is just about completely useless. I have done extensive testing for both of these forms and have the numbers to back these claims up if needed. When I look for a rogue for my group, it isn’t necessarily for the DPS, it is for all the other things they bring to a group (sap, stuns, etc) Cat form doesn’t offer any of that. There are so many things I could go on about, but rather I choose to offer some suggestions.

-We need to be more effective in indoor instances. I don’t know what game designers you have looking into the druid class right now, but I can guarantee they don’t have the experience I do with instance raiding. WE NEED SOMETHING for indoor instances. Roots too powerful? Give me a break.

- Our only AE line take THIRTY-ONE talent points to acquire

-You have made shape shifting into and out of forms form efficient, but our forms remain INEFFECTIVE. Look into some new abilities for the bear and cat forms. Up the hp / ac in bear form. I’m nowhere near a warrior because I cant use a shield.

- Give us shields

- This is a tauren specific problem, but unless level 60 plainsrunning is put in, there is little point in being a tauren druid.

- Look into our talents, most of them need a re-visit as they are either too expensive or too ineffective

One of the only reasons we are effective right now is because of the Nature’s Swiftness bug makes us a viable healer. Guess what, next patch we cant even do that. Thanks for reading.

If you are a level 58+ DRUID and have experience with high-end content please post your thoughts in a clear organized manner, this is not a whine. We’re only trying to get our issues addressed.

Thanks, Drippy
