 1. a suggestion for a new talent ability w/ pets | 9/25/2004 1:20:01 AM GMTDT
In order to help hunters keep distance between their opponents, particularly in PvP, an ability like this would really be handy. often times if you're caught unawares in PvP by a melee class it's very tough to attack from ranged, as you cant attack while moving and wing clip doesnt work well enough or last long enough, and concussive shot is too short.
Also, many classes, melee specifically, have some kind of snare ability (hamstring, crippling poison, etc) which makes it just about impossible to keep enough distance to use ranged attack or spells.

so, i suggest something like this be added:

Feral Clasp -
Your pet attemps to get behind the target (on its own), then bites (or grasps within claws, see below) down and holds the target in its jaws (claws), immobilizing it but also keeping your pet from doing anything else. The target is not able to attack the pet holding it, but can attack or use abilities if others are in range.
(or, for pets where biting wouldnt make sense, could hold it down with their claws.)

Damage on the held target does not break the effect.

However, one limitation would be that the target cannot be aggroed at the pet, cus otherwise the pet could never get behind the target. So, that means you or someone else must draw the aggro to use the ability.

If it's too difficult to make to try to make the pet get behind the pet due to pathing and other issues, could just make it so the pet just rushes up and bites down regardless of which way the target is facing.

IMO, this'd be a great way that really shows the bond and cooperation in battles between a hunter and its friend, and a unique ability in itself.

of course a simple alternative to this is some kind of Net ability (like Ensnare from orc raiders in Warcraft 3), but it's not very original and doesn't use the deep bonds between a hunter and its pet.
