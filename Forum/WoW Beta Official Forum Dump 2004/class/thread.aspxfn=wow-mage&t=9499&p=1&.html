As a mage is supposed to be the burst DPS class (Well, I guess so, b ecause the only other thing we have is AOE and there are only 4 aoe damage spells), I have some suggestions for improving mage talents

Firstly: Why does Shamans (a HYBRID class) and warlocks (A pet DOT class) have a talent that boosts their critical damage by 100% while the mages, the NUKE class, dont? The nearest mages have is combustion, but SHAMANS HAVE BETTER COMBUSTION THAN THEM! While mages combustion works only to fire spells, Shamans Elemental mastery does the SAME effect, for the SAME cost to ALL Fire, nature. That, summed up with the fact that shamans nuke for 100% more damage with these spells, make shamans have better nuking talents than shamans. But wait, there is more! In the same tree, shamans have a clearcasting talent that does the same Effect for only 1 talent point. Sure, you have to spend more points in the elemental tree, but if you are getting te last talents, you will be getting this one anyway. and altought Mages have other nuking talents like Arcane power (Wich comes with a downside mind you), the shamans have it all in the same tree. Mages have some in arcane, some in fire, wich means they will never get all of them
