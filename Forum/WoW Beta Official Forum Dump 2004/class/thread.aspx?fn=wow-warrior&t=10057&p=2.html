However, I don't agree with your MS changes since this is the only reason to use a slow weapon, ever. With a faster weapon you regenerate rage more often, meaning that if you only need like 3 rage to use say.. Intimidating Shout in an emergency, you will get those 3 rage points FASTER at all times.

Odds are when wielding a slow weapon that you don't get that rage fast enough because you have to wait longer on average to get that bump in rage. Even though you get plenty more rage per hit with the slower weapon you may not need that much and you end up being forced to wait longer for a small amount of rage.

This is not even considering unbridled wrath which you haven't taken into account at all. That talent alone would make slower weapons total crap without a doubt.

If all special attacks and abilities disregarded speed and weapon damage per hit completely as the suggested MS change, what point is there for these numbers to show up as stats? Or even be different at all? There is none. All weapons of equal DPS could just as well be identical in terms of speed.

The different special attacks need to work differently from each other, just in the same way that the weapon specialization talents do. There should however be an option for faster two-handers and that's why I agreed on the HS.

Slam is a different problem altogether and is completely broken if you ask me. So having that as a replacement for slow weapons is really not an option, not at this time anyway.
