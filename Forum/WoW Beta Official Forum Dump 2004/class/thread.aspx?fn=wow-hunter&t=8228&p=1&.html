 3. Re: Talent: Double-shot - (Passive) | 10/13/2004 8:47:57 PM GMTDT
While this idea is similar to crits, it is not the same. This would have you actually fire off two arrows at the same time, both toward your enemy, as if you actually notched them yourself. Each arrow would have a chance to hit and a chance to crit.

I like your idea of starting at 2% and going up to 10%. However, one could counter-argue that by starting at 2%, only those who PLAN to go up to 10% would bother buying it at all since 2% would be nearly worthless.

Therefore, to strike a balance, I'd suggest starting at 4% and going up to 12%... 4, 6, 8, 10, 12.

By doing it this way, those who want at least a little bonus would likely put one point in, and those who want to continue would find it worthwhile as well.

At 12%, that's 1 every 8.5 shots firing twice, probably averaging one "double-shot" per even-level encounter.
