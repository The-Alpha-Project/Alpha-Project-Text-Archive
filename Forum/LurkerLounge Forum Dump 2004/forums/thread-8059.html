Quote:This downloader is all kinds of bizzarre. Today I'm downloading at 15 kB/s, and not uploading a single byte. I guess I might be able to play tonight after all.

See, BitTorrent has a 1-to-1 ratio. Some people download a bit more, some people upload a bit more, but over all, everyone benefits by everyone else uploading while they download. The problem is that Blizzard's implementation of it is quite lopsided, in that some people are downloading at 150+k/s without problem, while others upload at their maximum, saturating their line, making their own downloads extremely poor.

If Blizzard had a fancier mechanism to determine upload speed, and set the torrent's max upload speed appropriately, everyone would be able to download fast, and upload at a reasonable speed, thereby evening out the upload to download distribution. So instead of 5 people getting 100k/s from 10 other people while those 10 get 1 k/s from the other 5, all 15 will get 50k/s, a little bit of bandwidth from each, evenly distributed.

Even distribution of uploads is the goal, but Blizzard is WAY off with their current downloader right now.
