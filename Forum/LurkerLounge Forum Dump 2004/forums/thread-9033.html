Just wanted to mention, because this came up while in-game, that an easy way to checkout who's online from the guild at any given moment is to:

1. Open the social window (default 'O' key)
2. Select the "Who" tab
3. Enter "Lounging Lurkers" in the edit box above the "Refresh" button.
4. Hit "Refresh"

All the online Lurkers will be shown in the window, and you can sort by location, level, and some other things. Everytime you want to check who's online, be sure to hit "Refresh", otherwise you'll be looking at old data. The good thing about this window is that it remebers the filter criteria in the window, so you don't have to keep re-filtering and re-sorting.
