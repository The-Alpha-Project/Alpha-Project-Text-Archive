Hi gang. I'm one of the poor schlubs Reptilehouse talked into helping admin the Basin guilds, so I can answer your question.

The short answer is that based on our experience with running multiple server branches in DAoC (at one point it was four), we found it difficult to manage the whole thing. Cultures on each server began to grow in differnet directions, in effect moving us away from our vision of a unified guild.

To avoid the same dynamic from happening again, we orginally wanted to just support one server, but compromized on two. Based on our read of the wants and desires of our guild members, we picked PvE Alliance and PvP Horde. Most of the hardcore DAoC PvP types like the underdog status that the Horde seems to represent and thus for balance Alliance PvE seemed like a good choice. This allows you to experience most of the games content and classes.

Anyway, we know that not everyone will be please with our choice, but most of our past and current members should be able to find a home.

I'm not a regular reader of the LL boards, so if you ever need anything, just send me or RH a PM.

Looking forward to playing with you all in WoW. :)
