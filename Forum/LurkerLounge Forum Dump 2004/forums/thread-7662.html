Having been a *huge* fan of previous Blizzard games, partly due to their insanely high-quality film-sequences, I didn't hesitate to download the intro from fileplanet when I saw it posted this morning. I uploaded it for you nice people, just in case you haven't seen it before.

Now I know the game can't possibly live up to these graphics, but even *I* played with the thought of spending $12 a month to play this beauty. See the hair on the dwarf's shoulders. I would have loved a StarCraft/WarCraft/Diablo-feature.

Well, enjoy the trailer, if you haven't yet. It's just under 50MB, so it should be manageable for most people. *Off to see it again*

Download G-spot (no, it's not what you think, you filthy bastards! :P). It's a programme which tells you what codecs you're missing for any particular file you want to play. You open the avi-file in Gspot, and it tells you whether or not you have the necessary audio and/or video-codecs installed. The programme also gives you the name of any codec you might be missing, so googling it, won't be much of a hazzle.

(Or you could go for Deebye's defiler-pack.)
