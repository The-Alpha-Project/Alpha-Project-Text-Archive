By the way, there's a relatively new instance called Razorfen Downs in the southern Barrens that's right across the road from Razorfen Kraul. It's a level 35-40 instance, and I found it to be very fun. It has a lot of undead in it (mixed with some non-undead), so priests and paladins should particularly enjoy it. There's one quest in the SW Cathedral for it with some great rewards iirc and one quest inside the instance itself. There's also a special alchemy trainer tucked away inside the instance. Anyway, if you get tired of running the SM over and over, give Razorfen Downs a try!

P.S. When the patch introducing Razorfen Downs came out, the quest in SW required you to be level 37 to get it. It's possible that the level requirement was reduced in the latest patch, though. I certainly sent in some /suggests about that.
