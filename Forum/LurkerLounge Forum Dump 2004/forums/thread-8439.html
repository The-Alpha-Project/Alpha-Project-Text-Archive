Now that the time is near at hand, I've got to say that I'm tempted to give the FvF server a try. For one, I think that people underestimate how easy it's going to be to get equipment again for their characters but if you start on the new server, you'll see everyone started from square one again. However, beyond that I'm kind of interested to see how much of an effect the way it is implemented really has on the game. It should be interesting to see which areas are designated as being contested and which ones are controlled by one faction or the other.

Then my choice really comes down to how I want to play. If I play on the PvE server, I'll probably go looking for new equipment and items for my Night Elf Warrior. It'd be only a small pain to find new equipment (more of a pain to find cool-looking equipment like I had before) but getting new tradeskill items would be a bit more of a pain (I had just made around 80-100 bandages this morning only to see them go up in smoke...). He's level 20, currently, and I'm kind of interested to see how things shape up for the levels after 20.

My other choice is to start over on one of the servers. If I do, I'm currently leaning toward using a priest (either Night Elf for sheer coolness, in my opinion, or a human which would probably be more fun to roleplay as a priest, for me). The advantage here is that I'd get something new, I'd get to see several of the tradeskills that I didn't use with my warrior, and I'd have a character of a class that I dearly needed in my party throughout the quests I did for levels 10-20. Being wanted would be kind of nice. :)
