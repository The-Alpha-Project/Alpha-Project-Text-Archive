There's also a last minute patch to try out for one day. Small changes, of course:

Code:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- World of Warcraft Client Patch 1.1.1 (2004-11-17)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Durability
&nbsp;Item durability costs have been greatly decreased for Superior (blue)
&nbsp;and Epic (purple) items. &nbsp;


Improved Unstuck
&nbsp;We've added an auto-unstuck feature that you can use prior to
&nbsp;petitioning a GM. &nbsp;You can use this feature in situations where you
&nbsp;are caught in a part of the terrain and cannot get out. &nbsp;You can find
&nbsp;the unstuck option by clicking on the "Help Request" button found
&nbsp;on the bottom right hand side of the user interface.


Spirit Healer
&nbsp;Using the Spirit Healer now results in a 25% loss in durability for a
&nbsp;player's equipped gear and items in inventory, instead of the
&nbsp;previous 100% loss. &nbsp;The length of resurrection sickness suffered
&nbsp;from using the Spirit Healer has also been decreased to a maximum of
&nbsp;10 minutes.


Reagents
&nbsp;Reagent requirements have been removed from the following spells:
&nbsp;Druid: Mark of the Wild
&nbsp;Mage: Arcane Intellect
&nbsp;Priest: Power Word: Fortitude


General
- The Daze effect has been reworked to have a smaller chance of
&nbsp;occurring against players level one to 29. &nbsp;The chance of a
&nbsp;successful Daze effect against players 30 and above remains the same.

Talents
- The Improved Renew talent has been reduced in effectiveness per rank.
- The effect chance of Seal of Command has been reduced slightly.
- Redoubt now lasts either 10 seconds or five blocks, whichever comes
&nbsp;first. &nbsp;
- The effect of Improved Moonfire has been reduced per rank.


Druid
- Bear Form has been improved to hold aggro more effectively.
- Growl: removed rage cost and global cooldown.
- Cower: increased the effect, added a cooldown.


Paladin
- Judgment: Moved to level 4.
- Divine Protection: Moved to level 6.
- Purify: Moved to level 8.
- Seal of the Crusader: Now introduced at level 6.


Rogue
- Feint: increased the effect, added a cooldown.


Shaman
- New Spell: Reincarnation (Level 30). Reincarnation is a passive
&nbsp;ability that will give you to option of letting you resurrect
&nbsp;yourself once every half hour upon death.


Warlock
- Inferno: Now dismisses your pet before casting to allow the Infernal
&nbsp;to start Enslaved. &nbsp;Useable outdoors only.


Warrior
- Defensive Stance has been improved to hold aggro more effectively.
- Taunt: removed rage cost and global cooldown.


Bug Fixes
- Night Elf characters in Wisp form will now have correct collision
&nbsp;detection.
- Damage modifiers will now be calculated before armor points are
&nbsp;considered, instead of after.
