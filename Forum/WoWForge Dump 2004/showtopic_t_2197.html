    Re: Lineage 2
    My Impressions.

    I managed to download this sucker tonight, took about 30 mins for the d/l and install combined. Loaded up fairly quick and smooth. Was kinda disapointed with the really ugly VGUI. The main thing about this game is the AMAZING graphics. Seriously, the graphics in thsi game are sick. I would have to disagree with wigen with the graphics thing for myself, but simply becuase I started as a dwarf and I only had about 25-35 people on my screen at any time, and i found my comp to be lagging VERY VERY slightly. ( Just at a level where its a noticible change, but not at all a bad thing) I noticed one small graphics bug that kinda annoyed me, but nohtign serious.. the dynamic shadows would appear on the roofs when entering and around buildings.

    But thats where the fun stops. Like said it takes an insane amount of killing to level up. I found there was FAR too much downtime in this game as well. You really gained more money/sp from the very first things you cna kill then going off to fight bigger thigns because you could chain them non-stop instead of fighting 2-3 then having to rest for the amount of time it takes to kill 2-3 more. The gameplay in this game seriously lacks. If I had people to talk to and play with, perhaps it would be better. A very annoying thing was the movement in this game seriously blows. Its hard to control exactly where your avatar goes. Not to mention sometimes he would just stop moving/turning on his own.. even though the buttons were never released. As well, like before the VGUI just plain sucks. You cannot change the sizes of windows, and you cannot really modify all that much within the options. Everythign loks the same and running 1600x1200 the screen is VERY empty (I have issues in other games where I have so much crap I can barely see the game window with 1600x1200...)

    Found the system very hard to learn as well. There is little to no ducumentation for the starters, and you really have to read through tons of context's to learn about how to play the game. And thes econtex's are very difficult to read fast as half the words are split on two lines.

    If perhaps there was a ton more information on how to go from lvl 1-20 effectivly, perhaps it would be better, but there really isn't.

    My few thoughts, will give it some more playing later.
