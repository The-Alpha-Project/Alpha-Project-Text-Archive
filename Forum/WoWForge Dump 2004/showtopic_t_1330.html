    400,000 Beta Apps - 10,000 CD-keys
    Stolen from worldofwar.net front page

    There is an article today in a California newspaper about the beta signups. You'll need to complete a free registration to read it. The beta drew 400,000 applicants and Blizzard plans on releasing 10,000 keys to those applicants. That means that 2.5% of the people who entered will get in. However, according to Blizzard this number included Korea, and by the time duplicates are removed, it's more likely to be around 275,000. Good Luck!
