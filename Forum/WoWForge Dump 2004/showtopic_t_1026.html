The New Face of a Community
After taking care of some business yesterday, as well as talking to some other people about the current issues, i've decided to lay down a new face to this kind of thing. This whole thing started with the posting of logs of a conversation that a Frontman of SC claimed SC to be going private. After these logs were posted, insted of replying(the front man could of also posted news on wowforge denieing it) the SC team claimed them to be false logs, and decided to bash WoWForge for posting "Fake" logs on the website. In turn to prove that the logs were true i posted a Screen shot of an unalterable Chat window where the logs where taken from. This in turn triggered a group of squbbles and DDOS's Inbetween WoWForge and SC. As it go's now, i think we have resolved all issues with these groups.

From here on out, Unless the news is posted from an Offical of that group or absolutly confirmed via an offical or website or chat, then we will not be posting news about anything that may affect the PR status of that group.

As for the aguments in the forums, i have gone through and closed all the threads. Aguments like these arn't going to be a part of wowforge anymore.

I want to try to make wowforge the respectable site that it was before, so we're just going to pretty much mind our own business now.
