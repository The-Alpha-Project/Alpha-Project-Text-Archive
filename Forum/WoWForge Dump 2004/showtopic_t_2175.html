    Beta Update from Beta Tester
    Written by AfterBurn

    Aww a tip – Write long posts in notepad first. Anyways this post will be long and cliff notes will be at the bottom. I will indicate areas and point of more importance than others. Some of you may remember me from War3 Betas and I did posts similar to this, this is a different type of game and all, but I hope this is well received by the community. It is early in beta, but I have played a long time over the last week or so and feel I know what I am talking about.

    Ok – I will start with the bad and the ugly – end with the good!

    The Bad

    - Tedious – This game is very very tedious at times. Everyone will attest to this, at times you will not play for an hour because you took a left to look at a cool building and got swarmed. For example – for many the first time they see undead is in Duskwood – they check it out at Raven Hill and maybe die, then they realize exactly how bad of a placement BIND STONES can be. Die at raven hill – ten minute getting body +_ time getting wherever you wanted to go. Bad placement. I don’t see or get the thinking about having Bind Stones In or near town – I think some thought should be put into bind stone placement. Westfall is good, Red Ridge is good, Loch Modan is good – Duskwood, StrangleThorn, and Wetlands are just HORRIBLE.

    Along the lines of being tedious – Some of the quests are horrible for groups and others for soloing. If you have a quest to collect 10 scales from dragons and you have a party of 5 – you are killing 200 Dragons at least – If you solo – maybe 20. On the other hand – If you need to just skill ten dragons period. A group of 5 rolls that in a second. Some say that balances it out between the two types. I say no way, The only quests I see people reject are collecting quests.

    Orc Sentries - Take them out – if its an instance type quest – make it an instance. Losing your body in Stonewatch Keep often means taking an Exp hit – Thanks for telling us about that! But really – give them a Dramatically lesser “aggro” radius vs ghosts. Either that or make it an instance…..Like it should be…

    • Van Cleef – “…..”
    • Sap – Abused
    Classes:
    • Warrior – Boost 2 Hand weapons
    • Priest Buff Healing and such
    • Mage – Mana regen
    • Paladin – Nerf healing – boost two hand weapon
    • Rouge – Sap needs to be nerfed – One per rouge
    • Warlock – Not sure – they seem self sufficient
    About some balance between races:
    - One hand weapons are fast and can deal the same if not more damage than 2 hand weapons AND they get a shield?!?! Seriously come on Blizzard, be the first company every to make two hand weapons cool and usefull? Buff the damage and speed quite a bit please, or better yet nerf one hand weapons a lot. They get +70 armor and stats and have a faster and better weapon than me and I use two hand? I don’t have a craptastic weapon either, its very nice for a 2 hand mace.

    Told you it was going to be long.

    Quest Log – First off a quest log limit is good, but not at what is now. It should not be an overall number – 20 that’s all! No – that’s wrong. You need to make it a limit of quests per AREA. I get punished because I explore? Thanks that’s cool! I recommend 7 quests per area as the limit. I have quests in places I have never been and all and then I cant accept one in front of me because it is full.
    • Lag – Server lag – “….”
    • Memory leaks – “….”
    • XML stack overflow “….”
    • Fatal Errors – “….”
    • Bad or no maps “….”
    Now to the biggest issue to me – and it goes with my previous “….” points:

    Lack of communication from Blizzard to testers:
    You have a kick ass reporting system with /bug and /suggestion – but where are you guys? Kat posts on the forums sometimes about things, but Kat – I think you are doing an awesome job and all, but this is Beta to get things done. You did a wonderful job pre beta and you are still doing well, but it is time for the Dev team to come to us – I don’t want or trust you to “relay” the comment to the Dev guys. No offence, but that could mean a number of things. - - - No contact. I send bugs some serious, and I have nobody to talk to about it and get it fixed, I need to rely on the /bug to hopefully work.

    I will admit I have seen ONE GM doing a good job, shout out to Triss for showing interest in improving the game. He or She cared and it showed. But the fact remains, I see nobody on I can talk to about issues. It really sucks –It does, I do not like testing a game not knowing if I am being useful. If someone posts about a crash in tech forums we are treated like we are complete idiots – though some are. We are asked for the exact code/error we get etc etc – But then no response or comment – which is 100% needed in the Tech Forum – That place is a train wreck…..

    When you enter a new area there should be a guy around the road with like and orange “!” that tells you if you are not ready for the area or not, I can be ignored of course, but I think it is good for everything boarding the newbie lands. Something along the lines of “You are a brave soul, but just not quite fit for the perils of this land just yet!”

    Ok Random comments now:

    Fishing sucks! What the hell is the point of going to master fisherman? You do not gain ANYTHING from improving, Just spend one skill point and you can be just as good as any other fisherman for that. There is ZERO point in improving – Many skills are like this. Label the water difficulty at the peers around the world – It stinks having zero clue besides the type of area you are in level wise.

    Increase ghost speed a little more – CR’s suck, not as much as EXP hits, but it’s bad.

    Mobs aggro for a LONG time and from a extreme distance at times – I heard this is getting addressed, so Ill just leave it at this and see what happens.

    I killed ooze with one other person for over an hour looking for one item in the wetland….. Give me a break

    If you are going to kill me for entering an off limit area – don’t make me run for my body. Either that or tell me its off limits before you kill me. I died by the Hand of Blizzard to a “off limits” area and it took at least 30 minute to get my body and run back to my bind stone. Not to mention no map for that area.

    Bugged quests – Take them out until they are unbugged –Arg, it is SO annoying….

    Please drop players off randomly ofer a certain area at griffs – Everyone just stacks in one place and it takes some people a LONG time to load that.

    Can you add a public chat channel.
    1. General
    2. Trade
    3. Help Please – thanks

    MANA COSTS LABELED AND LISTED FOR EVERYTHING

    -----/stuck takes to long – 2.5 minutes.





    That was a lot of bad: There is quite a bid of good though. Though it won’t take up much of the post I will go over them.

    Quests are pretty diverse and cool, decent story so far, variety is decent, a few tedious quests though.

    The Tradeskill systems for most of the skills are AWESOME – I think you did a wonderful job with that and kudos to you on that.

    Graphics and world are simply amazing.

    Travel is cool and affordable.

    Parties and looting are great.

    Money system is cool so far.

    Diversity in areas is cool.

    I love the Z axis 

    UI and HUD are cool! The XML is great when it doesn’t stack overflow.

    Emotes and such are awesome – Dwarves have the best speech by far.

    Guild features are cool – I love the guild chat and all.

    Good signs around the world and in the city.

    There is a ton of stuff I love about this game – Much more love than hate, but as a tester it is my duty to highlight and address the bad in detail while commending the good at the same time.

    I Missed quite a bit, but its been an hour and I think I have said enough for one post.

    Cliff Notes:

    Read the Bold and Italic parts and: told you there were good notes!

    This is 1500 words for a game…..
