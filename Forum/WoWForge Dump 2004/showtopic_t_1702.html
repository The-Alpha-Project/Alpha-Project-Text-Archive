    Let me explain swgs system. Almost the whole game is about faction based combat.. galactic civil war, rebels vs imperials blah blah... you can have guild wars were every single member of the 2 guilds has a TEF (temporary enemy flag) to eachother. if you just wanna kill other rebels or imperials (depending on what faction you are) you must declare cause if you dont declare then it shows you as neutral to the opposition, but basically you can kill eachother anywhere you want if your declared and the other person has a tef (by killing a rebel or imperial npc) or if they are declared also.
