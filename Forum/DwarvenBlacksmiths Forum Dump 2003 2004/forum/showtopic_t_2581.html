I was aprenticed to a witch furher up the street, what my mother didnt know was that she taught me the demonic arts of a warlock, I myself didnt realise it till the rictual was finished, but then i was enslaved. However I found small release from the torment by making little machines in my spare time, its the Gnome in me I guess.
When my aprenticeship ended I left Ironforge and wandered the wilderness trying to fight off the corruption, finally I left for kalimdor to see if the great new tree of Teldrassil could save me from possesion by the infernal forces...
In kalimdor my status as a warlock kept the satyrs and other demon-spawn at a respectful disatnce but ment I must be wary of Night eleves...
When I reaced Teldrassil I drank of its sap, and took a twig,with it i could control and hide my powers. Once in Ironforge I applyed to join the Dwarven Blacksmiths who might welcome for my love of complicated devices and my magical abilites.
