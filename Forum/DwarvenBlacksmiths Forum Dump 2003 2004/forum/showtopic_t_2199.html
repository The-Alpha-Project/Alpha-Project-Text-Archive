My character is called vicious and this is his story.

Vicious was happy person growing up in the Night Elf clan, one of the kindest as well (despite his name). When growing up his best friend Malthus always hung out with him and went exploring with him evryday. They had a great time and never fought whatsoever. However, on the day when the moon was blood red, a female entered the friendship and she was accepted as the trio. Her name was Danielle and right off the bat Vicious became interested in her kindnest and comedian like personality. After some time they began to fall in love, Vicious never felt this way in his whole life and he never wanted to let her go.

One day Vicious and Malthus went hunting in the woods, where they stumbled upon a Baselisk that look weak. Vicious and Malthus both decided attack it from both sides. Malthus, an expert in bowmanship, attacked from the front while Vicious, an expert in swordsmanship (mainly Katana) snuck up and attacked from behind. The sweat poured from Vicious' face as they counted down until they would attack. The time came and Malthus popped up and and shot his arrow through the thigh of the Baselisk. Vicious rushed out, drew his sword, and was ready to deal the final blow when the Baselisks call was answered. About twenty other Baselisks surrounded Malthus and Vicious showing them their rotting jaws. Both of them tried to fight their way out incuring injuries but Malthus getting the worst of it.

Vicious carrying Malthus back to his hut where they met Danielle, she was a studying priestess and helped bandage Malthus up. Over the next few weeks Danielle spent most of her time with Malthus helping him recover yet seeing her self falling in love with this kind Elf. When Malthus was feeling somewhat better they found themselves sleeping together, Vicious was just returning to the hut excitedly carrying new medicine and bandages when he stumbled upon them seemingly so happy together something at that moment died in Vicious never to be seen again! His heart was broken, he felt betrayed, and that he could never know love again. He shut all that he was out of him becoming something cold and brutal.

Not willing to talk to either of them he dissappeared into the woods for weeks. On the night he returned feeling a stronger man, raided his own village killing the elders who perceived Vicious to be a kind man returning from an exploration. He killed them all, all those that opposed him, few were left. "From now on, my power is the only power!" Vicious now hunted for Malhus, but he coudn't be found Malthus wanted out. So Vicious went to Danielle giving her an ultimatum, "Kill Malthus or else I'll kill both of you." Danielle dissappeared as well not wanting to kill Malthus or be killed.

Now my charcter Vicious is a cold man believing in the survival of the fittest, the weak feeding the strong, and believing in the life of a clan. He wanders the lands of Duskwood and Dun Morogh these dark, cold, and bitter lands just like his heart!

After many years of killing mindless orcs and hordes, Vicious looked to someone greater than himself, someone who would inspire him to become even stronger than he is now. He found this source of greater power in the DBS clan, a group of skilled and intelligent fighters who some members, like himself, were looking to rise in the ranks and achieve a great sense of power. This was his new goal, with his dark past behind, yet still an independent person, Vicious looked forward to new challenges that would make him stronger.


(Hoped you like the bio, i'm not going to be a menace to the clan or evil to everyone giving us a bad image. Just want to have fun with this character and everyone else in the clan!)
