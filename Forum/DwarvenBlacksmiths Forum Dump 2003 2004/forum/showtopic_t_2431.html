My Bio


I grew up in a strange town filled with many short people with hammers. not knowing anything about the surroundings i have just joined. the first thing i had to learn after birth was how to defend myself. my defense? a huge hammer than i could hardly drag. nothing was easy for me as a kid. then, my father had to go to a war, he said he would be back for me and mother. i havent seen him since. I had to fend for my mother when she got older but I had to go to work most of the day. my job was a blacksmith. My fathers training got me strong enough to be able to sling a 400 pound golden hammer. i can also bend a thick piece of metal into a half then bent the halves bend again. 2 years after i became a blacksmith my mother died. I was alone in the world. i knew now what i had to do. I went into the forest and built a log cabbin. it took 2 months but was great for the time it took. then, i set out to find my father. I went to nearly every town. exept one, the dark kingdom. I had to continue looking, so I entered. The town looked so strange, it was very dark and everyone there were theives. I had been mugged twice. i asked around and got some information. i went to one of the houses and hit the door knocker... a man answered the door. he only said one thing. "son..."

As my father looked at me a woman from inside of the house asked father who i was. He said in a short voice, "this is my son" Shocked, the woman walked inside.

my father asked me how old i was. said why? he handed me a bottle of ale. I drank happily from the bottle. I walked into the house and shook hands with the lady in the house. Father introdused me to the lady. Her name was gwen. I was home.

My life changed. I went from hero to Dark lord. I had to defend myself now more than ever. Even tho i hardly ever left the house. on my 10th day there i rememberd somthing... I caled for father. he was pamting. he was running. I told him to come with me. so he desided not to ask questions and followed. We traveled to the log cabbin i had built. I said to my father, we should live here. My father liked the idea not being able to stand the dark dongeon he was inprisoned in. we quickly moved all the belongings there. we then led a nice life as heros.

Two years after moving, my father weakened in pain. he aged and aged untill finally he couldnt get out of bed. with me and gwen by his side, he died. we baried him in the forest, next to a garden. The garden contained flowers of gold and silver. we laid a hammer by his grave. On his tome it read : Here lies the greatest father. Greatest husband, and greatest blacksmith of them all.

He was visited by all of the town, they often traveled with goods for father in the afterlife. We all wished for him to come back. It happened. 5 years later. I was in bed. I wasnt fully asleep yet, but I was trying. there was very little light, I couldnt really see. but suddenly a vast shaddow appeared infront of me. asif there were a wall directly infront of my face, and I could see my reflection. A mysterious voice called out in a vaint whisper.

the voice... said one thing. "son" and disapeared. I didnt get to sleep the rest of the night. the only thing i could do was sit there. I had no idea where he was or what had happened. i tried to understand the shaddow... i walked into the bathroom and there was a meesage written in the mirror.

The Message read: Son, I have came to you in search of my bosy. I have found it and i am back. you will find me in the morning. I will probably be in he snow. Wake me. i need to talk to you.
The next morning i walked out side and there was a pile of snow infront of my cabbin. i unburried the sleeping man and woke him. He stardledly awoke in a shiver and we walked inside. My father was explaining the not on the mirror and the shadow. He said he had to find his body as a ghost. then he could come back. Gwen walked into the room and quickly jumped at the sight of my father. she to had not ever known that the spirits could revive themselvs. My father called my name, and i quickly jumped to attention. He told my to get my hammer. I had finally gained the streingth to lift the mighty hammer, but not for very long. for it took the strength of a mighty blacksmith to lift. he forged my a hammer that was exactly like his exept a bit smaller. he told me of our mission and we set off to the dark town that once housed the nobile hero. we went to face our biggest foe. *place name of the really bad.... bad guy.... guy....*

Another chapter of the story (5-18-05)

As we ventured to Ironforge to find the gryphon master, we came apon a young boy. The young boy was selling a kitten that he want allowed o keep. we decided to take him, and we took care of him.

We found the gryphon master, and put the kitten in a cage. then we hopped on gryphons and soared to the harbor. When we made it to the harbor, the boat was about to leave! we ran as fast as we could and leaped onto the boat. luckaly we made it. we sailed through the great sea and into elvan territory. the night elves are nice people of the aliance so we were safe for a little while here. but we needed to go to ashenvale. we hopped on our mounts and rode into the dark forest.

As we rid, we encountered a guard of the horde. he saw us, and yelled. the words remained undecyphered as we could not understand the orcish language. My father was a hunter, aand pulled out his blunderbus. He took aim and shot a heavy shot straight in the orcs head. The orc screamed in pain and ran at me. I strenghtened my grip on the maul and slammed it into his ribs. The orc fell over from the blow, and remained steady and stiff. We checked his pockets, and took his armor and weapons ad ventured on.

After the encounter with the orc, we needed ammo for the blunderbus, as e figured we may need it more on the trip. we stopped at a general store. I had 1g and my father had 53 silver from all the trouble hes had with his life. we wandered onward to find the orcish environment.
