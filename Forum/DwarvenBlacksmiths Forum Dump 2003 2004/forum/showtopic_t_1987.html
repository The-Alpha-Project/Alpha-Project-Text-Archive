PROLOGUE

WAM! As the door flew open a Dwarf of great stature entered. He stood a bit taller than most dwarfs. The Dwarf had a somewhat scared face and a long white beard. As he strutted by you could tell he was of noble blood. His eye’s were dark from years of trials. Finally after a long silence he asked, “Aye, does anyone know where I can find a Guild of Dwarfs that’s looking’ for a stoat Warrior?” “ I do.” said a dwarf. Our hall is about a days walk from here.” The dwarf paused for a moment and than replayed, “Well I have had a long journey and need a bit of rest. Let me tell you about myself a bit than we can get going, said the dwarf.
Where ought I start, well my name is Thorhammer and I am older than most of yer. But my story starts when I am just a lad. See my father was a very honorable Merchant and was always travelin’ around as merchants do. My mother and I would go with him where ever he went. I am sure you all remember all the problems good people had when we tried to expand are trading routes into other Orgrimmar. See my father believed that all races sept those veil Undead could be trusted. This statement brought forth a Cheer from all in the Tavern. Well see there is a place called Burning Pass that lead straight to Orgrimmar. And we were traveling through there, I can still remember that morning, just as if it were yesterday.

“ Get up lad, we got to get movin’ I want to be within’ a days march of Orgrimmar by tonight.” I heard my father shout. “ Alight, getting up dad.” Why can’t he let my sleep in, just today. We had been marching and trading throughout all of Kalimdor. Get up I told myself, got to get up. As soon as I stepped out of the tent I was greeted by the sounds of morning. Some dwarves were just getting up while others were cleaning guns or eating. I walked over to one of the older dwarf, one of my fathers friends. “Morning Thorhammer, have a good rest?” he asked. “It wasn’t bad, considering it was out here.” he chuckled. “ What you say I start some breakfast? You go get some water from me canteen, its in my tent” I obeyed partially became this whey I wouldn’t have to make my own meal and would have time to relax. We talked for a while, nothing important just such things as how the expedition was going, how long till we went home and such things. As we were talking and finally finishing our meal I heard a clamor. What could that be about? Maybe the scouts got frightened off by lizards of some sort again, I laughed at my own joke. As I approached I had to fight my way through a ring of dwarves. Why was everyone just standing there I wondered. Than I finally saw what there were staring at, No it couldn't be! “What happened?” I wailed. “ A dwarf stepped up, I was walking back to my tent and I heard the sounds of a fight. As I went towards the noise I saw your Father fightin’ at least half a dozen things. I couldn't tell what they were cause all this fog. I ran to aid him but it was to late. Than I saw your pa drop.” There was deafening silence. Than we heard shouts in the distance, and shapes coming towards us, No one moved. Than drums began to beat, they sent a chill down your spin, this shock someone out of the trance than had fallen opon us. “ Arm yourself, there comin” instantly everyone started moving. Went back to my tent to grab my Axe ready to fight. I was ready to repay those who had killed my father, my sorrow and fear was replaced by anger and madness! As my fathers son I was in command, even though many others around me had many more winters under there belts than I. “Line up!” I bellowed. “ Battle formation, lets show these monsters what happens when they mingle in the affairs of dwarves!” An old dwarven battle song broke out somewhre and quickly everyone joined in. With spirts boosted the line was ready for the charge, than the soudn of metal and metal filled the air. Our line was hit and began to buckle, we were surrounded than soon flanked. “ I came face to face an Undead Warrior, he was clad in mail and a helmet. He appeared to be welding a Great two-handed sword. And his face, some long dead man whose sole was now and forever tormented. He swung at me and I barley ducked. “ That the best you can do?” I taunted. I could only hope I was doing the right thing, don’t want to end up in two separate pieces I thought. I deflected his blow and spun in to strike at him. But he was quicker than he let on. His long sward swiped across my face dropping me instantly. Than as the blood and despair clouded my vision, and my world went black.
As I awoke everything was blurry. Than as stuff slowly came to focus I was meet by a horrific scene. Death and destruction was everywhere. The caravan was completely destroyed. wagons were overturned and everything valuable appeared to have been taken. I stood up, still a bit shaky and began walking around. I was’t sure what I would accomplish by this but I was confused. What had happened, first they found my father dead. Than what happened, it happened so fast everything had. The charge the battle, than I remember facing that Undead Warrior. A cold shiver went up and down my spin, I felt my forehead. The blood had clotted, probably the only reason I was still alive. It was still a very nasty cut, and the pain was searing, I could barley stand. But as I began to look around for food the reality of what had happened began to sink in. Every was dead, my friends, father, and everyone else. The world closed in around me for I was alone, all alone in the middle of this dark lands. The Undead either didn’t eat or didn’t prefer food, so I was able to round of some hard biscuits and salted pork. I filled up my canteen and headed North. My only hope lied in the Elves to the North, but it could take days to reach them, yet I trudged onward, determined to make it, somehow....
