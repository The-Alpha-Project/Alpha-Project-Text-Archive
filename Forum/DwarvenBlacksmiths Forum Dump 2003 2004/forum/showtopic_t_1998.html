Ok guys, this game is REALLY scary! If you are one of those who get scared easy, then I dont recommend it to you!
Its almost always dark in the rooms, and you cant hold both the flashlight and your gun in your hand at the same time, so I have to switch to a gun after you see something jump out of the dark towards you.
If you want an example of a scary moment, Ill tell you one.
Imagine a VERY dark and tight room with a short stair that leads to a door. You have your flashlight in your hand and examine the room before prociding. Everything looks clear so you begin to walk. Its almost complete silence in the room. At the very moment your infront of the stairs, you hear the sound of metal breaking (which is the stair). A monster jump on you through the stairs. You cant see a shit, because when you get hit, you cant see much, the characters head kinda goes to the sides. Plus you cant see through your blood.

Anyway, I havent got any far in this game, but its pretty good. Its worth a try :] Though you need a good pc to handle the game :/
