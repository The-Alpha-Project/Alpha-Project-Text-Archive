Ever heard of the famous Dungeons & Dragons??? It is not really a "Online" game, more a multipater, so.........

If you ever want to play a RPG with totally freedom paly the game.
I think most people here heard of it or even played it (, lucky bastards). But i will make up the mind for the ones who haven't played it.

First of all:
1) Pick your race (,depending on the version 1,2,3 3.5)
2) Pick your class '' " " " " "
3) Assemble your start equipment
4) PLAY

There are million rules in the game, but there is also an DM (Dungeon Master), he will tell you everything about the game. He is the leader, he is the maker of the gameplay in the game.

If i must score the game: 10, definitely.
Why?

Graphics: No graphs it is a boardgame, you don't need a pc to play it.
Gameplay: Depends on the DM
Sound: Every possible sound, put a nice Celtic cd in the stereo on the background.
Addiction: High, very high, extremely high.

I play D&D; every weekend totally about 14 hours a week!
I play with some other friends.

By the way, the game can't be explained on this short message, it is impossible but it give's you a smaal idea!

I play D&D; based on the 3rd rules.
I have a party of 4 5 players,
Mostly i improvise, but i also prepare a lot.
I currently play a game i written myself.
"The King's Daughter"
It is about the daughter of the king being kidnapped by Sahaugin, some amphybian monstrous humanoids. The King's daughter is really so spoiled that she don't listen to the players, very funny. She don't wanna go back to the kingdom. She want absolutely nothing. The main quest is to bring the daughter back, but it is a really big problem, because they just got her free from a Sahaugin fortress and now have to go outta there.

We play a good campaign,
We are currently lvl 4,
We are playing in the weekends, from fridaynight till sundaymorning.
And, yes we became addicted to the game.
I have the books: Dungeon Master Guide, Monster Manual, 2 Player's Handbooks, a Psionic's Handbook and i buy the Draconium Handbook (, about Dragons).
