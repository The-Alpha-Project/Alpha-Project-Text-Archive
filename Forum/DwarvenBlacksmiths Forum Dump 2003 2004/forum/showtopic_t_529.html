On a beautiful Sundaymorning, a cloaked man with a present wrapped in clothes went in the great citadel of Ironforge. He dropped the present before an Dwarven orphanage. He put a small letter and small package on it and then left, as quickly as he could.

The next morning there was rumors in the big city, what would a Gnome do here, this was very unusual.
But the orphanage accepted the little Gnomester and took care of it as it was an other Dwarven baby. So the young Gnome, his name was Darren Darkwillow {wolfcry} was "accepted".
He grew up with other Dwarves who often became jealous of the very high intellect of the Gnome. So he was bullied a lot.
But young Darren was pure in his heart, went to the church every Sunday and prayed before he went to bed.
But on one day when some older Dwarves bullied him around again, he became insane and summoned forth an Imp. The Dwarves ran away in fear and the Imp disappeared again.
The days after Darren was afraid of crowd's of people, they were looking at him if he was something awful. Darren buyd a house and was studying the art of summoning and controling creatures. It was an long and intense study, but he mastered it piece by piece. When he was at the age of 22 (still young for a Gnome) he went outside Ironforge to follow his "star". He heard the great mages and priests talked about it a lot, a rumor of an ancient tomb, and so our little Darren continued his journey through the snowyland of Khaz Modan. Still finfing out who his real parents are, maybe he found some answers in the wilderness..........

One day when Darren was walking south of Khaz Modan he heard a yelling sound coming from the bushes. Darren, still a kid, went to the bushes and found an Imp, bleeding to dead. Darren immediatly get his bag and put some bandage around the Imp his arm, the bleeding stopped.
"Thanks", says the Imp in a soft voice. Then he magically disappeared.
Darren wiped the sweat of his forehead and was searching further in the wilderness where he was suppose to find an ancient tomb, according to ancient lore.
So he was searching further an luckily he found a tomb. It was an great hole on the foot of an enormous mountain. He grabbed a torch from his bag and went in the cave. It was a long and dark road, but Darren knew no fear, he know he found some answers here. Answers from his past, maybe who his parents are.
This thought was running through the mind of the 23 year old Gnome, brave in his heart and friendly in his soul.
The path still continues, there was seem to come no end to the dark road the lil' Gnome choose.
But after hours walking on the path, Darren suddenly saw light further in the cave, he was running to the light. He found actually a tomb, it was a great place covered in gold and other treasures, but Darren added no value on the gold and treasures. He saw an old desk where an sinister figure was writing something on a piece of paper.
Darren asked the man:"Who are you", his voice echoed through the tomb.
The man stood up, he has the size of Darren.
Then he said with an soft voice:"Master Darren. I am glad i have the honour to meet you finally after all these years. I see you become a great Gnome"
"Who are you", Darren asked again, while taking one step back.
"Who i am, Darren, i was the righthand of the Lead-wizard of Gnomeregan, of the royal Garreth Darkwillow", the man said, when he was pulling his cape of his head. Darren saw a friendly Gnome face.
Darren stood shocked and wanted to say something but nothing came out of his mouth just:" uh......ah........uh.......uh......"
Then the men said:"All your questions will be answered in time, for now call me Gabriel"
"Gabriel, right", said Darren with a shivering voice.
"Now, follow me" said Gabriel when he lighted a torch an pulled a lever.
A big door opened leading to another chamber.
Darren followed the man who moved forth without making a step, he was levitating, Darren noticed.
They came in a chamber where a giant grave was standing right up to the wall.
Gabriel was mumbling a spell and the grave opened.
There was an wannabee-mummy Gnome, nailed with spikes on the wall.
"This is your father, Garreth Darkwillow. Darren was making an ugly face and asked:"Why is he dead, what did he do"?
"Nothing, but when the Trogg arrived, he protected the king with all the magic powers he had within, this was his death."
Then, from the way he disappeared the Imp sat on the shoulder of Gabriel.
"Did he passed the test, Noctor?"
"Yes, he did, he was very friendly and kind, also he was not sedused by all the gold" the Imp said.
"Alright then, Darren, let me tell you something about your past. You were an orphan raised in the tough world of Dwarfs. This was a safe place for you to grow up. I put you there because we had to run, i was teleporting you an me away with the trust of your father to protect you, but i failed in that duty."
"Why," Darren asked.
"You see, master Darren, what you see know is a ghost, the last summonig i created, to give you a path in the right direction, you see. Your mother, is still alive. She lives not far from here, pay here a visit and tell her, i said hello".
Then the men disappeared and he left behind a sheet of paper, it was a world map.
With new hope and a open path our young Gnome went out in search of his mother. One day he will find her.
Then with new hope, Darren continued his journey to find his mother.

The picture before Darren faded in darkness. He woke up between all the races and everybody was asleep except for the mysterious Dwarf who was washing her clothes in the creek. Darren walked to her and said:
"You are the Wise Lady! Why are you playing games with me?"
The Wise Lady aswered while facing Darren:
"Visit Mt. Xeron, there you'll find you're answers. But I warn you, come alone!" then she disappeared in the water.
Darren looked around and saw a towering mountain. He was hoping that it was a small one, but this one was complete with snowy peaks, slippery rocks and very deep craters where magma was still emerged from.
So Darren begun his climb at mount Xeron. He was only at the half when it started too snow heavily. You couldn't see a thing. Darren strongwilled too reached the peak continued fighting agains the fury of the wind and the chillness of the snow.
He was exhausted when he reached the top. Once arrived he catched his breath and took a look around. He saw a small shack in the middle of the mountain's plateau. He entered and saw the Dwarven Lady sitting infront of the house.
"Be careful for entering this place, since it is cursed. Don't worry, enter this place, since it is holy."
Darren was a bit of surprised but was willing too found out who he was, so he entered the shack.
Once inside he saw only Telvin standing there in the middle of the room.
"So, young one. Are you ready too reveal your past?"
Darren aswered:"I am, just stop playing games!"
"I only want too play one game with you, young Gnome!" Telvin said while the dark side of his body covered him whole.
"And that is?" Darren replied.
"You dead!" Telvin shouted while charging on to battleready Darren.
Darren quickly summoned an Infernal too his side and pulled out his staff.
Telvin hit the Infernal hard, so hard, he dropped on the ground and broke into pieces.
Darren took a swing at Telvin which made him smack against the wall.
Telvin retaliated by sending Darren flying with a force released from his hand.
Darren lied on the ground, just managed too breath when Telvin sit on him and tried too strangle him with Darrens staff.
Darren mamaged too hold on for a few seconds but then loosing strength. It almost went black before his eyes when he saw some flashes. At first he saw Telvin and then he saw that the light side of him covered him whole, revealing the Wise Lady. Darren was speachless and asked:
What am I doing here?
There is not much time! Dont bow before the Darkness, keep your light burning strong! she said when Darren got carried back to batlle.
Darren closed his eyes and concentrated on what the Wise Lady said.
Telvin was smacked away and Darren opened his eyes, it was if he was like reborn. White light came shining out of his eyes and a strange voiced spoke too Telvin:
You, embodiement of two people, split up!
Telvin was torn apart and at both side a new one regained. At one half the Wise Lady appeared and the other one was a Shadowy creature.
Who are you? Darren asked.
The Shadow escaped and fled too the deepest of Mount Xeron.
Let him be, the Wise lady said while trying too stand on both legs.
He will plung himself deeper and deeper into darkness till even the darkes of all gets consumed by foul hatred of himself.

Together they went out and returned too the foot of the mountain. Camino was waiting there and summoned the almighty Earth-Mother too his aid. Clouds of thunder covered the sky and with enormous impact Camino pounded on the ground, like he never did before. The ground was trembling and shaking. The whole mountain erupted and crumbled down.
Then the storm subsidies and a rainbow appeared.
Thank the powers of the Earthmother my friend! Camino siad while taking up his little friend on his shoulders and they began walking towards the village of Beltharusill.
Once arrived they saw that the statue had some cranks in it, and was nearly falling apart.
Camino said towards Darren: Well, this is the end! Flee now before the Orcs, Trolls, Tauren and Undead will reunite and try too slay you all. My magic totem did not have enough power too withstand the tremor that appeared. It was nice knowing you, my friend. Farewell!
Darren, confused by the words of this Tauren move on toward a save distance of the village.
He saw that a crank appeared in the earth and that the whole village got swallowed in it. Including Camino.
Darren mourned.
Why cry, young one? You fullfilled your task and the world regained too its former glory now! Be happy!
How can i be happy when one of my best friends die. Sacrifycing himself too protect the others.
Dont cry! I am sure Camino finds a way too restore the statue and let the world taste of peace again!
