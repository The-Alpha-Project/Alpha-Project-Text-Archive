Sup all i just wrote this story off the top of my head. I dont think its all that do but i hope it kind of makes sense. Enjoy and excuse whatever gramtical errors u may find.

This story is copy made, written, and thought of by me Dkalis all words are my own for the most part..and anyone caught making fun of it will be proscuted

Ah been a long day but this food is good and maybe it will be me find my next coarse of action.

Starts to daydream while eating when i was young i was adopted my a orc by the name of Grall and a Nightelve by the name of Nesity. A unique couple my mother ( Nesity) took pity on Grall when she saw him being attacked my a group of soldiers.She rescued him, they became friends, lovers, and married defying all cultral rules and decided to move away into the Redridge Mountains to get away from the chastising they were getting from society. Everything was good for a while they adopted me at the age of 3 buillt a small house in the mountains and were happy until Grall started getting abusive with my mom.
" I see flashes on Grall hitting Nesity."

Finally mom not being able to take it anymore took me in the night and ran away.

" remebers the fear in her eyes and she rushes me to gather my things.....remebers Grall waking up and seeing us trying to leave...remebers my mom mumbling something under her breath and throwing a small ball of fire at Grall...grall screaming....running in the night soo tired....mom pushing me to run faster..Grall...still...alive." Bah im a grown man think of the past isnt doing me any good. Continues to eat food and i slowly start to daydream again

After that night We moved into Elwynn forest and my mom started teaching me how to make cloths and how to "focus my mind into finding my inner strenth". My mom even thou she was a nightelve and technically counldnt do magic was able to with great concentration do small amounts like the small fireball she threw at Grall. She started teaching me what little she knew in hopes that where she has failed to master her unique art I would prosper..I did learning how to control fire, It came easy to me..somethings too easy..I would find myself it controling me instead of me control it and somethings it lead me to do things like burning a tree or burning a deer. Anyways I grew of age where I decide to travel. The day I decided to leave me mom gave me a beutiful red/white robe and a pure white staff along with bread cheese and some salted pork..she kissed me goodbye and sayed something in Darnassian and I was off.

I did a lot of things in my travels..protected travelars who were being terrozied my a group of bandits...fought agaist the Tauren, broke up a undead warlock group, fought in small battles, spent some time with a few red dragons ( my time with them were amazily educational and scary experiance) etc...My time with the dragons is what led me to stormwind. The dragons wanted to know of any word of Deathwing They had a feeling that the child king Anduin Wrynn was being controled by a Highlord Bolvar Fordragon and mage named Lady Katrana Prestor who had some type of weird connections with Deathwing. They sent me to find out all of what i could. My journey thou took a interesting twist to a soldier by the name of Marshal Windsor to make a long story short the i was lead into a maze of deciet lies and evil magic that toped even mine knowledge.. and the mage with the child kind is no other than Onyxia kin to Deathwing. Marshal Windsor died by Onyxia but, before he passed away he gave me a amultet which was to help me defeat Onyxia. I needed reinforecements to help defeat Onyxia so I traveled to the undercity to find a old friend. On my way to the undercity I met a Night Elve hunter and a dwarf warrior who pledged to stick with me. Once we got to the undercity we where caught in a trap pledging to come back for them I quickly made a portal and found myself near this town. Tired, distraught, hungry I've now found my way to this inn called DBS Tavern. In my short stay here Ive met a gnome rogue a hunter and witness the the bartender being knocked out. Tomorrow I must contact the towns mage leaders and convice them to help me kill Onyxia.
