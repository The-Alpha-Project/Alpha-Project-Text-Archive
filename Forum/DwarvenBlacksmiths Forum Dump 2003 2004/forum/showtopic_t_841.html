Well if ya want to know me background, sure then ill tell ya!

I grew up wit’ me dad, old Gurni Steelbender, never knew me mom she died right after me birth.
Bu’ me dad is known all over Khaz Modan as the crazy bu’ bloody good engineer.
Well one day when I was playin’ around with Granaut Opust, me dad called me. “Son hurry come” and I ran over to ‘im. ‘e told me ‘e had a surprise for me. And out of me dads ‘ouse an elf came out with a baby on ‘is arm. “Say ‘ello to ya new lil’ brother” he said, “’is name is Magnus” then the elf walked away.

Well as Magnus grew and became older he stated to play with me an’ Granaut. Though ‘e wasn’t me real lil’ bro I loved him as if he was, we always had fun. Bu’ slowly we started to go each our ways. ‘e loved to play with gunpowder and he blew the roof of our ‘ouse a few time. Bu’ as old dad use to say, “Shit happens”.
Me self, I started to open me eyes for tha light. An’ I started to “hang out” in tha church. I know it isn’t normal, bu I liked company of priest. Old Gurni asked me w’y I didn’t become a priest when I loved to be in the church. I said that I didn’t like to read an’ stuff like that. So he said then why don’ ya just spread the words of light as a Paladin. Then I was caught. I knew what to do wit’ me life.

Well before I left me home and said goodbye to me old dad an’ to Magnus. To honor me dad I took the surname Gurnisson, the same did Magnus…

Before I left we ‘eld a big party for me, an’ me an me bro made a blood oath that we would help each other in times of need. LONG LIVE THE GURNISSONS!
Me dad was sad bu’ ‘e agreed that it was the best….

On one of me journeys in tha cursed lands of Lordaeron to spread tha words of light, I heard som’ skreamin’. I ran towards the sound, a big spider like creature! I smas’ed it an’ be’ind it I saw me old matey, Granaut Opust!!! Toget’er we moved up north not only to spread tha word of light, also because Ive found out that I couldn’t turn undead to the light, so therefore t’ey must be slain! So we started our little adventure in the cursed lands of Lordaeron!

After I settled down in Khaz Modan and joined this greate guild in ‘ope to spread tha words of light to sinners in Karz Modan an’ maybe if I one day becom’ a great Paladin, maybe me words will reach tha rest of tha world!


Last I wanna honor me dad again:

T’ank ya dad, if it wasn’t for ya I wouldn’t be what I am today! T’ank ya! Good luck to ya in tha future with ya gun making! And I just heard som’ rumors t’at ya is still tha best engineer in tha world :D!
