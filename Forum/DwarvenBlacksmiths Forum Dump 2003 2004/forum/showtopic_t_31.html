The Dwarves


  • Magni Brozebeard

    Not image yet.
    Class : The king of the dwarven kingdom
    Affiliation: Allianse

    Alive in WoW? : Yes, in Ironforge of the dwarven kingdom.

    Magni Bronzebeard was the revered king of the dwarven nation Khaz Modan. His throne sat in the Citadel of Ironforge and he led his people against the Orcs as they drove the dwarves from their ancestral homeland. Magni ruled while his younger brother, Muradin, sought to gain aid for the reclamation of his homeland and acted as ambassador to the dwarves. With the discovery of fragments of the origins of dwarves, Magni’s people are scattered searching for insights into their ancestral past. Magni’s place in the establishment of exploration outposts all over the world is unknown.


  • Muradin Bronzebeard



    Class: King of the Hill
    Affiliation: The Alliance

    Alive in WoW? : No - He killed of a ice shard. A Scourge Undead's CAUSE!!!! :evil:

    Muradin was the brother of the king Magni Bronzebeard. During the dark hours of the second war, Muradin served as a courier between Lordaeron and the dwarven kingdom of Khaz Modan. When he was in Lordaeron, Muradin became Arthas's friend and taught him to fight with many types of blades.

    Muradin was one of the first dwarves to enter the Explorators Guild. This group was interested in archeology and the exploration of unknown world. Even if Muradin came back to Khaz Modan and Lordaeron only a few times, he supported the dwarven and human alliance. In 617, during a mission in Northrend, he helped Arthas in his quest by revealing him the existance of Frostmourne. Ironically, he's been the first victim of the fallen prince.

  • Kurdran



    Class: Griffon Rider
    Affiliation: The Alliance

    Alive in WoW? Unsure, i hope so....But he could be killed.

    The proud dwarf Kurdran was one of the most famous hero of the Second war. He killed by himself nince dragons and since then, Kurdran and his griffon were the uncontested masters of the sky. During his mission in Draenor, he faced the same destiny as Khadgar, Turalyon, Alleria and Danath: death.

  • Brann Bronzebeard



    Class : Explorers Guild
    Affiliation : The Alliance

    Alive in WoW? : Noted Dwarven historian and veteran of the Second War Brann Bronzebeard has gone missing!

    Having undertaken the noble charge of his Explorers Guild to survey the uncharted lands of Azeroth, the celebrated adventurer made his way through the regions of Elwynn Forest and Westfall before filing his last report from the misty jungles of Stranglethorn Vale. According to his most recent correspondence, Brann had just uncovered the ruins of an ancient city hidden deep within Stranglethorn when he was beset upon by a band of savage Jungle Troll headhunters. Presumably, Brann evaded capture, but the only real clue pertaining to his whereabouts comes in the form of a vague final sentence: "I resolved to head east…"

  • Aggronor

    Occupation: Mountain King
    Alignment: Survivors of Lordaeron

    Aggronor the Mighty was a Thane of Khaz Modan who survived the demonic invasion of Lordaeron, and sailed west with Jaina. With Greymane either dead or missing, Aggronor was chosen to command what remained of the Gilneas Brigade. Jaina, sensing a strange power coming from Stonetalon Peak, ordered them to build a settlement at its base.

    Aggronor and the four other commanders built settlements there, and soon, to their surprise, they came under the attack of Grom Hellscream and the Warsong Clan. They went to retaliate, but attacked Thrall's base by mistake. To keep them at bay, Thrall was forced to kill Aggronor and destroy his base.


  • Baelgun

    Occupation: Prospector
    Alignment: Explorer's Guild of Ironforge

    Second in command of the expedition into Northrend, Baelgun served under the leadership of Muradin Bronzebeard. When Bronzebeard was killed, Baelgun took command of the remaining dwarves and led them deeper into the heart of the frozen land, straight into Icecrown. There, he began to dig deep into the ruins of Azjol-Nerub, despite the many hostile creatures who attacked his party, and he inadvertently freed a race of powerful, mysterious creatures who had been trapped beneath the earth - the Faceless Ones.

    Driven out of the lower tunnels by their malicious attacks, Baelgun and his people sealed the entrance to the Inner Kingdom, guarding it to keep the Faceless Ones within. When Arthas entered, Baelgun warned him that there was an ancient evil within the Inner Kingdom, but Arthas paid him little heed, and slaughtered Baelgun and his forces.


  • Falstad Dragonreaver

    Occupation: Gryphon Rider
    Alignment: Khaz Modan

    Noble Gryphon Rider of Northeron, Falstad Dragonreaver commanded a small flight of Gryphon Riders after the Second War who patrolled the shores of Lordaeron for any draconic activity. They protected the town of Hasic from certain doom, and then saw Rhonin and his companions across the Great Sea to Khaz Modan. But they were ambushed and his kinsmen were killed, but at the incentive of Vereesa Windrunner, Falstad continued to Khaz Modan to assist in looking for Rhonin.

    With the help of hill dwarves, Falstad reached Grim Batol and fought in the final battle to free the Dragonqueen.

  • Bandis Forgefire

    Occupation: Mountain King
    Alignment: Survivors of Lordaeron

    A Thane of Khaz Modan, Bandis Foregefire was a skilled warrior and smith, who served his people well throughout their times of trouble and turmoil.

    When Jaina sent word throughout the land that there was no hope of overcoming the enemy that would soon be at hand, Bandis knew enough to heed her warning, and took a small band of followers and left with her to Kalimdor.

    Leading a human expedition, Jaina gave Bandis the charge of defending Stonetalon Peak from the orcish invading force. Bandis defended the Peak bravely, but was no match for the persistent forces of Thrall, and was killed.

  • Gimmel

    Occupation: Warrior
    Alignment: Rom's Band

    Gimmel was a hill dwarf who managed to survive the Second War, and then joined Rom's band of roaming warriors. He helped to save Vereesa and Falstad from a trio of trolls.

  • Murgen Hammerfall

    Occupation: Mountain King
    Alignment: Khaz Modan

    Murgen Hammerfall was a celebrated Mountain King, whose name has become legend amongst his fellow Thanes of Khaz Modan. One of his more famous exploits was during a battle in the Alterac highlands, during which he used the first ever "Thunderclap" technique to ward off an army of invading gnolls.

  • Molok

    Occupation: Gryphon Rider
    Alignment: Khaz Modan

    Molok was an aerie dwarf in Falstad Dragonreaver's contingent of gryphon riders. Prejudiced, as were most, against wizards, he quickly got into an argument with Rhonin after they met in Hasic, and the wizard suggested that the gryphon riders fly them across the sea.

    Molok helped to transport Rhonin and his party from Hasic to Khaz Modan after their argument was resolved.

  • Narn

    Occupation: Warrior
    Alignment: Rom's Band

    Narn was a hill dwarf who managed to survive the Second War, and then joined Rom's band of roaming warriors. He helped to save Vereesa and Falstad from a trio of trolls, and was badly wounded during the fight, and couldn't travel with them any longer.

  • Rom

    Occupation: Warrior
    Alignment: Rom's Band

    Rom was the leader of a band of hill dwarves in Khaz Modan who rescued Vereesa and Falstad from a trio of Trolls after the Goblin, Kryll, had led them into a trap. Rom eventually turned out to be in the service of Krasus, the Red Dragon and lover of Alexstrasza. Rom helped to lead them to Grim Batol, and then retreated into the underground with his fellows.

  • Feranor Steeltoe

    Occupation: Blacksmith
    Alignment: Dragon Hunters

    Feranor Steeltoe was a dwarven smith of some renown, but his pride and joy was the thrill of the hunt. He led a band of Dragon Hunters, who roamed the wilderness south of Strahnbrad in search of their elusive prey, a black drake called Searinox. The heart of Searinox could be used to enchant the weapons of anyone who held it.

    Upon finding the evil creature, they were kept at bay by Searinox's vile offspring, and the undead forces he summoned to do his bidding. Feranor was forced into a retreat.

    Feranor was soon approached by Prince Arthas, who was in the area looking for an orcish encampment. Feranor lent Arthas his followers, and Arthas led a successful attack on Searinox's lair. The dragon was killed, and Feranor fashioned an orb of flame from his heart.

  • Sky'ree

    Race: Gryphon
    Occupation: Mount
    Alignment: Lordaeron Alliance

    Sky’ree is the gryphon mount of Kurdran, the legendary hero of the Second War and afterward. With his master, Sky’ree went beyond the Dark Portal, and acted as a scout during the excursion into Draenor. After the battle, though, Sky’ree, along with Kurdran, were lost within the Twisting Nether.
