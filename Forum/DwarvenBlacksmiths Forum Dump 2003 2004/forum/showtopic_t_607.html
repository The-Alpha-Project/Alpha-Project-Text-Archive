Swift and Deadly he is, Hadir was a cunning Night Elf Hunter. He had mastered the ways of nature and balance in his long life. For his history is only remembered by the dead, and himself. Bright was his past, but who knows about his dark and mysterious future. Here is my story:

Hadir was born with a legacy. A legacy bound towards many generations of Night Elf Hunters. He was born five thousand years ago, when the Night Elves created Nordrassil, the first world tree. Like all Hunters, he patroled and protected Ashenvale with the Night Elves in peace. Like his forefathers, he had great passion towards nature. Thus, he became mindful with mastering the ways of Nature. A skilled hunter he was, knowing every forest from acorn to the top of an ancient tree. Yet, his forefather's were among those who entered the emerald dream with their apsence. Until recently, most of his kin and the Night Elves perished in the battle of Mount Hyjal. With sorrow and sadness, Hadir seeked to rebuild his ancient homeland, and find the rest of his kin. But it could not be done. With the remaining Night Elves, he traveled to the island of Teldrassil, estaplishing a new home. For in his sleep he had always been haunted by his long lost kin. Months had passed,and Hadir had assasinated any of the Horde that set foot in his new homeland. Thus many leaders of the Horde put bounties on his head. For many years, Hadir stayed hidden in his new homeland. Hadir had also studied the Alliance in those long years, which became intresting to him. He became facinated by the dwarves, thinking of them as proud and hearty warriors. Until then, news reached Teldrassil. The Undead had sided with the bloodthisty Horde. Many Night Elves were grieved of this news, for Hadir was prepared for anything. He had heard about the Dwarven Blacksmith, an elite Alliance guild. He was given the weapons of his forefathers from the remaining of his kin with honor. A mystical bow, with a quiver of swift arrows, a deadly longsword of the forest, and an emerald knife, all passed down to Hadir. For many weeks, Hadir traveled alone towards the guild, defeating any foe that stood within his way. It was a journey full of danger, though many were hunting him. As well as it was a journey towards hope. He traveled towards Khaz Modan, and it was not until then he reached the slopes of the white mountains, he was filled with a sudden hope and freedom..................................................... TO BE CONTINUED

He does not only seek to eliminate the Horde, but to find trust, friendship, and teamwork among the fierce Dwarves. For he was known as the Elvenstar Hunter, and thus he still is.
