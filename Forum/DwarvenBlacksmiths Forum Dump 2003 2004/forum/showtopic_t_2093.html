Well, it all started a long long time ago, when man named Randyfag Swiftfinger was looting the treasurehold of Lord Cuntworthy's seaside castle. 'Twas a difficult task indeed, seeing as the treasurehold was located in tallest tower of the castle.

Randyfag was not only known as a master thief, but also as the biggest horndog in all of fair Azeroth. After he had filled his sack with trinkets from the dungeon, he smacked a rope-arrow in the edge of the window, and then started rapelling down one side of the tower. He has decided to take a different side down, seeing as the guard's route would have changed by now. All seemed as though he was on easy street, untill... He got a glimpse of something through a window in the towerside...

There she was...
Lying... Dreaming. Swept in her silky sheets. She had full red lips, lucious breasts and an ass that would make J-Lo envious.
As you've probably gathered by now, Randyfag couldn't resist a damsel, and surely not one who was donning only silky sheets.

He silently jumped through the window. and sneaked his way to her bed. She was still fast asleep. Randyfag removed the silky sheets from her naked body, put his hand upon his pants. And whipped out his blackjack and smacked her in the head!!

"sweeet, she's knocked out cold now, time for me to do my business"
He thought to himself, as he mounted the damsel and took advantage of her naked, unconcious body.

After a quicky.. Randyfag continued his flight down the tower and headed for the woods. With a smug smile on his face, naturally.

9 months later, I was shat out of my royal mother like a bad christmas dinner.

"Such a handsom child." All the castle maids said.

"Not too mention he's hung like a horse!!!" Chuckled all the castle guards.

Lord Cuntworthy, however. Was outraged by his grandson. "A child born outside of marriage?!" he complained "Such blasphemy won't be tolerated at my castle." he continued, while the spit flew from his mouth faster and more violently than when a youngster blows his load the first time he's with a girl.

He instantly ordered his guards to take the child, and chuck it in the woods.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A few years later, a young boy was happily frollicing around in the woods. after being left to die in the woods by the royal guards. He was found by a pack of squirrels and raised the only way a squirrel knows of, as a Mighty Thief.
Suddenly, the boy tripped over something which stuck out from beneath a bush. He moved away some of the branches and founda man lying there, passed out, most likely from the content of the bottle which lay beside him. He threw some acorns at the bum. Who woke up and started grumbling. When his eyes caught ahold of the boy. He quickly stod up and yelled..

"Sweet Arthas on a pogostick!! It's you!!"

The boy said: "Insert the sound a squirrel makes"

The drunken man instantly knew that the kid infront of him was none other than his son. He named him Tanelorn, which is drunktalk for "He who is hung like a horse, but got raised by a squirrel"
The squirrels were mighty angry at the drunk, for stealing Tanelorn away from them. So they challenged him to a duel at high noon, Twas a duel in their ancient squirrel way.

A no-holds barred steel cage match to the death!!

Within a matter of minutes, the match was settled. Randyfag passed out drunk and fell of the top of the steelcage, only to land upon the little family of squirrels, squishing them all to a bloody pulp. Randyfag himself also died, seeing as he hit a few big branches on his way down.
Tanelorn was left alone once again, so he travelled around the kingdomof Azeroth, stealing from people to makes ends meet. Many years went by and Tanelorn grew taller and taller. That he did not understand, seeing as the people around him stayed short.

Tanelorn quickly learned that these small people weren't the best people to steal from, seeing as they were prone to hold a grudge.

Tanelorn recently came to the city where the famous Dwarven Blacksmith guild was. And he joined it to make some extra cash. Seeing as he knew it could be very lucrative to work with a small group of people like himself (Plus he needed a place to lay low if the Templars ever caught him stealing... And the Tavern would be a perfect place for that). The people there gave him a warm welcome. And he quickly got the feeling he'd be staying there for quite a while..
