The story begins in Ashenvale Forest.
Our young hero tiger_hunter has lived there for many years alone since his parents where killed in the time the burning legion attackt.
Our hero grew up alone in the forest he learnd how to survive from the things the forest could offer food/water/sleeping.
At one day our hero was attackt by a group of wild furbolgs it didn’t look good it lookt like this was his last our!! but then out of nowhere this gold tiger appeard and saved our young hero.
The gold tiger left a little young orange tiger for our hero since that day they are bound together.
Thats how he got the name Tiger_hunter.

The rumor of an elf and a tiger living together like a family reachd Teldrassil pretty soon.
Many elves where wondering if this could be really true so they where going on an expedition to see if this rumor was true.
But this expedition was full of many dangers and it didn’t take long untill they where attackt by a great group of harpies.
They stood no chance against such a big group they screamd for help and lucky for them our hero and his pet heard there call.
Tiger_hunter climbed on his tiger and off they where.
They came on time to rescue the group of elves and since that day everybody in Teldrassil knows him as Tiger_hunter and they wanted that our hero came and live with them in the big city.
But he wasn’t used to a big city and a lot of people so he stayed in the forest of Ashenvale.

At one day there came some kind of big green monsters with large axes (later know as orcs) and some large bulls (later know as taurens) our hero had never seen this creatures but he felt that this wasn’t wright.
So our brave hero stormed at these creatures but he didn’t stood a chance even with his tiger by his side.
At that point some brave dwarves/humans/elves and gnomes from the clan dwarvenblacksmith walked in the forest of ashenvale they saw what happend and decided to help the young elf.
Under the command of their leader Durin Blackhood they fought with great honor and strenght.
They had saved our hero and his pet from the orcs and taurens.
From that day on the elf tiger_hunter and his tiger pet decided to stay with this clan forever.
