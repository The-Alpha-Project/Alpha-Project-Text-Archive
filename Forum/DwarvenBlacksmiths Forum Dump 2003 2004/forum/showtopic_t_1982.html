Greetings. My guild, Shadows Edge, has been in contact with the Arathor Alliance for several months (on and off), discussing our joining the Alliance. We had a small chat about this on the Arathor forums back in April, and there have been some discussions between our guild leader Ignis, and Durin going back to March.

We at SE have now decided to go ahead with the membership process, and seek to join Arathor. Our current Arathor proposal thread is here.

We hope to get to know you all a bit now, outside the game, and to get to know you better when the open beta starts.

To get to know us better, feel free to check out our fan site and our guild site.

Our guild leaders, Ignis and Sintel, are busy making various tools and maps for WoW. Since we are not in the WoW beta, they have tested the tools on DAoC. You can see some of their work here.

We hope for good fellowship and good gaming in the future.

Cheers,
