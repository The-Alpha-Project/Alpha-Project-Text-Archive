Story of Durin Blackhood :

Born : Ironforge, captial city of the Dwarven kingdom.
Present alder : 185

Story : Duirn's father, Brid Blackhood, he followed the famous Griffon group of Kurdran, Griffon Rider leader. Brid's familiy moved to a small dwarven villiage after the first orcish armies partial controlled the Dwarven Kingdom. Brid was killed in Secord War against the Orcs. Durin's mother and a sister murdered of the Frostmane Trolls after they attacked the small village in north of the Dwarven kingdom. So I am now the last family-name, but i am family with the dwarven population. Kurdran took me as a apprentice, since Kurdran know Bird Blackhood was a brave Griffon rider and saved his life one time. Thus Kurdran could't forget the rewarding and will learn myself to become a best hunter.

He learned of Kurdran to be a good hunter, the well-learned Griffion Rider and wish to make better that Kurdran. Serve of King Magni Bronzebeard's military groups. My king accept me to build a important guild after Kurdran recommanded - The Dwarven Blacksmith. To make value items to our allies.

During Kurdran's mission in Draenor, he faced the same destiny as Khadgar, Turalyon, Alleria and Danath: death.

Possible never to see him again.

One of my old histories :

"BROKEN RITUAL"
-----------------------

" GYIIIIAAAAAAAAAA "

A dwarf hunter think :

Hunter : "Never have I heard such anguish in a cry" - "It came from that town. I'm tempted to steer clear of it but i haven't eaten since this morning..."

"It looks deserted -- But there's almost ALWAYS some activity in a town this size...."

"A dog....Well, at least there's some sort of life here!..." Sudden the dog "vov...?" and run away...

"It seemed absolutely terrified of me.....There's definitely some mystery here!.."

" Hmm...there's some light between the boards of this window "

I said : " Hello there! Anybody home? I need some -- "

*Sudden light went out!*

"Streange... People in these outlying towns are usually so hospitable..."

"There's another light -- look like an INN"

*He open the door "CREEEAAAK!"*

Peoples in the INN : *GASP!* - *GASP!* - *OOK!*......

Hunter : " What's the matter? - You all look like you've seen a GHOST! "

Hunter : "Innkeeper -- bring me some drink to wash the dust down my throat.....and something to eat -- Rice and beef will be fine...... and one or two easy supper and.......Is something wrong???!!"

Innkeeper said : "Er....No, Hunter, I'll get your meal, so you can continue on your way..."

Hunter : "Oh..By the way, what was that WAILING i heard a little while ago?"

All peoples *Shock!!!*

A bookstone man said : "That was the ghost of Captain Stormwood, a vassel to late Lord Stoneim"

Hunter : " Stormwood? I served with him! - Why is his spirit in such anguish?"

A bookstone man said : I am Thor. I own the corner bookstore - I'll tell you the tragedy in the morning..."

Hunter : "Why not now?"

Thor : "It's not a story to be heard in the dark..."

*Hours later...*

Thor : "Here's my shop. We'll talk in the back..."

Thor : " Ever since the haunting begin, the people have been leaving. Soon this town, itself will be only a GHOST TOWN. I tell you the story because i hope you can help us."

Hunter : " I will try. Now please continue "...

Thor : " Much of the story you already know. A orcish, were the victor at the battle of Dun Algaz (Northern of the Dwarven Kingdom), forcing Lord Stoneim's warriors to scatter throughout the countryside --

Captain Stormwood, with a young lieutenant, made it to this village, pursued closely by the enemy. A bandage covered half his face. I heard later an arrow had pierced into his eye --

I remember that night well. The moon was full shadowy daylight. First, they demanded our food and drink as was their right and we gave them what little we had --

Then they tried to organize us against the orcish army - But we are just peasants -- Merchants and artisans -- not warriors. What had armies to do with us? One Lord would treat us just as badly as another! --

So we all hid in our homes --

Captain Stormwood called us cowards, traitors and WORSE but er could not be moved.... --

His lieutenant wanted to slay us all -- Punishment for turning our backs to them -- But the Captain stopped him --

The lieutenant fell to his knees. It must be difficult to be so young and know you may soon be dead --

Suddenly, a ram came through town..." Lord Stoneim is dead! Lord Stoneim is dead! He cried " The orcish army holds his fortness! "

He rode out of as suddenly as he arrived a phantom of the dust...

Thor youth aged : I was close snough to see the Captain's face then. it made the sweat run cold down my spine. He removed his helmet slowly, reverently -- as if he had all the time in the world -- As if the enemy were not barking at his heels. Continuing those same reverent motions, he undid his sword and dirk. Then he untied his belt, his waist cord and shoulder pads. He took off his armor...followed by his under-armor padding and sleeves. Then he sat on the dirt. He closed his eyes for a minute.....Then placed his dirk down in front of him.....I saw the lieutenant swallow then slowly nod in understanding.

" SUICIDE! " I had heard of the ritual but never thought i would withess it. Rather than face the shame of capture he would follow Lord Stoneim into death.

He would show us all how a warrior dies

I stood transfixed behind the safeety of my walls

"First, the cut across the stomach, left to right" -- "Then the cut upward...." I saw the lieutenant -- Saw the fear in his eyes. His sweating hands tightened their grip in anticipation of delivering the death stroke -- of severing his leader's head- The Captain nodded -

- THEN ALL HELL BROKE LOOSE !!!

The orcish army had found them. The lieutenant ran -- but not far......

"Incredibly, Captain Stormwood, in his pain again snatched up his dirk and attempted to make the final cut but the blade was wrenched from his hands"

"They were foolish orcs warriors and when they saw the famous Captain helpless before them, they laughed..

Lots were drawn to see which of them would have the honor of killing their prize and thus insure the rewards and reputation for himself and his descendants.

The Captain was a fearsome warrior and even though he was incapacitated i could see the fear in his executioner's eyes. The man was dying but the legend was still all too real.

"GYIIIIAAAAAAAAAA "

Suddenly, he gave a single anguish-filled scream which filled everyone who heard it with a blood chilling dread.....

--- Then the blade desended. To this day, the blood lies puddled in the street, reminding the entire town of our cowardice. Nothing we do will remoce the stain -- or our shame. And now, on the nights when there is a full moon, Captain Stormwood's ghost appears and re-enacts his death...and we all hide behind walls as we did so long ago.... When morning comes, there is no evidence of the Captain....except the blood. There is ALWAYS the blood.

Now you, know our story, Hunter. Our fear had cursed this town.

Hunter : Has no one ever tried to CONFRONT the specter?

Thor : Once. A mercenary, with drink to bolster his courage, went out on a dare.

The mercenary : "Ahhh...That's GOOD drink...*Urp!*.... Stupid fleas -- biting alla time!....Now, where's that ghost....? Ignorant townpeople -- probably making the whole thing -- *** ULP!!!***

Thor : When the Captain appeared, the mercenary ran away.

Hunter : Hmmm....It's not your village that is cursed, but the Captain's spirit that is in auguish..

Thor : What do you mean?...Have you solved the mystery of why the Captain returns?

Hunter : I'm not sure...But Captain Stormwood was a staunch comrade. I must investigate this. Tonight is the secord night of the full moon. I'll need your help to put the Captain's spirit to rest...

Thor : *GULP!* A-all right, Hunter.

*The secord full moon come....*

Thor : Hunter, night is coming on...I don't know how you could SLEEP knowing you'll soon be facing a GHOST!

Hunter : *Yawn* (he waked) - I've learned to get my rest whenever i can..... Comes with being a wanderer, i guess. Now, what's for dinner?

*Later*

Hunter : I'll need a bucket of water and a ladle...

Thor : But water will not wash the stain away...

Hunter : It's not for the street....

Thor : Oh...? I'll get what you need right away....

Thor saw him : He's just standing by that blood stain....He's washing his sword.....now i see something....the blood's disappearing!....."

Hunter said to the Ghost :

"Captain Stormwood, I am Durin Blackhood. My family has been retainers to the Stoneim dwarven clan for generations. I, myself, served Lord Stoneim's personal friend during the northern. It would be my honor to act as your secord.....

The first cut......

the secord cut....

the head dips......

I did as his last demand.....

Thor : Hunter!!!! The Captain's GONE!!!! -- And there was no scream.....

Durin Blackhood : And there never will be again......

Thor : The Haunting is over? HOW???

Durin Blackhood : No matter how great a warrior's renowin, an unworthy death is a lasting shame. To be slain by some nameless subordinate with no family history brought dishonor of the Stormwood name. I completed the ritual as it should have been....
