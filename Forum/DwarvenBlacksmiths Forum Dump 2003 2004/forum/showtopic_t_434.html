Be respect of the forum.....


  • Language
    There is no language filter. Please try not to over-use this, or one will be added. It's ok to curse, as long as it's for a reason, or atleast funny. We are all (or should be) mature, here.

  • Spam
    This is not such a big issue anymore, a lot of the spam has died down (good job, guys). Please try not to spam; remember: it's the QUALITY of the posts you make, not the quantity.

  • Personal Attacks
    When you roleplay an attack (i.e your character is racist against alliance, the undead, etc.) please make sure not to make it personal. I know these are forum rules -- but a member shouldn't make a personal attack ANYWHERE to ANYONE, especially another member.

  • Extremes
    Racism, Sexism, Real war's cause and any other form of REAL LIFE descrimination is simply prohibited. You get one warning, then your account is banned from the forums. Simple: DONT DO IT.

  • No double posts
    On same day (Ok if a new post after 3-4 days)......

  • Moderation
    There are forums with moderation, and forums without. The non-moderated forums are there because we trust you can use your own judgement on what is good or bad to put in there. Please don't prove us wrong -- I love the freedom we have here. :twisted:
