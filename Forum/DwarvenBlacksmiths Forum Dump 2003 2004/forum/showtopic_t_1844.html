The Bio of EugeneKuzmin


I was born on an unnamed island. My father, Igrigon Kuzmin, was a Dwarven paladin. He was exploring the island south-west of Azeroth, since back then not much was known about the world by the alliance. While my father was traveling to it, the 3 ships that were traveling with their boat had seen a storm, and tried to escape. The captain on my father’s ship decided to keep going. Then the storm hit. All 3 of the ships that turned back were struck by lightning, and all aboard them died. My father’s girlfriend was on his ship, and when he saw all those people die, my mum (his girl friend), Maya, was very worried, and she thought they were all going to die. Well, to calm her, my father laid her, and then she was pregnant. When the ship got to the island, everyone got off, and then that ship too was hit by lightning. It took the dwarves to build a ship to carry them all 2 years. During that time I was born. On the 398th year, I was born, and my mother died a month later. When we got back to Khaz Modan, I was at first being trained as a paladin. Then, when the second war started, me and my father both took up our hammers, and were some of the first to volunteer to defeat the orcs. During a small skirmish between 400 trolls and 90 dwarves, there were enough trolls for all of us. In 20 minutes, me and my dad had killed 26 trolls, but then my dad was surrounded by 20 of them, since they recognized him as the best fighter in the group. He fought bravely, but in the end one of the trolls through an axe in his back. Right there I yelled a battle cry, and charged into the group of the trolls who killed my dad. I destroyed all of them, and then charged into the middle of the remaining ones. Once all of the trolls were dead, 20 of the dwarves lay dead, but it was still a victory. I ran to my father’s body. He gave me a ring in the shape of a hammer, and a strange rune on it, and told me to use it once the time was right. No one could tell me what the rune meant. Then, when my father died, I abandoned the holy light. I dropped the hammer, and took up the rifle. For the rest of my days, I spent hunting trolls. Then I found a guild named the Dwarven Blacksmiths. I joined it, since it was the only one that would accept a hunter with seemingly no background, like me, and a war seemed to be hovering on the horizon. Once it starts, I shall destroy my enemies, and avenge my father by destroying the trolls, or die trying.
