Well I just realised how little information we have about the groups on our website, but I'll try explain it as good as I can.
The reason why we have divided us in these groups are to have the skilled players of a certain type of character in the same main group as the not so skilled ones with that particular character, so we can learn from each others failures as well as succes. Well that's at least some of the reason. Now which one to choose?
For your class you can choose between to groups (as you already have noticed):
The Fellowship of Figthers (the FoF) is where all the fighters, meat shields, hunters etc. go. This group focuses on strength, taunting, meat shields and other aspects of fighting...
The League of Spies (the LoS) is easy to know from their name what they do. We need to keep an eye on the Horde and any other enemy we might have, and LoS is a key group in that activity...
