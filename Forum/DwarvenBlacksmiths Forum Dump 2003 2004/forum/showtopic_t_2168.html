http://zone.msn.com/en/support/article/support307.htm says:

To access ZoneFriends

ZoneFriends opens whenever you sign in with your .NET Passport and go to any Card & Board, CD-ROM Required, or other multiplayer game page. To access ZoneFriends:

Step 1
Go to http://games.msn.com and sign in to .NET Passport.

Step 2
Click any game listed under Card & Board, except Solitaire, which does not use multiplayer-game software.

Step 3
Click Play with Others at the top of the page, under Select a mode of play.

Step 4
ZoneFriends will launch and your nickname will show up in the status bar as Logged on as <nickname>. (Online) and you should be listed as Connected. If ZoneFriends does not launch, make sure you are logged in to your .NET Passport. If it launches but does not connect, click the following link to get help:
ZoneFriends status bar displays Logging on . . . or Disconnected

Step 5
The Friends category will be populated by any MSN Games members you have added to your Friends list.


To exit ZoneFriends

When you close the ZoneFriends window, the application will continue to run in the taskbar (near the clock), so you can receive ZoneMessages while you are logged on. To turn ZoneFriends off, use one of these options:

Click Exit on the ZoneFriends File menu

With your right mouse button, click the Z icon in your system tray and a pop-up menu will appear. Click Exit.
