Anyway, here goes.

-------------------------

IC:

I was was born in Stormwind, my mother is working as a waitress, while my father is one of the remaining paladins of The Silver Hand that survived the tragedy in Lordaeron. He is ok now, if you want to know. Anyway I was only 10 when my father wanted me to become a paladin. I wasnt intrested much in the path of a paladin so I refused, but instead of letting my father down I decided to rather tend to the poor and wounded as a priestess, aye a priestess I am.

When a few years passed after I was fully educated to be a priestess, my father told me to go to Khaz Modan. He feared that Khaz Modan would be the first one to be brought down by the Scourge if they would attack Azeroth. Though my father was afraid that I will die in battle he thought that this would be the best anyway. I was going to travel there with a shipment of ale and food, it was a heavy stock and plenty of escort was sent with it, I guess Stormwind didnt want to get the dwarves starved if the caravan was to get attacked by gnolls or other foul beasts. So the journy began, I became friends with a quite friendly dwarf on the way. Almost through the whole journy we were talkin'. He spoke highly of a clan of dwarves. Aye, ye thought right, it was The Dwarven Blacksmiths. It turned out that his whole equipment was made by the DBS.

When night fell and we were in this forest near Ironforge, the caravan had to stop. The escort men were tired and couldnt keep up for much longer. Me and the dwarf sat down by the caravan, we werent much tired so we took the "watch duty". We told eachother jokes and tales, tis was a nice night until those Frostmane trolls began to throw axes on us. We sounded the alarm by shouting *chuckles*. Tis was a good thing that the escort men armed themselves fast enough, but unfortunately we were outnumbered. The battle was soon over, which we would end up as the fallen. The men dropped like flies, they couldnt even get near the trolls before an axe hit them. I tried to heal as many as I could in the battle but it seemed that I couldnt change the tide of this battle. I guess a rain of axes during the night proves to be a nice tactic for trolls.
My hands was full og blood of the wounded. As I tended to my wounded dwarf friend, I looked around me. Dwarven reinforcement had arrived! The scores was now even, the dwarves were pushing the trolls back into the woods.

The battle ended with a great loss of the human escort and a victory against the troll ambush, and the caraven was still intact. Since that day I am greatfull to be among dwarves, they truly are master warriors.

When I arrived to Ironforge, my thoughts was only bent to find this DBS guild, and so I did. Tis be my tale, lads.

----------------
