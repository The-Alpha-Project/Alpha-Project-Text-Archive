Shadow's adredaline pumped through his veins and arteries as he pushed his legs off the grass over and over again. Bolts from crossbows and arrows plugged the green countryside as his Shadow's pursuers chased him all over the vast landscape. Shadow was almost out of breath when he came to a dead end. It was a small rocky ledge that led to Blood Killer's dojo. Looking back at the five pursueing humans shooting arrows at him.

He cleared his mind gripped his pistols and was prepared to do one of his masters greatest tactics. He ran, turned himself backwards jumped on a rock near the ledge then pushed off of it with all his might, which sent him into the air at least eight feet. The humans watched in shock as they saw a dwarf do that. Shadow did a backflip while drawing his pistols then aimed each of them at different humans, then fired both of them at the same time. Both hit. His right handed one smashed through the human leaders brigindine armor and peirced his heart. His left handed one hit the man's left collar bone. The standing persuers ran and tended to their comrade and leader. Shadow smashed chest first into the dirt upon the ridge and fell into a deep sleep. Little did he know that Blood Killer carried him off to his dojo to tend to his wounds. Why did Shadow travel there? The elf was about to find out if he could wake the bastard.

A voice was screaming in the distance. His own or another persons? All he knew was he was in a warm bed and he was hungry. He tried to sit up, but his mind clouded over and he fell asleep...

Blood Killer returned to his dojo with a dead deer he had brought to feed himself and Shadow. He was puzzeled why Shadow would come looking for him after all these months, but he was hungry and decided to cook the deer before worrying about it. He had heard about how Shadow had killed the great bandit lord Shadowtarrasque and taken his name. Perhaps he would find peace after avenging his friend Maria who had taken care of him when he was lost and did not have a home. Knowing ShadowT7 however, Blood Killer knew that Shadow would never find inner peace until he had solved the mystery of his past...

Gathering the the ashes from the fire after the deer had been cooked, Blood Killer mixed a drink that would wake even those who had been declared dead. Ashes, vinegar, rabbit blood, and salt would be enough to wake Shadow he decided as he mixed the drink. Maybe a little wolf hair would help he decided, remembering Shadow's great hate for wolves. Finishing his creation, he walked over to ShadowT7 with a large amount of meat with him as Shadow was probably very hungry. Pouring the drink into Shadow's mouth, Blood Killer stepped back as Shadow started to cough loudly and wake up. He spat out the foul drink and grabbed the meat after seeing Blood Killer, stuffing it into him mouth as fast as he could...

"Been a while hasn't it Shadow..." Blood Killer looked at him while peeling the rinds off of his pears with his iron tanto. He handed him a cup full of the water from his stream. Even if you did not purify the water from his stream, you would still not get a fever for a mysterious reason. "Why were you running from those humans? And who were they?"
Shadow continued eating the meat he was given. After a strong swallow and a burp the Dwarf spoke. "They we're smugglers. I was raiding their boxes when I came upon this, and stole some gold from them and this. Does it look firmiliar?" Shadow pulled out what looked like a silver idol, in the shape of a naga siren, with sapphire eyes and the top of her staff that she weilded in her hand.
Blood Killer reached for the idol greedily, and Shadow tossed it to his hand. Blood Killer pulled the idol close to his face. "Where was this?" He asked.
"In the smugglers hideout. It was a cave near the road that leads to Teldrassil." Shadow was quick to respond as he stood up. He seemed to have finished his meal completely in that short amount of time.
"This is a demon hunter's dream to have such a prize as this..." He smiled.
"Your a demon hunter?" Shadow was suprised.
"Yes. I knew Illidan, and I fought along side him with about 20 other demon hunters. So I guess I was just another brick in his wall." Blood Killer said turning the idol around in his hand.
"What is this idol? Can I see it?" Shadow asked reaching for it. Blood Killer handed it to him and started to explain but suddenly when Shadow looked at it from a certain angle he started to have a flashback.

Instead of him seeing Blood Killer talking about in and the walls of the dojo, he saw fire, screaming people, naked corpses with burning skin, and...


...................Him............


Everthing went to normal when he dropped the idol from his hands and starting wheezing.
"Shadow? Are you alright?" Blood Killer faced him. His blind eyes narrowed with curiosity.

Blood Killer knew that something was not right. "Are you okay Shadow?" he repeated. Shadow had a look of shock, anger, and despair on his face. What could he be thinking? Blood Killer wondered. Perhaps Shadow would answer these questions soon as he started to talk...

"I need to know everything you remember from the battle. I believe I have been there and I may find a clue as to why I have no parents." Shadow said. As he said this, Blood Killer sat down across from him gathering his thoughts about the battle and Shadow's presence there on that fateful night...

Blood Killer wondered where to begin his story. Should he tell Shadow the truth? Blood killer ran his fingers naga idol as he wondered what would be best for his friend. To answer Shadow could lead him to his doom. To not, and Shadow would grow increasingly restless until he risked finding the truth on his own. He would need help with his journey he decided as he started his to talk...

Shadow thought about his own experience on that night. He needed answers, and he hoped Blood Killer could supply them...

Blood Killer walked away with the idol in his hand and put it on an altar where it suddenly glowed a turqoise color, not too bright though. Shadow ignored the way it looked and decided he wanted to know more answers.

"Master..." Shadow asked, sitting back down on his bed. Blood Killer faced his direction with his arms crossed. Shadow continued. "Please tell me some things, or at least give me a hint. Please..."
"I can't." Blood Killer said. But then somthing came to his mind. "But maybe she can..."
"What?" Shadow asked. "Who is she?"
"If you fall asleep you will know. The powers of this idol, are not what you think..." Blood Killer said, then walked out of the room.

Shadow fell asleep after cursing to himself repeativly, and then...

"Shadow...."

An image of Maria appeared in his mind and spoke to him.

"A grave thing occured when you we're but two years old. Your village was ransacked and burned by the daemons that arrived shortly before the first war, even I cannot name the leader of the daemons that slew your parents. There is one that can name him though. Her name is Urandone Evermoon, a Night Elf living in Teldrassil. Seek her out, and you will know much from her..." She slowely faded away...

MARIA!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

Shadow almost had a heart attack from that dream, he tried to reach out and touch her but he noticed he sat up right into the real world. It was still dark outside, and this time the idol glowed crimson. He put his hand down, got up and starting gearing up.
When he was about to step outside, Blood Killer stopped him.
"Leaving so soon?" He asked. "Prehaps you have learned somthing from Maria?"
Shadow looked up at the stars. "Yes... I need to go find someone named Urandone Evermoon." He said memorizing the name.
Blood killer suddendly starting panting and dropped his clay cup full of Darnassun blue tea and backed against the wall. "MY WIFE?" He screamed.
"She's your wife?!" Shadow was almost as startled as Blood Killer.
"She hasn't shown up in years. I know and feel that she still longs for me. She took my four kids and our unborn child with her. He, or she is bound to be born now. Five years old that is..."
Shadow nodded then walked out.
"STOP! Let me go with you." He went inside and came out with his traveling outfit on in less than twenty seconds. He walked with Shadow for four hours until they finally reached the city....
