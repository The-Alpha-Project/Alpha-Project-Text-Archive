"In the year 26, after the opening of the Dark Portal, the dwarven king Magni Bronzebeard ordered his loyal Ironforge dwarves to see to the creation of numerous guilds loyal to the King and the Alliance. These dwarves were to go forth into the world, each with his or her own purpose; some to rebuild what was lost in the last 3 decades of war, others to make sure that the evil that beset the world in these dark ages gone, would not rise again. One of these Ironforge dwarves was the stoic Durin "Wisdom" Blackhood, apprentice of the late Kurdran of The Gryphon Riders. Durin was given the task of creating a vast guild of merchants, warriors and craftsmen, to rebuild Azeroth and restore it to its former glory; scouring the carrion Dead which had destroyed all that The Light held dear. Durin rallied many to his cause, and his banner flailed in the winds, as his brave men fought against warmongering Undead and savage Orcs. Though constantly beleaguered by opposing factions, Durin and his men’s courage and valor, fought with a pride and vehemence never seen before. As more and more flocked to his banner, he knew that his guild would grow powerful. Thus the Dwarven Blacksmith was born. Meeting with two other growing guilds, The Riders of War and Strength and Honour, a sturdy alliance was formed—Arathor. Around a large table, Durin met with the other guild leaders and discussed, planning for their retaking of Azeroth and cleansing the world from the taint of the Undead.

The grass upon the fields sways in the wind. Three banners are proudly displayed, and horns blaze. Dwarves, men, elves, and gnomes stand in formation, carrying weapons and the pride of their races with them. Durin stands at the front, along with Isenbright and Stuart, their weapons ready and their hearts filled with an insatiable lust—to see Azeroth cleansed. The horns sound once more and Durin speaks: “Today is a fine day!” The hearts of those rallied around his swell, overwhelming pride and vehemence fill them. “This land is, as of late, bereft of all sanctity. It should not be foul, for it was not always this way! This alliance was formed on the basis of retaking Azeroth for the Alliance, and we shall hold true to that word! Today, is the day when the cleansing of Azeroth shall begin! If you are to die, then you shall do so with honor! Know that your cause is a faithful one. The light will protect you, and the strength of the Titans will aid you. Fight, with valor and courage! Onwards!”

As he thrust his rifle in the air, grasped by an iron fist, a cheer rose from the ranks. Many smiled, Durin’s stoic leadership, along with the valiant commanders of the Riders of War, and Strength and Honour, would see the promise they had made come true. It had begun.

- From the records of Kelv Sternhammer, Historian of the Dwarven Blacksmith, with the aid of guild member Magnus "Boomstick" Gurnisson.
