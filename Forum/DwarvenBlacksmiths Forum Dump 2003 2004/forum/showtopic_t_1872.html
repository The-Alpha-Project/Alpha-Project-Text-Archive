I saw in the WoW forum that someone made a "When you have played to much Warcraft" thread. So I thought we could make one too : >

I picked some good ones from that thread, so I'll start the ball rolling.

You know you have played to much (World of) Warcraft when:

You go swimming with plate armor.
You are stuck in a traffic jam, you yell "Damn lag!"
You take a walk in the forest and exclaim: "What marvelous graphical improvements!"
You jump in a fireplace to test the new Death System.
You go to sleep for weeks to try out the new Rest System.
You walk by the scene of a major car accident and begin to loot the people. (My favorite, gave me an endless laugh : > )
You go to a church and kill the priest because you were trying to disable their healer.
You dye your skin green, grab your trusty axe, and yell "FOR THE HORDE" while riding your dog to work.
You don't have a house because "It hasn't been implemented in yet".
Your parents ask you why you failed your test you tell them that your sorry you didnt put enough skill points in intelligence.
You can no longer play baskeball well and complain that blizzard keeps nerfing your class. (Another favorite)
Your find your mothers shopping list and think its a recipe scroll.
You shout out "NOOB!" to someone asking directions.
You wonder why your science project isnt getting done when you've put 4 peasants on it already.
