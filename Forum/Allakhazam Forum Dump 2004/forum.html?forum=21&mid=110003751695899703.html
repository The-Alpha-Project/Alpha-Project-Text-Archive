So far from what I see the elite quests have little story and no multi-part quests. THere is some damn good loot to be had inside them though. So its fun, but skippable if you don't want to group or would have a crappy group.

Yep. Elite mobs have special status: the level you'd have to be to solo them efficiently is such that you wouldn't get any experience out of it. Seriously. All instances eventually get to two- and three-mob pulls; they're linked, no way to avoid it. I'll give you an example. Last night, after a long run through Ragefire Chasm, I decided to try an experiment. Two 19s, a hunter and a warlock, wanted to do Ragefire with me (15 Shaman). Well, we did alright at first, though it was really slow going. But then we got to an area of Magma Elementals and got a three-pull, which was going VERY poorly. Then an add... WHAM! Instant wipe, we hadn't a chance. And those elementals are only 14-15 elites. We were substantially higher than them, and we had no chance.

Just goes to show you that instances really require a group of 5. It's no big deal, and if your groupmates are decent human beings you'll have a blast, but that's how it goes. But the loot and experience from instances is terrific, so make your choices.
