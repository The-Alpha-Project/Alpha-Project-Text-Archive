The instances are really nice- you'll start doing those at about level 20- and they require groups.

The quests will generally be 1 of these types:
1) Gather monster parts or items
2) Delivery (deliver this item to that guy)
3) Kill xx creatures
4) Explore a place
5) Escort a NPC

There are variations on them- and it definitely feels like a step forward from games like City of Heroes- but those are your basic templates. A lot of times fun things will be tossed in- like requiring you to destroy a catapult. Some instances are just places to kill monsters, but others have a very strong personality, and interesting quests in them. Deadmines is one that you can start exploring at 20, and is pretty nicely done. I'd say that if you and a group do deadmines for the first time and don't have fun, then the game will not impress you. If you DO have fun, take heart- the game just gets more and more like that as you advance in levels.
