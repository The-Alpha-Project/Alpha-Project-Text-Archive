*Coming from a former FFXI player and a WoW Beta player for 5 months*

Rogue could be considered the equivalent of the FFXI Thief. However, they're really quite different playstyles. Thiefs relied on getting behind their opponent, and without a group, they had an incredibly difficult time even farming. Rogue moves are mainly done in the front of the opponent, and for the few that require you to be in the back, you can easily accomodate for it by using one of the many stuns that a Rogue has. A Rogue is a great solo'er, which i'm sure is refreshing for whomever played a Thief.

As for the top damage dealer, at the moment it's a trade-off between the Hunter and the Rogue. Hunter used to be quite far behind, but with their talents finally in, a proper build allows them to do incredibly steady and powerful damage at range, oftentimes even outdoing the Rogue. They're both excellent classes if you want a damage dealer, and i'd recommend trying them both out to get a feel for which playstyle you like the most.
(ie: If you liked Beastmaster and Ranger, go with Hunter. If you liked Thief and Ninja, go with the Rogue)
