I then moved to Undead Warlock. I liked playing this class a lot. Got him to level 13.

The above I tried in the stress test.

For open beta...

Moved to human Pally. I always avoided human chars on EQ. I like the humans in WoW. Very good race to play. Good starting zones. The Pally is pretty good. Well balanced. He can tank pretty good and has lots of good support spells. He is level 15 and counting.

I made a Night Elf druid which I got to level 12. I like him a lot too. Very fun to play, and I really like the way you turn to a melee class when in bear form rather than just a druid in bear skin. very nice. Night Elves are very cool and some very fun quests and starting areas.

OK, so these two characters above I am enjoying...so i may not have given the ones below a fair chance, but I kept coming back to the Druid and Pally. Note, I have not disliked any character just preferred some over others:

Orc/Warrior - Was ok. Not my style obviously.

Troll/Mage - Again, was ok. Did not hold my attention. Maybe because the starting zone and quests were the exact ones I had just done with the above mentioned orc.

Gnome Mage - Fun because of how they look. I did this with my youngest daughter so we made a little girl...cotten candy pink hair...looked like a cupie doll. For the looks alone was a novelty...but I don't know, two mages aqnd neither one seemed to interest me.

Then...when my server was down (and up and down and up) over the weekend, I got into another server that wasmore stable (and that is relative...lol) and made a Tauren Hunter. I like him a lot. I am having fun with this guy.

I have not played a rogue at all. never liked them in EQ, but this is a case of do not compare, I am sure. The WoW rogue seems much more fun than the EQ rogue.

So, I have to say that come retail I will be on a Human Pally, Night Elf Druid, Tauren Hunter, Dwarf Priest and undead Warlock...in that order I think.
