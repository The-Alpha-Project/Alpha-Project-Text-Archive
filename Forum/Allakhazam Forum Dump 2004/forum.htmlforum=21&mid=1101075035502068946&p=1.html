Hoping that will stop 2/3 of the pointless threads from cropping up. Please bump this routinely so it's remotely near the new thread button.


To answer why...
Starting stats don't matter. No. They don't. A difference of 7 agility is nice at level 1. At level 60, it's irrelevant.
Racial bonuses are meant to add flavor. They are to let you feel like you are playing a Tauren or a Gnome, while at the same time not unbalancing the game.

Yes, orcs get a pet bonus, night elves have shadowmeld and the highest starting agility, and dwarves get a gun bonus. None are the best hunter.

Yes, humans get a bonus to swords and maces while night elves get a bonus to dodge. That does not make one decidedly better than the other in melee.

Yes, gnomes get an innate intellect bonus for mages and warlocks, while humans get...that they aren't gnomes. This puts them on even footing.

Mileage might vary a little, but don't pick your race on the sole basis of it being the uber-est combination for your race. Your choice of race is your chance to express a little individuality and personal taste.
