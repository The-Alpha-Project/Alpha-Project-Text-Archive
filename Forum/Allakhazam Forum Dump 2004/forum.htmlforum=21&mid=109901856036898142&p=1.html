Well, I didn't exactly get to finish, since I didn't devote my week to it and the servers are down to mark the end of closed beta...but since closed beta is ending, I figure there's no point in posting if I don't do it now.

You can find my photobucket account here:
http://photobucket.com/albums/v332/Azuarc/

I was planning on filling up 3 folders with Azeroth by region, 3 with Kalimdor, 1 with instances, 1 with maps, and 1 with other miscellaneous silly pictures. You'll note that a couple of those folders are sorely lacking.

In general, I tried to take three pictures of each zone - one panoramic, one close-up of a creature or something, and one active picture. I didn't always succeed, considering having an active picture almost requires you to be questing in the zone, and I didn't have time for every zone, so some areas just have whatever I had to throw at you.

My apologies for the proliferation of Night Elves in the foreground of many of the pictures. That is what I play, though, and seeing the characters is as important as seeing anything else.

I also apologize for any bias that comes with my selection of screenshots. They were all taken by me, and they contain no PvP raid pictures, or examples of many of the other nuances of the game, as well as a few incomplete maps. If I had time, I would have gotten to these things.

I *really* wish these were more convenient a viewing format, or at least that the pictures had tags on them. Thanks Photobucket. I recommend you click on the first picture, and scroll forward one at a time - at least that way they have numbers.

I'm going to describe what's in each folder, one reply at a time, starting with the maps.

WoWspecial
Picture explanations, beginning with the WoWspecial folder to put things in perspective...actually, I just want to comment on the two continent maps. The rest of the maps in the maps folder speak for themselves.

Anyway, ignoring my Teldrassil guide picture, there are the two continents in this folder. The "Eastern Kingdoms" is also knows as Azeroth and the zones are numbered as follows:

NORTH AZEROTH (generally undead) -
1) Tirisfal Glades. Haunted farmlands. Undead starting zone, containing The Undercity. Zeppelin to Ogrimmar in Kalimdor. Levels 1-12. Instance: Scarlet Monastery. Levels 32-40.
2) Western Plaguelands. Plagued farmlands. Neutral zone controlled by the Scourge. Levels 50-60. Instance: Scholomance, school of necromancy. Levels 56-60.
3) Eastern Plaguelands. Blighted countryside. Neutral zone controlled by the Scourge. Levels 55-60. Instance: Stratholme. Levels 60-60.
4) Silverpine Forest. Undead zone. Levels 12-18. Instance: Shadowfang Keep. Levels 20-30?
5) Alterac Mountains. Neutral zone, controlled by ogres, rogue humans, and the Kirin Tor. Levels 30-38.
6) Hilsbrad Foothills. Neutral zone, controlled by humans and undead. Levels 20-35.
7) The Hinterlands. Grassy wilderness. Neutral zone, controlled by NPC trolls, high elves, and simply wildlife. Levels 40-48.
8) Arathi Highlands. Neutral zone, controlled mostly by loyal humans, rogue humans, and ogres. Levels 30-40.

CENTRAL AZEROTH (generally dwarf) -
9) The Wetlands. Bog. Dwarven zone, with ports to Darkshore and Dustwallow Marsh in Kalimdor. Levels 20-32.
10) Loch Modan. Lakeside region. Dwarven zone. Levels 12-20.
11) Dun Morogh. Snowy mountains. Dwarven (and Gnomish) starting zone, containing Ironforge. Levels 1-12. Instance: Gnomeregan. Levels 30-35.
12) The Badlands. Rocky wasteland. Neutral zone, controlled by dark iron dwarves, ogres, troggs, and wildlife. Levels 35-42. Instance: Uldaman. Levels 38-45.
13) (between the N and G in Kingdoms) Searing Gorge. Volcanic mountains. Neutral zone, controlled by Dark Iron Dwarves. Levels 40-50. Instance: Blackrock Depths. Levels 50-60.
14) Burning Steppes. Ash-covered mountains. Neutral zone, controlled by wyrmkin. Levels 50-60. Instance: Blackrock Spire. Levels 55-60.

SOUTH AZEROTH (generally humans) -
15) Elwynn Forest. Gentle countryside. Human starting zone, containing Stormwind City and Keep. Levels 1-12. Instance: The Stockades. Levels 22-28.
16) Redridge Mountains. Craggy hillside. Human zone. Levels 16-24.
17) Swamp of Sorrows. Neutral zone, but with only Horde outpost, controlled by draenor? and wyrmkin. Levels 35-45. Instance: The Temple of Akai'Hakkar. Levels 48-54.
18) Blasted Lands. Neutral zone, controlled by demons and corrupted wildlife. Levels 48-55.
19) Stranglethorn Vale. Indian jungle. Neutral zone, controlled by goblins, trolls, and anything else you can name. Contains Booty Bay. Levels 30-45. Instance: Zul'Gurub. Levels unknown.
20) Deadwind Pass. Eerie chasm. Neutral zone. Levels unknown. Future instance pending.
21) Duskwood. Haunted forest. Human zone. Levels 20-32.
22) Westfall. Prairie. Human zone. Levels 12-18. Instance: The Deadmines. Levels 20-25.

And now for the other continent, Kalimdor.

NORTH KALIMDOR (mostly Night Elves) -
1) Teldrassil. Elven tree-home, contains Darnassus. Night Elf starting zone. Levels 1-12.
2) Darkshore. Thick forest. Night Elf zone. Levels 12-18.
3) Felwood. Corrupted woods. Neutral zone, controlled by furbolgs and satyrs. Levels 48-56.
4) Mount Hyjal. Unknown.
5) Winterspring. Snowlands. Neutral zone, controlled by furbolgs, giants, and wildlife. Levels 52-60.
6) Azshara. Highlands. Neutral zone, controlled by furbolg, naga, undead, and giants. Levels 52-60.
19) Moonglade (almost forgot this was a real zone). Enchanted glade. Night Elf zone. Levels unknown.

CENTRAL KALIMDOR (mostly Horde) -
7) Ashenvale Forest. Mystic forest. Night Elf zone. Levels 20-32.
8) Stonetalon Mountains. Neutral zone, controlled by goblins, harpies, and dryads. Levels 24-30.
9) The Barrens. Savannah. Horde zone. Levels 10-30. (Not joking.) Instance: Wailing Caverns. (20-25) Instance: Razorfen Keep. (28-33) Instance: Razorfen Downs. (35-40)
10) Durotar. Starting Orc (and Troll) zone, containing Orgrimmar. Levels 1-12. Instance pending.
11) Dustwallow Marsh. Neutral zone, controlled by Humans, Horde, murlocs, and dragons. Levels 38-45. Instance pending.
17) Mulgore. Grassland. Tauren starting zone, containing Thunder Bluff. Levels 1-12.
18) Desolace. Barren wasteland. Neutral zone, controlled by Centaurs and wildlife. Instance pending.

SOUTH KALIMDOR (mostly neutral) -
12) Thousand Needles. Southwestern canyon. Horde/neutral zone, controlled by Tauren and goblins. Levels 25-33.
13) Tanaris. Desert. Neutral zone, controlled by goblins, ogres, silithids, and human pirates. Contains Gadgetzan. Levels 42-52. Instance: Zul'Farrak. Levels 45-50.
14) Un'goro Crater. Lost World-style jungle. Neutral zone, controlled by wildlife. Levels 48-55.
15) Silithus. Unknown.
16) Feralas. Rain forest. Neutral zone, controlled by Night Elves and Horde, ogres, and wildlife. Levels 40-40. Instance pending.

Instance

1) Zul'Farrak. Standing atop a ziggurat, looking out over a crowd of trolls. There's far more down below than you can see.
2) Uldaman. The boss, off-screen to my right, looks like these little statues, but a lot nastier. Not surprisingly, they spring to life as the battle rages.
3) Uldaman. This is some sort of city floor model on the ground in one part of the dungeon. I took this picture because I thought I might find significance in it later. I haven't yet.

7) Scholomance. My guild completes a raid on the school of necromancy by attacking the lich that presides over the dungeon.
8) Razorfen Kraul. My guild on beta 2 decides to crawl through this dungeon in its entirety, without anyone knowing what to expect, and certainly not a giant killer warthog to attack us!
9) Gnomeregan. My first experience fighting the end boss of the befouled gnome city. By his green skin, I'd say he's been irradiated like all the other lepers still in the futuristic city. Very difficult fight now that the bombs work.
10) Blackrock Depths. I look out a window at the inside-of-the-Death-Star-like core of the area.
11) Blackrock Depths. Most of the foes here are dark iron dwarves or fire elementals, but there are a few gnomes with their contraptions here as well.
12) Blackrock Depths. A very familiar view when doing this dungeon -- running back to the dungeon as a ghost!
13) Blackfathom Deeps. My group assaults the hydra boss Aku'mal.
14) Blackfathom Deeps. ...right after disposing of the sub-boss of the zone, an Orc warlock that leads the cult in this subterranean temple.
