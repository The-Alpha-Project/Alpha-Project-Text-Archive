There's nothing stopping you from meeting your friend. If you play on a PvE server, it would be easier for you two to meet up, as you can only be killed by the opposing faction if you have been flagged for PvP. If you play on a PvP server, it will be darn near impossible. The best way is for you to stay on certain factions. On PvE servers, you can create multiple characters from either side. On PvP servers, you can only create characters from the side you have chosen, for the specific reason stated earlier. Blizzard wants players to have the ability of "suprise" for lack of better word, so you can't go and inform your friend, or hop on an opposing faction character and shout in town/area to pre-warn them. Whispering/telling should still work though. I don't honestly know, I've never had to give a /tell to a lighty. =)
