Generally all of these things will happen during the same day. Unfortunately Fileplanet (who was in control of the emails for the stress test) mucked up big time, but people were eventually playing the same day as the opened up the stress test.

The hardest part will be downloading the client if you haven't already. There will be a 19 meg patch that you have to get through the blizzard downloader, but it shouldn't take you but 10-20 minutes if you're on a decent internet connection. The nicest thing about the client is that it will be the same one that you can use for retail, so hopefully there will not be as huge a patch for people who have the beta client already installed.
