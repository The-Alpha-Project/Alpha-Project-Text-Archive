Creamed, you have to realize that while you have a point that it isn't all there is to the class. Other stats make up for the lower Sta.

Other than Night Elf, the gnome has the highest Agility.

Agility does the following for Warriors:
Increases your attack power with ranged weapons.

Increases your armor.

Increases your chance to score a critical hit with a weapon.

Increases your chance to dodge attacks


The last thing to keep in mind is that this is at level ONE. because the game picks what stats you gain each time you level, Sta and Str are auto-picks for Warriors. As you gain levels it will become even less of an issue. Also, as you get gear it will be less of an issue. Everyone will have different gear and different builds and the higher level you become the less those earlier level stats will matter.
