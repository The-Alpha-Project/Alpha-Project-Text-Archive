Uridus,

I'm in the same boat you are...I don't have 15+ hours a week to devote to a MMORPG...it just doesn't work out like that.

WoW is a very friendly game for the casual player. Hell, when you log at an Inn for the night and rest up you gain an experience bonus for the next time you go out questing...a bonus to being logged off, great stuff :) (1 bubble on your experience bar is included in this for every 8 hours you are logged off)

As Arimer said, you can solo. No waiting 30+ minutes to find a group. Also, if you read the quests, they are all well explained so you can keep going in the game and not have to look up where you need to be constantly. Spawn time on monsters are great, instances for bigger fights keep the wait down as well, plus leveling doesn't take days like it did in FFXI (all you 50+ from FFXI know what I'm talking about).

It will be much more friendly towards your schedule. I think you will like it.
