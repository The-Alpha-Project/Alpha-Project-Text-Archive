for anything. I love money. So therefor the game has to be
something special to earn my money.

This is that game.

Ive read alot of reviews here and notice some things i dont
agree with also i have many of my other reasons that im going
play this game and what makes it fun for me.
They may be **** i just made up but thats why i play the game.

Blizzard has done a good job advertising this game because ever
since i played the beta ive been advertising for them.
the fact is they know if u play this game u will have to buy it
AND u'll tell every known human being the same thing.

Anyways i heard about the game going to have an open beta on my
other forum (a mushroom cultivation forum).
It was some guy that was playing closed beta and had gotten the
inside about the open beta download for the next day.
Im a person that has a Haaaaard time playing games nowadays.
i have never found a game worthy of be dedicating so much time
too. I think getting older(24). makes it hard for me to play.
maybe its just me.
i was always dedicated to quake2, teamfortress, Age of kings,
Diablo2, Wartcraft 3, and most recently Unreal2004.
I played many games but none i could ever get hooked in.
I always played reflex game to tweak my refex in for gaming skillz. I think just waiting for the next good game.

I signed up for the beta and downloaded the ***** for 2 days.
i left work in antcipation of playing it. Even stopped by my
friends house. He was in the shower and i told his girfriend
that i have WoW and im going to play it tonight.
i leave and am hom for 2 min and he is shows up. Excited? who knows?
I install and start up the game. Of couse we had already had 2 beers each and had smoked a joint waiting for it to get done.
I look threw tweaking out my first taurens looks. I decided to go
warrior. I run around smashing stuff thinking man this control is
funky and the game is slow. this is coming strait off of unreal2004
remember.
I continued to run around drinking beers and smoking more.
i ahd a hard time getting into the game that night.
it was interesting but i couldnt focus keep track of where i was
going and ****.
but anyways i never played gain for like a week.
thats about when my friend asks me if i played it anymore. I said
no that i was again that night.
I go home and started to play it again.
didnt stop untill they ended the beta.
i even called in sick 2 days just to play.(was sick to but normaly
would have went).

During that time i leveled a lvl 5 tauren warrior, lvl 15 nightelve
hunter, lvl 14 undead warlock, and a lvl 14 nightelve druid.
also used a few different combinations of tradeskills.
playing each noticed this game had alot of game actually to it.
it was completely differnt for every class i played.
i soloed for awile cause i was cracked out from diablo 2 and
not understanding what made a mmorpg a mmorpg(wich i still dont know
what mmo is).
once i got used to how to game looked and controled i had a blast.
questing and fighting all kinds of ****, learning how to train pets, and skin deer.
there was just so much to do it felt never ending, and this was
not even getting past lvl 15!!!
Then i read a few articles about how the game doesnt actually
start for most people till the get to max lvl (lvl60)
i mean when i learned what made this game a game it was an exciting
thought.
THere where times in the game (after i started getting better at
it, learning tricks and ****). Id go to the town and grab all the
quests i could.
First few quests are easy teaching u how it works.
Quests lvl u. Not only do they give u EXP from completeing them,
sometimes quite a bit, they actually tell u to go kill 7 so and
so and get theyre belt(an item they dropped) ud go out and have
to kill 20-25 of them to get belts, sometimes it only took 10 trys.
It makes killing monsters fun and not noticable. its lvling u.
u can go off and kill random monsters for EXP but i believe its
not nessicary. maybe a little.
Once u get good at learning stragety to quests such as if u have
to go kill a monster full of caves, most likely there is another
quest from a different person in town wanting u to kill the head
guy in the cave and return his head.
thats 2 quests u can do in 1, some people ive read in reviews get
angry for having to run back and forth 20 times.
Im sorry its not the game it was how he was playing.
get alot of quests and do multiples at once.
I think the game slowly introduces u to the mmorpg type game play
(i think to be playing in group).
i played my druid to lvl 10 where i needed to get my shapshifting
skill threw a quest i got.
it was no problem getting there solo like i played most the game.
but once i got there and had to fight the thing i needed to to
get the quest.
it was lvl 13 and i was 10.
I was no match as the druid.
I notice for tings like if u just wait a little while killing
mosters eventually someone will come, aske them for help kill
the mosnter.
the first guy i asked basically told me to shove it he had his own quests to do. would have taken him 1 minute.
but soon a group came by doing the same quest. what luck.
Its also cool u run around and hit a hotkey to do an emote.
basically he waves and says high. Alot of people emote back, wich
i hope never changes once the game gets into retail.

I hear alot of people complaing out casters and not having enough
tool bars. NOT TRUE. All u need is cosmos from this website and
u can make tons. http://www.cosmosui.org/

hmm what else is there.
Oh yeah, i love how the control works, alot of people say its
sometimes constricting. It works so well once u learn the tricks.
u can do so much. its fluid and wonderfull. best controlin a 3d
game ive ever played. besides FPS like unreal2004.

I noticed its a game where u are sucked in at times and somtimes
u wont need to be. This is my favorite part of the game.
it takes u time to go places but theres an auto run button.
u click it aim and sit back and relax, drink a beer, smoke something, or even get up and go take a ****.
but theres other times were i have to get real close to the monitor
so i can tell whats going on sucking me in, actually being in the game.

this game is so amazing i know if anyone plays this game will
get hooked and need to buy it.
I never thought id ever find a game that could actually play
ever again.
Blizzard has done it again.

Oh yeah before i forget.
I think anyone can get good items in this game, and they are completely unique. The tradeskills give andyone the ability
to create theyre own good weapons and create currency.
its not running around killing mosters like in diablo 2.
i mean it is kidn of but the best way is making ur own.
im looking foward to the end game after hitting lvl 60
killing that ******* alliance. creating my own weapons and becoming
the best that i can be.
also wanting to get that 40 person raiding party and going to kill
the dragon that is probley going to waste us all.

Well i hope this isnt to long for anyone to read.
i figured it was something to do cause the 23rd is sooo far away.

Im hooked as im sure will be everyone.
