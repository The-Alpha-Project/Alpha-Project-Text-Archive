Honestly, I can't see why people compare MMORPGs. Each one is tailored for different people. It's not the same as comparing one First Person Shooter to another one. Game dynamics are COMPLTLEY different between MMORPGs. FFXI, for example, focuses on group mentality. WoW dosen't as much. FFXI has a job system that makes you work very hard to get anywhere in the game and the storyline is very slow to reveal itself. WoW's story, from what I've concluded, is built on the quests you do. You start quests when you start the game, and a lot of them you don't need to rely on other people for. You need people to get anything done in FFXI.

Stop comparing the two and calling one better than the other. I've had FFXI since it's North American relase, and I will also be getting WoW. FFXI does not suck.
