I've only been playing WoW for a week, and so far I LOVE the game...it doesn't feel like a beta at all. The UI is far superior, and the gameplay is more fun. I like the combat system...it feels more like diablo or a regular RPG than "turn-based" style combat.

What really sold me, was last night, fighting underwater dinosaurs in Auberdine. hahah what the heck! that was hilarious...and a lot of fun :) though I almost drowned a few times.
