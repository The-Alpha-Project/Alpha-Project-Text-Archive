The problem with FFXI is purely in the lvlingspeed after lvl 40 cause it get's horrible.

Certain jobs don't get any invite and if you get an invite after waiting 2 hours(wich is completly normal in FFXI you grab a choco right 30 minutes to your camp do a couple of fights and come to the conclusion that this party aint working.

The things I like about FFXI are:
- Option to choose all jobs you want to play(the min. lvl of 30 for some jobs could be left out but isn't a big problem)
