Couple things:

1) You can download it from www.fileplanet.com. You may have to wait in line for it but it will be faster than Blizzard.

2) Blizzard Downloader is a distributed DL system. This means that you are downloading it from someone who is uploading what they have already downloaded.

This person could be on a 56kb modem line which means, even if you have cable access, your DL will be no faster than what they upload as. Furthermore, even if they have cable, cable usually has restricted upload speeds so you are still SOL.
