Well, after playing for 2 days as a night elf, tauren, human and orc, I must say that this is what I want in a MMO. This game is so polished, so lagfree and bleeding with so much content I forgot that this is stress test beta. From lvs 1 - 9 I have experienced quests that requires you to go questing in a very dangerous but fun like the Awakening Quest found in Dolanaar to the countdown to get the antidote to Iverron before he dies from poison. This is not all you'll find in the beginning, there's more.
