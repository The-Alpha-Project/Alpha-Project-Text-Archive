Okay, playing as a Rogue, I had pretty much no problem with any classes.

However, I did notice a priest had a very good chance against me, which was surprising. They have PW:S (Power Word: Shield) which makes them take no damage, and their spells can not be interrupted when casting in the shield.
My clan leader played as a priest, we were both level 23. First duel, I lost. Second duel, I lost. Third duel, I lost. Then I started getting a hang of it, and decided to put crippling poison rather than instant poison, this worked well.

With crippling poison he was unable to turn around very fast or run away very fast, or anything. (Crippling poison decreases movement to 50% and has a 30% chance of hitting) Then, all I'd really do is jump around behind him and backstab him. When I got 5 combo points I'd Rupture him, and he'd be hurting.

Then he decided to throw on PW:S in the beginning of the duel, so he could cast damage spells and whatnot, then towards the middle of the duel he'd cast it again and heal himself fully, and then continue you with the damage. And I'd eventually lose.

Any class played well can be a master in PvP.

As a level 23 Rogue I dueled a level 30 Shaman once, and not saying I had much of a chance, but I got him down a good 1/2 to 1/4'th health. And he even complimented me by saying "Wow you hit hard," it was a pleasure playing rogue.

However, I noticed the class I had most trouble was with a Hunter. Rogue vs Hunter is probably one of the hardest battles. There was a level 18 hunter in XRoads and I'd duel him all the time, while I was level 23. He had ****** gear, low level, ****** weapon, and a ****** pet. However, he actually beat me a couple times. He beat me more than I beat him. Because of the way he played, first he'd run around, shoot me with a slow arrow while his pet mauled me, and he'd constantly jump about shooting concussion arrows and scorpid sting or something. Basically while I was trying to get to him, I was losing health; FAST. By the time he got to level 20 with Aspect of the Cheetah, and his slow spells, and frost trap, I was doomed. I didn't always lose, sometimes I'd get lucky and get a really good hit off of ambush doing somewhere around 120-250. And then he was cake from there. And a lot of times I actually got him just by sin strike + evisc, all in all I'd say we were really a good duel. Even though I feel like I got my *** handed to me, it's really just cause I usually never lose duels.

Anyways, you can interpret that how you please, but, all-in-all any class played right can dominate.
