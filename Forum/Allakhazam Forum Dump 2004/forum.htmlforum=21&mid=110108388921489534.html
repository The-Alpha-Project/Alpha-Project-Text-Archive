If the store said it was shipped tonight (Sunday) and you had it shipping overnight.. You probably wont get it til Tuesday anyway.

Being that I have ordered a lot of stuff from EBGames.com and knowing that even if they said they shipped the game doesnt mean it has actually left their holding department... especially on a Sunday evening and seeing that FedEx doesnt run on Sundays...

On the other hand, if the store said it shipped today and you actually got a tracking number and you goto FedEx.com and it actually says that is was picked up from the store.. that is a different story... (btw... the first log on the tracking number list sometimes isnt FedEx picking up the item, it is FedEx getting a tracking scan at the store's warehouse... basically a store employee scanning the item's FedEx shipping tag to put it in the system as being shipped by FedEx)
