Literal physical customization is limited to the following:

Race & gender (16 options)
Face (~8 types)
Hair Style (~8 types)
Hair Color (varies)
Facial Hair % Color (varies, and not always a beard, per se)

However as far as the character model itself, there is only one body. From the neck down, your human female will look just like every other human female, not counting the equipment you have on.
