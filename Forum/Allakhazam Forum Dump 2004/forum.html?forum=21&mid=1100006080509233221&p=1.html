Mage = Black Mage/Red Mage. Highest burst magic damage in the game. Also uses frost spells to enfeeble the enemy. Best AoE caster in the game.

Hunter = Ranger/Beastmaster/Red Mage. Ranged fighter that can own pets that level with their master and also have traps that enfeeble/damage. Also have spells that increases own stats and pets.

Warrior = All melees in FFXI. Warriors have the ability to change stances depending on the battle situation. Makes for a great tank in defensive stance, a steady damage dealer in offensive stance and a melee killing machine in berserker stance, though berserker stance brings the lower def penalty.

Warlock = Red Mage/Summoner. The best damage over time dealer in the game. Also have the ability to summon pets depending on the situation. Can also open portals to open areas and summon other players.

Paladin = Paladin/Bard. Has the best def in the game. Can support a party through tanking, healing and provide seals and auras that give benefits to the party.

Rogue = Theif/Dark Knight. The best melee damage dealer in the game. Has the ability to stealth and backstab as well as sap enemies and stun them. With each success attack, rogues can build up combo points that a rogue can use for a minor boost in stats or execute a finishing move.

Shaman = Bard/Red Mage. The jack of all trades. The shaman can use totems to heal/enfeeble/attack depending on the radius for the uses.

Druids = Red Mage/Thief/Warrior. Druids have the ability to transform to either a bear or a tiger to make up for slots in a party. In bear form a druid acts as a replacement warrior and in tiger form, a replacement rogue. Druids are also the second best healers in the game in caster form and gain the most powerful buff in the game (Mark of the Wild).

Priests = White Mage/Red Mage/Black Mage. Best healer in the game. Not only a healer, priests can buff party members and cause good damage with holy spells as well as shadow magic.
