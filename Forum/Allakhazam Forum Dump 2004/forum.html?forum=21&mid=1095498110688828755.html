As you all may know, Blizzard has stated that World of Warcraft will be divided into 4 regions: US East, US West, Europe and Asia. Each player will then choose the region and set up their accounts according to the region. While this option will become available, come release, North American players will be forced to pick one of two servers (US East and US West) while Korean and Asian gamers will be seperated from the American servers. Europeans won't have this problem because they have to wait months before getting their copies so I won't go there. Not to say Europeans can't post (Same as Asians or any other person from whatever country), so if you guys wanna voice where you will be, go ahead. Also keep in mind that Blizzard plans to allow people to pick any region they please, it just means you will not be guarantee certain services and benefits.

Anyways, I'm sure that many of you have guild members, friends and the such that you may want to alert before you finally decide on a region. Maybe you are a leader of a guild that wants to let people know what region you will be located in. Well, this thread is for you then! Just follow this setup when you are posting:

Character Name: (No need to fill this now if you have no idea what you are going to call yourself)

Region:

Guild Name:

Faction/Race:

Type of Server: (Either PvE or PvP)

Additional Comments:

If you want to make a simple change, I assume you guys know how to edit a post so I won't get into that. This is a tool for you all to use so please, no trolling, fighting, conversations and yata, yata. Do rate me up. Me likes that. ;)

Side Note: If you plan on having multiple characters, just edit and make another set. Only post additional characters if you plan to actually use them seriously.
