There's nothing wrong with dual swords or dual maces, sure you miss out on backstab and ambush, but your Sinister Strikes will be way more damaging. Plus, if you go mace, you get 6% chance of stunning (when you max out mace spec) on each hand. Slice and Dice that and you've got some evilness going on.

However, a lot of Rogues don't like it because it eschews the whole sneaky sneaky bit (which isn't true, it's just that you're less reliant on getting behind the target to do damage).

As for Fist Weapons, Polearms and all other sorts of fun gear, I'm sure they're saving that for retail.
