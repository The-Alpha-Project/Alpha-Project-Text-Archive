Here is a flash game some of us already know, some don't.
GROW-The game
yOu have to drag items to the grow-button,and see how they work together.
Due to some request I posted up a strategy guide, in which we can post our strategies and remarks.
So far, this is how far I've got
(IMG:http://members.chello.at/baboonland/screen1.jpg)

So I'm gonna post my ideas and observations.
First of all I'm gonna write what benefits from what, and how to max them out, and on top of it include combos.I´ll update it soon!

:::::::::::::::::
::::UPDATE::::
:::::::::::::::::
Combinations :
NOTE: all those combinations are refering to LEVELED items,every item will level from turn to turn, and hereby influence other items.I'll probably list the lvls and the duration for the effects as well.

Ladder + metal cube will result in a robot climbing down the ladder,merging with the cubes.
TV + the radar tube will result in both maxed out, the radar tube connecting with the TV, making some tv channel :P
The metal pipe + the mountain + the orange ball (also known as the weather pole ) is the trickier part of the game.They will result in the pipe blowing out clouds which cover the mountain.The weather pole will create a sun, and will hereby force the clouds to produce rain.The rain will result in meadows all over the mountain, and that will result in a lake coming from the mountain, covering the pipe, and hereby maxing out the pipe, by creating a forest around it.
The hurricane and the pole with the fan will result in another weird thing.If the hurricane gets big enough, it will go to the fan-pole and try to destroy it.You will recieve about +2000 bonus for it.BUT this is not the good way.You have to get the cog wheel so that the hurricane WONT destroy the pole, instead he'll stay there.
that will result in the next one :
The egg + the weather pole is another combo.The egg will evolve to lvl 6 without any help, but you need the weatherpole to be maxed out in order to get a lvl 7 egg.This is probably the most trickiest one.First of all, you need that egg to evolve into a lvl 6 egg.That means it will be this white fattie.Then you have to create the Hurricane+fan combo, with the cog wheel.There will be a big lightning, and this will cause the WEATHER pole to morph into a weather pole with a moon.(because of the lightning!) .AND THEN! The egg will transform into some weird were-egg.

so far, those were the combos available.You now have to think about WHEN to place WHAT item.The metal cube for example needs 10 evolving steps, so you have to place it SECOND in order to max it + the ladder.Stuff like that, I'll probably post more of my thoughts.stay tuned ;)
so far, byebye mates!
