Yeah, so i thought i would open up a thread for people to discuss what they thought of the movie.
I am not quite certain what I thought about it. As a stand alone movie, it was good. Good story, good action. But as far as being the conclusion to a kick ass movie trillogy, I am not so certain, and here is why:
A lot of the second movie was set up, new characters, new situations, nothing was really resolved. The first 2/3 of the 3rd movie is set up too. A lot of shit is in there that I find unneccessary. Like the Oracle changing bodies, whats with that? Why was that neccessary? And them rescuing Neo from that train station, seems so useless, and it doesn't add any story or action really. And the littel indian girl, who the fuck cares? so damn pointless it makes me a bit sick.
As far as both Neo and Trinity dying, this was a bit to expected to me. I mean, Neo had to marter himself, that was just too odvious. But why did he have to go all the way to the machine city to talk to spikey head thing? He should have been able to connect with the source directly, as the Oracle said he did at the end of reloaded, and negotiate a peace. It seems that them going to the city was just an excuse to be all flashy and shit.
I guess I was just hoping for a more crazy revalation like we got in the 1st one, but I probably had too high expectations.
Ok, I am sure no one will reas all of this, so I will wrap it up now. :)
So as i said, as a stand alone flick it was a good action and drama movie. But as far as the conclusion of the Matrix series, I am a bit dissapointed. But then again, I hated reloaded when I saw it the first time, and loved it when I got the DVD. So when I see it on the IMAX this weekend, my opinions may change.
