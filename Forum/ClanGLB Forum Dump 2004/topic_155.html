Here we go, time for a rundown of what to look for during the gameplay trailer:

- The movie starts off with a hero standing in front of som huge gates. Then fades into an even larger looking town that contains what looks like barracks from warcraft III.

- Then we get to see a taste of the Tauren town.

- It cuts to a pack of orcs on wolfback. The front orc has a cool white beard showing some more cutomizabilities.

- We then go back to our hero on horseback with a group also on horseback. You can see he has a blue glowing sword on his back.

- He is then flying on the back of a Gryphon over a human town.

- We then catch a glimpse of the Night Elf town. The unexplored world. Lined up are night elves with their glowing eyes and one with a leopard or white tiger.

- We then get the title, "LINES ARE DRAWN". With that we are taken into the arena. The worgs seem ready to fight.

- Cuts back to the night elf scene where we hear the holy light sound and a female elfs fists light up. Such a great transition to heroes running up to a huge giant in the battle arena.

- "THE STRENGTH OF MANY", and we cut to alot of fighting scenes. Large battles and some small, but they all look very fun. Not just hit... evade... hit... evade...

- "THE COURAGE OF ONE", where we see our hero again letting his troops know that its time for battle.

- An Ogre is then shown being attacked by a melee fighter. Cool clip as it seems the hero in armor casts a flame spell on the ogre and then swings at the ogre with his sword.

- Cut to another Ogre punching one a group at night.

- Our hero is panned again during the night in a human town.

- We get a frost giant thunder clapping the ground in the arena vs a tauren and his ally.

- Here we catch a glimpse of a battle on a ship. Then it cuts again to a sweet shot of the docks. Blizzard proudly invites us here to wipe the drool off our chin and watch whats in store for us the rest of the movie.

- We see a bit more of the last movie with all the heroes lined up shooting guns and arrows.

- Some summons are shown here: a bear, treant and a leopard.

- Trolls are being fought against during the night.

- We enter a castle or some type of ritual room where it looks like 8 warlocks surround a slaved Orc. As the Orc lets out a cry, the mages all blast him with a spell at the same time. We don't know if this is a buff, or a execution, but this is where my heart stopped.

- Our hero is shown walking into the huge Blackrock Spire. The lava looks so cool dripping from high part inside the spire.

- A world awaits, and our hero is shown staring up into the spire.

- After we get the cool new animated logo, we see our hero flying off on a gryphon, seeking out another adventure.
