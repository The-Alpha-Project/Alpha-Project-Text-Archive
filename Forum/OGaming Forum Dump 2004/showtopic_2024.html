Defenders of Elynn

For what seems like eternity, the Defenders of Elynn have stood guard over the rich wood and forestry. They have not been known to come out into civilization, instead living in the wilds as rangers and keeping the forests safe for the Alliance. One day, as an eager party of warriors trying to get to Stormwind passed through the forest, they were halted by a group of monsters. The monsters destroyed everything in Elwynn, including the Defenders. One man from the human party managed to slip past with only minor injuries. Now he posted a request in all the taverns and popular spots in Stormwind, a group of fighters are needed to extinguish the dangers of Elwynn. If you are interested, you are to see the Elder Mage Liassus Acian.

OOC: The RP evolves from here, I will allow up to 7 (seven) people to take part in the roleplay. Sign up here with the following information:

Here is how the Roleplay will work: I am the GM (GameMaster), and I control all NPC’s, as well as my own character.

Characters introduced in RED are forum-user controlled character's, that are only controlled by the person who signs up for them. They are not to be killed, and can only die if the person controlling them specifies they die within their own RP post.


NPC's

Characters introduced in PURPLE are IMPORTANT NPC's that are only controlled by the GM, or designated players, and cannot be killed without permission of the GM.

Characters introduced in BLUE are NPC's that can be controlled anyone (within reasonable grounds) and can only be killed if it is RP'ed nicely, and the death has a reason.

Characters introduced in GREEN are minor NPC characters which can be killed and/or controlled by anybody. Remember to keep all actions descriptive and important to the story, in some way.

The posters were everywhere in Stormwind. You would pass by flower shops, merchants, taverns, just about anywhere, and the papers would be there:

Vile monsters have plagued Elwynn Forest! Stormwind is looking for a brave group of fighters to tackle the monsters and cleanse the forest! Villagers beware! Do not enter the forest until notification that it is safe to do so.

If you are interested in helping, there is a reward. Find the Mages' Covenant House, down the main street, second floor of the Valarin Property. Come to this place in one week from the first week of this month.


Drexler Asten roamed around the beautiful city of Stormwind, taking in the warm breeze. His robes fluttered in the wind, but it felt good against his hard skin. He walked around, enjoying the architecture and landscape of this land. He remembered the war, of how the Orcs had taken these lands once and how much it took to regain them, and to rebuild them.

In his train of thought, Drexler had been suspended in thought for almost two whole minutes. He snapped out of his day-dream and realized there was several people staring at him.

"Move along now! I was thinking is all" he said irritiably, he was embarassed but he walked on. He thought of the call for fighters, would he do it? He finally decided he wouldn't, such taks were meant for the armies of Stormwind and mercenaries, not for warlocks and adventures like himself. As he passed through the bustling main street, where horses clamped on and merchants yelled through each side of the street, Drexler was becoming a bit stressed. He needed some lively hood in his life, something to spur his love for life and taste for adventure. He pulled out a slip from his pocket, and walked on.

His boots were silent on the paved stones of Stormwind's walkways. He read the note once more, as he stood in front of a three story building, and entered in. He found a desk in the front room, with two gaurds and what seemed some kind of scribe.

"Who are you" pointed a gaurd with his sword.

"No need to be rude, we are all human here. I am Drexler Asten, son of the Wizard Deiseth, and what is to be one of your group. I came on the correct day, did I not?" he replied, handing the second gaurd the note that explained his reason for being there.

"Follow Lupin" gnarled the first gaurd, motioning toward the skinny scribe. The scribe went down the hall, took a left at a door and climbed a steep case of stairs until they reached a grand study. Books lined in shelves along the walls coloured mahogany, with the wooden floors and the bright ceiling - this was an odd room.

"Ah welcome. I am the Archmage, Liassus. I am here to brief you on the situation at Elwynn. I will await for more people to come, and I will talk to you all once they arrive" he said.
