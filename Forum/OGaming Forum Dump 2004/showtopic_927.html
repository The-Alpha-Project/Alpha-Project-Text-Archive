Well, I figured before I cast my judgement I'd recheck the description for ROGUE to see if maybe WoW changed it enough that a dwarf would fit the bill. Here it is:

"At home in the shadows and skilled at disappearing from sight, the Rogues of Azeroth are most comfortable when acting in the background. Twisting events to their favor, striking only when advantage is greatest: this is where a Rogue excels. With their cunning tricks, physical abilities, and mastery of concealment and disguise, Rogues have no trouble finding employment as thieves, cutthroats, spies, and assassins."

After reading that... NO WAY should a dwarf be a rogue. Only thing roguish about a dwarf is (like Squirlypete said) his mechanical abilities.

Other than that though NOTHING fits. Let's take them one at a time shall we?

"At home in the shadows and skilled at disappearing... acting in the background" - a dwarf loves to be upfront and in the action. Not back hiding. Not to mention the fact that a dwarf loves his armor and wouldn't be caught dead without it... how do you do stealth in armor??

"Twisting events to their favor, striking only when advantage is greatest" - a dwarf cares not for odds or such. If he's gonna hew a troll neck he doesn't care if there's 2 trolls or 20 he's going in axe swinging! (my apologies to the trolls out there) :P

"With their cunning tricks, physical abilities, and mastery of concealment and disguise" - I love my dwarves but c'mon... how do you disguise a dwarf?? As a shrub?!

Lastly, being HONORABLE, dwarves would NOT be "thieves, cutthroats, spies, and assassins" Perish the thought! And our history on dwarves by WoW states: "defend their friends and allies with unmatched courage and valor" Doesn't sound like a cutthroat to me!
