Thron Bonesmasher..... an orc that was born in a society of warriors...When this young orc was about the age of twelve, he started to learn about some fighting styles...but his father,Bul'nash, taught Thron that these fighting styles were nothing but nonsense...So his father decided that he would teach Thron the true skill of the warrior. About nine years later, while training with his father, a grunt on a wolf arrived at their hut....The grunt got off his wolf and started walking toward Bul'nash. The grunt pulled out a report and handed it to Bul'nash. He opened it and quickly read it. Suddenly he ran into the hut and strapped on his huge armor, and then went and got his runed axe. Thron went up and asked his father, "Father, are you going some where?". Without a reply, Bul'nash grabbed Thron and brought him to Thorak's Hut (a friend of Bul'nash). He quickly pulled him near the door of the hut and went in. Seconds later, Bul'nash came out the hut and started walking toward the grunt that had the report. Thron tried to call out to his father, but Bul'nash looked at him, then turned back to the grunt. After a few words, Bul'nash was on his wolf and was starting to follow the grunt. Thron had no idea what was going on, but he was worried.
