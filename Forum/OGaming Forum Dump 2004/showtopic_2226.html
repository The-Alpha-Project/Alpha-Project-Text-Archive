I thought it would be a good idea to post some of the important keys and console commands to help those new to the WoW Beta. CHECK BACK FREQUENTLY BECAUSE THIS IS CONSTANTLY UPDATED!!!

C - Opens up Character Info and Paperdoll
P - Opens up Spellbook and Abilities
O - Opens up the Social menu that you can use to find people and add friends to your buddy list.
L - Opens up your Quest Log
M - Opens up Map, right click on map will zoom out and allow you to select another zone if you wish.

B - Opens up your main backpack
Shift B - Opens up all your bags

X - Sit
Z - Pulls out weapon

/p - talk in party chat
/g - talk in guild chat
/tell <player name> - sends a private message to that player
/r - allows you to reply to your last tell message.

NumLock - Turns on 'autorun' so you can type while you're running and save your hand from cramping. But look where you're going, you still have to steer.

Alt Z - Removes UI (good for screenshots)

Ctrl R - Shows framerate (fps)

Shift C - Removes the combat log (lower right side)

PrintScreen - Takes screenshot

Shift Click (on item) - If you hit Enter to open up your chatline and Shift click on an item, you will be able to link an item through chat. This will produce a [item] on the chatline.

Shift Click (on quest) - If you hit Enter to open up your chatline and Shift click on a quest in your quest log, you will copy the name of the quest to the chatline. Although it does not link the quest, it does make it easier to share what quest you are working on with a teammate.

/bug - This will bring up the bug form to enter a bug. Along with your report, it will also send a screenshot of what you are looking at and your coordinates.

/suggest - This will bring up the suggestion form. Use this make any suggestions to enchance the game. This does not send a screenshot or record your coordinates.

To chat in General Channel - /1 <message>
To chat in Trade Channel - /2 <message>

How chat channels work is like this. The first channel you join is 1 and the next is 2, then 3, and so on. You always join general first then trade when you log on or go to another zone. To leave a channel just type /leave 1 or /leave 2.

Console Commands
----------------

profanityfilter 0 - Turns off the profanity filter. Changing the 0 to 1 will turn it back on
