Name: Feniks
Race: Night Elf
Class: Demon Hunter / Necromancer
Weapon: 2 runic swords

Feniks is young or a night elf but that doesn't mean he's weak. From the day he was born he had a talent to the dark arts so when he grow up he decided to become a Demon Hunter. When he ended his trying he left kalimndor in search for a adventure. He met many strong warriors and demons on his way but no one ever defeated him except a necromancer called Kel'thuzad. Fighting alone versus a army of undead under the control of Kel'thuzad Feniks had no other chose but to run. He was thinking long about this fight and he came to only one thing- "To defeat a Necromancer you would have to have a bigger army then he has, but there are more dead then the living, the power of the necromancy comes from the ground, from all the dead that are underground, I must fight fire with fire". From that moment Feniks started to learn necromancy. Having learned all that the human race knows about necromancy Feniks set of to Kalimndor to help his brothers and sisters to fight with the plague and the demons. He wanted to join Furion in his fight for the World tree but he and the other druids knew about his necrotic powers so he was banned form kalimndor just like Illidan. Knowing he could not fight with his brethren he razed his own army of dead and when the World Tree was under attack he was sending waves of his own troops to weaken and hold of Arhimonde's attacks. After the destruction of Arhimonde and the Burning Legion Feniks went of on a new journey, he went of to kill all the demons that survived the destruction of the legion and to get stronger before attacking the frozen throne and the Lich King.


Sorry for my grammar but I am still learning English, I hope you all like it :D
