Well my main character will be a Warlock for the purpose that they are a versatile character.
They can scout using the 'Eye of Kilrog'. Also they have the ability to counter the other Classes. Using many curses and fear spells it can counter almost every other class. Also it has differing pets for different situations. The Felhunter for enemy casters, Succubus for fragile enemies(priest), Voidwalker for taking on strong enemies, and the Imp for just general blasting of enemies. The Imp is good because it doesn't take any soul shards to create. So this is a good summon to kill minor creatures to get soul shards.
Also their are various stones that can be created. Soul stones for ressurection, Health stones(obvious what they do), Fire stones that are held in your off hand that add fire damage to your weapon, and Spell stones that are used to lower the effect of direct damage of damage over time spells.
The Warlock also has the ability to heal its mana and health during combat using direct damage spells that drain(Death Coil) or drains like Mana Drain and Health Drain.
I think the Warlock is good for every situation which is why i have chosen it.
