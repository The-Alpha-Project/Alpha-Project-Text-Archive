The rain came down hard on the soldiers that day as they treaded the countryside. All on alert as they walked slowly down an old dirt road. The ruined and deserted French homes lay in piles of rubble, mangled and bloodied bodies lay scattered throughout the street and grass. Tension was running high in the band, they held they're NO 4 rifles a little tighter and continued walking.

At the front of the group a man named John Graham lead the pack. He had seen almost three years of this war considering that he went off to help the British at the start of this god-awful war. Considering that they had been walking four five consecutive hours he held up hand and stopped his band and then quickly said "We will rest and eat here for a few minutes" John was a taller man about 6"0, he had brown hair and hazel eyes. He had many scars and his biceps where bigger than some men’s arms. He had seen many battles and was very wise and quick acting. He was a Sergeant and a good one for that matter.

The silence broke, "Damn it man, I wish it would stop bloody raining, my feet are soaked." A few of the soldiers laughed "Suck it up princess your feet are going to be wet for a long time." After pulling out what little rations they had they quickly ate and in no time were ready to leave. Just as they were heading out one man named Fred said, "Hold up Sarg let me have a quick smoke." Smokes were hard to come by when your walking the country side and soon a few other men asked if they could get one good puff out of it. After lighting it up and inhaling deeply he passed it on "Man that’s good..." Fred said has he exhaled. Another man quickly took a breath and the last man finished it off. Then a gunshot pierced the air and the sound of a bullet impaling into something rang out. The man with the cigarette had a whole the size of small tangerine in his neck. Blood gushed out, his artery had been shot and a he emitted a slow gurgle as blood squirted on his uniform and slid down the wall he was leaning on. This all happened in a matter of second and Fred yelled out "Sniper!" Quickly scattering John thought "There has to been Germans in this town, no sniper would camp out in the middle of this waste land"

Quickly jumping behind a slab of stone he scanned the area. There was a bell tower just north of where the man got shot. There was a glint as light hit the snipers scope. He was hiding in the bell tower for sure. He tried to aim his NO 4 but he couldn’t keep it steady and the wind would blow the bullet of course and it would give away his position. John shouted, "Take cover and stay out of his crosshairs! We will figure out a way to block his sight so we can run for it!"

The rain dripped down his helmet onto his face. This would be hard to overcome for sure. The Germans had to of heard that shot...
