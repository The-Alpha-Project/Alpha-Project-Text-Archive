Another unique Role Playing opportunity, this is a chance not to play a traveling mage, warrior, thief, etc. Don’t get me wrong they are welcome but the main point of this RP is to play the part of a Noble House where the powerful run their business and subterfuge. Where servants whisper and gossip, where bribes are accepted in the shadowed corners of taverns. Backstabbing, double-dealing and Murder abound. Corrupt Houses attempt to overthrow the honest ones and the twisted web of lies and deceit is so thick it sometimes takes a quick wit and a knife with the luck of the gods themselves to survive. All this is rolled up in a bundle of civility and law that shields the fools and the naive.

In this situations the merchant house Ulvrith makes it’s fortune Korbin the current head is a cunning, and shrewd business man with pale skin dark eyes and hair the color of onyx he leads the house on it’s quest for riches. House Ulvrith is an old house that dates back to the founding of Stormwind Castle they where among the wealthiest of Houses then but the fall of Azeroth and the ransacking of Stormwind destroyed House Ulvrith and it’s assets reducing them to poverty. They fled to Lordaeron with the rest of the refugees. Korbins Greatgrandfather Tathalm started the long path to restoring the glory of their house and he succeeded building their fortune off of Shipping, exploiting the commerce of the high seas they grew in rank and station quickly, even the second war against the Orcs did nothing to deter their rise. Then the Lich Kings Scourge came, but Korbins father was a wise man and pulled up roots, escaping with their assets to the rebuilt castle of Stormwind, their they restored the ruins of their ancient manor home and established themselves as a power in Commerce. Their ships brave the high seas and their Caravans travel the lands, trading even with the Dwarves in their high mountain peaks, Korbin even passes the time buying and selling land.

The House Crest: A flying raven with a balance scale clutched in it's talons.

So friends anyone is welcome to join the house I have created for my Character Korbin we are if you haven’t gathered based in Stormwind, but if you want to create a Dwarven Merchant House or Gnomish Trades INC. it’s up to you, Korbin could always use a Wife, Heir, Second Son or Daughter, you could be a servant or guard of the household or even... the Butler!

Remember don't feel constrained in your choices a wandering rouge is an ideal assasin and a noble warrior a welcome bodyguard.... or.. whatever.
