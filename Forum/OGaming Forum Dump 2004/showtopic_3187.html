Priests are more like monks or nons in our world. The Alliance Priests worship and believe and use the Holy Light. The center of their power was once Northshire Abbey, but it was destroyed during the First War by the Horde. Many of them became more adept in war to better protect the people from the orcs and became Paladins, Uther Lightbringer was the first of them to be blessed by the Archbishop. So now the Church is split up into the Silver Hand and the priesthood. The Priest have a sort of community, mostly abbeys or cloisters and cathedrals can also be found troughout Azeroth and Lordearon.
The priests of warcraft are in a sence the perfect Religious group, sort of like the Catholic ideals but without the corruption and all the bad things they did in our Middle Ages. They are devoted to the good cause and healing of the people.

The undead priest however are a different story.
