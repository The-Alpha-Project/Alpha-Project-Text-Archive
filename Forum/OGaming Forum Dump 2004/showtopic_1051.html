Here ya go, post your character requests up if you want a chance to be drawn up and displayed in the gallery. Thing to include in your request should be:

Name:
Race:
Appearace:
Personality:
Weapons:
Pets:

And that's about it. Now who I'm going to choose is up to me, so there. And note that I'm not going to do every single request here, so once in a while one.

Bribes work. :D

Felt obliged to throw in a few guidelines to the requests...more like tips on increasing your chances of your request being granted. Follow at your own risk.

The more description on appearance, the higher the chance. Same thing goes for history, personality and likes and dislikes (especially likes and dislikes). Hobbies and other little details are welcomed. Of course, there are a few requests out there that doesn't even specify a gender...yeeeaaah...I would love a bit more detail on those basic description please...

Characters that I am familiar with gets brownie points...aka characters in the RP forums...characters that are in the same RP thread as the family elf gets a higher bonus (characters who suffers from the family elf has an even higher bonus)...though if I really abide by that law a certain dreadlord should've gotten his pic a loooooong time ago...this just proves the randomosity of X...

The more creative the character, the higher the chances of being drawn...yes, make it interesting or it won't catch my eye.

Different characters that relates to each other or in some way has a similarity may be smashed together in the same picture. Examples include those that hail from the same RP thread, classes, races...etc etc...or just anything little thing that might just link them up together. For example, a playboy character might be drawn with a hot babe character....and such...Just like the pic I just finished right now...yeah, more about the pic later.

Note that your choice of appearance may be altered to suit artistic purposes...because after reviewing a few requests out there, some choice colours just clash...very very badly. -_- Sorry on that.
