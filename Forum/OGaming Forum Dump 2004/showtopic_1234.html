I love to discuss possible races. I think it could look something like this in a future expansion.

Expansion: Adds
- Northrend and Draenor
- 1 new race to existing factions
- 1 new faction

Alliance: High Elves (or) Pandaren
Horde: Ogres (or) Goblins
Outlanders: Blood Elves, Naga, Draenei, Fel Orcs, Nerubians

Other candidates I thought of for the Outlanders' fourth and fifth races include other aces from Northrend. Considering Northrend has been ravaged so much by the undead, anyone who still lives there is going to want revenge.

High Elves
High elves do still exist. The elves that followed Jaina have not been corrupted.
Problem: They are different enough from night elves to be there own race, but maybe not different enough from blood elves.

Pandaren
Pandaren used to be the night elves' old friends, and the tauren's old rivals. It would be fitting for them to join their old friends to fight their old rivals.
Problem: They seem to keep to themselves and cherish peace as opposed to fight in wars.

Ogres
My favorite. Warcraft III manual and Manual of Monsters says they aren't stupid at all, infact, they are smart, warlocks are the only ogres with two heads, and they are forming together and are watching others to make their own government.
Problems: They might be too big. Not many problems here.

Goblins
A widely loved race. They have a great place in Warcraft history and used to be allied with the horde.
Problems: It can be seen and Blizzard stated that they are already too widely used as a neutral race for it to be an easy race to make playable.

Blood Elves & Draenei
Both good humanoid races with roots going back to Warcraft II.
Problems: ?

Naga & Nerubians
They both hate the undead. The Nerubians have about the same story as the Blood Elves: Undead ravage homeland, survivors want revenge.
Problems: Neither of these are bipedal. That can be worked out though. Their racial ability could be natural armor for instance.

Fel Orcs
Illidan enslaved these when he took over Draenor. They would probably listen to him. In other words, they would help the faction.
Problems: Too close to orcs?
