The Wildlife Faction

Wildlife Mounts

Haresabres
In the lands of Pyrorm, savage mutated beasts walk the lands and sure enough, Haresabres are deffinatly included. Haresabres are mutated from the regular rabbit to be more large, faster, stronger and exchanged preditors for prey. The Rabbrith tame these wild creatures to ride upon and are very faithfull to their Rabbrith riders.

Mutadiles
Yet another mutaded creature, Mutadiles grew larger and grew sharper fangs but the thing that changed the most was their leg size and stregnth. Mutadiles gained suprising speeds making them good mounts to the Basiliski. It was hard to tame but they tamed them and are comonly mounted by Basiliski.

Wildgryphs
The colorfull combination of parrot and drake and also the cousin of Gryhpons and Hippogryhs originate from the savage lands of Pyrorm. They are often rainbow colored and are the only predator to the Haresabres. Avians tame these creatures and have shared them with the other races for good and quick air transportation.

Class Abilities

New Classes

Monk Humans, Undead, Trolls, Rabbrith, Basiliski
Warriors who lost the weapons and armor for martial arts and formadable speed. They use almost no armor but dont need it. Rabbrith where the founders of thos class and have mastered it but recently, the other factions adapted to this class aswell.

Reaver Humans, Orcs, Trolls, Undead, Basiliski
Formerly Death Knights who excaped the Burning Legion taking their magic with them. Undead recently tought the horde about the Reavers and trained them. Other races learned this class and have been using it ever since.

Necromancer Humans, Trolls, Undead, Necromancer
Also a class broken free from the Burning Legion. The Forsaken tought the horde this class aswell and the other races learned, studied and tought this class to others to use it against their foes.

Battleseer Orcs, Tauren, Trolls, Rabbrith, Basiliski
Although not shamanic, Rio, the leader of the Rabbrith, was the first who created the mighty Battleseer that used the power of the spirits to aid them. All shamanic races including the Rabbrith adapted to this class to defeat the more civilized alliance.

Tempist Humans, Night Elves, Orcs, Tauren, Trolls, Rabbrith, Basiliski, Avian
A knight who has learned the mage magic and the shamanic magic while also wearing heavy armor and deadly weapon and shield skills all rolled into one. Seemingly overpowered, they arent as good with magic as Shamans or Mages but arent as good with the Shield or Weapon skill as the warrior is either.

Basiliski Campaign WC3: Reign of Chaos
Journey to Survival

Ch 1, Scrounging in Stranglethorn
The prophet flies to the jungles of Stranglethorn to warn the Basiliski that the demons are returning but unlike the orcs, they must journey to Pyrorm. Heeding his warning, the Basiliski and the young leader of the Basiliski, Uro, escorts their brethren aroung Stranglethorn and freeing the captured Basiliski from the trolls. Uro gathers them and they head to southern Stranglethorn Vale.

Ch 2, Excape from Stranglethorn
Uro must get to the east coast with his Basiliski and invade the nearby troll encampment and take their boats and leave these lands for good but Uro's army is too little so they must establish a base and invade the trolls camp and steal their ships while they also must free Vili, the young shamaness from the trolls.

Ch 3, A New World
On the shores of the new land, the Basiliski gather the other Basiliski and must explore the island and start an encampment so they can prepare for the dangers ahead. Later, the encampment is attacked by the native Draks and the Basiliski follow them to their base where they create an invasion.

Interlude, An Alliance Is Made
After destroying the Drak's camp, the Basiliski discuss about the Draks as from out of the forest, rabbitmen and their leader meet and greet the Basiliski and the rabbitmen compliment the Basiliski's strengnth and that they would like an ally like the Basilisks so they ally with them and the rabbitmen intoduce themselves as Rabbrith.

Ch 4, Taming Nature
The Basiliski wonder inland with the Rabbrith where they set up a base and must tame the wildlife in the area if they are to defeat the Drak. Uro and Vili go to the pond areas and tame the mutated Aligators known as Mutadiles. Rio the Warseer gather their loyal mounts, the Haresabre. Giant sized fanged rabbits that are omnivours. They gather these wildlife creatures and head to the center of the jungle. Uro and Vili are now seen riding mounts.

Ch 5, Avian's Assistants
The Basiliski and the Rabbrith create a base unknowingly in the Avian territory where they attack and you must defend your base. Later, Uro, Vili and Rio meet the Avian leader, Feathron. Uro and Vili discuss they are from a land called Azeroth that was being overtaken by trolls and orcs however, Rio discussed to them that the Avian are evil bird men and must be slain. Uro and Vili try to recruit the Avians to their cause and try to create alliances between the Avian and the Rabbrith.

Ch 6, The Foothills
The Basiliski and the Avians head to the foothills of the Lajamag Mountains where they must gather their kind and create a fortified fortress and 3 Wildgryph Carriers to escort them up the steep hills and get to the pass leading to Lajamag Mountains to destroy the city of Draks but they must destroy the fortress of the Drak guarding the pass. The Rabbrith split up with them to get more recruits and help invade the Drak.

Interlude, War Upon Great Mt. Magmala Volcano
In the Drak capitol city, the Drak lords talk about the invasion of the Avians but discussing that they brought a new race to the land. While talking, they also discuss the ressurection of Silithinious, demon brother of Mannoroth and creator of the Drak. They say, we must send our armies out to get those puny opposers and capture their leaders so we can sacrifice them and summon our demon lord!

Ch 7, Rallying The Army
Upon the Lajamag Mountains right by the pass, Uro and Vili split up their clans upon the mountains and prepare for the Drak assault to the northeast. Uro's group known as the Moonsabre Tribe and Vili's newly made clan called the Warfury Tribe and Feathron's group called the Avian Expedition must fend off the Drak for 30 minutes until Rio's group of Rabbrith from the north come in to save the day but while they defeat all of the Drak, the Drak run off with Vili which enrages Uro.

Ch 8, All Your Base Are Belong To Us
Uro, Rio, Feathron and their armies come up to the base of Magmala Volcano and they must find the entrance but the mountains are full of Drak so they must establish mini bases and must create Wildgryph Carriers to escort them up the mountain and reach the cavern entrance.

Ch 9, The Burning Legion
In the caverns just outside the Drak capitol city, the blue and yellow dreadlords appear and explore the caverns in search of the three races. They must defeat the dreadlords in their way, all of the hero altars and must go up to the Drak demon gate where they see Vili fully sacrificed and they dissapear without a trace. Uro cries in rage and pledges that he will get revenge for Vili's death so leads their allies out and to the top of Great Mt. Magmala Volcano

Ch 10, Summoning Silithinious
At the top of Great Mt. Magmala Volcano, Uro and friends with their already fully upgraded base must send their armies to the top of the mountain while destroying the Drak before 30 minutes pass or the volcano will erupt and summon Silithinious and the races will all die with the flowing lava.

Cinematic, Battle Atop Great Mt. Magmala Volcano
Uro and his allies meet Tyranilious and slay him but they are too late. Silithinious is brought back and he begins the demon eruption which knocks the heroes off and in failure, they head back to the jungle. Atop of the mountain, Silithinious does an evil laugh and fire rages around him.
