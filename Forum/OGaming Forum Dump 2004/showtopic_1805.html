wcICE...a long name of mine from war3 is going to be the basis on my WoW Charater. My charaters past will be described here.



During the second war. A powerful warrior nicknamed ICE came to a fall of death after being killed by an orcish soldier. He didnt make it completely to death, but instead got caught in the undead. Turning against his own soldiers. For the final 40% of the war, he was under undead rule. This driving him deeper and deeper into the crave for evil.
Thus came the day when on a raid with the undead, a paladin did a holy light spell, that turned him and a few other undead units back to their life......it was basicly resurect. After being restored the the soldiers normal form, he kept his crave for evil. It just kept dwelling deeper and deeper inside of him, until one day, he was finnaly driven mad. He killed a mage. He then fled to the Woods of Azeroth. He kept becoming stronger and stronger in the wild, and was eventualy turned into a savage beast living off the land. When finnaly came the day, when he just looked and acted like a pure orc. Which he was immediatly accepted in and now lives his life as an orc. Though still having strong connections to the undead and their practices. He follows the way of the orcs, and has been known to have killed wondering mages in the forest.
