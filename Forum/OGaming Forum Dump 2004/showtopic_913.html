Cama Zotz had been bitten by a lone vampire two nights ago while he was walking through the streets late at night. Cama had been in a 'vampiric coma' most of this time. Once he awoke, he remembered nothing from his past...

It was raining hard and the wind was so heavy it bent large trees as it moved across them. Cama noticed chages in his body. Mentally and physically. He had a grown very pale, almost translucent. He felt very paranoid, confused and despondant. His eyes redened, and iris became hyperdilated, making him almost blind in light. His sense of smell and hearing was increased by more than 20%. His teeth werent pointed, but just longer. Cama had an idea about fileing them.

Cama stayed in his house for the next few days. Sleeping from morn to noon, and found himself streaching his muscles for no reason at all. He also noticed his body was getting a lot colder.

Cama wasnt eating at all for the few days he was 'readying' for something. Not knowing exactly what, but knowing it was important.

A growl was heard in Camas stomach around 11:30 pm, and he knew he had to get something to eat. Mindlessly he walked outside into the dark. He wandered the streets untill he found a person. He hid by a house door and waited untill the man came out of the street lights. As soon as he did, Cama sprinted at him and jumped. He dug his nails into his neck and felt the warm blood pour out. Cama put his other hand over the mans mouth to muffle his screams of terror. He put his pale lips to the bleeding wound and slowly licked the blood from his neck. He could taste the blood instantly.

When Cama was satisfyed, he stuffed the body into a nearby garbage can and ran home.

During the day Cama hard a knock on the door. He walked up and opened it, and was blinded by the light...
