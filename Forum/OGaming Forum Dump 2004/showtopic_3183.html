In depth:



Warrior - The tank, and an essential in any group. Provides some damage, soaks most incoming damage and has some group abilities.

Paladin - Can tank extra if needed, has healing and auras, as well as an additional ressurecter if the Priest falls. I love Paladins in my group.

Mage - Crowd control, damage, AoE, root, everything!

Priest - Massive healing, some buffs/debuffs, ressurecter. Essential.

Druid - I love the buffs druid give, as well as even more healing, and I think helps the group function faster/easier.
