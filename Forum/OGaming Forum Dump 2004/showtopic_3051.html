Also remember that the the majority of the Kaldorei were very haughty and arrogant prior to the War of the Ancients. The coming of the Legion and the Night Elves' subsequent alliance with the dragons and other races of the world did much to change their attitudes. In addition, the dwarves as the modern world knows them did not exist in this time; the majority of their ancestors were still sleeping beneath the earth (see: BLizzard's history pages for more info.) As a result, the few dwarf ancestors that were known to the Kaldorei were probably rather primitive anyway. Coupled with the Kaldorei's arrogance, it's no wonder they looked down on the dwarves. I don't expect that to greatly affect their modern relations though.
