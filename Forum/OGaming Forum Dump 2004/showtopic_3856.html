Blizzard Entertainment has stated that you will be able to create both Horde and Alliance characters on only non-PvP servers. On PvP servers however, you must create characters that are part of the same faction (all Horde or all Alliance).

These two factions will NEVER be able to group together 'officially'. There are a number of circumstances where an Alliance character will 'hang-out' with a Horde character in neutral zones (ie - Tanaris) and help eachother out.

As for trading between the two... I believe Kerry answered that with the neutral Auction House in Gadgetzan (the Goblin city within Tanaris) There is no way to trade one on one with the opposite faction as you would be able to your own faction.

The reason that Blizzard has implemented such interaction restrictions between the two factions (ie Language Barriers, Inability To Group) is not to 'blow monkey chunks' ;), but more rather to support the story of the Warcraft universe and immerse the player in the amazing realm that Blizzard has created.
