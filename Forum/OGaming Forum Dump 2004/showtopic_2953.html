Rejoyce Europe ... Blizzard announces that European Beta Sign ups are near.

GFraizer:
Following the announcement of the European World of Warcraft team, we are pleased to report that the sign-up period for the European Beta Test will soon begin.

A link to the European beta test application will be provided at www.blizzard.co.uk (UK English), www.blizzard.de (German), www.blizzard.fr (French). If you live in Europe and you’re interested in applying to join the European Beta Test for World of Warcraft, please stay tuned to one of those Web sites for more information.
