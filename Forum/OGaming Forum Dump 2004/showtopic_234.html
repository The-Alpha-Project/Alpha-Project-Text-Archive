One night, a lonesome Night Elf hunter name NightSoldier, was awoken by a terrible wind in the house of which he lived in. The leaves of the forest dancing in the air in front of him. He thought a storm might be coming... and then a big thundering loud noise was heard right above him. He left his house which was in an uprooted part of the Wispy Wallow Tree. The branches were being torn down by thunder! The bark was on fire and all he heard was screams from the entire tree in which all his community was in. All he could do is stare, he was in shock.... the tree was almost in complete cinders until he seen his mother lying on the ground... halfway out of the window in a broken down branch. She looked up, and in her last words she said... go to your fathers room and look for the secret door under the carpet... there you will.. find...............

As NightSoldier looked at his dead mothers face... all he could think about is why, why did nature have to be so cruel to its friends. I then lept into the air and was off to my fathers room... only thinking why the worst thing that could ever happen, happened, my families death...
I went into the torn down room and entered through the secret passage. It was only a hallway which leaded to an antique armoire... through the glass doors of the armoire was 2 bladed weapons and 1 long bow.. both enscripted with the name "NightSoldier". My fathers name was also NightSoldier and those weapons belong to him. But I thought that he would have left his prize possesions to me so I took them and ran off.

THE NEXT MORNING IN AN ORC CAMP
In the orcish camps that very morning, a warchief that goes by the name, Gorontuf Spellglaze, woke up from a terrible nightmare, it seemed so real.It was about the Alliance attacking the horde the following night, during the time that the orcs would hunt for food. Gorontuf felt it was actually going to happen. He quickly thought about the humans past... but then the present. He believed in his heart that the humans would not hurt them, after all they did stand side by side fighting off the burning legion. The day went by fast and the orcs were getting hungery, so Gorentuff sent a few raiders to go catch some fish. The raiders didn't come back so 2 hours later Gorontuf left with some mercenaries..AN AMBUSH! Some humans were attacking with arrows from trees and bushes. The arrows pierced almost right through the orcs' shields! But a witch doctor came out of nowhere and froze the mischievous humans. They were 15-18 year old boys! The orcs held them captive and may release them if they sworn that they did not kill the raiders.
