Well, guess I had to get out of lurk mode for this. *chuckles*

Well, firstly, unless you pose this question in the EQ2 forum as well, you are mainly going to get a one-sided response. This is the WoW forum after all and people aren't posting here if they wanted to play another game.

Yes, SOE seems to have problems (by the way, it's SOE that runs EQLive, not Verant anymore). But like all companies they are in it for the money. There would be no reason to run a business if there is no profit involved. I do agree that Blizzard seems to have things more under control but they are not perfect. This will be their first MMORPG and this is their shot in the dark; their risk to see if they can actually pull this off and make this game last for years.

I played the Stress Test for WoW. I found it amusing and fun while it lasted. But ultimately, it just wasn't my cup of tea. Since the beta is still going on and the NDA is probably still in effect I cannot state what I did or what went on during that week or so of playtime but in my personal opinion, I wouldn't choose to play it unless EQ2 flopped. And even then I would only play it until Vanguard came out which by the way, has some of the original devs from EQLive when it was still under Verant.

Though I agree that WoW will have new quests, new content, and a new world to explore, EQ2 will as well. It's a separate game just sharing the origins of the lore.

And about the different gaming companies spying on each other. Well, I wouldn't call it spying persae. Each company I'm sure builds their game upon the strengths of others and learns from the mistakes. That's how a game really gets good. They pay attention and pick things up.

But overall, make your own decision. Don't let the biases of either side affect what you want to play. Do your own research. Go to the offical websites of both. They have a ton of info. Make sure their features are something that you would be interested in. They both also have forums. You can easily judge the overall community by just browsing.

I for one, have so far played both games in beta (I don't think stating this goes against the NDA, if it does, mods please edit). I have made my decision for now. Whether that changes in the future is yet to be determined.

My two cents on the matter. *shrugs*
