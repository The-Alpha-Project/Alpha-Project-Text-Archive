Name:Rogrim Thalidon

Age: 50

Gender:Male

Race: Human

Short history:Rogrim was born during the first war in the 7 towers in lower Lorderan. Rogrim's father was one of the 7 lords that ruled these towers. Here Rogrim was trained in the arts of swordsmanship, and in the ways of the knight. When Rogrim reached the age of 17 all that the warriors of the towers could teach him he had learned so it was at this time that he set of to venture the wide world gaining experience and serving the king. When Rogrim was 38 the ill news that the Towers of his birth were under attack reached him, He rode to his home with all the haste he could muster.

He arrived in the heat of battle 5 towers already lost, the fighting continued for 40 days and nights untill at last the final tower had fallen. The few warriors who survived the battle guided the women and children to safety. Once they had made it safely to Storwind Rocklan, Rogrim's cousin, handed him a long wrapped package. Rogrim took it carefully and inside he discovered his fathers sword. Laneiathen, as the blade was called, was forged long ago by the finest craftsmen of Ironforge along with six others, one for each tower's lord. Each of this swords was unique for the family it was forged. The only thing they had in common was each had an inscription, telling about the house for which it was created. Laneiathen, was inscribed, in ancient darnassiun: By Blood, and by Honor, this blade shall vanquish all evil, in the name of the house of Thalidon. The one other item in the package was a short letter: My son Though our nation may fall our blood flows strong in your veins, carry our name proudly and bring it glory. Live your life by honor, I await you in the land of fore fathers. May honor guide your blade. Drugrim Thalidon.

Rogrim now spends his time wandering the lands in his continual battle against evil, and when he is called upon will fight for the king and his homeland he is their to lead his brothers into battle. Over the years he has met many honorable men and women and is respected amongst his kin and their elven and dwarven allies.

Character Personality- Highly values honor and glory. Enjoys spending time with freinds singing dancing and sharing storys of war. Rogrim Thalidon Will NEVER turn down a mug of ale. Doesnt share much of his past with anyone but family and close comrades. In his spare time he likes to write drinking songs and write or read lore.

Class/Profession: Ranger.

Possesions:
Rogrim is a master of the blade and is unmacthed with Laneiathen in battle. The sword is heavy and two handed some men cant even pick it up. This blade has the unique power of being able to channel the Light.

Rogrim has a white clydesdale named Shadowbane. Shadowbane is exceptionally smart for a horse and always ready to aid Rogrim.


Besids his sword Rogrim has his enchanted cloak that makes him blend in to his surroundings, making him all but invisible. He has a red bandana tied around his head to keep the hair out of his eyes and forehead. Rogrim wears a heavy chestplate like the rest of his kin.

Physical description: Rogrim Is a Hardened figure with broad shoulders and long graying hair and beard. He is muscled with experience and is almost 7 feet tall. He is twice as wide as many men. He carries scars from old battles and has deep blue gold eyes.

Name: Znish Derff

Class: Engineer (No equivilent class in the game)

Race: Gnome

Age: 57

Story: Znish Derff was born in the heyday of Gnomeragen and the Gnome people when it's technological advancments surpassed the wildest dreams of other races. His Father was an accomplished Mage and his Mother a skilled Herbalist, they operated a Lucrative little shop in one of the upper Tiers of the city where they cattered to the rich Enginerring households of the city's elite. As a child Znish was always eccentric but was also bright and creative, he had a technological skill that easily surpassed his parents wildest. Znish had a reckless side though that neighbors uneasily attributed to the psychotic inventors usually found among the Goblins. This didn't stop his parents from enrolling him in the most eminant Engineering school in the city though, and by the time the Troggs came and shattered the peace of the Gnomes ancient home, Znish had achieved the exalted rank of Master Engineer. It was rumored to be Znish and some of his like minded collegues among the Master Engineers to put forward the idea of a chemical strike against the Troggs. As it is well documented, the plan was a terrible failure. Hundreds of Gnomes died in the resulting chemical fallout, and though Znish was not found at fault for the tragedy it was a dark dark stigma on his persona.

Time has passed since then and Znish Derff is desperate to gain back the respect of his collegues and prove to the world his Genius, it has long been his goal to somehow fuse the arcane secrets of Magic and Technology. The weapons of war he could create with such secrets would make him famous, rich and more importantly respected. Now armed with his dangerous inventions and a burning desire to prove the world wrong he is on a mad misson to find an ancient cache of Magestone.
