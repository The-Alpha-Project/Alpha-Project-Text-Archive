I am going to run a mini-contest to promote more posting in this forum. People are asked to create their own faction in WoW. The format goes as follows:

*Every faction must have a name
*Every faction must have 4 races
*Every faction must have a unique class
*Every faction must have a region to start in
*Justification must be given for that faction.

An example (Ideas may be taken, as this is a popular one):

-The Forsaken-
Races: Draenei, Naga, Blood Elves, Demons
Unique Class: Mentalist - Specializes in mind control, hypnosis with minor healing abilities and debuffs.

The Forsaken start in Outland, accessable only when the Dark Portal is active.

====

Factions can be comprised of anything you think of that exists. Pandarians, Goblins, anything in WoW!

***The Federation of United Creeps***

Races: Gnolls, Kobolds, Ogres and Forest Trolls
Unique Class: Brute

Gnolls: The general race (human/orc-idea) can choose most classes, and has the biggest city, build secretly on the ruins of Alterac in the Alterac Mountains. The Federation was their idea, as the Gnoll clans had recently united themselves yet needed some other strong allies.
Warrior, Hunter, Mage, Warlock, Rogue, Brute

Kobolds: Taking the position of small, yet witty allies of the Gnolls, their allegiance was not much of a choice as the Kobolds have always been used as slaves of the Gnolls. They have recently discoverd great potential when allowed freedom and the once stupid race has become powerful in the arts of magic and engineering. They have a vast tunnel city in the mountains of Redridge where they ever undermine Alliance plans.
Warrior, Mage, Warlock, Rogue, Druids

Ogres: The one headed Ogres of the Stonemaul clan have turned against the Horde after the departure of Rexxar and have in stead joined the gnolls in their rogue Federation. Settled in the Dustwallow Marshes they have dealings with orcs and trolls every day, yet their tough race survives.
Warrior, Mage and Warlock ( you'll need to be blue, sorry) Brute

Trolls: The green Forest Trolls of Zul'aman were left defenseless when they stepped out of the Horde and were easy prey for the Elves of Silvermoon and the Scourge and Forsaken not much later. In need of Allies they understood that the Alliance or Horde would never accept them and appeared to the gnolls in request of a union. Deep in the giant woods of Zul'aman they house in a great city where arenas and ziggurats dominate the skyline.
Warrior, Hunter, Priest, Mage, Warlock, Rogue


Brute: Combining great self-buffing with strenght, the Brute is a worthy opponent to all. Unlike the warrior he does not have great offensive abilities in battle, his powers lie in making himself better.


So this is my great faction, good idea, huh!?!

The Outlanders (or The Fallen)
Races: Naga, Blood Elves, Draenei, Nerubians
Unique Class: Spell Breaker (wears Plate, anti-caster)

Start Locations:
Naga - Broken Isles, South of Maelstrom
Blood Elves - Hellfire Penninsula, Draenor
Draenei - Hellfire Penninsula, Draenor
Nerubians - The Frozen Coast, Northrend

Capitals:
Naga - Nazjatar (ocean floor under the Maelstrom)
Blood Elves - Black Citadel
Draenei - Black Citadel
Nerubians - Ruins of Azjol-Nerub?

Classes:
Naga - Warrior, Mage, Hunter, Rogue, Priest
Blood Elves - Warrior, Spell Breaker, Runemaster, Mage, Warlock, Priest
Draenei - Warrior, Rogue, Hunter, Priest, Druid
Nerubians - Warrior, Spell Breaker, Mage, Rogue, Warlock

After the defeat at the Icecrown Glacier, Illidan and his allies tried to flee to safety. The forces of the Scourge had them surrounded. Suddenly, the Nerubian surivors still loyal to their fallen kingdom of Azjol-Nerub burst forth from teh ground and helped the others escape. Once fleeing to the relative safety of the northern coast of Lordaeron, Illidan thanked his insect saviors and joined an alliance with them. Their home also lost to them, they fled through a portal to Draenor with their new allies.

Although these races have shakey relationships, they band together out of the need for survival...and REVENGE on all those that have destroyed what they hold dear.

Naga hate Night Elves
Blood Elves hate Scourge and Humans
Draenei hate Orcs
Nerubian hate all Undead (but mainly Scourge)

PS >
Non-unique Class: Runemaster (available to Dwarves, Tauren, Blood Elves)
