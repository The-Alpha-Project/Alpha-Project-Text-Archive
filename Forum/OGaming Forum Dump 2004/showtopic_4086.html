The staff at WoW OGaming is proud to announce the MERGER of our site with that of WoW RA: World of Warcraft Roleplaying Association. WoW RA is a community of WoW gamers focusing on Roleplaying and will be bringing an active membership along with some impressive roleplaying forums. Join us in welcoming all the new members into our community just in time for the game to hit the shelves. This is a great opportunity to invigorate both memberships.




In order to facilitate the merger, the forums will be shutting down at approx 11: 59 PM MST Sunday 21 Nov 04. We will provide you more info in the event of a change. A few hours later our new and re-organized forums will be up and running. No posts or accounts will be deleted. Many forums will have tons of new threads added and new forums will be added as well.
