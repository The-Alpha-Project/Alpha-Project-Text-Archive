They sought out the camp of the Ogres and attacked that night. It was a feirce fight that took until morning to finish. Casualties on both sides were high and it ended up being Leahcim and the Ogre tribe leader, Mac-horith fighting it out for victory.
Leahcim took a critical hit and was almost knocked out cold. He was lucky enough to have cut off one of the heads of Mac-horith which left him unbalance and disorientated.
Leahcim slowly raised himself and in one outburst, thrusted his sword into the Ogres heart. He than passed out and was ready to except death.
Only by luck did a human patrol come by the Ogre camp and found him as the only survivor of such a massacre.
He was healed by a high elven priest and he vowed to protect the lands from any such pain that he and his people suffered.
