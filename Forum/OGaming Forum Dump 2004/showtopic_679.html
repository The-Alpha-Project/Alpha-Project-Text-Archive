It was a misty cold night, the wind blew through the wooden window of the inn. Jinto, a young sturdy pandaren brewmaster was sitting at the bar sipping his ale. Few came in today due to the snow.
The “Mermaid’s Inn” had few occupants at the moment but the bartender was confident that his rooms were going to fill up as soon as the yearly “Duel Tournament” at the local coliseum. Jinto would’ve like to sign up but the ferocity and gore of the battles changed his mind. He settled to watch them even though he disliked this kind of entertainment.
He was a casual drinker at the inn but he never really felt linked to anyone here.
He thought it would be good to go to sleep now, he threw a few coins at the bartender who caught them and wished him goodnight.
As he reached for the stairs, an elf opened the door slowly and fell flat on the ground in a pool of blood. The few people in the inn rushed towards him, but he was dead. The local authorities then erupted in the inn. Jinto had no intention to learn what happened… he left the inn for a walk in the night, whoever did that had to be stopped. Something evil had came to this city…
