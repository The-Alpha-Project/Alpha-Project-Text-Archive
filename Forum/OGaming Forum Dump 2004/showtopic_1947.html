Seize is a Dwarf's Dwarf. He loves nothing more than sitting around the tavern, drinking the finest ale and chatting it up through out the eve. It seems he has spent all his life in the taverns of Azeroth, yucking it up with the locals swapping stories of great adventures and of great exploits.

His favorite tale is how he lost sight in his left eye. As the story goes Seize was once again in a tavern, in Stormwind the famed city of Humans, talking trash and drinking mash as it were. He had quite a few kegs, as was the usual and lost most of his motor skills and common since, judgment left a long time ago. So Seize being inebriated an wanting to have fun decided to challenge one of his drinking buddies to game of darts. You can "see" where this is going. Seize was up first he had three shots from 5 feet away; he missed the board all three times. Seize was never known for his physical abilities while drunk. As he stumbled over to pick up his missed projectiles from the ground his equally inebriated friend took aim and fired. The first dart hit the board, right outside the bullseye. Seize got up off the ground slowly taking extra care of his oversized mug. His friend took aim again but couldn’t miss the pint-sized Dwarf. Seize was struck straight in the bum. In his shock he jumped up and around facing his intoxicated attacker. Everyone in the tavern, including the band, stopped as Seize screamed about his injury. His friend, unaffected by Seize’s yelps, took his last shot and hit Seize directly in the left eye. Seize fell to his knees covering his injured eye with one hand. Everyone in the tavern then stood up and rushed over to the Dwarf who was in a bowing position. Everyone was huddled around as Seize, still on the floor he pulled out the projectile and put it in his breast pocket. A second later he jumped up with renewed life, everyone gasped in amazement that this Dwarf could bounce back from such an injury. Seize looked around the shocked mass and said with whimsy "Aye, Its ok, me ale didn’t spill." The tavern broke into an uproar of laughter. The band returned to its stage and the people went back to their drinks. Seize sat back down at his table, with his friends and continued to drink the night away, paying little heed to his new injury.

Seize has never ventured far or explored the dangers of the world but with the revelation that he is a descendant of the mighty Titans he has taken heed and with so many other Dwarves. he has decided to venture out and explore the greater world for the glory of his people, the Alliance and for Khaz Modan!
