Here are my ideas:

I suspect there are 4 major happenings going on in the world.

First: the Burning Legion is planning its return and/or recovery, through both Humans and Orcs. I got the idea from the intro movies, wich tells both races should fear betrayel and corruption from within.

Second: The Titans. They have something to do with it all, and i suspect they have gone evil. Not evil in the true sense of the world, but i think they are not happy with what has become of their once perfect world. They may have send the Troggs, and both Gnomes and Dwarves will have dealings with the Titans, i think.

Third: There is something with the Dragons. The Night Elves have something to do with it for sure. Nordrassil was blessed by them all, but Teldrassil is not. As a Night Elf you'll probably be ordered to get these blessings, i dont know how. The Trolls and Tauren may have something to do with it too. Tauren because of their link with the Emerald Dream and Trolls, well... because i cant think of anything else for Trolls

Fourth: The Scourge. The Forsaken are doing something with them, or about them, yet i dont know what.


Yes i know: a bit vague, but there are a lot of clues of new things going on in that world wich i cant check out, and i am trying to make it clear for myself. I feel Dragons an Titans have a greater part in it than we all suspect, yet i dont know what it might be.

As for Ilidan, i dont get the feeling there is much going on about him, yet in the end it might all turn out to be about him. I suspect he is out there, but we'll only see his connection to it all in the end.
