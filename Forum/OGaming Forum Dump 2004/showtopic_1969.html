Clan Name and Brief History

The HammerFall Clan first originated in the Red World of Draenor. Those who could not contribute as warriors became blacksmiths, and created weaponry and arms as well as armor for the Clans under DoomHammer and Gul'Dan. Soon after, around fifteen patriarchers (leaders) and their families unified to create the HammerFall Clan. Being a clan that did not concern itself with notaries, or scholars for that matter, the event was not transcribed with detail, so the exact naming is unknown, but it is believed that the highly skilled families of the first generation HammerFall blacksmithed extensively, and were given the name "HammerFall" for their extensive forging abilities.

For three generations, the Turo'Sen House ruled over the HammerFall Clan. They were subtle leaders, who were not lusting warriors urging to war, unlike their ally Grom Hellscream, Warrior Cheftain of the mighty Warsong. They did not play a big part in the First War, but were one of the first clans to set foot on the fertile plains of Azeroth and fought against the humans in the first strike against Stormwind.

After the First War, in which the HammerFall had taken many hits, the Orcs took Blackrock Spire as their main fortress. The blacksmiths of the HammerFall used the miighty fires of the Spire to create rock-hard armour in which they would need to continue to fight against the Humans. Shortly after the Orcs began to develop their camps and bases, the humans struck back, in the Second War.

The Orcs eventually lost, and their fearless leader, DoomHammer, fell to the likes of Lothar and the other leaders of the Human Alliance. The history of the HammerFall is lost during the time of the Orcish Internment Camps. Legend passed through the cells of the child-progidy Thrall who had managed to break free from the seal of the human-chain and now roamed to try and free Orcs from the prisons the Humans put them in.

A small group of Trolls had gathered and charged into the camp during the night. Their jungle eyes had no difficulty in the night time, and could easily slip past ranged attacks and sneak behind the front-lines. While the attack went on, the main gaurd headquarters were blasted, and a troll opened a cell up, holding upwards from 20 orcs. They freed the rest, and it was known that most of the HammerFall stayed together, and when were freed abandoned their clan name and followed Thrall.

The Reign of Chaos came, and they stood ready to fight. As they allied with the Humans and Night Elves, they found each other once more. There were about forty of them left, and one of them was a master tailor. Before Archimonde's invasion at Mt. Hyjal, they sewed a great HammerFall Banner, and carried it into battle. Among them was the Elder Warchief Ogrim ShadowAxe. His son, the steadfast Mogrin stood as a warrior under the HammerFall and fought valiantly. He managed to escape into the thick of the Ashenvale Woods before Archimonde apprehended the World Tree, and was destroyed. He returned to find his father lieing on the battlefield, dieing.

"My son, take up this banner. You are now the Warchief, bring honor to that of the HammerFall" Ogrim pointed as the HammerFall banner lay next tom him. His last breath exhaled as Mogrin let out a fierce cry. He stood bravely, and gathered the bodies of the HammerFall warriors. He dug up an area at the base of the World Tree, put the bodies in all at once, in a big burial and covered it. He placed a metal plate above, and got a scholar to write "Here lay the remnants of the elder generation HammerFall. May their iron-will protect that of this World-Tree, so none may oppose it".

Mogrin left the World Tree and began to help rebuild the Orcs kingdom, in the South of Kalimdor. Durotar was the name of the Orcish nation. During the couple of years while Orgrimmar was being built, Mogrin found recruits that would join the HammerFall. Because of the new alliances, Mogirn allowed any race that was allied with the Orcs to join, including the Trolls, Tauren and Undead. Mogrin spoke with Thrall, and these undead were not the same undead that fought them at the World Tree, and were trying to rid their demons, just as the Orcs had.

Now the HammerFall is being rebuilt, in the Second ShadowAxe Generation.
