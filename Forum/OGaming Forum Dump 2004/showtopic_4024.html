WoW OGaming is proud to announce our first In Game Event: a WoW OGaming Elimination Tournament! It will be held on Sunday 14 November 2004 on Eastern Test Server #16 on 'Fray Island'. Registration begins promptly at 1PM EST (GMT -5) and will last 30 minutes. At 1:45PM... let the fights BEGIN! Here's how it will go:

*All fights will be Horde Vs Alliance.
*This event is going to be a 3vs3 Group Duel.
*Multiple levels based on player levels. Levels 5 to 29.
*No more then a 3 lvl gap between members of a group.
*Single elimination. You lose once, you're out.
*Winners will receive GOLD and other prizes.

See official rules and directions to the event:


************************************************

Teams cannot have more then a 3 lvl gap between members of the group. Example if you are lvl 5, then the highest lvl player you can have in that group is a lvl 8. *You must be at least lvl 5 to participate*

Groups should be decided within these lvl guide lines:
5-8
9-12
13-16
17-20
21-23
26-29

People without a group can try to group with others at the event during registration.

No player can be in more than one group.

WoW OGaming will choose who fights who based on levels etc.

All decisions made by Officials is FINAL.

Once registration is closed... registration is closed. Period. No late entries.

No fighting outside of a set match. Not even dueling. If you want to fight after your match has ended please feel free to go somewhere else.

If you or any member of your team leaves the event and does not return in time for your scheduled fight... you and your team forfeit.

Please don't be a sore loser. If you lose then sit back and enjoy the fights or simply go back to enjoying the rest of Open Beta. Any fights started outside of a set match, will be dealt with by death and a WoW OGaming Official will camp your corpse until said event is over. Please don't ruin it for the rest of us. There will be plenty more events when game goes retail.

Now with all that being said, the prizes aren't why we are doing this, we are doing this for fun and since Open Beta has only been out a week, don't expect the prizes to be uber. We here at WoW OGaming wanted to give you a sneak peak of things to come for Retail. Once WoW goes retail, events will be more elaborate and prizes will range from large amounts of Gold to rare/unique items and/or recipes of your choosing. So come on out and join us in some PvP fun. And after the event is over please feel free to let us know what you did and didnt like about the event and what we could do in the future to make it better for you.

Directions:

The Event will be held on Fray Island.

This is a mob free island south east of Ratchet.

Horde: Make your way to Orgrimmar fly (if you have the flight path) or walk to Crossroads. From Crossroads, head east to Ratchet.

Alliance: Make your way to the Wetlands north of Ironforge... find Menethil Harbor and take the boat to Auberdine in Darkshore then head south to Ashenvale. Follow the road to about the center of the zone then the road south to the Barrens. Follow the Gold Road in the Barrens till you're almost to the Crossroads then head east towards Ratchet. (mobs are safer than Crossroads)

Warlocks with porting abilities are welcome to help.


From Ratchet:
Head south down the Merchant Coast... there are mobs here but we will endeavor to keep them at bay. Once you near the end of Merchant coast (by the ships) Head east across a large sand bar to Fray Island. There are higher level mobs in the water but very few. The island is aggro free.

There are obviously other methods to get there, these are just the 'safest'.
