Hello all.

Myself & Fellow members of Warcraft Central were shocked to learn the Blizzard plans to segregate the servers based on Geographical location. Meaning Many hundreds of guilds would not be able to play together.

We were already aware that this decision was unpopular with the majority of beta testers. Despite the benefits of such a decision (lower latency) many of us feel this decision will destroy what is at the heart of an MMO-RPG. A diverse and community/guild based server population.

One member had an idea that maybe we could do something to prevent this from happening, or at the very least show Blizz just how unpopular their decision is. The member was Korudel, and idea was to create a letter expressing our great discontent at being separated from our guilds and online friends though this server decision.

THIS is the letter.

Please read it and if you agree with it's contents, send this letter to Blizzard along with us and anyone else who would like to express their feeling about this rash decision.
(Please note that we are trying to organise multiple fan sites to all send this letter on the same day, possibly to the same mail address. However the date and recipient is not yet set, so please bear with us.)

Renn Also created a petition which can be found HERE.
If you agree, please sign it with your real name, country and possibly make a comment further expressing your feelings.
