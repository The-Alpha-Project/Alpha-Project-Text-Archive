Vulpes had been scouting out the area right infront of the tomb, watching from atop his boar as the allied forces fought. He turned suddenly at a loud roar of a tauren, and commanded his boar troop to follow him. He hoped the humans hadnt heard the tauren or the boars as he rode.

a minute later, he came upon the battle of the scourge and horde and deterred his eyes. he kept his mind on other things and rode, eyes closed, into the battle. still with his eyes closed, he hopped off of his boar and jumped by the front line of ghouls. he opened his eyes and looked back to the front line of the horde to see, to his horror then anger a trollish corpse. His eyes glowed yellowish red, and his muscles started to grow much larger. He was used to enemies backing off, but these were the scourge, and couldn't think. instead several of them slashed at him. His troops, seeing this, speared many of the ghouls around him. a second later, they found a huge purple arm smacking them aside, causing them incredible pain. Vulpes was angry.

Vulpes' second in command, whos name was Zizray, was the only troll who had ever survived more then 3 battles as one of Vulpes' personal helpers, looked as his captain was smacking, spearing, and smashing ghouls left and right. he also saw that Vulpes would soon fall against the attacks of the crpyt fiends. He looked around for a battalion of spearthrowers to help him, but most were dead, probably from undead spell casters. Thinking fast, he did what he had to do. He walked up behind vulpes and hit him over the back of the head as hard as he could, knocking him out. As the huge mass of muscles went back to normal size, Zizray dragged him behind the front lines of grunts, hoping a shadow hunter was nearby.

As it became obvious that none were near, Zizray managed to pull Vulpes up onto one of the riding boars. He hopped on another, and they rode away from the battle.
