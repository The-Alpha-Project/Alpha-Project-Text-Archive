Hey guys, I'm back, and once again I'm trying to put the wheels for the Council of the Alliance (CoA) Back into Motion. So Lets see, now to explain..ONCE AGAIN...what this is and what it does.

The Council of the Alliance is for Human/Night Elf/Dwarf/Gnome Clans that wish to be part of an UN of Sorts. You will be liable to receive things such as

  • Aid in time of War
  • Weapons in time of War
  • Extra Equipment
  • Peace Keeping Support
  • Prizes and Awards for Outstanding Acheivements and Help and Support (kinda like the Nobel Peace Prize...etc..)


      What do you have to do

      • Give one memeber as a Representative
      • Agree to Help other memebers of the Group in times of WarFare
      • Help in the General Upkeep of the Organization (Money wise...etc...make a small donation every once in awhile)
      • Make Donations from Specific Talents (Details Below)


          Donations and Upkeep - More Info
          This means you should every once in awhile give some money to the Group to help in buying the finest of Items and Equipment. And Donations from Specific Talents would be, for example, encourage memeber with great abilities in diffrent areas, such as clothing, mining...etc...to help give support to the Group, say someone can make mighty fine swords. Encourge him or her to help in the development of the Group by giving a few of their fine work to the CoA. All Respects would be given to those who support to CoA.

          Basicly it acts as a shield for clans. Against Individuals and Groups. (I'm out of time, and any suggestions, post them, I'll review and add them in)

          Added
          This will also be a direct source of Trading. You will be able to have quick access to a marketplace developed by the CoA. The CoA will collect many artificats and Items and store them for Trading Situations. Along with being able to access other people and clans for trading.

Some guilds offer gold, and items but that won't work if the Council is already providing them with what they need.

Like if we hold an event somewhere and some guild is low on resources. We would provide some means of transportation.
For those who wish to 'invade' Horde territory we are fine with them to do that, or anything in that matter as they please. If you want you can even ask some guilds to join you in your wild conquest. Hell, my guild offers our services to do recon for any guild who wants to go conquer.
Unless you've only got 10 blokes under level 20 and want to assualt the gates of Ogrimmar!
It can basically be summed up pretty simple in this way:
1. It's a safety net for clans and is helpful for new players and new clans because we will support them in the early stages with basic needs(but we aren't a free ride, if you don't contribute you don't just receive again and again and again).
2. Do what you want, with who you want(but don't screw another clan in the Council over for your benefit).
3. We are here to help out and share our abilities
4. We are a community so we do community things, e.g. the events i spoke about.
5. You govern the Council of the Alliance. The top guy only stops things from getting out of hand!
6. What you want we will provide. Even if it's a campaign that leads us to Thunderbluff. If there are poeple that want it(and i don't mean 10, i mean like a few guilds atleast, and around about 40 or so people) we will provide for things. We also want people to suggest things. Like a round the world race, now that would be interesting!

Also on my more personal veiw. I think that when it comes to campaigns, like big ones. We will sort of set up a war council. So that if it does come to that we know the areas in question, e.g. Undercity if we decide to attemp to take it. We all know Blizzard is doing something to stop us from being able to take major cities but i'm sure we would love to give it a shot.

This Orginization has been Renamed to UGWA (United Guilds of WarCraft Association)

The UGWA would server like Graham said as a Saftey Net for clans. Giving Aid in times of war and such, and would also help the indiviudal player of the guild. The System was updated to include The Guild Leaders or Optional 1 Guild Representative for such reasons as Ace said. This organization would also help in forming of major events and would Always keep guilds informed. The UGWA would help setup and aid in Duel Contest (Prizes Awarded), War Campaigns, and Map Exploration and Conquering. The UGWA would also provide advice during War and other events. Though the UGWA would not be solely focused on the guild aspect, but would also act as a guild in itself. The UGWA as a Guild side would be a network of Spys, Assasins, and Map Explorers, and Strategic War Leaders to aid in the knowlage of the UGWA. The UGWA WOULD NOT Authorize the Infiltration of Allied Clans and Guilds. Meaning, Guilds associated with the UGWA would not have any such thing to worry about. The UGWA Guild would act as a CIA if you will (Central Intelligence Agency). The UGWA would try its' best to keep up to date on where the enemy is and where they are going next. Also, in the case that a Guild is having internal problems, there would be a Peace Keeping Task Force that would be dispatched to keep the peace in Associated clans. This keeping the bad members OUT. Bad members would be such that are hostile. If a Clan becomes out of hand the force would be dispatched to keep the peace. They would be authorized to use Forcefull action if necessary. Though in the case that a spy is suspected in an Associated clan, The Clan Leader would be informed Immediatly and the first suggestion would be the UGWA planting their own spy in the clan and if proven an Enemy Spy, an Assasin would be issued on the case immediatly and would be authorized to kill the suspect. This would only be done after careful investigation and sufficient evidence. The Clan Leader, and UGWA leaders would be required to agree on it. There is no majority vote in a situation such as that. It must be 100% Approved by all Sides.
