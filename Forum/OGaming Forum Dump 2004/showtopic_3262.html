Greetings.

My name is Elevander, and I run a future Horde guild on the PvP server named Nemesis. We are an extremely active, laid back guild for anyone interested in joining. Upon release, we will have a Teamspeak (voice chat) server for easier communication, and more fun. Our site went live about a week ago, and we already have 9114 page views and 630 forum posts. I believe it is safe to say that we will not fail on you. Many guilds that are established pre-release just, dissapear. With us, you do not need to worry about that. Now, on to the details.

As I stated before, the guild name is Nemesis. You can visit the site here. Currently, we only have 10 members, but as I stated earlier, we only began a week ago. We are going to be Horde, and on the PvP server. We are mostly for fun, but will participate largely in raids, arenas, and standing on roads making players pay us if they want to continue their trek unharmed. We dubbed this idea "toll roads" and it will be only a fraction of our income. If you do not want to participate in the toll roads, you can always grief virgin gnomes. Nobody likes those Gnomes anyway. I am a dedicated and experienced gamer. Although I spend time doing other things, I spend most of my time at home playing games. There isn't much more to do at home, other than read Stephen King books.

On to the site. As I stated before, you can visit the site here. Right now, it is littered with 2 ads. But I will pay to have those removed Thursday or Friday. Nobody likes a guild that neer had to give up a little. Also, our site is unique to others with our point system and guild medals and our item bank. All members that join the guild pre-release will recieve 50 points and a medal.

You can recieve points by: Attending guild events, donating money to the guild treasury, and donating items to the guild bank. Points are used for buying items from the guild bank, and taking loans out of the guild treasury.

Medals are gained by doing specific tasks or helping the guild in some way. For example. If you donate a certain amount of money to the guild treasury, you recieve points and a "donation medal". These medals make you unique to the other members.

Our forums are divided into a General Board, Announcements, Members Only Board, WoW board, and Other Games board. This variety allows you to talk about whatever is on your mind. While the general board is amusingly off topic and fun to read, the WoW board provides a place to find helpful hints or ask for advice.

The only requirement for joining the guild is that you have to act cool and friendly. Jerks are never fun. There is no age limit becease a lot of the time age has nothing to do with maturity.

We are not Roleplayers.

You can find out guild on this site Guild Database, and on Worldofwar.net's guild webring.

If you are interested in joining the guild, please go to the site here, register at the top, fill out the application, and start posting on the forums!

Hope to have you along!
