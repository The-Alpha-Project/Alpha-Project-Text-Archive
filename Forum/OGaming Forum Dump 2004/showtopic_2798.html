The trivia contest will be held on the site. The entire system is already up and running. You will each be given one (1) chance to participate. I will now explain the exact rules and rulings, it may sound harsh, but thats just how it is.
  • The contest will consist of thirty five (35) questions, covering various aspects of WoW, and requiring knowledge of data found on this site.
  • The contest will be held on this site, via a dynamic form. That form will be divided into pages. Each page will have a question and two (2) - five (5)answers, and for all except one (1) question, there is only one (1) correct answer.
  • After you have chosen you answer for your question, and have pressed next page, you CANNOT go back and change your answer. It is final.
  • At any point, you may close the trivia window, and can open it back to the question you were last at.
  • You are timed. From the first time you open the trivia window, to the point in which you submit your answers, including the time, if any, that you spend with the trivia window closed.
  • You are allowed one (1) chance. You are scored on correct answers as a primary indicator, and quickness of response as a secondary indicator. This means, you should never sacrifice being sure of your answer to quickness.
  • If you have left a question blank, as in not chose an answer, and pressed the next page/submit answers button, that question will count as wrong, and you cannot return to correct it.
  • The top three (3) contestants that reside in North America will be declared the winners.
The trivia contest can be found here: http://wow.ogaming.com/trivia/


The trivia contest ends on SUNDAY, JUNE 6, at 12:00 pm [noon] EST. At that time, the winners will be contacted.



It does not matter if you take the test today or on Sunday, so please do not think that you need to rush in.

DO NOT DISCUSS ANY QUESTIONS THAT APPEAR IN THE TRIVIA!
