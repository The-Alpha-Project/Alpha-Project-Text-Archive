An Introduction:

The Order of Mithril Twilight was founded by Duke Theobane Endsong and Lady Kurumi Shepchild on October 6th of 2003. We are a roleplaying guild dedicated on the erradication of the Undead off the face of this world and more importantly, our old home of Lordaeron. Expect our forces to be amongst those crashing into the horde lines that will lie in shallow graves as we retake various towns and assault and attempt to retake our old capital. We do this in the name of lost love ones and friends, we do this for our lost King and brethens, we do this to rid the world of this blight, to destroy the corruption of undeath, to free any souls and mind tainted and twisted under the corruption of an eternal undeath. Only through the victory of the Alliance over the Horde and the utter elimination of the undead cancer shall there be a lasting peace for everyone. We will kill and die for our cause, let there be no mistake about that. Lordaeron shall be purified once again.

We believe in carrying ourselves honorably and assist our brethens and sisters in distress, be they of our own or of our allies. Our undead adversaries however will not find the same merciful kindness at the end of our manifested will.

Our more nobler members adhere to a sense of chilvalry. Some of us are agents living only in the shadows to assist those of us that are in the light. Generally you are free to roleplay your character as you wish under certain guidelines which are specified in our enlistment requirements. If you wish for assistance in how to roleplay your character, we are here to assist you to become a good roleplayer.

We prefer to play with civil minded players, you do not need to be the smartest person in the world, so long as you know how to respect others, you will be welcome to join us for our discussions and roleplaying forum as well as in game.

Our website is under construction and is slated to be rehauled with new artwork sometime during the fall. It will also be updated with new guild content that was recently developed on our forum by active participents of this guild.

Currently I am painting digital character portraits of members who receives a rank of Captain or higher in our guild. It is a token of thanks for their hard work and their continued future efforts in jointly creating a great guild for everyone to belong to. http://endsong.proboards24.com/index.cgi?b...#=1090099648

Further artwork on the wishlist includes small generic character portraits to be used as avatars and signatures exclusively for the members of the Order.

We also plan to join a small alliance of like minded guilds to increase our chance in securing our endgoal (It is not possible to retake Lordaeron, the game does not allow it, however it gives us an eternal goal to keep us preoccupied when we are playing and make our roleplayed enemies' lives close to hell). Generally I dislike large alliances. Also we intend to roleplay some events with horde/undead guilds for some fantastic battles or story driven crisis.

We intend to have player journals that will be published onto our website.

The Order will be located on a roleplaying server and will be adhering to roleplaying rules. We intend to be roleplaying alongside with other guilds. That's all for the introduction, if you wish to find further information about us, please read the other threads and look around on our forum. There's little tidbits of information littered everywhere

You can visit our forum at http://endsong.proboards24.com/index.cgi
You can enlist to our cause at http://endsong.proboards24.com/index.cgi?board=recruit

Our website is located at http://www.mithriltwilight.com it is however unfinished and have not been updated for a long time with the new changes developed in the guild.

We hope to see your steel and magic along our side in this great crusade. We have a large number of paladins and warriors and are short on priests and other classes. However we are always happy to take on more paladins given how our guild is oriented

The Order's History:

(IMG:http://www.mithriltwilight.com/newbanner8.jpg)

The Order of Mithril Twilight is looking for fresh recruits interested in Roleplaying in a guild who's mission is to destroy the undead and reclaim the lands of Lordaeron.

We refer to ourselves as "The Order" for short and this is our brief summarized history:

the History in brief:

Part 1

The main character of the Story, Amilynn Twilight is visited by a gnome, Toddlehan, who was sent by Lord Uther Lightbringer to invite her to the celebrations and banquet for the return of Arthas from his campaign in Northrend. She suggests that Lord Lightbringer invite the minor Orders/Guilds for the celebrations specificly Lord Lightson's Lances of Light. The story then follows her as she dreams of when she was young child who survived the slaughtering of her village by Orcish Wolf Raiders. She lost her brother, father, and mother in that fight who tried to buy her some time to escape. She almosts gets killed by a lone wolf, but is rescued by a young squire. When the squire returns to the village with Amilynn, he promises her dying parents he would take care of her bounded by the dying fathers passing on his 2handed warhammer to the young squire. The squire reveals his name as Theobane Lightson. Amilynn woked up and recounted to herself her rise to a command rank within the Knights of the Silver Hand under Lord Uther Lightbringer. Due to the deaths of many Lordaerons soldiers and some amongst the Knights of The Silver Hand, she earned the position to replace the last commander through sheer valor and courage demonstrated throughout the short war and her past performance with the border skirmishes with the trolls. Althought she has no official noble titles, people still refer to her as 'Lady' Twilight.

Part II

Amilynn wakes up the next morning and heads to the celebration as Arthas had entered the Capitol city. As she heads to the main streets violence erupts and throngs of undead servants storms the city from every corner and shadow. She fights her way out of the city with a collection of guards and fellow Silver Hands Knights unable to make it to the castle to investigate what happened to the King. Outside the city, she meets up with Lord Theobane Lightson who now heads his Order after his father's retirement. He also after failing to make his way to the castle, retreated out of the city trying to save as many people and soldiers from many guilds and orders as possible. Together they regrouped with many other surviving units and orders south of the capitol trying to hold of the undead, but with no one in real command of the whole army of Lordaeron it proves ineffective and are forced to retreat after a large army of undead launched an offensive against them. Many died as they retreated for weeks towards the sea. The undead threatened to overtake them before they could reach the ships they managed to contact to evacuate them from Lordaeron. So, Lady Amilynn Twilight, Lord Lightson, and 2 scores of volunteers stayed behind to slow down the Undead's advance to buy the civilians, wounded, and other soldiers some time to escape. The battle was fierce with many casualties on the Lady Twilight's small force. Hard pressed, it looked grim for everyone against the tide of undead pushing them back. Everyone's energies expired, potions used up, everyone was expecting to die that night. But forunately and unfortunately, spurred on by the grisly death of an innocent, the guilt of failing to protect, Amilynn broke out into a furious divine rage. Focusing all her energies of her life, soul, and body she charged against the tidal wave of ghouls, skeletons, and abominations. Lord Lightson fighting against desperation and despair was inspired to fight on singing a song of the Holy Light that roused the surviving force into a spirited and powerful fighting force. One by one everyone joined their voices with the chorus as Lady Twilight carved a path through the undead sea. Men died, not with screams, but with the song of the Holy Light escaping their last breathe. Hours passed on as the roar of steel ran into the night until dawn had arrived. Lady Twilight's force had defeated the first wave of the UD army. Lord Lightson's hammer that Amilynn's father had given to him had broken in the battle, but he still fought on with using it as a parrying stick and a short shafted club. But the time they bought for the survivors, they paid with many lives, including the life of Lady Twilight, falling to the ground, blood smearing her pearl white armor. She smiled softly and closed her eyes one last time. Men wept and spoke of thanks and prayers to her soul. The few survivors collected the bodies of their comrades and made their way back to catch up with the others.

Part III

Lord Lightson, now called by all as Lord Endsong for the rousing song that brought the end of many Undead that day, brought Lady Twilight onto the ship ordering the priests to preserve her body as best they can. She could not be resurrected but there was a promise he made to her that he was determined to one day fullfill or die trying. When the survivors escaped to Azeroth, through the same route the survivors of Azeroth had escaped to Lordaeron, they spoke of their tales of the horrors of the Undead and the valor their soldiers had displayed to those that ruled there. In return for the honorary title of noblehood, the survivors vowed to serve them against all enemies especially the Undead. They added this vow, and many personal vows made by each soldier, to their list of promises. Thus, Lord Lightson, now Duke Endsong, called onto the surviving orders and guilds of Lordaeron to join together and form The Order of Mithril Twilight, in honor of Lady Twilight and her now legendary greatsword, Seraph-Mithril. Since that day, The Order of Mithril Twilight served the Alliance honorable and nobly against plots and incursion by the undead and the horde, rescuing those in need, and defending those whom are of the Alliance. However, they still vowed to one day return to their former home with new friends and retake what was stolen from them, at the same time end the enslavement of undeath that was cursed on their lost families, friends, and comrades, and ultimately to show the Great Betrayer, Arthas, the meaning of their Justice.
