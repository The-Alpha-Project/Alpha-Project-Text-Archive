Actually, I like the idea of PvP with little or no penalty to the loser. If you have a big penalty then PvP will be much less popular to the players. If you have little or no loss then it is possible for a noob to learn the skills and techniques necessary to be competitive in PvP. If they are going to get hammered while they learn the skills then very few players will risk the loss and will simply not participate in PvP. It will also make it nearly impossible for the new player to progress in PvP without getting thier character wrecked or all their good equipment taken. That will make the PvP aspect nothing more than a clique of players who had early success and will feed on any new player that tries to come up the ranks.

PvP should be played strictly for rankings, not rewards. Just develop some system to have a standings board for PvP and let the players who want to participate work their way up the boards. If there are rewards then they should be more on the order of control of territories to fit into the storyline of the gameplay. That makes at least some of the PvP more into raids and epic battles as opposed to one on one.
