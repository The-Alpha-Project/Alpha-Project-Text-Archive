Hehe I did the best I could, Tauren characters are not my forte but I hope you like it:

Name: Aratheon (Does not use his last name)

Race: Tauren
Class: Shaman
Sex: Male

History: Aratheon began life on the run. His tribe often hunted wildkin and other creatures of the Barrens and never made too permanent of an establishment. He has roamed the Barrens for many years, learning the great use of herbs and medicines to make powerful potions and replenishments.

Aratheon has not seen the likes of civilization up until now, when most of his tribe has disbanded and his parents have embarked on the epic journey of death. Aratheon visited ThunderBluff and decided to call the establishment is home. He has grown to love his fellow tauren.

His father was a great tribesman, who taught him many things like advanced healing and hexes that eventually spurred a greater interest in magical healing and shamanism. When the Orcs arrived, a great Shaman under Thrall taught the young Aratheon much of what he knows, and soon after years of knowledge and wandering the Barrens hunting. Aratheon has become one of the most knowledgeable tauren in his area, who can serve as a guide and is a most pleasurable person to have in your group, both personality wise and in power.

Aratheon has much wisdom, who has physical strength as well as a great mastery for Shamanism. He carries an axe with him, in case of a battle that requires it, otherwise he has ceremonial shaman equipment with many blessings from his ancestors. He is a quiet person, with a booming voice and a crouched back. He is quite noticeable, he uses his staff as a walking aid as well as wears red-fur, captured by him on one of his more recent hunting parties. It is both hard and durable, and comfortable. His staff is very decorated, with many tauren symbols and runes along with enchantments of all sorts.

Aratheon’s last name was lost during a time that he wishes not to discuss. “There cannot be peace without war” is his lifetime philosophy, he will fight for his honour, and his freedom. He hopes the world may come at peace one day, so he may leave the world not in vain, but in legend.

Name: Ana Stormsage
Race: Human
Class: Mage
Sex: Female
Description: Ana Stormsage was the girl next door in her life. She was born in Lordaeron, where her parents became refugees from the Orcs. After Azeroth had been rebuilt, the parents decided to return home, in the grand city of Stormwind and begin life in their home soil once more.

Ana is a young and talented woman. Her father’s friend, the Wizard Valarin Dun’ant had first showed her a spell as she was little. She was fascinated by it, always asking the Wizard to show her more things. As she grew older she bought herself a staff and spellbook, and began practising the most basic of spells. These were so simple almost commoners could grab a book and staff and do it. They were basic vision-enhancing spells, like making your sight better at night for a minute or so, or even just using a small version of telekinesis on objects.

As the years passed, Ana left her dream of becoming a mother and raising a family in Stormwind. She was an attractive woman, perfectly capable of drawing a good husband but after her training, she knew her course of life could not be cooped up in a house for years and years taking care of her family. She wanted to explore, adventure and gain experience and knowledge. Ana always loved to read books, ancient lore and stories. She is very respectful, paying close attention to advice given to her from her Elders. Some of her favourite things to do is cast an invisibility spell in Elwynn Forest and just walk around enjoying the peace without anybody knowing she was there. She also loves strolling through the Hall of Heroes, where she pays her respects to the Legends of the Past.

Ana has trouble concentrating, she often veers off course toward other things, and can never really put her mind to one thing. This is not true in combat, when she is very able and is completely concentrated. She is quick, agile but frail and weak – wearing nothing but some magical robes that hold some kind of magical armour around her, but nothing enough to withstand blows from a melee attacker. Ana strives to find a solution to getting stronger against melee attackers, but those problems never arise too much, her spells are devastating, damaging and killing target enemies almost instantly. She also wastes mana quite quickly, needing to take potions or other means to get it back. She hopes to recover some kind of charm or amulet to help her regenerate her mana quicker, and also learn to use spells with less mana.

In a group state Ana is a quiet person, she has no problem following the leader and occasionally puts in a word of advice, but usually does what she is told. When she is alone adventuring, she never knows quite where she is going – she always veers off course somewhere else. Ana dreams of exploring the world, and after she is satisfied in Azeroth she hopes to travel to Ashenvale, to meet the reclusive night elves and see their homeland. She also wishes to see the great World Tree, keeper of life for the night elves.

Name: I’straz Neverwinter
Race: Night Elf
Class: Druid/Demon Hunter/Soul Hunter
Sex:[/b] Male
Description: I’straz is an old druid who was born a little while after the high-borne and the new well of eternity was created. He soon fell to the addiction of magic, but under the Arch-Druid Malfurion, he learned the ways of Druidism and the healing of the land, nature and Earth.

I’straz continued on to fight in the Reign of Chaos. During this time, he found Illidan and began one of his close followers. He felt he knew Illidan on a level no-one else had, and that he shared a special connection. Because of this, he took an extreme step for his pledge – to become a soul/demon hunter.

He began the dark ceremony, gathering ancient herbs and oils into a thick concoction. He lit the lamps on fire, and began reciting the dark vows and shadowy gestures. Finally the time came, and he fused his eyes with the oil, blasting his eyes. He felt no pain – he rose up once more and had a keener-sense, he could feel everything.

He followed Illidan through heaven and hell, to Outland and with the Naga. I’straz is now a self-righteous demon hunter who has retained his shape-shifting abilities while slowly adapting into his demonic-form. His healing powers are slowly diminishing as his powers of darkness grow stronger. He is somewhat of an arrogant person, but will follow Illidan and his order anywhere.

I’straz has a deep hate for Arthas, and basically has betrayed his race as a Night Elf. He has met the Warden Majev but little does she know I’straz now follows the very prey she sought to hunt. Over the years I’straz toned down his own rage and righteousness to become a solid person with foundations. He has returned among civilization in Ashenvale, gathering stead-fast night elves to join Illidan’s cause. He never strays too far in the hands of authority but does take many risks for his benevolent master.

Power and lordship is what he seeks, and following Illidan is the way I’straz believes he will obtain what he wants.
