Træveren had left Stormwind on foot early this morning, bound for his home of Ironforge. His wife Ærensa had taken a griffon for she needed to get home quickly if she was to make use of the rare alchemical herbs they had found.

Træveren tried to walk everywhere since he felt that, as a Paladin, it was his duty to be available to any and all who might need his assistance, from the lowly to the lofty. He felt deeply thankful to the Knights of the Silver Hand for expanding their ranks to include Dwarves. He had grown up in a time when this was not so, and was one of the first dwarves to go from footman to Paladin. He always strove to prove his devotion to the Light, looking for any way that he might help, so on he walked.

Ærensa would be waiting for him to arrive around mid-day of the next day due to the length of the trip, so he had one night to spend out under the stars. As the day was drawing to a close and the snow crunched under-foot, he began looking for a place to camp. As Light would have it, he spied a party of three Gnomes and their tents around a camp fire. Approaching and hailing them, he found them quick to agree to share their site. They drank and talked, finally agreeing to all head for Ironforge together in the morning.

Træveren fell into a deep sleep feeling content and looking forward to when he would be awoken for his turn at camp watch ... he dreamt not at all.

Pain exploded in Træveren's mind, fell creatures and unworldly horrors danced through his vision. Awaking slowly and painfully, he took stock of his situation: he was bound hand and foot by chains to a great horse and a gag was in his mouth. He looked around and saw the three Gnomes dead and horribly mutilated, and all around were black cloaked and hooded figures that he could make out no details of.

He knew his wife's Priestly abilities would alert her to his danger... He knew that when he didn't turn up as expected the Knights of the Silver Hand would try to figure out what had happened to him... He knew the Gnomes and his fellow Dwarves of Ironforge would be alerted by any such commotion of a disappearance... with mounting horror he knew all of this was why he was still alive...

OOC: This is open to both Alliance and Horde members, members of the Burning Legion, neutrals, and anyone who wants to have a grand adventure. Alliance members will in one way or another hear of someone in danger (or happen upon the scene); give yourself as much or little information as you see fit. Horde members can either be in on the capturing of Tre or want to capture him away from his captors, demon and fell can do whatever they want :P I don't want Tre rescued too quickly, nor do I want any uber / godlike characters, kick-ass yes, but not over the top. This is open, so I have not put in who captured me, nor why, this will develop through the story. Enjoy :D
