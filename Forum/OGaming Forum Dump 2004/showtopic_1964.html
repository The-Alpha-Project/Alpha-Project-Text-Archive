Legend tells of the Ancient Sleeper who was put to sleep ions ago by the Titans. He was a lumbering creature who had great powers of seduction, control and magic. He ruled over the Sleepless Void (aka the Twisting Nether) and controlled many of the lower minions and demons. THe other generals, such as Kil'Jaden did not mind him, he was not a problem.

After the Ancient escaped the Nether, he let loose of his mind control powers and released the fiendish demons he controlled. He travelled worlds causing terror, until the Titans decided to put him down. One of the Titans put him into a sleep of such deepness, they needed to weave his destiny to a treasure of great power, an ancient amulet.

They used the amulet's powers to hold the sleepers spirit, while his body was asleep. They connected the amulet to a series of items and chests that were put into a chest, seperated and locked. They then found a great cavern among what is now Kalimdor and put the Sleeper there. They called many of his old minions to his sleeping place, to gaurd anyone who may try to awake him out of his eternal slumber.

They filled the entrances, and closed away the cavern entrance to the Sleeper's Domain. Great treasures will filled in the Domain, dozens of chests but that Amulet held the greatest power, the power of the Titans themselves. But if anyone tampered with the Amulet, they would pay the price.

A Dwarven Expedition Party used dynamite to blast away metres of rock from the mountain-side. A Geomancer had forseen a hollow cavern within the mountain, and the Party was searching for treasures and hunting. They blasted away and opened the cavern. They travelled inside, and found the inscription, which was unfamiliar at first but magically turned into red-ish Dwarven runes (letters). It read:

"The Palantir (Titan's name for The Sleeper) lies here, in his eternal slumber. Any that dare wake him will forever suffer the consequence. Nothing will find you here but death and eternal damnation"

Mogrin ShadowAxe and his party of hunters (not the class) wandered around the barrens searching for prey. Days later, after the blast of the cavern entrance, they walked in front of the mountainous region. There was an unfamiliar musk in the air.

"Its the human exploding devices" advised Mogrin to his party. They continued on towards the entrance, and surveryed the area. They found Dwarven equipment minutes after they entered, ignoring the glowing sign as they blew by, getting ready for a fight.

They found three dwarves dead, as well as some demon beasts and one of the men had found a journal of one of the dwarven men.

It read:

In aspect and in eyes
You meet him
Glaring, speechless, impervious
Hear his scream, the deafening sound
Feel his force, a killing cry
He has awakened, to our dismay
And now the world will fill with tears,
For the redemption of the Palantir is near

What this could have meant, no one knew, but the party quickly made for Orgrimmar once more. They needed to notify the proper people of this discovery, of the cavern, the poem and the dead soldiers and the rising possibility of a dungeon filled with hellish beasts and monsters.

The question is, who was this Palantir that the peom describes? Are there more Alliance parties coming for the Dwarves?
