VERY VERY important piece of advice on fighting and overpowering with your character.

Always pretend that your opponent is stronger than you, at least the slightest bit stronger. Although there may be an exception when fighting creeps.

Powering your character, you can make it very powerful, as I have. You must remember however a weakness. For example, mine is that I eventually run out of energy and become extremely vulnerable until I make my special herbal tea.

Another thing, be sure that when you fight, you get hurt sometimes, then it will seem much mire realistic.
