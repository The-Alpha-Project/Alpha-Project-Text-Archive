1. WoW - Hotmail & Yahoo! clarification | 2/3/2004 1:46:40 AM GMTST
Upon further examination of our signup process for the World of Warcraft beta test, we have decided to permit the use of a Hotmail or Yahoo! email address when filling out the signup forms.

-If you have already submitted your application using a Hotmail or Yahoo! email address, then everything’s fine: you don’t have to worry about your application being filtered out; you will receive equal consideration with everyone else.

-If you have submitted a duplicate application because you applied using a Hotmail or Yahoo! address and then you reapplied with a different email address, everything is also fine: we have combined your duplicate applications into one entry, so you will not be disqualified, and you will also not receive any unfair “extra chances” to be included in the beta test.

-If you have not yet applied to participate in the beta test, please feel free to do so regardless of what your email address is.
Kenny Z
