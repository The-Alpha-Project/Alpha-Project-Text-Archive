Time will always and should always be a major factor in MORPG character ability. BUT I think I speak for most people when I seriously hope this is not a lvl>item>character template>skill&tactics based system. In a perfect world I think it should be the other way around. Skill&tactics>Character Template>Items>Levels. That reduces the emphasis on levels and lootz and puts the greatest emphasis on tactics and character build.
One thing that always helps to make PvP more skill based is fighting Group vs Group or in large scale battles. Then you have tactics and skill come much more in to play.
You have to quickly identify your primary targets (healers/casters/glass cannons) and use focus fire to take them down while protecting your own heavy hitters. That's where the strategy really comes into play.
That is a major reason why I think a global FvF server would be even better than a FFA server. Because you would see more group vs group fights, instead of individual PvP where the outcome depends more on pure stats.
