The only thing a naming policy helps create is atmosphere.

Personally, when I am playing a MMORPG, I get very wrapped up in the game.

Names like "Imma Killsjoo" shock me out of my "gaming" state of mind. I am no longer wrapped up in the game, it reminds me of the real world. It gives the feeling that these aren't fantasy characters, merely people posing as such.

I like the gaming experience in a MMORPG to feel real, and have a true fantasy "air" about it.

Atmosphere people, atmosphere.

Names should ONLY be letters. First letter capital, others lowercase. Second name will have the first letter capitalized, the rest lowercase. Enforce a character limit to keep the names from being too long, and filter bad words.

Every now and then, we may see "Ipwn Joonewb" sneak by, but that's what ingame name reports are for.
