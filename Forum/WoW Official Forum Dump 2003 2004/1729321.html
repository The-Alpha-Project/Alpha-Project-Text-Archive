1. WoW.Catacombs Beta Submissions! | 5/31/2004 10:42:39 PM GMTDT
For those of you still interested in winning a beta account, the Catacombs Network has received their beta contest accounts and are now offering them to beta contest submissioners.

To find out more, head to: http://wow.catacombs.com

Here are the rules and regulations of our contest:

1. You must have a mailing address within the United States.
2. You must submit your entry by June 9th.
3. Multiple entries are not allowed and will disqualify you.
4. You must be a registered member of the Catacombs Network.
5. That's IT!

The Contest-

You may only submit an entry into ONE category below:

1- Write a movie script based in the Warcraft lure: Please keep your script to one or two scenes, we only want to read a couple of pages at most. Make it funny, sad, exciting, or dramatic.

2- Write a short story based in the Warcraft lure: Keep it under 5 pages, please!

3- Draw your favorite race in your favorite class. Please note, you can either email your submission or host it on your own address.

To submit your entry please email this address: yagamonkey@yahoo.com . Please include your registered Catacombs account name!

Winners will be announced and submissions displayed on http://wow.catacombs.com on the 9th of June.

Fur further questions, you can contact me at legendscafe@catacombs.com
