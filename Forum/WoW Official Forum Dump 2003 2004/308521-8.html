Creeps to me is silly for two reasons:

1) The word originates from the scandinavian/germanic word "kryp", which basically means a small animal, a bug. The word is still used in Scandinavia at least to describe insects like flies and spiders and such... things that "creep" over your skin :) As far as I know, mobs in WoW won't be insectoid, at least not all of them, so by definition the word has a different meaning. Kind of confusing and misleading.

2) Creeps in WC3 have one purpose; giving XP. Mobs is a broader category. Creeps would be your regular grind-mobs, while mobs would encompass not only those but also things like "ubermobs" (bosses) and such.
