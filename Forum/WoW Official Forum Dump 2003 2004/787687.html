6. Re: What happened to ogres? | 11/26/2003 5:00:19 PM GMTST
Ogres (one-headed and two-headed) still play a MAJOR role in the Warcraft saga, although WC3/TFT didn't portray this.

You will see more about Ogres in the Bonus Campaign coming in the TFT 1.13 patch, trust me.

Also, several Ogre NPCs acting as quest givers have been seen in WoW already. The way I interpret "reputation" working is not with the player controlled factions (Horde and Alliance), but rather the NPC factions - Naga, Ogres, new Shadow Council, Defias Brotherhood, etc.

Also, the way I interpret the difference in lore is that two-headed Ogres are smarter, as they are more proficient in magics (Warlock Ogres turn blue) and the second head was created not to make them smarter, but more controllable.

The inane chatter didn't really make them dumb, just oblivious to the fact they were being manipulated.
