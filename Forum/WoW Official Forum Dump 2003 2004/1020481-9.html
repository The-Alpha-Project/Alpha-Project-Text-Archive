Heres some of my memorable moments from different online games.. (sorry for the long ass post, I just have alot to share :)

Diablo II:

I came up with this on my own and tried it out with 2 friends... You take two sorcs, both high level'ish and make sure they both have the cold armor spell that defletcs ranged attacks by absorbing them and shooting back a bolt of ice. Also you have to make sure both sorcs have max lightning/cold resits and absorb. Now what you do is move about half a screen from eachother (both sorcs are hostiled) and start to shoot charged bolt at eachother. For every bolt that hits the sorc, a bolt of ice is reflected back to the other sorc. The bolt keeps reflecting, so one bolt will go on forever back and forth. Now try doing this for 10 min or more (make sure your shield is always up so cast it every min or so) and what happens is you get this blue/white line between both sorcs. Keep doing it and fps starts to drop and all sorts of weird sounds start occuring.

If your shield drops your instantly dead, and you have to keep casting charged bolt at eachtoher to keep healing up from the absorb. My other friend tried going through the beam, as soon as he hit it, the game just froze for a few seconds and then he died (getting hit by 200,000 ice bolts in under 0.5 of a second can do that to ya). This has had to be the coolest thing we had ever done in this game.


Also there was one point in the game in which you can use telekanises on hostiled players when you were in town. The three of us made sorcs just for that purpose. Even though it didnt do much dmg, people got knocked back and were constantly stunned. It was soo halarious seeing people constantly get stunned and knocked back thill their off the mini map (all you had to do is click and hold down the mouse button and it would keep hitting him even if your not on the screen). Not sure if anybody else tried this also, too many posts to read :)

This game has got to be the best thing there is when it comes to doing stupid $!@%. For example..

Enchanters can cast illusion spells that turn them into different races. Now on one of the pvp servers in EQ, it was faction based. So basically, if you were a wood elf and you saw a human or a dwarf run around in the area where most of your faction members hang out, you would naturally attack them. Now being an enchanter had to be the best thing there was in this game. So if your in a forest, it turns you into a tree, if your in a city, turns you into a lamp post or something dumb. Either way, this spell worked great, as almost always you would turn into something most people wouldnt notice out of place. Now the coolest thing was you could drop 1 copper on the ground and it would look like a very small pouch/bag, and if you cast the spell while near that bag you would turn into it as well. However, normally when you use that spell you cant move while illusioned. I mean picture this, a team of two, walking, spell casting bags, the size of about a normal characters foot.
