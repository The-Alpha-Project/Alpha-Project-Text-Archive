First off I'd like people to realize that this game wont be great for everyone..

Everyone has a different Ideal about what the most awesome MMORPG will be, and you cant please everyone, period.

With that being said, there's alot more than 5 things I'd like to see blizzard spend extra time on, but in the interest of Short attention spans, here's 1.

1. PVP --
I love competition, heck, Id love PVP, but I can see the reasons that most people dont.

I think of PvP as chocolate cake, in the fact that its great and stupendous, but not something that MMORPGS should focus around. Go Play CS if you want to be able to kill everyone everywhere.
My motto : If I dont want to do it, then thats final.

My Main Observation/Worry:
Make sure PvP is consensual, but make it worth it otherwise no one will do it. Make it Sensible, so people wont think its the only way to be the best in the game. Make it enjoyable, so that people will try it and not go back totally to staying away from everyone.

Little things:
  • Over the top Death animations for PVP. Adds enjoyment.
  • Special PvP only Moves that each class/race can do, makes PvP different than PvE.
  • For Arenas, perhaps a "last man standing wins the treasure" kind of match, would add variety.
  • Reward the Winner with Full health after he gets done, or Full Mana. Ammo users should get their ammo back. It makes it a little more worthwhile to be the victorious one in an epic PVP encounter, and perhaps more apt to do it again.

    Lastly Music Should play a big part in this game. I dont care if we will have to run it clientside, but certain events like a PVP arena battle or Frontier PVP will have to have a certain sound, and Music will be something that everyone will experience almost constantly while playing, so make it immersive and emotional. Think Braveheart or other movies where important midevil battles took place. That should give you some Idea of the music quality and type that Im wishing for.

    I would like to see manual evasion implemented into the game. Kinda like in zelda where you could do strafe jumps and backflips to evade potential attacks. I was thinking that it could be implemented by setting it up by a timing mechanism where if you pressed a button early, or late you would get certain penalties. Heres how I would set it up.

    Evasion implemented early = a stun effect. You would avoid the initial attack but you would be open for another attack that cause more damage.

    Evasion implemented perfectly = The opponent takes a stun effect and is open for a direct strike.

    Evasion implemented late = You get knocked to the ground and have to recover from the fall. While on the ground you are open for more devastating attacks.

    I think this might add alot of fun tactical actions in hand to hand.

    Another thing I would like to see for ranged fighters is a varied range damage ratio. Like the closer a unit is to the archer the more damage he will take. But the archer would have a cooldown after the shot before she could shoot again thus the archer vulnerable to a melee assault. So there would be multiple advantages for being at varying ranges. I also think the farther the archer is the harder it is for a succesful hit and a tad less damage. Or a instead of a less hit% maybe make the archer have to hold a bead on the enemy a tad longer before she can shoot. I just thought this would add alot of unique attributes to the archer.

And as the character levels up the time it would take to reach 100% would be shorter for certain spells.
