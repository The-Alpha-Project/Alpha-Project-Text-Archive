247. Re: Do You Read? | 1/23/2004 11:44:29 PM GMTST
Sorry I didn't read the entire thread. But one thing I do notice every time I see a fantasy book discussion is a lot of people talking about Wheel of Time, Lord of the Rings, Forgotten Realms, Sword of Truth, etc. but very few people mention George R. R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire series. I see Kat mentioned it so I'm happy for that.

A Song of Ice and Fire is better than all the other series I mentioned IMO. I've read all of them and the characters in A Song of Ice and Fire are just too realistic. There are no "good" or "bad" characters. They're all varying shades of gray with their own pasts, motivations, and agendas. The plot lines are thick and constantly changing. The main character one moment can be insignificant the next moment. You never know what is going to happen.

Sorry for going on so long but that series just blew me away. I've been nuts about fantasy for a while and for one series to stand out so much as better than all the others is mind-boggling. If you haven't read these books hurry up and get on it.
