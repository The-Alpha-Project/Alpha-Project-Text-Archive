6. Re: Katrica- ambiguities with improving skills | 1/27/2004 5:56:22 AM GMTST
Ravak's got it down.

Talent Points: As stated above, you receive 10 talent points per level which are used to enhance your stats.

Skill Points: You receive 1 skill point per level which is used to obtain things like trade skills.

Trade skills: Trade skills will increase with usage.
