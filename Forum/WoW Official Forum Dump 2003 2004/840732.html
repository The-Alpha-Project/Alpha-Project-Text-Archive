168. Re: What pages would you like to see on our site? | 12/11/2003 1:49:27 AM GMTST
Several of these have been mentioned. However:

  • Hero System - Description on the way it works or what you must do.
  • Leveling - This includes information on stats (are they automatically raised, for example) how to allocate skill points, etc.
  • Equipment - Containing information about the different types or weapons and armor, examples about the stat modifiers, and information on the type of inventory items we will see.
  • Loot System - An expanded definition of the loot system would be nice. This would include whether or not loot is centrallized for certain areas, if there are unique items per area and if there are specific drops from specific creatures or not (like boss monsters).
  • Mounts - More detail on the types of mounts, and what might be required to obtain one.
  • Interface - A detailed description about the User Interface.
  • Spells and Skills - Not neccessarily a guide on what spells and skills there are, but more on how they are used, where they are aquired, and so on.
  • Game Development - The idea about the Game Developer Journal was a nice one.


The following is more of a personal list that might be of interest, possibly updated into the FAQ:

  • Naming Policy - What will the naming policy be (if any).
  • Graphical Options - Types of graphical options that the user can configure. This might include the rearrangement of windows.
  • Gamemasters - How will we be able to get in-game support? Will there be a 24-hour Customer Service line? General information about that.
  • How will storyline affect gameplay - Information about whether or not changes made in the game will affect the story at all.
  • Dynamic Quests - Will these be implemented in a manner that would allow for specific people to finish quests and be rewarded with rare or unique items? Will they be open to multiple people? Etcetera.


Those are only a few that I could name off the top of my head.

Keep up the good work, Blizzard. And thank you for listening to these suggestions.
