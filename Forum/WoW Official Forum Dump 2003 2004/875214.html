I don't think you need a guide to learn how to RP. The biggest misconceptions about RPing is that there's some wrong way to do it. Just act and react how you feel your character would. You don't need to speak in an accent, you don't need to act any pre-designated way. RPing isn't scripted, RPing isn't something that can be done with rules in mind. Action and reaction. That's it. Have fun and try not to break character and you're doing just fine. Anyone that tries to slap around rules and regulations in order to dictate the "right way" to role-play isn't exactly what I would call a role-player.

Enjoy yourself. And check the links in my sig for fantastic examples of hardcore and non-elitist RPers that welcome anyone and everyone without judgement.
