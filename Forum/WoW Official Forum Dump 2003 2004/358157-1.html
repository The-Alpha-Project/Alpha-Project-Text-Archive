The Level cap directly relates to the Skill LEvel cap.

We know that skills are leveled through use--not points. We also knows that skills can only reach a certain level, and that the cap raises as your level raises. Sooooo, when you reach the level cap, the skills won't be able to go past a certain level.

There are also no advanced skill trees. When skills reach certain levels, you may get a more advanced form (Death Coil I, Death Coil II and so on), but new skills are learned through trainers. Simpler than a skill tree, yet has many more possibilities.
