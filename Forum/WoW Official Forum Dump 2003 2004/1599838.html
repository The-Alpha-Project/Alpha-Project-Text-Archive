Good day to you,

I fully support your effort. When I first heard that Blizzard was coming out with a Warcraft MMORPG, I said to myself, "I hope they implement two headed ogres." We both decided at that time that the only thing that would take our interest away from the mass of other MMORPGs would be the implementation of Ogres as a playable race.

Then Blizzard posts on April 1 that they will be implementing Two-heads in the game. Knowing it was likely to be an attempt at a cruel joke, I just shook my head sadly. The only suggestion I would have is that there should be a toggle for random character placement or only being placed with another player specifically.
