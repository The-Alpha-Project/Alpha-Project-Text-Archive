    Also, my response to the critics of inoriginality:
    Q u o t e:
    Despite the fact that the methods used to create it can be identified, I would be infuriated if you were to think that I did not put effort into the aesthetics and combination of those images. I don't care what these 'artist circles' think, either; the fact that they are accepted and liked nearly unanimously is quite enough.

    Keep in mind, please, that these are some of my very first attempts at creating public imagery. I do, in fact, have fairly solid knowledge and sense of quality in artwork, and am slowly attempting to incorporate that into my works.

    You should have seen the original version of my website, for instance... I shudder at the memory.

    I have yet to see somebody show me that they could do the same thing I did and improve upon it, as well! And for that, I am immensely satisfied. In the future, the not too distant times, I hope to make more original and detailed pieces of work.

    And so, I hope you enjoy these admittedly simple pieces while I increase my skill in computer imagery. Thank you.
