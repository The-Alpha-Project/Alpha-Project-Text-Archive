Hi fellow Blizzard fans,

This is the thread for suggesting areas that you would like to see in WoW. What's great about this aspect of WoW is that it will always be dynamic, always changing, much like other MMORPGs, so virtually anything is possible, the only limit is your imagination:-)

I'll get things started by suggesting a hell dimension, which is accessed by a magical portal, kind of like a worm hole, located throughout the different continents, and would only appear at night time. It would come in different colors, like bright green and light blue, with a mist surrounding it. Once you've entered, the only way back to your world is to fight your way out by defeating the evil that lurks within this nether region. In this area there would be undead creatures, like demons of various sorts, and it would be filled with molten lava, fire and brimstone, horrifying screams heard in the distant background, skeletons and corpses strewn around the ground, and other odd and eerie noises would emit from the dark corners of this evil, parallel dimension.

Please let's try to make an effort with lots of really cool areas, and who knows, maybe some of our ideas will eventually make it into the game, somewhere down the line:-) I look forward to seeing what you guys can come up with, so keep the ideas flowing!
