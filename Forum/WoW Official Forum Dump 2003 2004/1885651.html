I was noticing that there is a topic on the Beta forums floating around about the paladin class.

http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.aspx?fn=wow-general&t=155822&p=1#post155822

It seems from their comments that the paladin is too easy to play. If all you are doing is pumping the Holy Strike button, then where is the challenge?
The problem with giving them more options is: if you give them more attacks and damage, then they are they will outdamage rogues and warriors plus be much much more resilient than either. If they give the paladins more attacks then they will have to take away the ability to use any two handed weapons or cap their damage output from weapons or make them swing slower perhaps. They need to make paladins worry about their power they are using so they will have to balance healing themselves and damage output during the battle. Otherwise, the paladin will be way too easy and get boring.

I have played paladins in many games, and I could not say that any of them were boring. This paladin seems to be the most powerful of any game. Heh, usually all you see is complaints on how weak they are....

Anyone else have an opinion on this?

 2. Re: Paladins too boring or too easy? | 7/27/2004 1:16:46 AM GMTDT
Paladins aren't too boring.
They're a little slow, but not so much as some people make out.

Paladins already outdamage Warriors. Well, except for some of the ones with certain talents.

You can't take 2h weapons away from paladins for story reasons. Making them swing slower would just make the problem worse. One of the main reasons I switched to sword/shield is the attack speed. I actually do less damage, but I like swinging more often.

We already have to worry about damage output vs healing. In most solo fights, we only get one heal, with a 5 min timer. Healing is too slow without the shield. Uther's ring helps with that sometimes, but can't be relied on. If things really go south, we have a second heal, but on a 1 hour timer.
In a group it's different, but only if the paladin isn't taking hits. Even then, our heals cost so much that we can't do it very much. Especially if we're also using Holy Strike.

It's true that the Paladin has fewer buttons to push than other classes, but I don't think it's boring or weak.
