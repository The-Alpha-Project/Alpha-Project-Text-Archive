419. Re: All those who want ogre concept as realit | 4/5/2004 6:44:18 PM GMTDT
As I'm guessing that most people won't read the first 32 pages, there have been a lot of valid points as to why this WOULDN'T work, but then, throughout it all, every point has been countered with a valid way to make it work.

To summarize, the Two-Headed ogre would:

1)get half the XP, as it would be like a group

2)be restricted from certain classes, like druid, which can morph

3)only get to use one handed weapons, as a two handed weapon would take over the other half's ability of choice

4)not cause a delay in WoW's release-- before that is brough up, it would, if considered for addition, likely come in a future patch or expansion, so as to not make the game take longer to be released (even though it would add more to the game).

5)be *hopefully* able to select a partner-head; i.e. when you create a two-headed ogre, you can set up a password on your ogre, and only the person who knows the other half could join up with you, or you could leave it open, and anyone could join.

6)be unbalanced for PvP- it's a fact. Perhaps in PvP situations, all attacks could deal half-damage or something... although I think that PvP will never truly be fair, and everything should be left as is-- as the person will know who they are against before they take the challenge.

7)not require a timer to control, both players would have control at all times, and they would simply have to ration out controls. It wouldn't be fair if someone had a 5minute turn, but had to go AFK for a minute to take the dog out or something, and the two-headed ogre is dead when they come back because of this. I think it should be a mutual-control that is an internal struggle between the two halves.


Again, it's an April Fool's joke, but is a wonderful idea that would add another dimension to an already revolutionary game. I know it would be one other feature that most wouldn't use, but I personally would like it, and I know many more would as well.

It would be one more feature that would add just that little ammount more to the game that would keep people playing for that much longer.

Please, just keep in mind that when posting about the two-headed ogre being unfair, that there are two people playing it. It is like.. if you have a friend that you play with all the time... except with handicaps. Yes, they share HP, so if one is level 30, and the other is level 3, they have the combined HP.

On the other hand, if both are level 30s, their combined power is slightly less than that of two level 30s fighting independently (as they can use two-handed weapons, move individually, aren't as class-restricted, and each of them gets full XP for a kill, rather than sharing with the other half(assuming they are online-- otherwise, the one half gets full XP entitled to the collective ogre).

More HP, Less damage output. It seems like a semi-balanced class, and with a little work sometime in the future, it could become balanced, or handicaps could be implemented/removed to keep everything at least semi-fair to everyone (including two-headed ogres, if needbe.)
