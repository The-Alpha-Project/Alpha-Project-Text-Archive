 1. Ω The Casino Ω | 7/29/2004 8:25:44 AM GMTDT
After nearly a year i've decided to bring back my casino idea back since people are suggestion many ideas about money sinks. Here it is:


The Casino

The casinos will be huge and there will only be two in the whole world of warcraft.(One for each faction) They are ran by a bunch of bandit goblins and theives.(And a few theive brewmasters at bars) In the casino there will be drinks and foods and of course the casino games and shops.

The Casino Pass

Since this is a black market casino ran by theives there will be the need for a pass to enter. At (Around level 20) you will be able to talk to the bouncer (Big goblin) to receive a quest to prove yourself worhy. This quest will be pretty hard but not too hard.(Mostly killing bandits that he doesn't like) If you do this you will receive the casino pass witch is binded to you (You don't need to carry in it backpack). With the pass you can enter the blackmarket casino.

The Casino Games

The casino games will be simple games like slots and jackblack and most of them will be verse NPCs but certain games like poker will be able to be played against players. There will be a maximum for betting against players so that people don't go bankrupt too fast or even worse get rich too fast. All the games will be WoW Based.
EXAMPLE: Cards will be Gnome, Human, Nightelf, Dwarves for the Alliance and the Horde races for the horde.

In the casino no one bets money instead you play for chips there are two chips blue and red (In different shades 1 Dark blue Chips=10 Medium blue chips). These will be used for different things.


The Chips

The chips are different colors because they are spent at different shops. Both are worth the same amount of gold and are bought for the same amount of gold 10 Bronze=1 light shade chip(Either color). The blue chips are spent in the blue shop witch carries a selection of items (All only availble at the casino) and can be traded in for gold(With Other Players Or At The Shop). The Red chips are spent in the red shop witch carries a different selection of items(All only availble at the casino) and can be traded in for gold (With Other Players Or At The Shop). When betting you can bet for different chips and different combinations like you could bet 5 red chips and 5 blue chips and you will receive double that if you win (In black jack).

Casino Events

Beside the casino will be a black market race track where you can bet on NPC horse races (That you can watch happen). This will be only for gold not chips. You do need your casino pass to enter the race track. On holidays and events the race track will be closed for specific casino events. Like apple bobbing (Halloween) and easter egg hunts on easter. (You will need the pass to enter here).

Reasons For The Casino

-A good money sink.

-A very fun feature.

-A good thing for lower level players to look foward to. (At Level 20)

-Promotes chat in the casino and a good break from the world of warcraft in the world of warcraft.

-A fun place for freinds to bet.


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I Hope you like the idea.

All feedback is welcome and encouraged. The more feedback the better the possibility of this being implemented.
