Since my EQ guild is moving to WoW, as are many friends from other games, I saw an opportunity to make use of my free time while I am waiting for my BETA invitation.

I am providing a kind of transition service to other MMORPGers who are switching to World of Warcraft.

If you have a moment, please check out the site. Any feedback is greatly appreciated.

Thanks! -Wreckerbill

Site Info:

"The site dedicated to helping MMORPGers make the switch to World of Warcraft" 

Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game (MMORPG) players are the most dedicated and resourceful gamers around.  In no other way, for about $12 per month, can people of all ages and walks of life get together and accomplish such incredible feats of teamwork.  Even players who, in real life, have obstacles that would otherwise prevent them participating, MMORPGs level the playing field.  Anyone can be a hero, villain, master crafter, courageous leader, and the list goes on.

Since the first MMORPGs landed on store shelves, people with no prior gaming experience have been drawn into a world known previously only by hardcore computer geeks (like the one writing this ).  Games like EverQuest, Asheron's Call, Ultima Online, Shadowbane, and many others, have replaced TV time for people everywhere.  Why watch TV for entertainment, when you can be the star of your own show?

Because there are now so many MMORPGs to choose from, filled with so many players, there is no doubt that the majority of new WoW players will leave other MMORPGs to do so. This site is dedicated to helping those players make the switch.

Most veteran players belong to guilds, or have a group of friends they regularly play with.  Changing games is not always easy to do.  It is not an easy decision, and most people handle the subject like they handle their political views.  Some keeps their cards close to their chest, while others let the world know what they think.  Not everyone is compassionate or understanding about a guild mates decision to move on.  Some players may even change identities after the move to avoid unwanted attention.

This site gives you the opportunity to discuss your decision to move to WoW with other players from your current MMORPG, as well as players making the move from other MMORPGs. The forums are organized by MMORPG/Server to better help you get in touch with players you may already know from your old, or existing, home server.

Rising WoW guilds/clans are also welcome here!  I am happy to create forums for you to use until you are on your feet.
