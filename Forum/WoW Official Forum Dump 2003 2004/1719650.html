14. Re: WoW Guide Graphics Update: Leatherworking | 5/26/2004 4:21:05 PM GMTDT
Wombat: there are basically two types of enhancments available to items, permanent and temporary. Any permananet enhancment, in enchanting called an enchantment but this also includes armor patches or what not, replaces any existing permanent enhancment. Any temporary enhancment, in enchanting called an imbue, replaces any existing temporary enhancment. So for example with enchanting you can imbue your weapon with +4 damage vs. beasts for 30 mins, or you can use a sharpening stone made from blacksmithing which adds +2 to damage to all types for 30 minutes, but you CANNOT have both. You can however have say a Beastslaying Enchantment, +2 damage vs. beasts permanently on your weapon and the beastslaying imbue, another +4 damage but temporary, at the same time and they WILL stack.
