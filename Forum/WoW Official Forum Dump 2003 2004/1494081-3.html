58. Re: FanSites Post Here | 3/21/2004 2:01:46 PM GMTST
For those who want to know how to become part of the official Fansite Program here is the official word from Blizzard.

-------------------------------------------------
Blizzard Fan Site Program

Blizzard Entertainment is looking for a few good fansites. Web sites that are interested in participating in our official fan site program are required to meet the following guidelines:

The content found on these sites must be entirely about one or more of our games and must be updated at least twice a month.

Fan site administrators must be over 18, or have written permission from their parent or guardian.

Your site must already be completed and online. Your site must already be receiving public visitors.

In return, fan sites will receive regular updates and access to exclusive chat sessions with Blizzard developers. Fan site administrators will also get a heads-up on news and insider information along with some additional surprises from time to time.

Think your completed site should be part of our program? Write fansites@blizzard.com and ask for an application.
------------------------------------------------

In other words to get into beta your site must have up to date content, and established community, site specific content (articles and such) be complete and operational, be active.
You cannot slap together a few pages and call it a Fansite. That would not only be shameful, Blizzard would see right through it.

If you apply for the Fansite Program now you will probably not get into the closed Beta. It took at least a month and a half before Blizzard responded to our application and accepted us.

If you are going to make a Fansite then do it properly and not have the pathetic goal of simply recieving WoW Beta. The Maelstrom staff and admins have put in countless hours of their own time not to get the Beta but to build a community. That is our goal. During the entire time up to our acceptance we didn't believe that we were going to be accepted into the Fansite Program in time for beta but it didn't matter becuase that wasn't our goal.

If your going to do it, then please do the right thing and build something respectful, worthwhile and service to the WoW community.

Thankyou.
