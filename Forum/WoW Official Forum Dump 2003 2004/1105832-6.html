"im confused... i thought the whole game was supposed to be a mmorpg. why would we need specific role playing servers, isnt the whole game role playing?"

If you've played EverQuest you could better understand the concept of this... most players don't roleplay in a MMORPG. Like me -- they play to be the best in the game. They play to be at the top, to be the first to reach the highest levels, to be the first to see new things, to have the best equipment, stats, etc in the game. And to have fun with friends and guild(s) and people around while they do it.

Roleplaying servers have a much different feel than a "normal" server. People don't care as much about levelling up, or as much about what target they're going to raid. They play to roleplay, and that's it.
