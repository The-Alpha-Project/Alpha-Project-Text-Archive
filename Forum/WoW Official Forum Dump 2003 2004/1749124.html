The 4 continents were formerly one continent named Kalimdor. The collapse of the Well of Eternity created the Maelstrom, a neverending sea whirpool/storm that separates all continents.

****S P O I L E R S ****
Don't read beyond if you don't want spoilers!











- Blizzard approved timetraveling: Rhonin and Krasus the dragon mage are pulled by a time anomaly caused by the ripples of magic of the Well of Eternity-- 10,000 years ago and are drawn to the past along with an Orc who was following them when they arrived to Kalimdor. Sadly, they didn't know an Orc had followed them ... so their efforts to not change the history of the past turned out in vain. The question is ... will there be alternate realities(parallel presents). Is World of Warcraft from the original universe or from the second universe caused by the change of the past? Will there be invasions from alternate presents or alternate futures? Hmm ...

- You can read how Queen Azshara's vanity and blind thirst for a golden age didnt let her see what was really going on until it was too late.

- The love triangle of Ilidan, Tyrande and Malfurion

- The first class of Malfurion into walking the Emerald Dream and using Druidism magics with Cenarius as his teacher.

- We see closely what Druidism is really about

- 10,000 years ago there were 3 types of magics.
The Well of Eternity's magic, Elune's magic by the Priestesses of the moon and Druidism. There was only 2 Druids: Cenarius and Malfurion(his student).

- We are introduced with the fact there are other demi-gods like Cenarius. They were just mentioned, and it seems book two might bring more details about them since it seems Cenarius will take Rhonin and Krasus to meet the demi-gods.

- I was curious by the fact that Dragons hide from Cenarius shapeshifting into trees inside Cenarius's garden and he seems not to detect them. The Dragons wish to spy on him but not make themselves seen or unveiled by him. Since Cenarius was a demi-god left behind by the Titans and the Dragons were created by the Titans, this seems confusing that they don't work together originally at this time period. Some secret is behind all that. He possibly knows about them but won't work with them directly. An intersting twist to the mystery is that Cenarius teaches Malfurion to walk the Emerald Dream for first time ... but the Dragon Ysera is the dragon of the Emerald Dream ... yet Cenarius doesn't know about the Dragons? Or is he hiding something? The Dragons do know about Cenarius and spy him.

- Druidism is to draw magic off any nature lifeforms. An interesting concept. Druids dont draw their magic directly from the Well of Eternity, but from Lifeforce. We see Cenarius asking Malfurion to draw magic from Rhonin, Krasus, Cenarius, a dragon, Tyrande and an orc's lifeforce to fight against Queen Azshara's counselor who was wielding magics from the Well of Eternity and empowered by Sargeras.

- The book introduces the first human/elf breeds as Rhonin the Mage married Vereesa(sister of Sylvannas and Allerya the ranger) and will have twins. Vereesa said this is an omen since there is no record of a human/elf offspring that had survived and far less twins. Twins are a portent of being important for the future of their people. So do we have future heroes for a Warcraft IV or V? Ehh?

It would really be a mind twisting thing if those twins turn out to be Ilidan and Malfurion. ehh forget it, im starting to get a timetraveling /Paradox headache.
