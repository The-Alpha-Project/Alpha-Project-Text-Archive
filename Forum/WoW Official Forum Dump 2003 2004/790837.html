February 22nd, 2002


"This is Warcraft," explains Dabiri, "there is always going to be conflict. Whether it's between this race and that race, him versus her, this is war. Some servers will only be PvP while others will have certain "areas" that will be PvP. This is combat folks so be prepared when you start to play. If you don't want to play on a PvP server, then don't play on it. They'll be plenty of other servers where you can play without it."

He wouldn't just say that if they hadn't put quite a bit of thought in to how they were going to deal with the issue.
Blizzard has always been keen on PvP. They aren't going to ditch those who are interested in it. And they aren't going to force everybody to play that way either.
