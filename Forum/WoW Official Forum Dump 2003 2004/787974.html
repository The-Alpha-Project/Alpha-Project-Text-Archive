This is how it works.

Somebody finds a bug. The bug is where a Tome of Strength does not get deleted after its use, so you can level up your strength a million times. Ok great. So that somebody doesn't report it, and thinks he is god. He goes into a PvP area as a level 20 character, and totally owns all the level 40 players. Those players smell something fishy and come back to the forums and scream "WAAAAAAAAAHHHH I GOT KILLED BY A LEVEL 20 WHO HAD FULL STRENGTH!!!!!" and rightly so. At that point, at least half of the players who got killed will report that somehow a player is getting unlimited strength, supported with screenshots. The devs take a look at the character who killed them all (all character info is server side), and realize what happened. Bug Fixed. Character has the benefits removed, and the tome deleted.

OR

Somebody finds a bug. The bug is where a Tome of Strength does not get deleted after its use, so you can level up your strength a million times. The player takes some screenshots, explains where he got the Tome, and what its doing. The devs are like OH SHOOT! THX THX!, the player is given points toward being invited to the friends list, and everyone is happy.

So even if you report the bug or not, somebody will, and somehow the bug will get back to the devs.
