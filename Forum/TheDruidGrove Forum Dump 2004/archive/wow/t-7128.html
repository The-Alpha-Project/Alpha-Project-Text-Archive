I would prefer to see a little more variety a little more frequently. I like the idea of 3 hour days--that makes for a very neat and predictable yet dynamic 8 WoW days per real day. 10 or 12 WoW days per real day would also work well I think. This makes sense to me since many people's playtimes are structured around 2-4 hour blocks.

Edit: I'm not sure that it would be sensible for Blizzard to designate particular servers for particular timezones, as processing load would be an issue. At peak servers would be staggering, while offtimes would see a great deal of processing power go to waste. By making each server timezone independent they can spread out the burden more evenly. Players inevitably will organize themselves anyway, and the "time zone advantage" goes away in instanced dungeons.
