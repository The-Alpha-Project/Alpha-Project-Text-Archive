Its long been known that blizzard was working on removing some of the tedium involved in previous MMORPGS.

One of the first things they announced with that in mind was their no fail combines for trade skills.

That's right. If you have the appropriate skill and components, you make what you aim to make, evidently. Find more on WoW tradeskills here (http://www.blizzard.com/wow/townhall/tradeskills.shtml)

Secondly...they've recently announced that the banks in WoW will not hold coins...because coins will have no encumbrance and there will be no limit to the amount characters can carry. Unfortunately that means no more fun playing with /split and monks. Again, further info can be found here (http://www.blizzard.com/wow/townhall/banking.shtml)
