It seems that the WoW Fan's are looking forward to the next push/patch!

As most know from other MMO's, Content keeps people interested.. new toys.. new spells.. new levels.. more damage.. more challenges are all part of each MMO's future..

But let me remind everyone.. that this is BETA.. and so far I have enjoyed my beta experience. Sure we are looking towards PvP, Raid type challenges, and more of what we have already seen.. But quite frankly, I rather not rush Blizzard into an early release of a push/patch.

Tigole explained Blizzards position on the next beta and I have to agree with him on this.

The patch is not out yet because we do not want to push until we can provide enough content that allows us to raise the level cap.

In addition to a number of zones that we are working on (Hinterlands, Tanaris, Feralas to name a few), we're also trying to include two new dungeons (Gnomeregan and Uldaman, possibly more) as well as some pretty major game systems.

In the future we hope to patch on a more frequent basis.
As for things to do.. while waiting:

- Try another Class!
- Try another Race!
- Build up your Trade Skills!

And most importantly - Just have FUN!
