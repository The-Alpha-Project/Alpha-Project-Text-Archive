Cosmos UI (http://www.cosmosui.org) - Very popular during beta, and very nice, although it includes a lot of junk that I'll never use. GypsyMod (http://gypsymod.the-mad.net/) - Just found this one, looks quite nice. This one has the added feature of allowing all UI elements to be dragged and moved to new locations, as well as all the usual ui mods. I havent tried it yet, but my one beef is that they seem to have eliminated the lag-o-meter completely, which I like, being on dialup 8)

I've written my own, for myself and my guild. It's pretty simple and i provide no support for it or promised updates as pretty much I created it for myself and have made it available for others to use. There is NO menu for options, if you don't want a feature, remove it's folder from the Interface folder.

Feature List:
BuffStat: Shows the time remaining on your buffs to the left of your radar
ItemBuff: Shows the time remaining on your item buffs (up to 2)
Clock: Shows the time... and so much more. (courtesy of Telo)
LeftSideBar: Puts six Action Buttons on the Left side of your screen.
RightSideBar: Puts six Action Buttons on the Right side of your screen.
SecondBar: Puts twenty-four Action Buttons right over top of your exp bar.
AzerMod: Moves your FPS counter (when you turn it on with CTRL+R) to the top left of your screen, also has a little codelet in it to speed up the quest text fill.


They're ordered by your time compared to the server time (to adjust the clock to match)
