1) Not worth it. Pitch up and down just do camara angle changes really. Hit page up all the way and you're in first person view. Hit page down once, you fall to back view, on level with your character. Hit it again, and it pivits up to normal 3/4 view style. Hit it again and it zooms out to more a wide area view. And that's it's max. I find using the mouse wheel much more useful in this aspect.

2) As stated before there's only two states of XP now. There's rested and normal. Normal you get 100% of the XP from NPCs. Now do note that XP does retrograde as you level. Ie; a lvl 5 NPC will not give you the same XP at lvl 10 as it did at lvl 6. Rested XP will give you 200% of XP yeilded. It's based on XP amount, not kills made. It's also seems limited to 3 bubbles (I have yet to have more than 3 bubbles of rested XP).

3) You must not have selected it or you got a bug. that's all. Quests don't time out, unless they specifically say there's a time limit (in that case you'll get a clock pop-up window at the time of selecting it).
