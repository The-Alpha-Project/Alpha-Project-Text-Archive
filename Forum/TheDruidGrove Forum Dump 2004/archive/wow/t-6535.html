You could (temporarily) go to Internet Options -> Security -> Internet -> Custom Level -> then click on Reset to Low.

Or you could add blizzard.com to your trusted sites list.

If neither work, it could be some other firewall on your system blocking it, like ZoneAlarm or Norton Internet Security.
