The open beta through blizzard is done differently than the previous betas or the fp open beta. If you created your account directly on the WoW site rather than through File Planet you will not recieve an email nor need to use the account key later on. The account creation page has a general account key and verification process and once you have completed all steps of the process and gotten to the download page you are set. It won't matter where you actually download the client as long as you recieved the account created confirmation on the blizzard site.
