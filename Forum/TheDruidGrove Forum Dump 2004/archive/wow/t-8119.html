I'd advise going mining/engineering and picking up guns on a hunter. If the aesthics of the guns really bug you, and you'd rather stick bow, I can definently understand. Guns just have a very large stastical advantage over bows. Engineering can craft good DPS guns, as well as allow you to make ammo on the fly (which is higher DPS than any store bought). So you have an easier time getting weapons, ammo, and do more DPS. Though a blue bow will still out damage a green gun, given equalish levels, but that's to be expected =-p

Also with making your own ammo, you can carry however many stacks in your ammo pouch (8-16), and then carry 20 bars and blasting powder on you = another 4000 ammo in two slots.

Just something to think about with a hunter, if you haven't already =-)
