My only concern about bearform is that it will be overvalued when it comes to class balance consideration. It's a very cool trick, but that's really all it appears to be. (as it should be IMHO) You can offtank in an XP group lacking melees or in an emergency, pretty cool but hardly class defining. It appears to have no use solo or in a raid setting. Hopefully they will view it in that light, unlike the old argument against improving raiding Druids in EQ because they have Ensnare. (utterly useless in raids, but admittedly extremely powerful in groups/solo)
