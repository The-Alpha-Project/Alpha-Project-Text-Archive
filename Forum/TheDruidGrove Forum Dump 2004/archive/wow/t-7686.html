The concept of the forms is that it be something you use in proper situations to gain an edge, but give up your spells and items in return. You won't be a "lame duck" in these forms, the idea is for them to be a viable choice in the right situation. It isn't like an EQ wolf form, where it is a buff that also changes your appearance.
