Hi, just joined up at your cool site. Yeah I will be playing a druid at release.

Ive played several other classes too high level and finally decided on druid (course hes not high level yet).

One thing that druids are top notch at is healing. Preists are not really ahead of us in the healing department, we are pretty much dead even, which I found suprising.

Preist Renew (Top Level)
Heals 810 over 15 seconds
365 Mana
Healing Per Second: 54
Mana Per HP: 2.2

Druid Rejuvinate (Top Level)
Heals 756 over 12 seconds
335 Mana
Healing Per Second: 63
Mana Per HP: 2.3

Our regeneration line of spells is both more mana efficent and heals for more in a shorter amount of time.

Priest Greater Heal (Top Level)
Heals 2674
960 Mana
4 Second Cast
Healing Per Second: 668
Mana Per HP: 2.8

Druid Healing Touch (Top Level)
Heals 2230
790 Mana
3.5 Second Cast
Healing Per Second: 637
Mana Per HP: 2.8

Our biggest heals are tied with mana efficency to a priest, however their heal heals for roughly 30 more HP per second, which is rather small. The big difference (for PvP anyway), is that often times the shorter casting time spell is better, the more you get hit the longer the cast time of your spell and the more chance of interuption. Often the extra 0.5 casting time on the preists heal can turn into 1.5-2 additional seconds when being hit. Additionally that 0.5 seconds extra may be just too much for the tank to hold on for.

Edit: Interestingly if you are spamming the big heal spells back to back, since the druids is faster casting, the difference in healing power becomes NULL, the preist coming ahead by only 8 hp per cast.

Bear form vs Warriors is a bit off at the moment, but thats because of an AC issue with leather armor, I expect that blizzard will bring us more in line with warriors tank wise, and I think you would be suprised. Likewise cat form needs a Direct Damage finisher move in order to fill the DPS role like a rogue does. So just a small few changes and we are a really rockin class.

Druids are also extremely good at PvP. I love the fact that we can Stealth and move around, one of the only 2 classes that can travel invisible.
