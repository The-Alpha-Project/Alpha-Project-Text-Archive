09-17-2004, 02:02 AM
Fighting casting harpies always turns into a johnson contest. My nuke is bigger than your nuke! Once you learn that, they are so simple to kill. I didn't even run upto them anymore. I just wait for the right time, Moonfire and then nuke it down!

I'd suggest getting Skinning/Leather crafting so you can make armor for yourself. It's also a good way to double your income just on NPCs. You can make items that sell for double what you bought the material for originally. Of course, you do need to have the gathered materials as well.

Don't bother with first aid, save up the linens for clothers that will spend almost anything for good stacks of them. First aid isn't much use with all the food avaliable in game on top of your own spells.

Other than that, learn your spells, know what they can (and can't) do. Learn the strengths of your forms (for example, bear form gave my lvl 22 druid over 150 more HPs, which made it idea for fleeing fights or doing toe to toe tank fights. Cat form grants stealth abilities which makes it ideal for scouting.).
