Unless this has changed in the last week, there are no explicit limits on group member levels. Last week a level 53 druid was escorting a group of characters level 28-30 thru Felwood. Some of the kills resulted in minimal XP. The kills that were XP appeared to follow 3 rules:
1) the mob was XP for the highest member of the party (i.e. green or above),
2) the mob was XP for the party member (i.e. green or above and the mob level was displayed)
3) the character attacked (by spell or melee) the mob.
This is not an effective way to power level a character. The XP from a mob 10+ levels above a character was in the 20 to 60 range.
