The answer:

On a PVP Server:

- Your characters (on your account) have to be all on one side or the other.
- You can only group with players in your faction

On a regular or an RP server:

- Your characters can be on either or both sides.
- You can only group with players in your faction


So, you can never group with a member of the opposing faction, regardless of server.
