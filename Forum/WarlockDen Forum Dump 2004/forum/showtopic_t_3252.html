RombusMindspire wrote:

Answered by our very own....
Mongul wrote:
None of the 4 people involved in the summoning can be moving. The person you have targeted needs to be standing still; once you start casting the spell you need to be standing still; and once the other 2 people right-click on the portal they need to be standing still. And none of you should move at all until the target appears.. don't start moving just because it looks like the spell is finished.

I don't think I've ever had the summon fail if all 4 people were able to follow this.
