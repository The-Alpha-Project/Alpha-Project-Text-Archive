The Apostles of Aerthyr is a group of battle-hardened men and women fighting for the Alliance. Their goal is to stave off the advance of the Horde while protecting the freedoms and safety of all free peoples. Each and every Apostle swears by this oath and charter that they shall fight against the Horde and against evil in all it's forms.

Aerthyr is both a guidebook passed down from leader to leader of the Apostles for generations, and the great founder of the Apostles. The beliefs and way of life of Aerthyr are of the highest standard- among these are that each member will live to attain their fullest potential; that each Apostle will protect the weak and the innocent; and that each member will assist one another in their path for greatness. It is only through harmony and teamwork that an Apostle thrives, and it is thus one of the most important credos of AoA.

It is said that there is much more to the Handbook of Aerthyr, but that each book is kept secret by the leadership. It is also said that secret enclaves are held to bring about knowledge to the Apostles. Only AoA members know for sure.

-----------------------------------------------------------------

If you are interested in our guild, feel free to visit us at www.aerthyr.com and sign up to our forums. We have an online application as well.

We are looking for others who wish to do some rp'ing and have a good time. A good group of gamers are hard to find and we take special precautions to include only those who fit into that group as well. Regardless, we'd love for you to visit us and say hello.

Thank you for your time.
