From what I've seen so far the rock/paper/scissors game has not been a total failure, though yet could never be called even a partial success.

For example: Patch 2 (Horde push) Pvp was limited to dueling but use your imagination.

Overpowered Classes: Warlock/Mage. Most overpowered class: Warlock. Class that it can't beat: Mage, comparably overpowered as well. Class that the mage cannot beat: Priest. And priests were pretty low on the general foodchain then.

Then again, that's all the farther the balance went.

Now pre-750MB patch, there was only one class I could beat in a straight fight. Druid. Not a fair comparison because Druids were the low man on the pvp totem pole, not warlocks. Druid in humanoid form? Tongues, fear, immolate, mana drain. Cat form was much harder, required a pet. If caught with a voidwalker; Sacrifice, fear, weakness or elements, fear immolate, fear, corruption, more fear, shadowbolt and it's still a toss-up. With a succubus; seduction, agony, corruption, fear to break, immolate, run away. A smart druid in cat for would just run away rather than give chase. If not, rinse & repeat. Not much for balance in patch 4 it's true, but those classes with talents just ran the show correct? Not exactly. A paladin could beat an equal level warrior, and fall to a mage just as easily. It's due to how the classes played themselves out. A paladin is a warrior who can out-dps his tank counterpart but can't tank, but the paladin also has the ability to stretch out a fight by inches. Fist of justice, seal of reckoning, divine sheild, and if it's played right another fist of justice can help keep the opposition off of your back. Unless of course you got a warrior that was a fan of the Mortal Strike exploit, in which case you could count on dieing without much of a valiant fight no matter what class you were.

But since this is a tread about how effective we are in groups, and in the pvp section, I think I'll say that with those areas combined we are utterly useless. I am (or was) told, in raids, to cast detect invisibility and cast tongues on the healers before I died. And that's basically all I could do anyway.
