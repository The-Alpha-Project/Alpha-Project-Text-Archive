I thought I would share my little adventure today as I was really surprised after 5 years of gaming, that something could actually make me scared. The last time I was scared was when I started playing EQ and I had no idea what anything was... the first time I had to run through the Karana's I nearly opted out because I was petrified.

I remember when I was around mid 20's or 30's a friend of mine wanted me to Evac to Emerald Jungle, back then you could only do it from inside Karnor's Castle which was a high level dungeon in the middle of another high level zone. It will be cool he says, we'll use levitate and be fine he says, I promise you won't die he says. So I port us to Dreadlands with the promises of security, and as he knows the zone from running through it before I put my trust in his hands. We zone in and make our way to the tunnel to run through and with lev on set off into the zone. It snows in Dreadlands which makes vision next to impossible and I realise I am really high off the ground... so high if I fall I would make a very very big mess and the "splat" would echo across the lands.

About half way through I was so petrified of this zone I sat, loaded gate and got the hell out of there. 20 level's later I am xping in that zone laughing at how scared I was... but I loved the fact a game, something not real that can't hurt me in RL in any way or form got me as scared as when I have to put something in the outside bin when it's dark.

For the first time since pretty much then I had the same feeling, I was scared! For the past 3 days I have pretty much lived and breathed WoW, I have gone from over 2 weeks of not playing to getting back on at level 16 and getting to 20. I was planning on going to bed at around 2am yesterday but as the boyfriend knows me well enough he kissed me goodnight with a "Good luck on the Succubus pet". Honey I say... I'm only just into 19, that would take hours, I will be in bed before that.

Jump forward to around 6am and I am level 20 set on a path to get my pet. I hate it when he's right. After getting the quest from the guy in Stormwind I try to work out where Kalimdor is... Hmm can't see it on the map... oh.. wrong island... it's over there I head for the griffons.

I fly from Stormwind to Ironforge, easy. I run from Ironforge to Loch Modan, easy. I head into Wetlands... nerves creep in, I have never been in this zone before and I hate not knowing where I am going and what could jump me. I follow the road and kindly ask in General chat if I will be safe. Don't you love it when you get replies like "Lots of Nasties here, be careful, don't leave the road" *Gulp* but determined I head in... running along I look left, I look right.... then... it happens! I get jumped! I panic, adrenaline kicks in and I fang it down the road... then realise my pet isn't getting to hurt so turn around and head back. Level 21?! Pfft I can take that easy.

Nerves settled a little I continue on, after reaching the docks I get teleported over to the other island where it starts to set in I am not in cansas anymore. Following the instructions I run through Darkshore and head into Ashenvale... checking a few mobs there I feel pretty safe, they are all high teens and low 20's...until... I start getting level 29 rotting goo's on my screen, there's that panic feeling again. Not so much scared of dying, but scared of dying in a zone where I have no idea on where I might end up.

I manage to make it across the entire zone in 1 piece and only loosing 1 pet, then I see The Barrens entrance, and the level 30 red con guards. I stand for a while pondering if they would attack me or not, see for some reason the fear had removed the knowledge from my brain that if they are red around their name they will attack me.. .just like the mobs I have been fighting for the past 20 level's will. I take a step forward... another step... another step.... AAAH crap (think a different word there) they jump me! I wish my pet the best of luck and bolt out of there.

A little up the road I feel safe to stop and get a new pet, this time I ask in general chat how I am meant to get through, get told to go to the left of the entrance there is side gate. Woo hoo! Prepared I run back in and go through the gate without stopping...aah safety. I continue down the road and go around the crossroads as mentioned in the directions and am running down the road extremely wary but feeling fairly secure.

Out of the corner of my eye... I see it... it's huge... it's gigantic! It's looking at me!!! I swear my characters legs buckled from under her and she moved back on her butt in a scrambled state of panic. I didn't know what it was, I just knew I didn't want it eating me. After getting far enough away I managed to target it and with the yellow around it's name I knew it wouldn't attack me. That's when I got the shot of 'A Big Lizard".

I continued down the road until I found my quest guy, got my next piece and high-tailed it out of there so fast. I didn't really feel secure until my feet touched back in Stormwind. With that my quest was complete and I got off the computer to wake the boyfriend so he could go to work. He simply laughed that I was still up. Because of the adrenaline from that quest I had managed to stay up 48 hours on only 3 hours sleep. I promptly passed out at 9am and didn't see the rest of the day.
