The earth was Scarred with destructive was of both the burning legion and the scourge but here.. at Mount Hyjill.. we see below us the great battle field the plains are covered with both humans...Orcs...Tauren and even soem of the night elves. beside them lay the wretched demons of the burning legion. What once were friends soon became foes in this battle. for everytime one warrior fell it would soon raise to serve the burning legion.. but no.. our story does not take place here.. it takes place back in Lorderon.. where many of the scourge are now free from the control of Nez'hrul.. Also known as the lich king.. Sylvanas Windrunner.. mistriss of the Foresaken accompanied by a Dreadlord who has killed one of his own. attempt to revive the spirits of great spell casters who had fallen with in the battle against the burning legion.

For they must bolster their forces, the foresaken were of their free wills but they didn't have enough forces to defend themselfs from the human paladins they been merly useing their banshee's to posses the hearts of men, and the wemon. With the new break through in their dark arts they bagan to reincarnate the fallen spell casters spirits into liveless Human Bodies that they belive were fresh just killed moments ago.
This is the fate of what was once a great Shaman Warrior who led the shamans against the Demons forces his name was Zieg. He and Archimond had Locked hands in a Mage battle trying to give enough time for the Night elves his warriors used up so much of their mana to their warriors with the blood lust that some of them had even fallen from that.

Blood ran down the side of his face as he snarled at the Demon he was left with Grome hellscream earlier the haze was lifted but he had the powers of a powerful Warlock, he knew this would close his fate to the horde but he had to buy them enough time though befor he could make another attack Archimond threw a blast of fire towards him burning away another part of his armor as he skidded back on the ground the ghouls shrieked and ran to him to start devouring him though with the luck a mighty Tauren came charging in swinging the axe blindly sending the whole lot of them flying behind their evil Master.

Zieg began to rise the scent of blood and death were getting to him he could hardly control his rage it was as if he found a new source of Mana. He roared with fury sending a intensive fire ball towards some ghouls who were about to devour a wounded orc burning them all to a crisp. He turned seeing his fallen warriors all sound blurted out only hearing the clashing of metals in the distance the rage was taking over him blood continued to run down his face over his chest.

Archimond was laughing in the distance which brought him out of it growling from his finger tips he blasted a powerful chained lightening towards his Doom gaurds not stopping until their fell. from his left the Death knight Arthas seemed wounded. Though Zieg couldn't bother himself with matters of that Coward. He growled as Archimond was fueling his rage sending off massive ammounts of Lightening Shields on his men causing them to hurt themselves. To him now that he had helped his Brothers in wounding the hideous enemy befor them it was if all faded leaving him and Archimond alone on the battle field,

Zieg was breathing heavily trying not to Stumble, from the lack of blood he didn't even know that he was actually dieing.. he was making his death sentance clear for he was using up to much of his energy that blast of fire was causing internal bleeding inside of his chest his lungs were filling up with his own blood.. he coughed watching the blood come out he growled then him and Archimond began their assault he sent off four fire balls towards him but they were again easily engulfed by Archimonds' Dark energy's it seemed that all hope was lost for Zieg could hardly stand on his own 2 feet it wasn't until he seen the Arrow come from behind him and turned around seeing the pack of Night elves he found the strength the courage to bring Honor to the horde..

As the sun began to rise over the trees onto the battlefield Archimond the greatest enemy the world had faced was killed by the night elves. many of thralls forces were killed off. the Witch doctors scoured around finding warriors who were half alive. many of the orc and tauren warriors roared for their fallen breathern as laid the corspe of Zieg the last Shaman from the Storm Weavers tribe.

Meanwhile now that the foresaken had aquired some kind of new source of mana.. they captured the spirits of many shamans, mages. Warriors and others. The necromancers gathered around they began their ceramony. Soon in one lifeless human corpse the spirit of Zieg began to fall into it.. soon the corpse's eyes flashed open a cold icy green color they were he gasped able to breath his mind was corrupt in and the necromancers seen this in him.. They took him off in chains for he was violent not knowing what happened.. They threw him in a cell for him to tire himself out befor they tried another attempt they looked back at the 4 necromancers that were apart of the ceramony.. Now they laid slaughtered by Zieg's Magic. Now Zieg Shaman orc. is now to become Zieg the undead Warlock..
