Let me Preface this by saying that I am of the belief that Talent Builds are pretty much only needed for PvP purposes. I cannot find any evidence to convince me that for example, an Affliction/Demonology Build would make my Destruction spells any less effective in PvE than they normally are(i.e. I can still triple-DoT with my Pet tanking, or supporting, and still throw my DDs and have them do good damage). to that end, my build ideas are focused on PvP, since I play on a PvP server. i believe that talents simply improve certain areas of your character, but do not detract from the 'neglected' areas.

with that said, here is my bid at a "Burn" Warlock, focused on Improving his Fire Spells as well as Direct Damage, and Pets, while focusing less on DoTs and Curses.

Affliction Talents (5 points)

# Improved Corruption - 5/5 points
Reduces the casting time of your Corruption spell by 2 seconds.



Demonology Talents (13 points)

# Improved Imp - 3/3 points
Increases the effect of your Imp's Firebolt, Fire Shield, and Blood Pact spells by 30%.

# Demonic Embrace - 5/5 points
Increases your total Stamina by 15%, but reduces your total Spirit by 5%.

# Improved Healthstone - 2/2 points
Increases the amount of Health restored by your Healthstone by 20%.

# Improved Succubus - 3/3 points
Increases the effect of your Succubus' Last of Pain and Soothing Kiss spells by ?, and increases the duration of your Succubus' Seduction and Lesser Invisibility spells by ?.



Destruction Talents (33 points)

# Improved Shadow Bolt - 5/5 points
Your Shadow Bolt critical strikes increase the next 4 sources of Shadow damage dealt to the target by 20%.

# Bane - 5/5 points
Reduces the casting time of your Shadow Bolt and Immolate spells by 0.5 seconds.

# Shadowburn - 1/1 point
Instantly blasts the target with 87 to 99 Shadow damage. Requires 1 Soul Shard. If the target dies from Shadowburn, and yields experience, the caster gains a Soul Shard.

# Devastation - 5/5 points
Increases the critical strike chance of your Destruction spells by 5%.

# Improved Searing Pain - 5/5 points
Increases the critical strike chance of your Searing Pain spell by 10%.

# Ruin - 1/1 point
Increases the critical strike damage bonus of your Destruction spells by 100%.

# Improved Immolate - 5/5 points
Increases the initial damage of your Immolate spell by 25%.

# Emberstorm - 5/5 points
Increases the damage done by your Fire spells by 10%.

# Conflagrate - 1/1 point
Ignite a target that is already afflicted by Immolate, dealing 197 to 251 damage and consuming the Immolate spell.

with this build in PvP I'd be focusing on Searing Pain as my main single-target DD, and Immolate/Conflagrate (along with the Insta-Dots) with Rain of Fire and Hellfire for AoE fun. Shadowbolt would be relegated to Stand-Off/Beginning of Raid status (or another situation where I have time to cast) due to the long casting time, and lack of Nightfall for Instas.

I don't have much experience, but from looking at this, between me and my Beefed up Imp/Succubus, I could pump out a LOT of damage in a short period of time with this build, while still maintaining a decent amount of long-term damage/support with my Base(read: not improved by Talents)Affliction talents.

the question I have here is, How do I tweak this to fit Pyroclasm and Intensity in there? i imagine I might need them both for Hellfire/Rain of Fire. and are the Improved Pet Talents giving a good enough Improvement to be truly worth taking/maxing out?


tear it apart guys, you are the experts, and I'd appreciate the feedback. thanks!
