This will be a story about my name.
I will keep continuing it as long as I have something to say.

Chapter 1: The Quest

"I was used to be a treasure hunter,
my name WAS BlackFox.I WAS a human spellcaster.
I had a small shop in Stormwind, in time before the Legions 2nd return,
I was a trusted by everyone in the city, and they often asked me for help.
One day a man in came to me. As I saw him, I was completely petrified.
He had a cloak on himself, which completely covered his face and his body.
Althought as he was walking towards me I could here his armor's clashing.
Then he finally began to talk:
-Are you the famous BlackRox, the treasure hunter? -he asked
-Yes I AM BLACKFOX
-There's no need to be rude young one
His voice was so chilling, that i began shake.
-I'm looking for the ancient Sword of Corruption, and that is what you should get for me
-I'm sorry but I have other matters to attend to firs...
-I said you get me the sword!
I suddenly felt a powerful piercing pain in my mind.
-I can keep doing this as long as I want, so will you get me the sword?
-Yes, sir...
As suddenly as the pain came, it went away.
-The sword is in an ancient temple somewhere under the Barrens. I will watch your progress, and I hope you won't fail, and I don't need to deliver more pain to your body than it can take. See you soon...
The cloaked figure went towards the door, but instead of going through it,
he dissaperared. My assistant, Ailui came in. I told her that I go on a treasure hunt, and don't come back for a long time. She was unhappy,
but I had to go. I went out to the nearby Inn, where my friends always stay. I met them at our favourite table.
-Hi guys. - I said
-Hi - they both said.
I sat down to them and ordered the best dwarven ale. I told them about my new Quest. They said they come with me, because I would fall. I was happy that both Alitta and Retep came with me. Alitta was a tall, sturdy warrior. Retep was a short hunter. After drinking the ale we all went home, to have our last rest before the Quest that changed our whole life."

Chapter 2: The Last Ride
"During the night, the wanderer came to me. He woke me up ans said this:
-You will begin your journey, when the Sun comes up. You'll look for Monag, a mad orcish shaman. He lives in the Barrens. Once you find him,
I will talk with him.
-As you wish - I answered.
The wanderer dematerialized before my eyes. At morning, I met with All and Rep at front of my house. They both were on their horses. I called forth the spirit of Ynad, my white wolf. I grabbed on his back and we all went forth. The objective was the Barrens, but getting there was a long journey.The route was: Dalaran, Booty Bay, Barrens. We stopped at a small village about 2 hours from Dalaran. We leashed are mounts to a column near the inn's wall. When we get in there was something strange going on: everyone was running from 2 moving skeletons, and a living dead creature! When we saw that we grabbed our weapons. All used a huge sword, Rep used a dwaven gun, and I used a 1-handed sword. All went against the zombie, (as I called it) Rep and I went against the skeletons. The zombie grabbed a table, and threw it against All. He was too slow. The table hit him and he fell on the ground. When Rep saw this, he began to shoot the zombie (as he had no chance against the skeleton).
The 2 skeletons came after me. I used one of my powerfull spells against him: the skeletons skull was ripped apart by purple energy. It's body fell on the ground. The other one however came into melee range. I hit it's sword with my own: he dropped it. Seeing the opportunity I quickly cut his skull down. I took a look at the zombie: it was full of holes, it's head was on the floor but it was still moving. Only magic could stop it: I used another spell on him, it froze, and All quickly crashed it's frozen body.
The villagers were very happy, and they let us stay in the inn for free.
The next morning we had some bread in the inn, and moved forward with our mounts towards Dalaran. During the route we encountered the undead (as we called them) ten times! I began to feel that everywhere we went, the undead came. Well, in Dalaran we met my friend Arcanister who teleported us into Booty Bay. There we asked around about Monag.
We heard that he lives in a tent right in the middle of the Barrens. A zeppelin took us there. I began to fear about what we gonna face through-out the rest of the journey. From the zeppelin we saw the tent. Luckily the centaur didn't see us. The zeppelin put us down near the tent. There was enough space, so are mounts came with us. We got off the zeppelin, and it got away! Suddenly the wanderer materialized next to us.
He went in the tent. We followed him. In the tent, the wanderer asked Monag:
-Old one, I'm looking for the Banned Temple
-Ooohhh, de themple! Ooohhh, its rigghhtt here under da thent!
(He casted a spell, and a trapdoor in front of us opened.
-Whass ar ya laaking far dere?
-The Sword of Corruption.
-Whash? Nah, U moustn't ge-
(He was unable to countinue, as he had the wanderer's sword in his heart...)
-You heard that? Get moving!
(He dissapeared.)
We went forward down through the trapdoor. After about 500 steps, we finally reached a door. A skull was over it. All quickly touched the door. It opened, but All froze. When I saw that I tried to dispel the effects, but there was no way. Rep suddenly rushed trough the door. I shouted "no",
but he didn't stop. He almost reached the sword, but a huge spike came out from the floor, and it went right trough him. I ran towards him but he was dead. The wanderer came again.
-Give me the sword. -he said
-My friends lie in blood thanks to you.
-Give me the sword.
-Never!!
He pointed towards me, and a great deal of energy came towards me, and pushed me on the ground. He started to go towards the sword. I got up, with great pain and grabbed the sword myself. As soon as I grabbed it my mind become to get full with evil thoughts. I had to kill him. There was no other way. I ran towards him and pushed the sword through his heart. He fell on the ground, dead. Then I got another idea: I went to the bodies of Rep and All, and pushed the sword into them. They both woke up, and said this:
-At your command Master"

Chapter 3: Enter the Evil

2 months have passed since the dissapear of BlackFox. He was away from the human world. He opened a portal using the swords powers, which lead him trough the Twisting Nether, right into a small dimensional island. That was his home. He changed his name "Lord BlackFoxus" as his servants called him. He made many things under the 2 months. With the sword corrupting his mind, he got grear ideas. He went back into the mortal world, looking for small guilds of men. He found 10, and he corrupted the souls of those who were parts of the guilds. Like that he gained over 100 servants. With the servants and his own powers, they built up a castle in his dimension. He lived there for after. Although he realized that he will need more servants, so he came up with a new idea: he wanted to corrupt
one of the greatest Paladin orders those years: the PlagueKillers. He planned everything. One day he opened a portal back to the land he once called home, and got back with Rep. They went to the PlagueKillers guild hall. Rep chainged into his human form (when his body corrupted, he changed into a humanoid bug-like demon) and they went in. They met the Guildmaster. In his private room there were 5 guardians and 2 mages.Lord BlackFoxus quickly chanted a short, but powerful spell, which locked the main door. The guardians and mages saw that and they pulled out their swords, equipped their staffs. Rep changed into normal form and quickly jumped over a guardian, and broke his neck. Meanwhile Lord BlackFoxus went against the guildmaster. The guards tried to attack him but Lord BlackFoxus threw them away with his mind. He got the sword in to the gm's chest. His corruption started. By that time Rep teared apart the last guard's armor, then his flesh... From that time the corruption of the guild was an easy one. Lord BlackFoxus mentally commanded the gm to send his warriors through his portal. At the castle's cloister he made a powerful corrupted magic source, where his servants threw the newcomers. 1 week later the whole guild was his. He was quite pride for all this. For months he prepared everything: he fought guild battles, hunted down ancient creatures, and went into a new dimension, the word told him about. There he gained a powerful armor and robe. He also meditated and taught a lot. He became a truly powerful entity and magicuser. For years he lived like this, but one day something has happened....

Okay, 1 last chapter, it is soon coming!

Excerpt from "Of Beginnings and of Endings"

Today is different. I knew it before I was aroused from my slumber. I felt it in his dreams, in the suns waning warmth, fear. Many men have claimed to have mastered fear but to have been met with something he had never imagined in his worst nightmare...a nightmare so vivid and terrifying that he would have rather gouged his eyes out than to face looking upon this abomination summoned forth from the black pits of hell! This was fear and it gripped my frame like a noose on the neck of a murderer. Never had such a fear of this magnitude been conceived to assail the world of men. A fear of invasion! The Dalaranian mages had no warning for the attack and magnitude of the invasion! Nothing prepared them for what was to come...The Burning Legion, save one prophet whos ramblings where dismissed as nothing more than a madmans tale of genocide and a fanciful encounter with other worldly beings. All of the mages scoffed at the prophet, so arrogant where we that no truth reached our minds. We who had built a city of grandeur, the likes of which the world had never seen! We...oh if only we had believed his tale, perhaps Dalaran would still be standing. Yet this is merely a supposition, nothing could have been done...the power the demons wielded against us was far too powerful. After Antondios fell we tried a counter attack yet we failed...all our efforts where in vain! And as the sky blackened and behemoths of emerald flame rose to tear asunder what was once called our home, I fled. Fled far from the torture of watching my brethren die in combat with the demonic invaders. I fled, but that was not enough. The demons reached deep within the twisting portal of hatred that served as their domain and pulled forth from the darkness hounds with eyes of fire and teeth and claws of steel! Fear grips me whenever I look into the darkness and know they are there waiting for me. Waiting to drain my life of my Mana* and then rend my flesh from my bones to display on their war banners of discord and chaos! Let not one sing a eulogy of my death nor let no one herald me as an avatar of peace for I am nothing but a coward! I fled from my brothers and left them to fates that are too gruesome to describe! I fled for my preservation! I fled out of fear...*sigh* it has been many months since those times of shadow and flame. I have set about making as little spectacle of myself as possible. A nameless vagabond, who came on horseback, weary and weathered by months of flight. I had arrived at my destination. Kahz Modan, the realm of the stoic dwarves lords of the mountains and kings of the subterranean. Here I would be safe here I would be protected from that which pursued me so relentlessly...
