Truely I haven't seen Ultimus resurface in a long while...anyway a little CC is good but being a pure CC class is out of the question for the warlock. In my vision the Warlock can become three things each one corresponding to his or her talent choices...

1. The Fire Cracker

yep with the arrival of the second and third tier Destruction talents the Warlock gets a big up to his AOE effectiveness. Also I have noted the change to Hellfire now has the spell doing around 3150 at level 60 in 15 seconds. Now at the highest levels...Hm...24% to stun for 3 seconds and 12% to daze for 5 seconds, as I said earlier big up. Oh and lest not forget the 70% resist disruption of your AOE spells. Need I list the advantages?

2. Killing you Softly

Corresponding talents are the Affliction talents which allow you to instant cast Corruption which is a big plus then when you go lower on the tree you get improved life tap, improved drain life, improved drain mana-each of which causes you to gain health, and mana while harming the enemy (the only exception here is life tap which uses your health to convert to mana). This allows you to attack constantly with little down time between fights. Strong points are the strong DOT damage that can be put on multiple opponents and the new and improved curses.

3. Creature Feature

Super strong utility is the shining point of this design stronger than average pets are a highlight as well. In fact this tree coupled with the last talent of the Affliction tree could be a very popular option among this class. Mostly due to the fact that the 250 mana drained from the pet can be drained many times and with fell intellect you can drain more and increase your SDPS (sustained). The stones are improved increasing damage absorbed and health restored by the spell stones and health stones respectively. Which increases utility of the class, and with a plethora of casting time reductions you know they won't be without a pet for long. The true shining star of the demonology tree is the 25% damage circumvent to pet called soul link. The rest I don't really feel like commenting on....Super strong pets and strong utility are the strong points.

Well those are what I see for the class...of course this is completely of my opinion feel free to disagree with any part of my post if you will. If you envision something different for the class then please do post your ideals.
