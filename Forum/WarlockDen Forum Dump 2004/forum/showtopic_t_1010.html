We don't like Ultimus here? bwahahaha cool!

Lately I disagree with most of what he suggests, but in his earlier write-ups he was spot-on so he got a following. I do alot of CC, especially emergency CC, and I only DoT very effectively when soloing. Alas that will be the nature of the beast, especially in pvp as warlock are actually not the desired class I'd pick for DoT if I had to pick a ground-up method.

Look at the classes that can DoT (Let's not count warrior's rend and druid's moonfire, since they are small).

Rogue, Warlock, and Priest.

How do they do it effectively?

Rogue: Semi resiliant medium armor class that can dodge.

Priest: Healer and shielding class that can off-tank (Or could, less so now).

Warlock: Uses pets as a distraction.

Now I'm not going to say that garrote + poison + slow poison is a really good DoT combo, basically because I don't know rogues, but I do think the other class abilities of priest and rogue are better suited towards useful DoTs. Our other spells make us some kind of survival knife: Life Drain, Syphon Mana, the shard based abilitied and soul draining, the curses, fear, and pets. CC sounds like the easy way out for warlocks to be effective, and that's why people push for them I gather.

If you were to ask me which could be spread out to be more effective, CC or DoTs, I would have to say DoTs. Giving all classes a DoT for long fights or solo play seems a bit more logical. WoW is too fast paced for a DoT class. Of my DoT spells, none ever make it to be worth their mana cost in a full group (against non-elites) except maybe immolate, and none ever make it to completion. In fact agony never makes it to completion in solo play anymore on a mob that is not guarenteed to kill me.

That link about Crowd Control from blizzard's main site is pretty basic too. Recklessness, Banish, Seduction, and others are not on that list for the warlock, and neither is Mind Control from the priest.

If it is our ill fate to be destined for crowd control in WoW, I'd like to see it done by killing the crows instead of enchanting them. A nice AoE snare + Dot spell, hellfire and ran of fire being leaned on more, cripple (I'd love cripple), and a set of spells to make snaring and DoT go together for adds or even chain pulling.

But if we're going to make it at DoT we need some new abilities too, being able to cash out our damage with an instant cast spell would be nice, and a DoT that starts weak and gets progressively more deadly as the fight goes on appeals to me too for those nasty raid bosses.

Oh well here's hoping (but being realistic)
