Is there any goal greater than undeath? Only one, serving the Lich King...

"Arthas the Death Knight and ruler of Lordaeron traveled to Northrend to rescue his master, the Lich King Ner'zhul, who had been imprisoned by the demon Kil’jaeden 17 years before. He arrived in time and freed his master from his icy prison, where after the Lich King merged with the Death Knight. This was the end of the Scourge but the beginning of a new era..."

The Eonian Council is a guild for the World of Warcraft game by Blizzard. The Council is serving under the will of the Lich King and works to further his cause, no matter to what end (while having a good time). The aim of the Council is to run an organized group of individuals who long for the days past gone. The Lich King must be returned to his former position of power and the Scourge must be reunited. The Forsaken, the treacherous Undead, who have turned from the King, must be stopped, petty squabbles must be set aside and alliances must be forged, not broken.

The guild was founded at the end of 2003 to be a small guild only for the undead, which focused on role-playing and social interaction on the European server.

Interview found on Only-WoW

Guild Master - Bhaerau Spellvein

How long has your guild existed?

Offgame: Since late December 2003 when the forum came online
Ingame: Since forever.

How many members do you have?

We have been raising a couple of bodies per week. Currently we have 6 members. 5 from Europe and one from the USA.

Are you a Horde or Alliance guild?

Horde, or more precisely Undead. We serve only the Lich King.

List three words that describe your guild and tell us why.

Evil - You can't get around the fact that the living dead are evil, so no need to hide it. We relish it.
Honor - Just because you are evil, you don't have to shed decency and become a monster. You could call us the "good" badguys.
Reinstate - We are on a quest for our liege. Once his power was nearly supreme, and so it will become again.

What do you look for in a new member?

Currently we are looking for the willingness to play Undead. For the more sober virtues, then we are looking for serious players who know how to behave and role-play.

How important does the guild view role-playing?

It can be easily stated: role-play > the Lich King. We prioritize role-playing immensely.

What are you most excited about with WoW?

WoW features a historically lush world with ample opportunities to have fun.
Besides that, knowing Blizzard's past deeds, then WoW will shake the very foundations of the world.

How did you find Only WoW?

We read about the new WoW guild forum at battle.net and stumbled upon a post introducing the site. Always eager to promote the Council we checked it out.

Thank you very much, Bhaerau Spellvein!!
