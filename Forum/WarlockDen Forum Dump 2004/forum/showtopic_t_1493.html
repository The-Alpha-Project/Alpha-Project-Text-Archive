Enchanted Gold Bloodrobe

Quote:
I just spent the last couple hours doing the majority of this quest.

I was lucky and found 2 gold bars in a chest a day or two ago, so that part was extremely easy.

Now, here's How the quest works.

At level 32 you can get this quest.

Here are the components needed.

1) Robes of Arcana
http://www.thottbot.com/?i=1542
The stats of the robe are +8 intellect +6 spirit.
The thottbot link shows the rarity of the pattern, and the stats of the robe in question. In fact, this pattern can only be recieved off of mobs found in alliance zones. So, come retail, you probably won't see any horde warlocks doing this quest.
If you don't have the pattern but know someone who does, this requires
16x Silk
2x Spider's Silk
2x Fine thread
Or if you don't have that they tend to usually sell for anywhere between 3 and 6g at the AH.

2) Gold Bar
For some this may be easy, for others not as much so. Gold bars can be hard to come by.

3) 10x Vial of Hatefury Blood from level 31-33 Satyr in NorthEast Desolace.
It took me quite a few kills to get this. In the 20-30 range.

4) Lesser Infernal Stone
These are level 36/37 mobs in Mannoroc Coven in Southernish Desolace. Not an easy mob at level 32 when you can first get this quest.

5) Soul Shard - Easy enough, you make those.

6) Golden Thread - Easy enough, you already did that part, you just have to pick it up.

7) Smoldering Coal - Here's the problem, the mobs in question that drop this, are level 38/39.
This is quite the difficult task for a solo level 32 warlock to walk up to a 38/39 to try and get these smoldering coals for their level 32 warlock quest.

Granted, you don't have to do quests as soon as you get them, but in this case you do, and here's why.

http://www.thottbot.com/?i=3967

You do all that work, and turn a +8 intellect +6 spirit robe into a +8 stamina and +11 spirit robe. That's pretty lame IMO.

Now, let's compare this robe, to other robes available.

Link

There's a list of all the robes available between level 32 and 38.
Here's a couple singled out.

http://www.thottbot.com/?i=20268
http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=12698
http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=12808
http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=10989

In fact, here are the robes i wear. I got them in RFK a few levels back.
http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?i=2761
IMO much better than the enchanted gold blood robe, and takes much less effort.

The named that drops these is near the front of the instances, and has a 38% chance to drop those robes.
http://www.thottbot.com/index.cgi?n=57744

Not bad IMO, and vastly superior to the Enchanted Gold Bloodrobe.

All in all, i think this robe needs to be VASTLY improved.

At the level that you can obtain these robes, you can easily get a much better pair of robes for much less effort. Especially on horde side come retail, i bet it'll be rare to see people wearing these robes.

Just got this robe, compare to the Orb of Orbil quest item, this robe is far more worth questing for...

Anyways, the hardest part is to do this quest in PvP server and go to Rachet all the time for the next step... When I did this quest I had the "fortune" of meeting up with 2 full boat load of hordes raiding elwyn forest and redRidge... (btw Emb are you on PvP server? Because I just like you to know that on our PvP server right now, hordes>alliance in number and level, which is the reverse of what is happening on CB servers I heard)

On to the quest itself, to save some hassle have the Robe of Arcana and 1x Gold bar ready when you go to rachet, since you can complete the first step right there. Well not really since you have to return to BB to give the gold bar to the tailor there and get him to make "gold threads" for you.

Return to Rachet again...

Then you will need to fly to desolace and get 1x lesser infernal stone, 10x vial of Hatefury... I was unlucky and had to stay there a long time for the stone... But it shouldn't take so long...

Now go to Rachet , and you will be send to get Gold thread from BB, and 2x Smoldering coal at arithi highlands from the fire elementals...

Then return to rachet ONE MORE TIME... and give Ms. Voidrender, and give her everything and a soul shard and you are DONE!!!

Besides the ganking and the bug in drop rate that I ran into... (I am just so lucky right? ) I thought the quest xp, journey and reward to be good... Highlights for me:

1) 1500+ xp at every step (on average, more for later steps, less for earlier ones)
2) Nice way to see new places, give me an excuse to travel, to desolace NOT rachet
3) While hunting the infernals and such I felt for the first time COMPLETE!!! I mean ALL of our spells are useful at that place! Sense demon is great, banish worked like a charm, and... err I was gonna say "Enslave Demon" was great there too, but that would just be lying...
4) I like my new robe, it fits warlocks, red and black in color and it is warlock ONLY!!! :P

Oh just a reminder, while you are picking up this quest, you should get the "knowledge of the Orb" quest too. I know I said that it was crap for the work you have to put into, but you will be hunting in desolace anyways so why not kill two birds at once?
