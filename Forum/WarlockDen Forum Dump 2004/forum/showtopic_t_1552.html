Since we are primarily casters, the better weapon is which one gives you the best stats. For example, if you are going for a stamina template, and you are choosing between your best staff which has +5 to stamina, and your best sword has +4 stamina and your best off-hand item has +2 stamina... then you are better off with sword and your off-hand.

I personally tend to equip my best stats items when I'm in a group and use either a dagger or sword when I'm solo. I like to keep all my weapon skills high, and the staff doesn't hit often enough to help me interrupt caster mobs when I'm solo.
