Okay so for the longest time I thought I was going to play PvE but one thing led to another I ended up on PvP server (test 9) for ST2 and OB. So why do we suck so much!? Okay I just have to get that out of my system first...

1) Either Hordes players are more experienced or that Horde side quests got a lot better, but there are a lot more high lvl hordes on the PvP server...

2) Priests and shamans are too powerful in pvp and all other classes need some empowerment:) (This is from my observation and from talking with alliance CB testers), esp. UD with their anti-fear...

3) According to CB testers that I played with the environment in the current PvP server is much more hostile... It is very hard to do quests in contested areas... And hordes seem to absolutely love to hunt down a single lone gnome...

4) It seems that as a warlock we are not very good at 1 v1 pvp, or running away... And that we HAVE to take the initiative to attack someone first... Or else you will never get fear off... I still make the mistake of walking by a lower lvl hordes thinking "live and let live" only to be zerged a moment after...

5) Not all is bad though, on the rare occassion that we are not being camped, BS'ed while fighting, and actually have a even grp vs grp pvp, it is such a blast!!! And locks does well in that environment...

On closing I would like to say I hate shamans, priests and rogues on the enemy side

Oh and I am a lv 37 gnome lock so look me up if you are a alliance or horde and want to have some fun pvp
