The Succubus is by far our weakest Pet. *UNLESS* you need her one skill--Seduction.

The Imp will out-damage her in terms of DPS if you put points in Improved Imp and Improved Firebolt (Improved Imp also helps groups). You can also use a weaker Firebolt to drain less mana--the Imp can generally out-DPS the Succubus in any fight of 30 seconds or less, plus you get the HP.

Lash of Pain is worthless, it does no more damage than an ordinary attack--at least not at high level. Perhaps this is different at the lower levels. At high level, the Succubus hits for 72-75, and can whip for 87 once every 12 seconds. This is not particularly impressive.

She has 30% Damage Reduction and 50% the Voidwalker's hit points. Voidwalker has ~48% Damage Reduction. This number does not seem to scale; if it rises it does so VERY slowly (on the order of one-quarter of 1% per level).

More than any other Pet, the Succubus needs some loving. Save for Seduction or in rare situations where her balance of lower DPS / slightly longer life is directly advantageous, she is not a useful Pet.
