Medivh...

Magus, madman, monster... All of these and more, but most importantly the greatest sorcerer to walk Azeroth in generations. He who could bend all others to his will, even the fearsome lords of the Burning Legion, who quake at the mere mention of his name still. He who rent open the dread Dark Portal and spilled the orcs out upon this great domain, then bought them to heel and drove them on to Stormwind by his command. All this and so much more, the merest fraction of the power at his command.

Power that should rightfully be harnessed again, and harness we shall. Let others cower and speak of the dangers of wielding such fel magics. While they remain blinkered and ignorant we shall stride tall accross battlefields littered with those who oppose our acendancy. Neither the barbaric orcs, the odious Scourge, or the vile spawn of the Legion shall stand against the Alliance after we unleash the savage onslaught of the abilities we have at our command.

We are the Scions of Medivh, the heirs to his power and greatness, we shall not be denied, and woe to thee if you attempt to do so.

OOC

You can find basic info on the Guild here

A brand new guild of my creation and I'm looking for some interested players who don't mind the semi-evil theme.

The Scions of Medivh are an Alliance guild that accepts all races and classes, though casters fit the theme best they can't do everything themselves, so there's always a place for others.

Looking for mature players (not necessarily in age) who like to engage in RP, if you like PvP too, even better, but it's not a requirement.

For now since we're just starting off, things will be more or less a loose fellowship, with few demands on members, but as more people join and the guild knits together participation will be able to increase and we can hold raids, tournaments, or go on quests. (I plan to make one of the requirements to be promoted to officer taking a 'pilgrimage' to Karazhan.)

I do have a couple rules though, nothing to radical, but they are important to me.

1. Have fun! Not everyone will want to roleplay every second they're online, so if you don't feel like it at the time, no problem, no one can force you to and it's not as if you'll get in trouble for it. If you feel like soloing, working on your profession, or just dancing, then go ahead.

2. No dealing with eBay on either end of the sale, and no setting up bots. I probably don't have to say this, but it's better to get it out of the way anyway.

Anyone who's interested just send me a tell in game to Citharius, or post here. (the latter is probably better because I'm having alot of problems with 131 and 132 errors)
