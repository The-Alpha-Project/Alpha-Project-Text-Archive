I have slowly been working on a write up of info for the shards and stones to help those newer to the game, and just the other night, less than 3 days ago now, I was working on the info about the Soulstones, of course those notes had to be thrown into the trash with the new changes Guess laziness can pay off, don't really have to re-do the page now Ok, so with the new changes Soulstones are now soulbound, and basically a buff.

I made Thor my guinea pig and in Duskwood we stopped to see how it all worked. Yet another spell description that didn't really make a whole lot of sense to me, so when I first tried to do it I thought I had to target the person I wanted to cast it on, eegh wrong! It still summons like a normal soulstone, except now, from your inventory you right click to the person you wish to put it on. In this case I had Thor targeted and when I right clicked it showed up like a buff on him.

Now lets get some facts about this spell....

  • It has a 30 minutes cool down timer.
  • The spell only lasts for 30 minutes on the person you buff
    • That makes the spell only available to one person, not a big deal when it's only Thor and myself, however full groups will be interesting.
  • It still takes two soul shards to cast, and the annoying part is that if the effect isn't used (ie the person dies) then it's basically like throwing two shards over a cliff.... fun but pointless

So now I have a spell that has a 30 minute cool down timer, which is the same as the refresh timer, making it only available to one person, which in groups obviously goes to the rezzer... so a spell that I don't even get to use? I can see the Warlock buff bots already being created. Another thing that irks me is that we farm shards enough as it is, and while I am lucky with Thor in that I don't go through that many (unless he "oops" eats a stone for no reason... ) that it's not such a huge deal for me, but there are a lot of Warlocks out there that chew through the shards like they are candy, and not to mention full groups that all want their healthstones.

Don't get me wrong, I'm in no way saying that group members shouldn't ask, or be entitled to their stones, but given how much we have to collect shards (or farm if you aren't already getting them in an xp group) I am surprised to see Blizzard give us a spell which is basically, although helpful, is a shard eater. We were fortunate... sort of, that Thor did in fact die while this buff was still on him, purely due to me not paying attention *snicker* to make the shard use acceptable. I didn't however, cast it on him for the rest of the night, which if you calculate it :

2 hours followed that death of more fighting...
2 shards every 30 minutes = 4 shards an hour...
That's 8 shards I can now go and happily throw off a cliff

Personally I do think this spell needs to be tweaked or at least looked at as it makes it almost useless now. The only thing that occurred to me later in the night is, if you are alert and paying attention, you could summon the soulstone at the start of the night and keep it in an easy to access bag. If you see one of your members near death, or you know that the pull with extra adds on the side is going to prove a problem, chuck the buff up on whomever needs it (in my case Thor, in a groups case either the tank or rezzer).

It's not an instant buff, there is a short channeling time.

As far as shard collection goes, that is actually a good point about these new soul stones.

Before patch: I kept at least 8 shards in my bag specifically to hand out SSs in case of a party wipe. Now, I only need to keep 2 shards that are flagged as being available for the next soul stone. Then if I cast myself another soul stone as soon as I used the first one, that's taking 2 bag slots down to 1, I just can't use the ss for 30 minutes.

For the argument of shard usage, the current way is better than the old way. I went through shards like mad in an instance. Now, having to use only 2 every half hour is like a sigh of relief.

However, I wish the cool down was a lot lower. Right now if your rezzer dies right after you buff him, you have to wait 30 minutes before you can buff him again. Bah.
