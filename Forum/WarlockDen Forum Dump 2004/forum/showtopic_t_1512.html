Depending on what stat you are going to focus in (you might want to look at +sta just in case you need to help tank occasionally, but embla would be the best to talk to about that) I would definitely say beefing up the pets would be a good idea. I also play in a perma duo, but I'm lucky enough to have a Pally as my partner so he can tank for me.

I would think about making Corruption insta cast too (Improved Corruption) as that will help you with the extra damage. I normally put Curse of Agony and Corruption (mines insta cast) on straight away so that those are eating away from the start.

I keep getting stuck on thinking you will probably need to either tank as you level up, or group up a little more than I do because some of the elite mobs could munch through my Voidwalker later on. And you don't want to bounce that agro by over nuking and have the mage turn into a mob snack.

Play around with what you two feel comfortable with and see what works best. If I remember correctly, later on Mage's get a form of root which will come in handy I'm sure, and with the Sheep + Succubus, adds shouldn't be too much of a problem.

I love hearing how different duo's work (as I'm always happiest in a duo or small group) so keep us updated on how you're finding it.

Well as most here I know I strongly recommend sta build for warlocks since this helps either if you want to tank (like I do, 3900hp unbuffed is nice at lvl 58) or if you use vw/felhunter to tank since you then have more hp for life tap.

Anyway for duoing with a mage it's a bit tricky since mages like to burst dps while you do sustained, there are ways around this but usually the mage will outdmg you.

First of all there is no reason not to get improved corruption since that turns a half decent spell into an instant spell making it very good especially when you also have nightfall (btw I love the changes to that since the last patch).

And it is like you says that almost all warlocks are going for affliction with a little in demonology/destruction (demonic embrace/shadowburn) and the reason for this is that the demonology tree and the destruction tree is considered weak, there really aint many things in both of those trees that are really usefull.

The only usefull demonology talents are demonic embrace and to a lesser degree imptoved imp, vw, succu. rest of the tree is doing things that are near useless or are buffing spells that are useless to start with (spellstone anyone?)

Destruction is a little better however but it still is lacking in usefull spells. The highlight here is shadowburn, the best finisher we have. But rest of tree is lacking with severeal semi decent talents but overall not good unless you plan on having an aoe build.

The highlight for talents are afflction where nice things like Nightfall, Improved Corruption, Dark Pact, Improved Drain Life, Fel Concentration, Shadow Mastery all help things that you will use a lot especially in instances where it is most needed to play as effective as possible.

Aggro is such a huge problem for mages. If mages use the highest level nukes, chances are, neither Torment or Shadowbolt is not gonna pull aggro off of them. So... what I am really trying to say is that a mage should use the same technique they do when in a full group. Use lower level spells most of the time, keep your mana up and aggro off of you. I think the warlock is actually the faster of the two. If the mage is nuking like they would in solo, the warlock will be twiddling their thumbs while the mage is drinking and eating

The warlock in the duo really doesn't have a whole lot to worry about. Use your pet to tank. Tank yourself if you need to. While it seems kinda like a waste to throw DoT's out, it is damage and pretty low mana cost damage at that. I don't usually worry about a curse when duoing, although Curse of Recklessness is nice when fighting running mobs. I really don't like Shadowbolt, it takes too long to cast and it's damage really ain't that great. I don't think we need a better nuke spell, I prefer using our other strengths. You can pretty much chain Drain Life and Life Tap forever. I would stick with Affliction build.
