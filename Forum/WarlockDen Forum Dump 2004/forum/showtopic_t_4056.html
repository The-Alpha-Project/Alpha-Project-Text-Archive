The following are personal rules of mine, things that bug me. They won't get you banned from the boards if you break them, it will be worse, you'll tick me off and no one wants to mess with that

My name is Akasha! I don't answer to variations, it's not that hard a name to get right. This also relates to babe, sexy etc.

I also have a long time partner so attempts to tell me I am pretty, sexy, hot etc thinking it will impress me, won't, if anything it will bug me. Especially when it's obvious, like you have never seen my picture and only know me by my avatar. By all means compliment me, a girl can never have too many compliments, but I can also see right through ones given just to get on my good side. Call my character hot all you want, I purposely make her hot!

I'm a girl... yes that's right, 100% female. Female gamers are no longer a rare species to be ogled, we wish to be treated like any other gamer you meet on line. The same goes for the forum, if you treat me differently because I am a girl, I will treat you differently because you don't have a clue

"Bump" is something that can bug me, I don't mind one or two, but threads that have repeated bumps (mostly guild section) will have their bumps removed. To help keep your guild known, provide some added info about something that the guild did or any changes. Example : We just killed xxx mob, we have xx amount of members etc.

Keep things relatively clean. While some of us live in Guttersville (myself included) and it can be amusing in small amounts, we don't want these boards to turn into an R rated film. Use wisely to avoid permanent banning of it.

I am a major animal lover so please refrain from posting tasteless animal jokes. I have a sense of humor and can laugh with the best of them, but there is a difference between humorous jokes and ones that make you cringe.

I like to think I have a very good sense of humor and love to joke around with members! Sometimes it's just easier to get these little annoyances out in the open to avoid any issues later on.
