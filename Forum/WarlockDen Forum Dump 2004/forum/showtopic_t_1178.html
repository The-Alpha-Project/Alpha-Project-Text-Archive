aye, it's a bit chilly there. but the great forge in the center of Iron Forge is nice and toasty. Iron Forge is nice because it has everything you need up until level 10 when you have to do your pet quests. then you have to go to stormwind and elsewhere.

i enjoy Iron Forge. especially since it's the only accessable auction house for a long time. auctions are a great way to sell those drops you don't need but someone else does. once i started auctioning things i found i was never short of money, even when i had to buy skills after levelling.

plus you can pick up many gnome quests when you get to higher levels in Tinker Town, which happens to be right next to the warlock area of Iron Forge.
