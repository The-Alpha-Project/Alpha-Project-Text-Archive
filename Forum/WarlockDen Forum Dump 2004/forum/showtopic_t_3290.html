In order to help solidify a leading role in World of Warcraft, House Maelstrom will be opening it's doors to new members once again.

The type of people we are looking for are self sufficient yet teamwork oriented. New members to our House are expected to be as much of a leader as the Officers of Maelstrom. We have always been the type of players who put the guild above our personal interests because we know that making the group stronger, makes ourselves stronger.

We expect all new recruits to focus on XP until you get to a decent PvP level. That being said, House Maelstrom was created for PvP. We are a "strike force" guild, attacking in small groups and would prefer to stay away from the mindless zerg.

The House as a Family is the most important concept for you to understand. We are an established, proven guild. The house stands together for one another in times of War and Peace and you will be expected to uphold our tradition of honor on and off the battlefield. We follow the Horde.

Read more about our guild here:
http://www.housemaelstrom.org

Access our Forums here:
http://www.maelstrom-swg.org/6/ubb.x

If we sound like a guild you would be interested in, feel free to introduce yourself in the General section of our boards.

We look forward to meeting you,
