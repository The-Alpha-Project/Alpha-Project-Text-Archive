(Story time)

Thor doesn't really paint the truest picture, let me tell you how it was

We were in the process of doing my Felhunter quest last night, which involved a bit of running to Ratchet which is in The Barrens, Horde territory. Thor always gets this crazy giggle going when we enter "their" area's, it's really quiet disturbing. We take a boat the Theramore Island and we're running through Dustwallow Marsh on our way to The Barrens. The crocodiles in this zone have been eating steroids by the way, they are HUGE, and for some odd reason I keep forgetting I am level 34 and think "oh crap" when I realise they are 36... 37 and 38 level mobs, I do it all the time... I get nervous and then Thor reminds me these are the level mobs we are normally fighting... oh... yeah... right I knew that.

So we're running thought quite happily, chatting along the way when a Troll runs past us (comes from behind and continues on in front) and he is faster than us so is gaining distance. This is when I hear "oops" from beside me and notice the Troll stopped dead in his tracks and turned around. "haha! I think I hit him," says Thor as I see the Trolls pet (he's a hunter) go rushing at Thor... who by the way is all "woo hoo" with excitement.

The Troll then casts a "rain" type spell on me (had arrows and blue effects) but as I can keep my hands to myself wasn't flagged for PvP, thus the spell had no effect. Realising this, the Troll turns his full attention to Thor while I am /v cheering in the background. Thor's dead pretty damn quickly and after the Troll jumped on his corpse a couple of times, turns to me and /salutes Of course I have to /salute back and he runs on his merry little way.... while I.... stand around and wait for Thor to return to his body.

I started to worry if Xmas had come early with the giggles and "that was fun" coming from beside me as he sounded like he had just received his first present. All in all he had fun, and I have fun watching him, I'm pretty sure the Troll had fun too.

So boys and girls, when you are running down the paths of the apposing faction's zones, be sure to resist the T button for the 'feel' of it as you'll never know when you kick one up the butt
