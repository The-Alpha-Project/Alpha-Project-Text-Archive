Talents are a way to specialize your character by enhancing certain attributes, characteristics, skills, or abilities. Each time you level, you receive talent points that can be used on the talents below. Note that not all talents are available to all classes. Your character must have the skill or ability that is being enhanced in order to get the talent. For example, Dagger Specialization requires the Dagger skill, while Fire Specialization is only avaialable to classes with Fire magic skills (such as the Mage and Warlock). All classes have access to the attribute enhancements, magic resistances, and slayer abilities.

The following number of talent points are awarded at each level:

Level 2-9: 10 Talent Points
Level 10-19: 15 Talent Points
Level 20-24: 20 Talent Points
Level 25+: ?

to see all the talents click the link http://db.wowvault.ign.com/View.php?view=Talents.List&category_select_id=1
