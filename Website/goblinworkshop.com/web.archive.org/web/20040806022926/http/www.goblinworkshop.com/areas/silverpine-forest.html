Silverpine Forest:

Level 10-24 Zone

Plagued by the werewolf minions of the arch-mage Arugal, Silverpine is an evergreen forest on the western coastline of Lordaeron. On the southeastern side of the forest, the city of Ambermill contains the remains of the denizens of the destroyed city of Dalaran. The Forsaken town of The Supulcher rests near the coastline on the northwestern section of town.
