Hillsbrad Foothills:

Level 1-50 Zone

Between the Arathi Highlands and the Alterac Mountains lies the rolling foothills of Hillsbrad. Home to both undead and human cities, the Hillsbrad Foothills are a pivitol proving ground in the war for Lordaeron.
