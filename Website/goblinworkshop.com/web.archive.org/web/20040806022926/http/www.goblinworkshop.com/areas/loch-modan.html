Loch Modan:

Level 1-30 Zone

Loch Modan is a rolling area covered with shrubs and pine trees. At the center of the zone stands 'the Loch'. It is the home of the dwarven village of Thelsamar, a cave complex and some ruins. Troggs have overrun most of the area, and the Mo'Grosh ogres have established a defended cave on the northeastern section of the zone.
