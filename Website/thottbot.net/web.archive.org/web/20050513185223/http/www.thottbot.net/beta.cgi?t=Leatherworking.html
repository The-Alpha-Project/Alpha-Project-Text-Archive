 Reply Posted by spermy on 2004-09-22 13:52:25
The subclasses are purchased from NPCs in different zones.  Tribal is in Stranglethorn Vale, Elemental is in Arathi Highlands, and Dragonscale is in the Badlands.  Each subclass merely adds 2 nicer items to your recipe list, hopefully this will improve at a later time.  Right now Tribal gives you the Feathered Breastplate and Wolfshead Helm, Elemental is Helm of Fire and Gauntlets of the Sea, Dragonscale is Dragonscale Breastplate and Dragonscale Gauntlets.
