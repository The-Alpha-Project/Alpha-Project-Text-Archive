Reply Posted by Senmaera on 2004-11-22 14:52:40
Actually, selling potions can make you quite a bit of money really.  Either by stupid people or by just selling large quantities of it.  For instance, (at lower levels) liferoot and kingsblood are really easy to get and they are the two components needed for greater healing potions.  Get up a bunch of those two components and make a ton of greater healing potions and sell those.  There will always be people that need or want those.  Same with mana potions, swiftness potions, underwater breathing potions, fortitude potions, etc..  So there is quite a few potions that will always be nice to have and that are fairly easy to make.  They may not sell as high as items produced from other professions, however you can have a much higher quantity to sell which would basically equal it out.
