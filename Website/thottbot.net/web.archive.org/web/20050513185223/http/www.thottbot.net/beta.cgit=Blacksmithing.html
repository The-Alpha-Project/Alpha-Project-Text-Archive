RE: Hunters and Blacksmithing
 Reply Posted by psychokitten on 2004-11-18 14:25:18
Leatherworking is far more suited to Hunters than Blacksmithing, especially at lower levels.  A large number of the high end leatherworking patterns are actually for mail because of Hunters and Shaman getting the armor upgrade.  The high end Blacksmithing plans, however, are mostly for plate because of the armor upgrade for Paladins and Warriors.

The only crafting profession that goes well with Enchanting is Tailoring because Tailoring does not have an associated gathering profession.  If you take up Blacksmithing/Engineering without Mining, you'll find yourself paying exortive prices for your raw materials from the AH.  Same goes for Leatherworking without Skinning and Alchemy without Herbalism.
