 Reply Posted by Kongg on 2004-11-09 11:32:08
Last edit: 2004-11-09 11:33:04
Rogue poisons is achieved through a quest that begins at level 20.  For alliance characters it starts in Stormwind Barracks upstairs.  You must first talk to an operative near a Keep in Westfall near Duskwood.  Next you will have to steal a key from one of the guards (pickpocket) outside, then make your way upstairs where there is a level 26 elite guarding a box.  This quest is best done at level 24-25.  Using sap you can easily dispatch the elite boss and open the box with the key you looted outside.

 Reply Posted by Daniszeman on 2004-11-20 13:52:08
For Horde/Undead etc. ppl, go to Orgrimaar-->Cleft of Shadows and look for troll. You should either go with a group of rogues of about 3 or 4, or go yourself with lvl 26+. I did it with me lvl 23 and 3 other ppl 20-22.

On another note, I personally selected herbalism/alchemy with  cooking/fishing. Reasons: Herb is easily collected. You'd be surprised how many skill points you can earn on this(also if you collect certain plants, they are worth A LOT!). Alch: you need herb with this. you can easily make potions to buff yourself up since rogues don't already have these abilites. Fishing is important because not only fish are there. sometimes there are locked boxes(perfect for rogues) and rare alch supplies. Notes: Don't upgrade alch too much unless you need it because it will make the already existing potions worth less skill points. Same goes with herb. try to max out skill b4 upgrading. :)
