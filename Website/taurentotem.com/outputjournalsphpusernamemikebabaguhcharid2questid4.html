After getting to know the basic controls of the game with my Human Mage, I decided to opt for a class that can take more punishment, as well as actually use some of the loot I was finding (Mages can only wear cloth armor). I thus created a Night Elf Warrior, and went about exploring the elvish homeland.

The Elves are quite isolated, having their stronghold located on an island only accessible by walking for miles and then taking a hippogryph flight.

After questing/exploring for several hours, I arrived at the magnificent elvish capital, Darnassus.

I get to the gates, and I find this towering beast:


I decide to explore the rest of the city, and find more sentient trees:





I take in the sights of my capital, and find a teleporting beacon on the western edge of the city. I find myself in a coastal town possessing a hippogryph flight to the mainland. I didn't take any shots of this flight, as it was just over open ocean.

I wind up in Auberdine, on the northern coast of Kalimdor. It seems there are no shortage of quests here. One takes me on a long run south to deliver a message to something called "Onu." Hmm, silver is silver, so I accept, and take off down the southern road. After running for what must have been a solid 10 minutes, I encounter this Onu creature.



Thinking the Gods must have neglected this forest spirit, I deliver my message and return to Auberdine. I meet an elven woman who claims my fighting prowess is laughable! She says she can train me up a bit, but first must do a favor for someone back in Darnassus. ><

It seems there are still demon remnants in our homeland! The vile beast must be slaughtered, and I immediately leave to seek this creature and end its life in the name of Elune.

I find it at the top of a mountain, bathing in the sacred waters of a Moonwell, no less!



I slay the creature easily, and pray that the forest spirits were not too harmed by its presence. Upon returning, I receive a book as a reward, which has knowledge on new styles of fighting. I study every page, and am newly confident in my fighting abilities.

But wait, now this woman mocks me further, claiming I have an inferior weapon! Augh! She tells me of a sunken ship off the coast of Auberdine, which was carrying valuable Elunite Ore. This ore could be forged for a weapon, if only I could retrieve it. Anxious to put this woman's mockeries to rest, I fly to Auberdine and seek out this sunken ship.

Preparing myself for an underwater adventure, I take in a view of the open water, thinking this place is surely gloomy.



I find the lighthouse on an offshore island, and take a dive under the waves. I find the ship on the far side of the island.



After doing some elunite recovery from the seafloor, I encounter the disembodied spirit, the former captain of this ship. She seems to be angry about her undead status, and so I move to free her of her unlife. I finish the job, but not before a strange underwater creature viciously attacks! Wounded from the fight with the spirit, and running low on breath, I flee to the surface, but alas, the creature kills me.

To my dismay, my ghost rematerializes on the other side of the ocean! I curse whatever foul Gods are toying with me, and decide to swim for it, thinking the experience penalty isn't worth it.



My map shows where my corpse is, near the mainland, and the green blip above it is me swimming my arse off (heading south). After a solid hour, I finally return to the crash site. More bad news, the spirit captain seems to have come back to life! Looking around and seeing no beastie sea creatures, I once again engage the spirit, and once again relieve her of her unlife.



Thinking I've had enough of this dreary coastal town, I return to Darnassus.





Upon returning, I deliver the goods.



I speak with a blacksmith, and he forges the ore into a nice sword.




For now, I rest, but eagerly await my next adventure!
