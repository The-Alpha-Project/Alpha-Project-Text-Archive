Released: March 2004, Beta
Location: Elwynn Forest

Herb Gathering Silverleaf Bush. After you cleared the immediate area of beasties, and there are usually a few, gathering only takes a few seconds. It can fail and also if you don't have enough room in your backpack you will have to clear space and then gather again before you can pick up and 'loot' the plant.
