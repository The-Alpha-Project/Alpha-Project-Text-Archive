Druid - Hunter - Mage - Paladin - Priest - Rogue - Shaman - Warlock - Warrior

The Mages of Warcraft once centered their powers within the mageocracy of Dalaran. After that kingdom's destruction by the Burning Legion, however, the arcane arts spread to the far corners of the world.

The Mage is a very important and helpful party member. One of the Mage's most powerful abilities is to put a monster to sleep. This makes things much easier for a party if too many monsters attack the party as the Mage can disable one of those monsters. The mage can also teleport around. Mages can even summon in food and drink to help everyone recover faster from battle.

Mages are only available to members of Warcraft III's alliance, the Dwarves and the Humans.

Mages can be interesting characters to play, with their Invisibility and Frost Armor skills. They also are a good addition to any group with their ability to cast Fireball.


Available Equipment
Cloth
Available Weapons
Staves, Wands, Daggers (with training), Swords (with training)

Skills
Arcane Magic - Conjuration - Fire Magic - Frost Magic

Arcane Magic
Arcane Intellect: Increases the target's Intellect by X for Y min. Always have this ability active on yourself and cast it on other players that need it as well.
Arcane Missiles: Launches Arcane Missiles at the enemy, causing X damage each second for Y seconds. This spell has a spectacular animation.
Sleep: Puts the enemy to sleep for up to X sec. Any damage caused will awaken the target. Only one target can be asleep at a time. Sleep is one of the most important spells for the Mage in parties. Sleep allows the Mage to do crowd control, which means using Sleep on an extra monster that is pulled on purpose or by mistake. This happens all the time, so Sleep should be used often. Whenever there is more than one monster, it's important to Sleep the extra monster. Pick a good target for Sleep. Often the most dangerous target or whichever monster the team is not attacking, should be put to sleep. Teach your party members to not wake sleeping monsters with a melee attack or area of effect spells. Teach them to use /assist also. If there are multiple Mages in a party, they can put multiple monsters to Sleep.
Dampen Magic: Dampens magic used against the targeted party member, decreasing damage taken from spells by X and healing spells by Y. Lasts Z min. Use this to protect party members from spell damage but realize that healing will be reduced.
Amplify Magic: Amplifies magic used against the targeted party members, increasing damage taken from spells by X and healing spells by Y. Lasts Z min. This would be helpful if you want to increase healing and are not worried about increased spell damage.
Arcane Explosion (AoE): Causes an explosion of arcane magic around the caster, causing X to Y damage to all targets within Z yards. This ability would be useful when the caster is surrounded by targets.
Detect Magic: Detects beneficial magic effects on the target for X min. This is an information gathering spell. It's helpful to know what buffs the monster is using so you know how to counter them or know what the monster is capable of. If you can remove those buffs it is even more helpful.
Khadgar's Unlocking: Magically unlocks treasure chests and other locked items.
Remove Lesser Curse: Removes 1 lesser curse from a friendly target. Curses can be damaging, last a long time and annoying. Use this to remove curses from yourself and friends.
Blink: Teleports the caster to the targeted location, breaking free of any bonds that hold her. Blink is a spell Mages enjoy. It has multiple uses from getting to locations easier to escaping from being trapped by a monster spell.
Mana Shield: Absorbs X physical damage, draining mana instead. Lasts Y min. This is a spell to use if you're in trouble and about to die. Since it drains your mana, your hope is the monster will die (usually in a party situation) before you do.
Counterspell: Counters the enemy's spell X% of the time, preventing him from casting that spell again for 10 sec. Generates a high amount of threat. This ability is to be used against other spell casters. Remember that the monsters will be really angry at you when you use it.
Lesser Invisibility: Turns the caster invisible for X min, though this spell is unstable and may end early. Invisibility is useful for scouting for yourself or a party. It's useful for traveling to avoid monsters. This is a very powerful ability for the Mage. Keep in mind it could wear off at any time. You can't attack while being invisible. There may be monsters that can see you even though you are invisible.

Conjuration
Conjure Food: Conjures X loaves of rye, providing the mage and her allies with something to eat.
Conjure Water: Conjures X bottles of purified water, providing the mage and her allies with something to drink.
Conjure Mana Agate: Conjures a mana agate that can be used to instantly restore X to Y mana. Give these out to other spell casters or use on yourself for emergency situations.

Fire Magic
Fire Blast: Blasts the enemy with fire for X to Y fire damage.
Fireball: Hurls a fiery ball that causes X to Y fire damage and an additional A damage over B sec.
Flamestrike (AoE): Calls down a pillar of fire, burning all enemies within the area for X to Y fire damage and an additional Z damage over A sec.
Fire Ward: Absorbs X fire damage. Lasts Z min. Use this ability when facing a fire damaging monster.
Scorch: Scorch the enemy for X to Y fire damage.

Frost Magic
Frostbolt: Launches a bolt of frost at the enemy, causing X to Y frost damage and slowing movement speed for Z sec. This ability is useful for stopping monsters from running away and warning their friends.
Frost Armor: Increases armor by X. If an enemy strikes the caster, they may have their movement speed slowed to Y% and have their attacks slowed by Z%. Lasts X sec.
Blizzard (AoE): Ice shards pelt the target area doing X damage over Y sec.
Cone of Cold: Targets in a cone in front of the caster take X to Y frost damage and are slowed to Z% of normal speed for N sec. This can be useful as an escape spell.
Ice Armor: Increases armor by X and frost resistance by Y. If the enemy strikes the caster, they may have their movement slowed down by Z% and attacks slowed by A%. Lasts B min.
Frost Nova: Blasts enemies near the caster for X to Y cold damage and freezes them in place for up to Z sec. This is a main ability for the Mage. It is useful in stopping monsters from chasing the Mage so you can get away. It can also be used in combat to finish off weakened, retreating monsters.
