Mages can spend talents (earned for each level gained) in three forms:

Arcane Mastery, Fire Mastery, Frost Mastery

Back to Mage main page

Arcane Mastery
Improved Arcane Intellect: Increases the number of Intellect points gained by up to 50%
Arcane Subtlety: Reduces the threat generated by your Arcane spells by up to 40%.
Arcane Meditation: Increases your Mana regeneration by up to 25%.
Arcane Focus: Reduces the chance that an opponent can resist your Arcane spells.
Wand Specialization: Increases your damage with Wands.
Improved Arcane Missiles: Increases your changes to avoid interruption caused by damage while channeling Arcane Missiles (up to 100%)
Improved Dampen Magic: Increases the effect of your Dampen Magic spell
Arcane Concentration: Increases your chances of getting a clear-cast buff after any damage spell is cast.
Improved Arcane Explosion: Reduces the cast time of your Arcane Explosion.
Evocation:While channeling this spell, your mana regeneration spell is drastically increased.
Arcane Power: When activated, your spells deal 33% more damage while costing 50% more mana to cast. The effects last 15 seconds.
Improved Mana Shield: Increases the damage absorbed by your Mana Shield.
Improved Counterspell: Reduces the cooldown of your counterspells.
Improved Blink: Reduces the cooldown of your Blink spells.
Presence of Mind: When activated, your next spell becomes an instant cast spell.

Fire Mastery
Impact: Gives your Fire spells up to 10% chance of stunning your target for 2 seconds.
Improved Fireball: Reduces the casting spell of your Fireball spell by up to 1 second.
Critical Mass: Increases the critical strike chance of your Fire spells.
Improved Fireblast: Increases the critical strike chance of your Fire spells.
Ignite: Your fire spells critical strike cause your target to burn for an additional percentage of your spell's damage over 5 seconds.
Burning Soul: Gives your fire spells a chance to not lose casting time when you take damage.
Flame Throwing: Increases the range of your Fire spells.
Pyroblast: Hurls an immense fiery boulder that that causes 139 to 173 fire damage and an additional 20 damage over 8 seconds.
Improved Flamestrike: Increases the critical strike chance of your Flamestrike.
Improved Fire Ward: Causes Fire Ward to reflect some of the damage absorbed back to the caster.
Blast Wave: A wave of flames radiates outwards from the caster, damaging all enemies caught within the blast for 83 to 91 fire damage, and dazing them for 6 seconds.
Improved Scorch: Burns the target for additional damage over 5 seconds.
Fire Power: Increases the damage done by your Fire spells.
Combustion: When activated, this spell gives your next fire spell a 100% critical strike chance.

Frost Mastery
Improved Frost Nova: Reduces the cooldown of your Frost Nova by up to 3 seconds.
Improved Frostbolt: Improves the range of your Frostbolt spell by up to 10 yards.
Permafrost: Improves the duration of your chill effects by up to 6 seconds.
Piercing Ice: Increases the damage done by your Frost spells.
Winter's Chill: Increases the power of your chill effects by slowing the target's movement.
Improved Frost Nova: Reduces the cooldown of your Frost Nova spells by up to 3 seconds.
Frostbite: Gives your chills effects a chance to freeze the target for 4 seconds.
Shatter: Increases the critical strike chance of your Frost spells against frozen targets.
Frost Channeling: Reduces the mana cost of your Frost spells.
Improved Blizzard: Adds a chill effect to your Blizzard spells, lowering the target's movement speed.
Cold Snap: When activated, this spell finishes the cooldown on all your cold spells.
Improved Frost Ward: 50% of the damage absorbed by your Frost Ward is added to your Mana.
Improved Cone of Cold: Increases the damage dealt by your Cone of Cold.
Ice Barrier: Instantly shields you, absorbing 183 to 184 damage. Lasts 1 minute. While the shield holds, spells will not be interrupted.
Ice Block: You become encased in a block of ice, protecting you from all physical attacks and spells for 8 seconds, but during that time, you cannot attack, move, or cast spells.
