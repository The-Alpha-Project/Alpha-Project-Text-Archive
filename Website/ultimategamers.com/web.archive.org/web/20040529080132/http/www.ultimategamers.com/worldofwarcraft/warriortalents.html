Druid - Hunter - Mage - Paladin - Priest - Rogue - Shaman - Warlock - Warrior

Warriors can spend talents (earned for each level gained) in three forms:

Protection Mastery, Fury Mastery, Arms Mastery

Back to Warrior main page

Protection Mastery
Shield Specialization: Increases your chances to block attacks.
Anticipation: Increases your Defense skills.
Toughness: Increases your Mana regeneration by up to 25%.
Iron Will : Increases your chances to resist Charm and Stun effects.
Improved Bloodrage: Reduces the duration required to generate rage from your Bloodrage.
Improved Taunt: Reduces the rage cost of your Taunt ability.
Defiance: Increases the threat generated by your attacks while in defensive stance.
Improved Shield Block: Allows your Shield Block ability to block an additional attack, and increases the duration.
Improved Sundering Strike: Increases the armor reduction of your Sundering Strike.
Improved Revenge: Gives your Revenge ability a chance to stun the target.
Combat Endurance: A passive ability that allows your hit point regeneration from Spirit to work while in combat.
Improved Shield Bash: Increases your chances to hit with your Shield Bash ability.
Improved Shield Wall: Increases the duration of your Shield Wall ability.
Improved Disarm: Increases the duration of your Disarm ability.
Concussion Blow: A brutal strike that leaves the opponent stunned.
Last Stand: Whean activated, this ability temporarily grants you 30% of your maximum hit points for 20 seconds. After the effect expires, the hit points are lost.

Fury Mastery
Improved Battle Shout: Increases the duration of your Battle Shout.
Enrage: Improves your chances to become enraged after taking melee damage.
Flurry: Increases your attack speed for your next swings after dealing a critical strike.
Cruelty: Improves your chances to get a critical strike.
Booming Voice: Decreases the opponent's chances to resist a shout effect.
Unbridled Wrath: Increases your chances to generate additional rage points when you deal damage.
Piercing Hawl: Causes all enemies near the Warrior to be dazed for 6 seconds.
Demoralizing Shout: Increases the attack power reduction of your Demoralizing Shout.
Improved Inner Rage: Reduces the cooldown of your Inner Rage ability.
Improved Intimidating Shout: Improves your Intimidating Shout's chances to cower in fear rather than flee.
Improved Challenging Shout: Reduces the cooldown of your Challenging Shout.
Death Wish: When activated, increasesyour melee damage by 20% and makes you immune to fear, but lowers your defense skill by 20. Lasts 1 minute.
Improved Cleave: Increases the number of targets affected by your Cleave ability.
Bloodthirst: After dealing a killing blow, your next swing deals 150% weapon damage and is guaranteed a critical strike. Lasts for 8 seconds.
Improved Slam: Decreases the casting time of your Slam ability.

Arms Mastery
Axe Specialization: Increases your chances to get a critical strike with Axes.
Sword Specialization: Increases your chances to hit with Swords.
Improved Strike: Reduces the cost (in rage points) of your Strike abilities.
Mace Specialization: Increases your chances to stun your opponent with a mace.
Improved Charge: Increases the rage generated by your Charge ability.
Improved Rend: Increases the bleed damage done by your Rend ability.
Improved Thunder Clap: Increases the damage done by your Thunder Clap ability.
Improved Pummel: Increases your chances to daze the target.
Improved Hamstring: Increases the movement slow effect of your Hamstring ability.
Tactical Mastery: You retain some rage points when you change stances.
Sweeping Strikes: Your next 5 melee weapon swings strike an additional nearby opponent.
Two-handed Weapon Specialization: Increases the damage you deal with two-handed melee weapons.
Improved Overpower: Increases the critical strike chances of your Overpower ability.
Spear and Polearm Specialization: Increases your chances to get a critical strike with spears and polearms.
Mortal Strike: A vicious strike that deals 150% weapon damage and wounds the target, reducing the effectiveness of any healing by 50% for 10 seconds.
Deep Wounds: Your critical strikes cause the opponent to bleed, dealing more weapon damage over a period of time.
Anger Management: Reduces your Rage decay by 33%.
