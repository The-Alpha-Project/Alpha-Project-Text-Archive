World of Warcraft is composed of two main continents divided into several regions that all vary in difficulty levels. The fact that some regions are connected doesn't mean that jumping from one to another is safe. For example, Redridge Mountains is adjacent to Blasted Lands, but a level 25 adventurer who could cruise in Redridge would have a very unhappy surprise if he/she was venturing into Blasted Lands.

Depending if you solo or group, the choice of the region where you should go will depend. A Human character who soloes a lot may find it easier to go to Dun Morogh (Dwarf starting retion) or Teldrassil (Night Elf starting region) after finishing Elwynn Forest, while the same character who relies on groups may decide to go directly into Westfall or Loch Modan.

We offer you a list of regions as well as the levels you should have to venture there. These tables are meant for people who group. As a rule of thumb, add between 3 and 5 levels to the minimum if you want to go there solo.
