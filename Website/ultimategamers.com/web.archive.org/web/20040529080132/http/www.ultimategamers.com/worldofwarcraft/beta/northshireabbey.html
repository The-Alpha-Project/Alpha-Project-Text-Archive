Northshire Abbey

I will assume that you have already read Elric's Dwarf Paladin Journal, and as such, have a basic understanding of World of Warcraft gameplay at this point. Therefore, I will not go into the specifics of skills and talents as he did.

Location: As a Human, you start off in front of Northshire Abbey. Players already familiar with Warcraft lore/history will know that they are in Ellwyn Forest. (Just a note that all Human players begin here.)

Quests: Northshire Abbey area: You will be offered seven different quests while you are in the vicinity of the Abbey. These quests, along with the experience you receive for killing the creeps required (and more) will probably get you to level five or six.

Quest: A Threat Within: The second you enter the game as a Human, you will likely notice Deputy Willem, who has your first quest. Once you are ready (hotbars and macros set) go ahead and talk to him. He lets you know that he is having trouble keeping the piece, due to most of the guards being off in distant lands. Willem asks you to talk to his commanding officer, Marshal McBride.

This quest consists of simply entering the Abbey and talking to the Marshal. Once you have done that, this quest will be complete. One important thing you should note, though, is that most future quests like this (that require you to *just* talk to someone) will have more steps after you actually talk to them. McBride does have his own quests, mind you, but this is not a multi-step "talk to" quest.

Quest: Kobold Camp Cleanup: After speaking with Marshal McBride for a moment, he will inform you that a group of Kobolds have moved in to the woods. He asks you to take down their numbers by killing seven Kobold vermin.

After you accept this quest, head back outside the Abbey, and to the Northwest. After a second or two of travel, you will see the Kobolds. Notice that they are yellow. *Do Not Get Use To This.* Future Kobolds you meet out of the Northshire Abbey area will be red, and will attack you on sight, unlike these. Anyway, take advantage of their yellowness and attack and kill seven of the vermin (they will not count towards the quest unless the creep name is actually "Kobold Vermin".)

Quest: Wolves Across The Border: While you are heading northwest from the Kobold Camp Cleanup quest, you probably spotted an NPC named Eagan Peltskinner, and noticed the exclamation point over his head. Stop and get this quest as it is easy, and will let you choose your reward.

To finish this quest, you need to kill the wolves that you see roaming the area. Do so until you have six pieces of tough wolf meat, then return to Eagan. You will now be able to pick your reward, Soft Fur-Lined Shoes (Cloth) or Wolfskin Bracers (Leather).

Quest: Investigate Echo Ridge: Once you return to McBride for your reward, he will offer another quest to kill more Kobolds. He lets you know that there are more Kobolds in the area than they had originally thought. McBride wants you to continue trimming their numbers, this time by killing 10 Kobold Miners.

There are miners in the same general location that you found the vermin. You should be able to kill 10 of them without having to go into the mine.

Quest: Skirmish at Echo Ridge: It seems like the Marshal doesn't like Kobolds, because again he is sending you to kill them. Your task this time is to kill 12 workers and 8 laborers.

The easiest way to do this is to head towards the mine, which is northwest. Kill any Kobold Workers you see along the way. Once you enter the mine, just kill workers as you head deeper. Eventually, you will come to the laborers. At the end of the mine is a chest. There are usually four (yellow) laborers in this room. Do not let the fact that they are yellow fool you. Should you try to open this chest with them still alive, they will all attack you. If you want to open and loot the chest, kill them first.

Quest: Brotherhood of Thieves: Deputy Willem tells you that a group of thieves has moved into the area, and he wants you to bring him some of their bandanas, 8 to be exact.

Head East from the Abbey across the river to find the Defias. Standing at the end of the bridge across the water are two Defias, so watch for them when you cross. All you have to do here is to kill until you get the quest requirement. You will likely have to kill more then eight Defias to get the bandanas you need.

Quest: Bounty On Garrick Padfoot: Willem tells you of an annoying thief and how he has been harassing farmers and merchants. Willem wants you to bring back the cutthroat's head.

Back across the river with the Defias is where you will find Garrick. He is near a small building to the north. Garrick is level 5, and he will attack with another Defias (a level 4). Just make sure you can take the damage they both deal, or kill one of them before they both reach you.

Quest: Report To Goldshire: This is the last quest from the Marshal. He tells you that you are now tasked with the defense and protection of the Ellwyn Forest. He wants you to do one last thing for him, deliver a report.

This quest will take you out of the Northshire Abbey area and on to Goldshire, the Ellwyn Forest, and the rest of the world where you will find more quests, more NPCs, and stronger creeps. To complete this quest, you need only to follow the road out of Northshire and on into Goldshire. You will soon see Marshal Dughan, who you are to give the report to.


[Note:] This part focuses on quests in the first area. The next part will cover the quests in Goldshire, but will also give a good look at the town and what is there.
