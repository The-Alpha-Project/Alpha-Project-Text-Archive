Tamable Beasts with Skills

Back to the Hunters main page


Hunters' pets currently only come with one skill, growl, which the Hunter can teach his pet up to level two.

After that, a Hunter needs to find a Trainer to learn higher versions of that skill.

So far, Blizzard has changed the idea from having specific trainers for each pet class (cats, wolves, bears, birds, etc...) to finding beasts that inherently have some skills. To acquire such new skills, a Hunter must leave his current pet at a Stable, tame a new pet, and verify if that pet has a skill.

After that, the Hunter must then make his new pet attack a mob, and the pet will eventually (after one or two fights) transfer the skill to the Hunter. From there, the Hunter can dismiss the pet, and go back to a Stable, where he can learn the new skill to his regular pet.

So far, only three new skills have been implemented, namely, Claw, bite, and cower. Just as not every pet class has skills, not every (already) tamed class can learn skills. For example, scorpids cannot learn bite, and wind serpents cannot learn Bite.

We are trying to put together a list of pets that possess skills, so that you do not spend too much time trying to find out where to find a pet to learn a new skill, and what pet within that zone has a skill. We are aware that this list is far from complete, but we will update it as many times a possible. If you have any information that should be added to this page, please contact us.
