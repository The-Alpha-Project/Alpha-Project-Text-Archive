Top Ten Lists

Each week, a new Top Ten List topic will be posted here on the vault as well as on the WoW General Board. Raarg27 will be accepting submissions via Private Message or posts made on the week's forum thread. Then he will choose the ten best (the most humorous) and they will be posted here. There is no limit to the number of lines you can post. So, start posting and sending in your responses and remember to include your name/alias/board name/etc so that due credit can be given!
