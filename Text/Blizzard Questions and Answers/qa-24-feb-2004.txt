What is the level limit?
The current character level limit is 60. This is subject to change in the beta.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_transportation.shtml

When can players get their own mount?
The current level requirement is 40. Players also must learn a riding skill which requires skill points. This is subject to change.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_realms.shtml

Will there be player-vs.-player servers where players will be able to fight other players from anywhere in the world?
We will likely have special rules servers, but we haven't made any final decisions on this yet. Watch the web site for any new information.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_characters.shtml

How many characters can a player have on their account?
The current limit is a total of 8 characters across all realms. This number is subject to change.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/index.shtml

Will there be a tutorial?
Yes, we expect to have a tutorial to help players learn the game.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_world.shtml

Will there be maps in dungeons?
Yes, the mini-map will display your immediate surroundings and it will function just like it does anywhere else in the world.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_features.shtml

Will there be environmental sounds?
Yes, from chirping birds to waterfalls, we will have all sorts of environmental sounds in the game.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_gameplay.shtml

Is the game easy to learn how to play?
Yes. We strive to make all of our games easy to learn how to play, yet difficult to master. We will continue to improve this process throughout the beta.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_world.shtml

Are there areas that must by unlocked by a key?
Yes, but there is a lock picking skill in case you ever find yourself without the proper key. There are even puzzles which require players to locate the proper key to open a dungeon.

http://www.blizzard.com/wow/faq/faq_gameplay.shtml

How can you hook up with your friends of different races early in the game?
For some races it's easier than others. For example, the humans and dwarves will have an easy transit between their two cities, while the night elves will find it harder to meet with their Alliance brethren early in the game as they are off in a distant land.

Yes, you can also run the game in a window.

Will you be able to customize the keys?
Yes. This is accessed via the options screen.

Yes. They can do this at any time with the “Z” key.

Will there be secret locations or Easter eggs?
There are plenty of special locations for players to discover. The level designers have gone all out.

What forms of currency will there be?
Currently there is gold, silver, and copper. And in case you were wondering, there are a lot of things you can buy with your money: from training, to equipment, to trade skill recipes, to trade skill components, to extra space at your bank.
