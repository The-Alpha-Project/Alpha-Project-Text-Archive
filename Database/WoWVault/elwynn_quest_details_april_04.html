A Threat Within
By Elena
Description: A quick starter quest for some small experience.
Level Requirement: 1+
Other Requirements: Alliance
Starting Location: Northshire Valley
Starting NPC: Deputy Willem
Reward: 10 Experience
Walkthrough:

Speak with Deputy Willem. He will tell you the following:

I hope you strapped your belt on tight, young warlock. Because there is work to do here in Northshire.

And I don't mean farming.

The Stormwind guards are hard pressed to keep the peace here, with so many of us in distant lands and so many threats pressing close. And so we're enlisting the aid of anyone willing to defend their home. And their alliance.

If you're here to answer the call, then speak with my superior, Marshal McBride. He's inside the abbey behind me.

If you accept the quest, the next step is to speak with Marshal McBride in the abbey behind Deputy Willem. When you speak to him, he will tell you the following:

Ah, good. Another volunteer. We're getting a lot of you these days.

I hope it's enough.

The human lands are threatened from without, and so many of our forces have been marshaled abroad. This, in turn, leaves room for corrupt and lawless groups to thrive within our borders.

It is a many-fronted battle we wage, Elena. Gird yourself for a long campaign.

Click "Complete Quest" and you're done! Marshal McBride will then offer you the Kobold Camp Cleanup quest.

Kobold Camp Cleanup
By Elena
Description: A kill quest to kill some nearby Kobold Vermin.
Level Requirement: 1+
Other Requirements: Alliance
Starting Location: Northshire Valley
Starting NPC: Marshal McBride
Reward: 25 Copper, 130 Experience
Walkthrough:

Speak with Marshal McBride in the abbey in Northshire. He will tell you the following:

Your first task is one of clensing, Elena. A clan of kobolds have infested the woods to the north. Go there and fight the kobold vermin you find. Reduce their numbers so that we may one day drive them from Northshire.

If you accept the quest, then head to the kobold camp just north of the abbey. There should be plenty of Kobold Vermin and Kobold Workers roaming the area. They are not agressive, so there is no need to worry about hunting right within the camp itself. Kill 7 Kobold Vermin and then return to Marshal McBride. He will tell you the following:

Well done, citizen. Those kobolds are thieves and cowards, but in large numbers they post a threat to us. And the humans of Stormwind do not need another threat.

For defeating them, you have my gratitude.

Hit "Complete Quest" and you're done! As a reward, you receive 130 experience and 25 copper. Marshal MrBride will then offer you the Investigate Echo Ridge quest.

Find the Lost Guards
By Mavi
Description: A few days ago we sent two Guards, Rolf and Malakai to investigate along the river, and they have not yet returned. To complete my report, I must know what happened to those men.

Travel north along the river and find the guards, or their remains.
Level Requirement: None
Other Requirements: N/A
Starting Location: Eastern river bridge
Starting NPC: Guard Thomas
Reward:
Walkthrough:

Head north along the west side of the river, and you'll find some bones and bloodied meat on the ground at some point. Inspect it and you'll receive a new quest to search for Rolf. Head east to the Murloc camp for the next quest, clear the area of all Murlocs then look for another similar bloodied bone/meat on the ground. Inspect it, return it to the guard back at the bridge.

He'll then tell you to go back to Goldshire to the Marshal and report your findings. Do so, and he'll then ask you to take a package to a lady in the Eastvale Logging Camp. Head way to the east of Elwynn Forest on the north side of the path to get to the Logging Camp, find the lady in front of one of the houses and complete the quest by speaking with her.

First thing's first, keep in mind you need Large Candles and not the Melted Candles you find at early levels.
